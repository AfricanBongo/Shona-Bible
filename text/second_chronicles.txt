\b	2 Makoronike
\c	1
\s	Soromoni anokumbira njere kuna Mwari
\p
\v	1	ZvinoSoromoni mwanakomana waDhavhidhi wakazvisimbisa paushe hwake, uye Jehovha Mwari wake waiva naye, ndokumukurisa zvikuru kwazvo.
\v	2	ZvinoSoromoni wakataura kuna Israeri wose, kumisoro yezvuru neyemazana, nekuvatongi, nekumachinda ose kwaIsraeri wose, misoro yemadzibaba.
\v	3	ZvinoSoromoni neungano yose pamwe naye vakaenda kunzvimbo yakakwirira yaiva paGibhiyoni; nokuti ipapo paiva netende rekusangana raMwari, Mozisi muranda waJehovha raaiva aita murenje.
\v	4	AsiDhavhidhi waiva akwidza areka yaMwari kubva paKiriati-Jearimi kuiisa Dhavhidhi paaiva aigadzirira, nokuti waiva aidzikira tende paJerusarema.
\v	5	Nearitariyendarira Bhezareeri mwanakomana waUri mwanakomana waHuri yaaiva aita wakaiisa pamberi petabhenakeri yaJehovha; Soromoni neungano ndokutsvaka kwairi.
\v	6	ZvinoSoromoni wakakwirako kuaritari yendarira pamberi paJehovha, yaiva patende rekusangana, ndokupa pairi zvipo zvinopiswa churu.
\p
\v	7	Muusikuihwohwo Mwari wakaonekwa kuna Soromoni, ndokuti kwaari: Kumbira chandingakupa.
\v	8	Soromonindokuti kuna Mwari: Imwi makaitira baba vangu Dhavhidhi ngoni huru, uye makandiita ndive mambo pachinzvimbo chavo.
\v	9	IkozvinoJehovha Mwari, shoko renyu kuna Dhavhidhi baba vangu ngarisimbiswe; nokuti imwi makandiita mambo pamusoro pevanhu vakaita seguruva repasi kuwanda.
\v	10	Ikozvino ndipei njere neruzivo kuti ndibude uye ndipinde pamberi pevanhu ava; nokuti ndiani angatonga vanhu venyu ava vakakura?
\v	11	Mwari ndokuti kuna Soromoni: Nokuti ichi changa chiri mumoyo mako, uye hauna kukumbira fuma, ufumi, nekukudzwa, kana upenyu hwevavengi vako, uye hauna kukumbira mazuva mazhinji, asi wakazvikumbirira njere neruzivo, kuti ugotonga vanhu vangu, vandakakuita mambo pamusoro pavo:
\v	12	Njere neruzivo zvinopiwa kwauri, nefuma, neufumi, nerukudzo ndinokupa, zvekuti hakuna ku mbova nezvakadaro kumadzimambo aiva mberi kwako, uye shure kwako hakuchazovi nezvakadaro.
\p
\v	13	Zvino Soromoni wakauya kubva panzvimbo yakakwirira yaiva paGibhiyoni kubva pamberi petende rekusangana, akasvika Jerusarema, ndokutonga pamusoro paIsraeri.
\s	Fuma yaSoromoni
\p
\v	14	Zvino Soromoni wakaunganidza ngoro nevatasvi vemabhiza. Zvino waiva nengoro churu nemazana mana, nevatasvi vemabhiza zvuru gumi nezviviri, vaakaisa mumaguta engoro uye pamwe namambo paJerusarema.
\v	15	Mambo ndokuita sirivha negoridhe paJerusarema  zvikaita semabwe, nemisidhari akai ita semionde iri pamipata kuwanda.
\v	16	Zvino mabhiza aiva aSoromoni aibva kuEgipita, nemucheka wakapfava, vatengesi vamambo vaiutora mucheka wakapfava nemutengo.
\v	17	Zvino vakakwidza vachibudisa kubva muEgipita, ngoro pa mashekeri esirivha mazana matanhatu, nebhiza pa mashekeri zana nemakumi mashanu; saizvozvowo kumadzimambo ose evaHiti nemadzimambo evaSiriya vakabudisa neruoko rwavo.
\c	10
\s	Rehobhoamu mambo waJudha, Jerobhoamu mambo waIsraeri
\p
\v	1	ZvinoRehobhoamu wakaenda kuShekemu, nokuti Israeri wose waiva auya kuShekemu kumuita ave mambo.
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati paaiva muEgipita maaiva atizira kubva pachiso chaSoromoni mambo paaka zvinzwa, kuti Jerobhoamu wakadzokera kubva kuEgipita.
\v	3	Zvinovakatuma  nhume idzo ndokumudana; Jerobhoamu naIsraeri wose ndokusvika ndokutaura naRehobhoamu vachiti:
\v	4	Babavenyu vakaita kuti joko redu riome; zvino ikozvino, rerusai pabasa rakaoma rababa venyu, nepajoko rake rinorema raakaisa pamusoro pedu, zvino tichakushumirai.
\v	5	Zvinowakati kwavari: Dzokai kwandiri mushure memazuva matatu. Vanhu ndokuenda.
\p
\v	6	Zvinomambo Rehobhoamu wakapangana zano nevakuru vaiva vamira pamberi paSoromoni baba vake pavaiva vachiri vapenyu, achiti: Munopanga zano ripi kupindura vanhu ava?
\v	7	Zvinovakataura kwaari vachiti: Kana mukava makanaka kuvanhu ava, uye mukavafadza, uye mukataura mashoko akanaka kwavari, vachava varanda kwamuri mazuva ose.
\v	8	Asiwakasiya zano revakuru ravaiva vamupanga, ndokupangana zano nevakomana vakakura pamwe naye, ivo vaimira mberi kwake.
\v	9	Zvinowakati kwavari: Munopanga zano ripi, kuti tipindure vanhu ava vataura kwandiri vachiti: Rerusai pajoko baba venyu ravakaisa pamusoro pedu?
\v	10	Zvino vakomana vakakura pamwe naye vakataura kwaari vachiti: Uchataura seizvi kuvanhu vataura kwauri vachiti: Baba venyu vaita kuti joko redu rireme, asi imwi tirerusirei; uchataura seizvi kwavari: Munwe wangu mudiki mukobvu kupfuura chiuno chababa vangu.
\v	11	Naizvozvo zvino, baba vangu vakakutakudzai joko rinorema, asi ini ndichawedzera pajoko renyu; baba vangu vaikurangai neshamhu, asi ini nezvinyavada.
\v	12	Naizvozvo Jerobhoamu nevanhu vose vakauya kuna Rehobhoamu nezuva retatu, mambo sezvaaiva ataura achiti: Dzokai kwandiri nezuva retatu.
\v	13	Zvino mambo wakavapindura neukasha; mambo Rehobhoamu ndokusiya zano revakuru;
\v	14	ndokutaura kwavari zvichienderana nezano revakomana achiti: Baba vangu vakaita joko renyu rireme, asi ini ndichawedzera pane izvozvo; baba vangu vakakurangai neshamhu, asi ini nezvinyavada.
\v	15	Naizvozvo mambo haana kuteerera kuvanhu; nokuti shanduko yaibva kuna Mwari, kuti Jehovha asimbise shoko rake raakataura neruoko rwaAhija muShiro kuna Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati.
\p
\v	16	Zvino Israeri wose pa akaona kuti mambo haana kuvateerera, vanhu ndokupindura mambo vachiti: Tine mugove upi kuna Dhavhidhi? Uye ha tina nhaka kumwanakomana waJese. Umwe neumwe kumatende ako, Israeri; zvino zvionere imba yako, Dhavhidhi. Naizvozvo Israeri wose vakaenda kumatende avo.
\v	17	Asi vana vaIsraeri vaiva vagere mumaguta aJudha, Rehobhoamu wakatonga pamusoro pavo.
\v	18	Zvino mambo Rehobhoamu wakatuma Hadhoramu waiva mutariri wevanhu vechibharo; vana vaIsraeri ndokumutaka nemabwe, iye ndokufa. Mambo Rehobhoamu ndokuzvisimbisa kukwira ngoro yake kutizira kuJerusarema.
\v	19	Saizvozvo Israeri wakapandukira imba yaDhavhidhi kusvika zuva rino.
\c	11
\p
\v	1	Rehobhoamupaakasvika kuJerusarema, wakaunganidza veimba yaJudha naBhenjamini, vakasarudzwa zvuru zana nemakumi masere, vairwa hondo, kurwisana naIsraeri, kuti adzose umambo kuna Rehobhoamu.
\v	2	Asishoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Shemaya munhu waMwari richiti:
\v	3	Taurakuna Rehobhoamu mwanakomana waSoromoni, mambo waJudha, nekuna Israeri wose muJudha naBhenjamini, uti:
\v	4	Ndizvozvinoreva Jehovha: Hamungakwiri kana kurwisana nehama dzenyu; dzokera umwe neumwe kuimba yake; nokuti chinhu ichi chaitwa neni. Zvino vakateerera mashoko aJehovha, ndokudzoka pakunorwisa Jerobhoamu.
\p
\v	5	Rehobhoamundokugara paJerusarema, ndokuvaka maguta enhare paJudha.
\v	6	WakavakawoBheterehemu, neEtami, neTekowa,
\v	7	neBheti-Zuri,neSoko, neAdhuramu,
\v	8	neGati,neMaresha, neZifi,
\v	9	neAdhoraimi,neRakishi, neAzeka,
\v	10	neZora, neAjaroni, neHebhuroni, raiva kwaJudha naBhenjamini, maguta akashoshewa.
\v	11	Zvino wakasimbisa nhare, ndokuisa madziri vatungamiriri, nematura ezvekudya, nemafuta, newaini.
\v	12	Zvino muguta nemuguta ripi neripi  wakaisa nhovo nemapfumo, ndokuasimbisa kwazvo-kwazvo. Judha naBhenjamini vaiva vari vake.
\p
\v	13	Nevapristi nevaRevhi vaiva pakati paIsraeri wose vakazvimisa kwaari vachibva kumigumo yavo yose.
\v	14	Nokuti vaRevhi vakasiya mafuro avo nefuma yavo ndokuuya kwaJudha nekuJerusarema; nokuti Jerobhoamu nevanakomana vake vakavaramba pakushanda paupristi kuna Jehovha.
\v	15	Iye ndokuzvimisira vapristi venzvimbo dzakakwirira nevemadhimoni nevemhuru dzaaiva aita.
\v	16	Zvino shure kwavo kwakauya kuJerusarema avo vakapa moyo yavo kutsvaka Jehovha, Mwari waIsraeri, kubva kumarudzi ose Israeri, kuzobaira Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo.
\v	17	Saizvozvo vakasimbisa ushe hwaJudha, ndokutsigisa Rehobhoamu mwanakomana waSoromoni kwemakore matatu; nokuti kwemakore matatu vakafamba panzira yaDhavhidhi naSoromoni.
\p
\v	18	Zvino Rehobhoamu wakazvitorera Maharati mukunda waJerimoti mwanakomana waDhavhidhi kuva mukadzi,  naAbhihairi mukunda waEriabhu mwanakomana waJese.
\v	19	Iye wakamuberekera vanakomana: Jeushi, naShemaria, naZahami.
\v	20	Zvino shure kwake wakatora Maaka mukunda waAbhusaromu. Uyo wakamuberekera Abhija, naAtai, naZiza, naSheromiti.
\v	21	Zvino Rehobhoamu wakada Maaka mukunda waAbhusaromu kupfuura vakadzi vake vose nevarongo vake; nokuti wakatora vakadzi gumi nevasere nevarongo makumi matanhatu; ndokubereka vanakomana makumi maviri nevasere nevanasikana makumi matanhatu.
\v	22	Rehobhoamu ndokugadza Abhija mwanakomana waMaaka kuva musoro kutonga pakati pevana vababa vake; nokuti kwaiva kumuita mambo.
\v	23	Zvino wakaita neuchenjeri ndokuparadzira pavanakomana vake vose munyika dzose dzaJudha naBhenjamini kumaguta ose enhare; ndokuvapa zvekudya zvakawanda. Ndokutsvaka chaunga chevakadzi.
\c	12
\s	Mambo Shishaki anorwa naRehobhoamu
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika Rehobhoamu paakasimbisa ushe, uye paaiva asimba, wakasiya murairo waJehovha, naIsraeri wose pamwe naye.
\p
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika mugore rechishanu ramambo Rehobhoamu, Shishaki mambo weEgipita wakakwira kurwisa Jerusarema, nokuti vakadarika kuna Jehovha,
\v	3	anengoro churu nemazana maviri, uye ane vatasvi vemabhiza zvuru makumi matanhatu; nevanhu vaiva vasingaverengeki vaiva vauya naye kubva kuEgipita: VaRubhiya, VaSukimi, nevaItiyopiya.
\v	4	Akakundamaguta akashoshewa aiva aJudha, ndokuuya kuJerusarema.
\p
\v	5	Zvinokwauya Shemaya muporofita kuna Rehobhoamu nemisoro yaJudha yaiva yakaungana paJerusarema nekuda kwaShishaki, ndokuti kwavari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Imwi makandisiya, saka ini ndakakusiyaiwo muruoko rwaShishaki.
\v	6	Zvinomachinda aIsraeri namambo vakazvininipisa, ndokuti: Jehovha wakarurama.
\v	7	ZvinoJehovha paakaona kuti vazvininipisa, shoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Shemaya richiti: Vazvininipisa, handingavaparadzi, asi ndichavapa kusunungurwa kudiki; uye kutsamwa kwangu hakungadururwi pamusoro peJerusarema neruoko rwaShishaki.
\v	8	Asivachava varanda vake, kuti vazive shumiro yangu neshumiro yeushe hwedzinyika.
\v	9	NaizvozvoShishaki mambo weEgipita wakakwira kurwisa Jerusarema, ndokutora zvinokosha zveimba yaJehovha nezvinokosha zveimba yamambo; wakatora zvose; wakatorawo nhovo diki dzegoridhe Soromoni dzaaiva aita.
\v	10	Mambo Rehobhoamu ndokuita nhovo diki dzendarira pachinzvimbo chadzo, ndokupa pasi peruoko rwevakuru vevarindi, vaichengetedza mukova weimba yamambo.
\v	11	Zvino mambo paaipinda muimba yaJehovha, varindi vaiuya ndokudzisimudza ndokudzidzosera mukamuri revarindi.
\v	12	Zvino paakazvininipisa, kutsamwa kwaJehovha kwakadzoka kubva kwaari, kuti asaparadza kusvika pakupedza; uyewo kwaJudha kwakava nezvinhu zvakanaka.
\p
\v	13	Zvino mambo Rehobhoamu wakazvisimbisa paJerusarema, ndokutonga; nokuti Rehobhoamu waiva wemakore makumi mana nerimwe paakava mambo, ndokutonga paJerusarema makore gumi nemanomwe, guta Jehovha raakasarudza kubva pamarudzi ose aIsraeri, kuisa zita rakepo. Uye zita ramai vake raiva Naama muAmoni.
\v	14	Zvino wakaita chakaipa, nokuti waiva asina kugadzirira moyo wake kutsvaka Jehovha.
\v	15	Zvino nyaya dzaRehobhoamu, dzekutanga nedzekupedzisira, hadzina kunyorwa here mumashoko emuporofita Shemaya, neemuoni Idho, maererano nemarudzi, nedzihondo dzaRehobhoamu naJerobhoamu nguva dzose?
\v	16	Rehobhoamu ndokurara nemadzibaba ake, ndokuvigwa muguta raDhavhidhi; Abhija mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	13
\s	Abhija mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Zvinomugore regumi nemasere ramambo Jerobhoamu, Abhija wakava mambo pamusoro paJudha.
\v	2	Wakatongamakore matatu paJerusarema. Uye zita ramai vake raiva Mikaya, mukunda waUrieri weGibhiya. Zvino kwakava nehondo pakati paAbhija naJerobhoamu.
\v	3	ZvinoAbhija wakasunga hondo ane varwi vevarume veumhare vehondo, varume vakasarudzwa zvuru mazana mana. Jerobhohamu ndokurongedza hondo kurwisana naye ane varume vakasarudzwa zvuru mazana masere, varume veumhare vane simba.
\p
\v	4	ZvinoAbhija wakamira pamusoro pegomo Zemaraimi, riri pagomo raEfuremu, ndokuti: Ndinzwei, Jerobhoamu, naIsraeri wose!
\v	5	Hazvisizvenyu here kuziva kuti Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri wakapa Dhavhidhi ushe pamusoro paIsraeri nekusingaperi, kwaari nekuvanakomana vake  nesungano yemunyu?
\v	6	ZvakadaroJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, muranda waSoromoni mwanakomana waDhavhidhi, wakasimuka ndokumukira ishe wake.
\v	7	Zvinovakaunganira kwaari varume vasina maturo, vana vaBheriyari, vakazvisimbisa kurwisa Rehobhoamu mwanakomana waSoromoni, Rehobhoamu ari mudiki uye akapfava pamoyo asingagoni kuvarwisa.
\v	8	Zvinoikozvino munoti muchamirisana neushe hwaJehovha muruoko rwevanakomana vaDhavhidhi; uye imwi muri chaunga chikuru, asi mune mhuru dzegoridhe Jerobhoamu dzaakakuitirai dzive vamwari.
\v	9	Hamunakudzinga here vapristi vaJehovha, vanakomana vaAroni, nevaRevhi, mukazviitira vapristi semarudzi edzinyika? Ani nani anouya kuzadza ruoko rwake nenzombe, mhuru yemombe, nemakondohwe manomwe, anova mupristi wevasiri vamwari.
\v	10	Asi isu, Jehovha ndiMwari wedu, uye hatina kumusiya; nevapristi vanoshumira Jehovha vari vanakomana vaAroni, nevaRevhi vari pabasa.
\v	11	Zvino vakapisira Jehovha mangwanani nemangwanani uye madekwani nemadekwani zvipo zvinopiswa, nezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira, nechingwa chekuratidza patafura yakachena, nechigadziko chemwenje chegoridhe nemwenje yacho kupisa madekwani nemadekwani; nokuti tinochengeta faniro yaJehovha Mwari wedu; asi imwi makamusiya.
\v	12	Zvino tarirai, Mwari anesu ari musoro, nevapristi vake vane hwamanda dzinorira kukuridzirai mhere. Vana vaIsraeri, regai kurwa naJehovha Mwari wemadzibaba enyu; nokuti hamungabudiriri.
\p
\v	13	Asi Jerobhoamu wakapoteredza vavandiri kuti vauye neshure kwavo; naizvozvo vakava pamberi paJudha, uye vavandiri vaiva shure kwavo.
\v	14	Zvino Judha paakacheuka, tarira, hondo yairwisana navo, mberi neshure; zvino vakachema kuna Jehovha; vapristi ndokuridza hwamanda.
\v	15	Zvino varume vaJudha vakaridza mhere. Zvino zvakaitika pakuridza mhere kwevarume vekwaJudha, kuti Mwari wakarova Jerobhoamu naIsraeri wose pamberi paAbhija naJudha.
\v	16	Vana vaIsraeri ndokutiza pamberi paJudha; Mwari ndokuvapa muruoko rwavo.
\v	17	Zvino Abhija nevanhu vake vakarova pakati pavo nekurova kukuru; ndokuwa vakaurawa kwaIsraeri varume vakasarudzwa zvuru mazana mashanu.
\v	18	Saizvozvo vana vaIsraeri vakaderedzwa nenguva iyo; uye vana vaJudha vakakunda, nokuti vakazembera pana Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo.
\v	19	Abhija ndokudzingirirana naJerobhoamu, ndokutapa maguta kubva kwaari: Bheteri nemisha yaro, neJeshana nemisha yaro, neEfironi nemisha yaro.
\v	20	Jerobhoamu haana kuzovazve nesimba pamazuva aAbhija; Jehovha ndokumurova, iye ndokufa.
\p
\v	21	Zvino Abhija wakazvisimbisa, ndokuzvitorera vakadzi gumi nevana, ndokubereka vanakomana makumi maviri nevaviri, nevanasikana gumi nevatanhatu.
\v	22	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaAbhija, nenzira dzake, nemashoko ake, zvakanyorwa munyaya yemuporofita Idho.
\c	14
\s	Asa mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	ZvinoAbhija wakarara nemadzibaba ake, ivo ndokumuviga muguta raDhavhidhi. Asa mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake. Mumazuva ake nyika yaiva murunyararo makore gumi.
\v	2	ZvinoAsa wakaita zvakanaka nezvakarurama pameso paJehovha Mwari wake.
\v	3	Nokutiwakabvisa aritari dzeutorwa nenzvimbo dzakakwirira, ndokuputsa zvifananidzo, ndokutema matanda ekunamata nawo.
\v	4	Zvinowakaraira Judha kutsvaka Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo, nekuita mutemo nemurairo.
\v	5	Wakabvisawokubva mumaguta ose aJudha nzvimbo dzakakwirira nearitari dzezuva. Nyika ndokuva nerunyararo pamberi pake.
\p
\v	6	Zvinowakavaka maguta akashoshewa kwaJudha; nokuti nyika yaiva ine runyararo uye kwaiva kusina hondo naye mumakore awa, nokuti Jehovha waiva amuzorodza.
\v	7	Naizvozvowakati kuna Judha: Ngativake maguta awa, uye tiite rusvingo ku apoteredza, neshongwe, masuwo, nemazariro, nyika ichiri pamberi pedu; nokuti takatsvaka Jehovha Mwari wedu; taka mutsvaka, akatipa zororo kukomberedza. Zvino vakavaka ndokubudirira.
\v	8	ZvinoAsa waiva nevarwi zvuru mazana matatu kwaJudha, vaiva vakatakura nhovo huru nepfumo; nezvuru mazana maviri nemakumi masere kwaBhenjamini, vaiva vakatakura nhovo vaitsika uta; ava vose vaiva varume vesimba veumhare.
\p
\v	9	Zvinokwakabudira kwaari Zera muItiyopiya nehondo yechuru chezvuru, nengoro mazana matatu; ndokusvika kuMaresha.
\v	10	Zvino Asa wakabuda kuzorwa naye; ivo ndokurongedza hondo pamupata weZefata paMaresha.
\v	11	Zvino Asa wakachema kuna Jehovha Mwari wake, ndokuti: Jehovha kubatsira hachisi chinhu kwamuri, kana kune vakawanda kana kune vasina simba; tibatsirei Jehovha Mwari wedu; nokuti takazembera pamuri, uye nezita renyu tauya kurwisana nechaunga ichi; Jehovha, imwi muri Mwari wedu; munhu ngaarege kukukundai.
\v	12	Naizvozvo Jehovha wakarova vaItiyopiya pamberi paAsa, nepamberi paJudha; vaItiyopiya ndokutiza.
\v	13	Asa nevanhu vaiva naye ndokuvadzingirira kusvika paGerari; ndokuwa kubva kuvaItiyopiya zvekuti hakuna kumbova nekumutsiridzika kwavari; nokuti vakaparadzwa pamberi paJehovha nepamberi pehondo yake. Vakatakura zvakapambiwa zvizhinji kwazvo.
\v	14	Zvino vakarova maguta ose akapoteredza Gerari; nokuti kutya Jehovha kwaiva pavari; zvino vakapamba maguta ose, nokuti maiva mune zvakapambiwa zvizhinji maari.
\v	15	Vakarovawo matende ezvipfuwo, ndokutapa makwai mazhinji nemakamera, ndokudzokera kuJerusarema.
\c	15
\p
\v	1	ZvinoMweya waMwari wakauya pamusoro paAzariya mwanakomana waOdhedhi.
\v	2	Iyendokubuda kunosangana naAsa, ndokuti kwaari: Nditeererei, Asa naJudha wose naBhenjamini. Jehovha anemwi kana muchinaye; uye kana muchimutsvaka achawanikwa nemwi; asi kana mukamusiya, achakusiyai.
\v	3	Zvinomazuva mazhinji Israeri waiva asina Mwari wechokwadi, uye asina mupristi anodzidzisa, uye asina murairo.
\v	4	Asipakumanikidzwa kwake wakatendeukira kuna Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri, ndokumutsvaka, iye ndokuwanikwa navo.

\v	28	Zvino vakapinda muJerusarema nemitengeranwa uye nembira uye nehwamanda, kuimba yaJehovha.

\v	20	Waiva ne makore makumi matatu nemaviri paakava mambo, ndokutonga paJerusarema makore masere; ndokuenda asingashuviwi; zvakadaro vakamuviga muguta raDhavhidhi, asi kwete mumakuva emadzimambo.

\v	8	Mambondokuraira, ivo ndokuita bhokisi rimwe, ndokuriisa pasuwo reimba yaJehovha panze.
\v	9	Zvinovakaisa inzwi kwaJudha nepaJerusarema kuti vauise kuna Jehovha mutero waMozisi muranda waMwari  wakaiswa pamusoro paIsraeri murenje.
\v	10	Zvino machinda ose nevanhu vose vakafara, vakaunza mukati ndokukanda mubhokisi kusvika vapedza.
\v	11	Zvino zvakaitika panguva yaiuiswa bhokisi kudura ramambo neruoko rwevaRevhi, uye pavaiona kuti maiva nemari zhinji, munyori wamambo nemutariri wemupristi mukuru vaiuya ndokudurura bhokisi, ndokuritora ndokuritakurirazve kunzvimbo yaro. Vakaita saizvozvo zuva nezuva, ndokuunganidza mari zhinji.
\v	12	Zvino mambo naJehoyadha vakaipa vabati vebasa reshumiro yeimba yaJehovha; ndokuhaya vaivaka nemabwe nevavezi kuvandudza imba yaJehovha, uyewo vapfuri vematare nendarira kusimbisa imba yaJehovha.
\v	13	Naizvozvo vaiti vebasa vakaita kusvika kuvandudzwa kwebasa kwaenderera mberi neruoko rwavo, ndokumisa imba yaMwari nechiyero chayo, ndokuisimbisa.
\v	14	Zvino pakupedza kwavo vakauisa mari yakasara mberi kwamambo naJehoyadha, vakaita nayo midziyo yeimba yaJehovha, midziyo yeshumiro neyekupira zvipo, nezvibatiso, nemidziyo yegoridhe nesirivha. Zvino vakapa zvipo zvinopiswa muimba yaJehovha rinhi narinhi mazuva ose aJehoyadha.
\s	Joashi anotsauka
\p
\v	15	Jehoyadha ndokukwegura, uye akwana pamazuva, ndokufa; waiva nemakore zana nemakumi matatu pakufa kwake.
\v	16	Zvino vakamuviga muguta raDhavhidhi pamwe nemadzimambo; nokuti waiva aita zvakanaka kwaIsraeri nekuna Mwari ne kuimba yake.
\v	17	Zvino shure kwekufa kwaJehoyadha machinda aJudha akauya ndokukotamira mambo. Ipapo mambo akavateerera.
\v	18	Zvino vakasiya imba yaJehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo, ndokushumira matanda ekunamata nawo nezvifananidzo; zvino kutsamwa kwakauya pamusoro paJudha neJerusarema pamusoro pemhosva yavo iyi.
\v	19	Asi wakatuma vaporofita pakati pavo kuvadzosera kuna Jehovha; zvino vakavapupurira, asi havana kupa nzeve.
\v	20	Zvino Mweya waMwari wakafukidza Zekariya mwanakomana waJehoyadha mupristi waiva amire pamusoro pevanhu, ndokuti kwavari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Mwari: Munodarikirei imwi mirairo yaJehovha kuti musabudirira? Nekuda kwekuti makasiya Jehovha, iye wakakusiyaiwo.
\v	21	Zvino vakamurangana vakamutaka nemabwe pachivanze cheimba yaJehovha nekuraira kwamambo.
\v	22	Nokudaro mambo Joashi haana kurangarira zvakanaka Jehoyadha baba vake zvavaiva vamuitira, asi wakauraya mwanakomana wake. Zvino pakufa kwake wakati: Jehovha ngaaone, atsive.
\p
\v	23	Zvino zvakaitika pakudzoka kwegore, hondo yevaSiriya yakakwira kumurwisa; ndokusvika kwaJudha neJerusarema ndokuparadza machinda ose evanhu pavanhu, ndokutumira zvakapambiwa zvavo zvose kuna mambo weDhamasiko.
\v	24	Nokuti hondo yevaSiriya yakauya neboka diki revanhu; Jehovha ndokupa muruoko rwavo hondo huru kwazvo, nokuti vaiva vasiya Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo. Nokudaro vakaita mutongo kuna Joashi.
\v	25	Zvino vakati vabva kwaari (nokuti vakamusiya ari pachirwere chikuru), varanda vake vakamurangana nemhosva yeropa revanakomana vaJehoyadha mupristi, ndokumuuraira pamubhedha wake, zvino akafa; ivo ndokumuviga muguta raDhavhidhi, asi havana kumuviga mumakuva emadzimambo.
\v	26	Zvino ava ndivo vakamurangana: Zabhadhi mwanakomana waShimeati muAmonikadzi, naJehozabhadhi mwanakomana waShimiriti muMoabhukadzi.
\p
\v	27	Maererano nevanakomana vake, neukuru hwemutoro waiva pamusoro pake, nekuteyewa kweimba yaMwari, tarirai, zvakanyorwa padudziro yebhuku remadzimambo. Amaziya mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	25
\s	Amaziya mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Amaziyawaiva nemakore makumi maviri nemashanu akava mambo, ndokutonga makore makumi maviri nemapfumbamwe paJerusarema; uye zita ramai vake raiva Jehoadhani weJerusarema.
\v	2	Zvinoiye wakaita zvakarurama pameso paJehovha, asi kwete nemoyo wakaperera.
\p
\v	3	Zvinozvakaitika umambo hwasimbiswa kwaari, kuti wakauraya varanda vake vaiva vauraya mambo baba vake.
\v	4	Asihaana kuuraya vana vavo, asi  wakaita sezvakanyorwa pamurairo pabhuku raMozisi Jehovha waakaraira achiti: Madzibaba haangafiri vana, nevana havangafiri madzibaba; asi umwe neumwe achafira chivi chake.
\p
\v	5	Amaziyawakaunganidzawo Judha, ndokuvagadza zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba kuva vatariri vezvuru nevatariri vemazana, kuna Judha wose naBhenjamini; ndokuvaverenga kubva pamakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, ndokuvawana vari zvuru mazana matatu vakasarudzwa, vanobudira hondo, vanobata pfumo nenhovo.
\v	6	Wakahayawokubva kwaIsraeri varume vane simba veumhare zvuru zana nematarenda zana esirivha.
\v	7	Asikwakasvika munhu waMwari kwaari achiti: Haiwa mambo, hondo yaIsraeri ngairege kuenda nemwi, nokuti Jehovha haasi pana Israeri, ivo vana vaEfuremu vose.
\v	8	Asikana imwi muchienda, itai, shingai pahondo; Mwari achakugumbusai mberi kwemuvengi. Nokuti kune simba kuna Mwari rekubatsira nerekugumbusa.
\v	9	ZvinoAmaziya wakati kumunhu waMwari: Zvino tichaita sei nematarenda zana andakapa hondo yakarongwa yaIsraeri? Munhu waMwari ndokuti: Jehovha ane zvakawanda pane izvi kupa kwauri.
\v	10	Zvino Amaziya wakavatsaura, iyo hondo yakarongwa yaiva yasvika kwaari kubva kwaEfuremu, kuti vaende kunzvimbo yavo. Kutsamwa kwavo ndokupfutira kwazvo Judha, ndokudzokera kunzvimbo yavo mukupfuta kwehasha.
\p
\v	11	Zvino Amaziya wakazvisimbisa ndokutungamirira vanhu vake ndokuenda kumupata wemunyu, ndokurova vana veSeiri, zvuru gumi.
\v	12	Vana vaJudha ndokutapa zvuru gumi vari vapenyu, ndokuvauisa panhongonya yeruwere, ndokuvapotsera kubva panhongonya yeruwere kusvika vavhunikanya vose.
\v	13	Asi vana vehondo yakarongwa Amaziya vaakadzosa kuti vasaenda naye kuhondo, vakawira maguta aJudha, kubva paSamariya kusvika paBheti-Horoni, ndokurova zvuru zvitatu kwavari, ndokupamba zvakapambiwa zvizhinji.
\p
\v	14	Zvino zvakaitika shure kwekunge Amaziya adzoka pakurova vaEdhomu, kuti wakauya navamwari vevana veSeiri, ndokuvamisa kuva mwari wake, ndokuvafugamira nekuvapisira zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	15	Naizvozvo kutsamwa kwaJehovha kwakapfutira Amaziya, ndokutuma kwaari muporofita wakati kwaari: Unotsvakirei vamwari vevanhu vasina kununura vanhu vavo paruoko rwako?
\v	16	Zvino zvakaitika paaitaura naye, kuti wakati kwaari: Takakugadza here kuti uve murairiri wamambo? Regera iwe, ucharoverwei? Muporofita ndokuregera ndokuti: Ndinoziva kuti Mwari wakatemera kukuparadza, nokuti wakaita ichi, uye hauna kuteerera kuraira kwangu.
\p
\v	17	Zvino Amaziya mambo waJudha wakatora zano, ndokutuma kuna Joashi mwanakomana waJehoahazi mwanakomana waJehu mambo waIsraeri achiti: Uya tionane pachiso.
\v	18	Joashi mambo waIsraeri ndokutuma kuna Amaziya mambo waJudha achiti: Rukato rwuri paRebhanoni rwakatuma kumusidhari uri paRebhanoni, rwuchiti: Ipa mukunda wako kumwanakomana kuva mukadzi; asi chikara chesango chiri muRebhanoni chakapfuura, ndokutsika-tsika rukato.
\v	19	Iwe unoti: Tarira warova vaEdhomu; zvino moyo wako unozvisimudzira kuti uzvikudze; ikozvino gara muimba yako. Unozvipinzireiko pane chakaipa kuti uwe, iwe naJudha pamwe newe?
\v	20	Asi Amaziya haana kuteerera, nokuti zvaiva zvaMwari, kuti avape muruoko  rwavo; nokuti vakatsvaka vamwari veEdhomu.
\v	21	Zvino Joashi mambo waIsraeri wakakwira, ivo ndokuonana pachiso, iye naAmaziya mambo waJudha, paBheti-Shemeshi riri raJudha.
\v	22	Zvino Judha wakarohwa pamberi paIsraeri, ivo ndokutizira umwe neumwe kutende rake.
\v	23	Joashi mambo waIsraeri ndokutora Amaziya mambo waJudha, mwanakomana waJoashi, mwanakomana waJehoahazi, paBheti-Shemeshi, ndokuuya naye kuJerusarema, ndokuputsa rusvingo rweJerusarema kubva pasuwo raEfuremu kusvika pasuwo repakona, makubhiti mazana mana.
\v	24	Ndoku tora goridhe rose, nesirivha, nemidziyo yose yakawanikwa muimba yaMwari kuna Obhedhi-Edhomu, nezvakakosha zveimba yamambo, nevaiva chibatiso, ndokudzokera kuSamariya.
\p
\v	25	Zvino Amaziya mwanakomana waJoashi mambo waJudha wakararama makore gumi nemashanu shure kwekufa kwaJoashi mwanakomana waJehoahazi mambo waIsraeri.
\v	26	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaAmaziya, dzekutanga nedzekupedzisira, tarirai, hadzina kunyorwa here mubhuku remadzimambo aJudha naIsraeri?
\p
\v	27	Zvino kubva panguva Amaziya yaakatsauka pakutevera Jehovha, vakamurangana chirangano paJerusarema; iye ndokutizira kuRakishi; asi vakatuma shure kwake kuRakishi, ndokumuurairapo.
\v	28	Zvino vakamutakura pamusoro pemabhiza, ndokumuviga kumadzibaba ake muguta raJudha.
\c	26
\s	Uziya mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Zvinovanhu vose vaJudha vakatora Uziya waiva nemakore gumi nematanhatu, ndokumuita mambo pachinzvimbo chababa vake Amaziya.
\v	2	Iyewakavaka Eroti, ndokuridzosera kuna Judha, shure kwekurara kwamambo nemadzibaba ake.
\v	3	Uziyawaiva nemakore gumi nematanhatu paakava mambo, ndokutonga makore makumi mashanu nemaviri paJerusarema; uye zita ramai vake raiva Jekoria weJerusarema.
\v	4	Zvinowakaita zvakarurama pameso paJehovha, zvichienderana nezvose Amaziya baba vake zvavakaita.
\v	5	Zvinowaitsvaka Mwari mumazuva aZekariya, wainzwisisa pazviratidzo zvaMwari; zvino pamazuva ekutsvaka kwake Jehovha, Mwari wakamubudiririsa.
\v	6	Zvinowakabuda ndokurwa nevaFirisitia, ndokuputsa rusvingo rweGati, nerusvingo rweJabhine, nerusvingo rweAshidhodhi; ndokuvaka maguta paAshidhodhi nepakati pevaFirisitia.
\v	7	ZvinoMwari wakamubatsira kurwisa vaFirisitia uye kurwisa VaArabhiya vaigara muGuri-Bhaari, nevaMeunimi.
\v	8	ZvinovaAmoni vakapa zvipo kuna Uziya; zita rake ndokufamba kusvika panopindwa napo muEgipita; nokuti wakazvisimbisa kwazvo.
\v	9	Uziyawakavakawo shongwe paJerusarema pasuwo rekona, nepasuwo remupata, nepakudzoka, ndokudzisimbisa.
\v	10	Wakavakawo shongwe murenje, ndokuchera matsime mazhinji, nokuti waiva nemombe zhinji, zvose pamipata nepamapani, varimi, nevasimi vemazambiringa, pamakomo nepaKarimeri, nokuti waida ivhu.
\v	11	Uye Uziya waiva nehondo yevanorwa vaibudira hondo nemapoka, zvichienderana neuwandu hwekuverengwa kwavo neruoko rwaJeieri munyori, naMaaseya mutongi, pasi peruoko rwaHananiya wevakuru vamambo.
\v	12	Uwandu hwose hwemisoro yemadzibaba evane simba veumhare hwaiva zvuru zviviri nemazana matanhatu.
\v	13	Zvino pasi peruoko rwavo paiva nesimba rehondo, zvuru mazana matatu nezvinomwe nemazana mashanu, vaiita hondo nesimba guru, kupa rubatsiro kuna mambo kurwisa muvengi.
\v	14	Zvino Uziya wakavagadzirira, hondo yose, nhovo, nemapfumo, nenguwani, nenguvo dzeutare, neuta, nemabwe ezvifuramabwe.
\v	15	Zvino wakaita muJerusarema michina, chivambwa chevakangwara, kuva pamusoro peshongwe nepamusoro pemakona, kufura miseve nemabwe makuru. Zvino zita rake rakabuda kusvika kure nokuti waibatsirwa zvinoshamisa kusvika asimba.
\p
\v	16	Asi paaiva asimba, moyo wake wakazvitutumadzira kuparadzwa, nokuti wakatadzira Jehovha Mwari wake; ndokupinda mutembere yaJehovha kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pamusoro pearitari yezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	17	Zvino Azariya mupristi wakapinda shure kwake uye ane vapristi vaJehovha makumi masere, varume veumhare.
\v	18	Zvino vakamirisana naUziya mambo, ndokuti kwaari: Hazvisi zvako Uziya kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira kuna Jehovha, asi zvevapristi vanakomana vaAroni, vakatsaurwa kupisa zvinonhuhwira; buda panzvimbo tsvene, nokuti wadarika; uye hazvisi zverukudzo rwako kubva kuna Jehovha Mwari.
\v	19	Zvino Uziya wakatsamwa; zvino maiva nembiya muruoko rwake kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira; zvino paaiva achakatsamwira vapristi, maperembudzi akasimuka pahuma yake pamberi pevapristi muimba yaJehovha, kubva paaritari yezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	20	Zvino Azariya mupristi mukuru nevapristi vose vakatarisa kwaari, zvino tarira, wakava nemaperembudzi pahuma yake, uye vakachimbidzika kumubudisapo; kunyange naiye akachimbidzika kubuda, nokuti Jehovha waiva amurova.
\v	21	Zvino Uziya mambo waiva nemaperembudzi kusvika pazuva rekufa kwake, ndokugara muimba yakasununguka ane maperembudzi, nokuti wakagurwa kubva muimba yaJehovha; Jotamu mwanakomana wake ndokuva pamusoro peimba yamambo, achitonga vanhu venyika.
\p
\v	22	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaUziya, dzekutanga nedzekupedzisira, Isaya muporofita mwanakomana waAmozi wakadzinyora.
\v	23	Uziya ndokurara nemadzibaba ake; ivo ndokumuviga kumadzibaba ake mumunda weguva waiva wemadzimambo; nokuti vakati: Une maperembudzi. Jotamu mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	27
\s	Jotamu mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Jotamuwaiva nemakore makumi maviri nemashanu paakava mambo; ndokutonga makore gumi nematanhatu paJerusarema. Uye zita ramai vake raiva Jerusha mukunda waZadhoki.
\v	2	Zvinowakaita zvakarurama pameso paJehovha, zvichienderana nezvose baba vake Uziya zvavakaita; koga haana kupinda mutembere yaJehovha. Uye vanhu vairamba vachiita zvakaora.
\v	3	Iyewakavaka suwo rekumusoro reimba yaJehovha, nepachidziro cheshongwe wakavaka zvizhinji.
\v	4	Wakavakawomaguta pamakomo aJudha, uye mumasango wakavaka dzinhare nedzishongwe.
\p
\v	5	Iyewakarwawo namambo wevana vaAmoni ndokuvakunda. Vana vaAmoni ndokumupa mugore iroro matarenda esirivha zana, nemakori egorosi zvuru gumi, neebhari zvuru gumi. Vana vaAmoni vakadzosa izvi kwaari mugorewo rechipiri nerechitatu.
\v	6	NaizvozvoJotamu wakazvisimbisa, nokuti wakagadzira nzira dzake pamberi paJehovha Mwari wake.
\p
\v	7	Zvinodzimwe dzenyaya dzaJotamu, nehondo dzake dzose, nenzira dzake, tarira, dzakanyorwa mubhuku remadzimambo aIsraeri naJudha.
\v	8	Waivanemakore makumi maviri nemashanu paakava mambo; ndokutonga makore gumi nematanhatu paJerusarema.
\p
\v	9	ZvinoJotamu wakarara nemadzibaba ake; ivo ndokumuviga muguta raDhavhidhi. Ahazi mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	28
\s	Ahazi mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Ahaziwaiva nemakore makumi maviri paakava mambo, ndokutonga makore gumi nematanhatu paJerusarema; asi haana kuita zvakarurama pameso paJehovha, saDhavhidhi baba vake;
\v	2	nokutiwakafamba munzira dzemadzimambo aIsraeri, ndokuitirawo vana Bhaari zvifananidzo zvakaumbwa nekunyungudusa.
\v	3	Uyeiye wakapisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pamupata wemwanakomana waHinomi, ndokupisa vana vake mumoto, sezvinyangadzo zvendudzi Jehovha dzaaiva adzinga panhaka pamberi pevana vaIsraeri.
\v	4	Wakabairawondokupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira panzvimbo dzakakwirira, nepazvikomo, nepasi pemuti umwe neumwe munyoro.
\p
\v	5	SakaJehovha Mwari wake wakamupa muruoko rwamambo weSiriya; iye ndokumurova, ndokutapa kwaari vakatapwa vazhinji, ndoku vaisa kuDhamasiko. Uyewo wakapiwa muruoko rwamambo waIsraeri, iye ndokumurova nekurova kukuru.
\p
\v	6	NokutiPeka mwanakomana waRemariya wakauraya paJudha zvuru zana nemakumi maviri pazuva rimwe; vose vari varume veumhare; nokuti vaiva vasiya Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo.
\v	7	ZvinoZikiri murume waiva nesimba waEfuremu wakauraya Maaseya mwanakomana wamambo, naAzirikamu mutariri weimba, naErikana wepiri kuna mambo.
\v	8	Zvinovana vaIsraeri vakatapa pahama dzavo zvuru mazana maviri, vakadzi, vanakomana, nevanasikana, uye vakapambawo zvakapambiwa zvizhinji kwavari; ndokupinza zvakapambiwa muSamariya.
\v	9	Asiipapo paiva nemuporofita waJehovha wainzi Odhedhi. Zvino wakabuda pamberi pehondo yakauya kuSamariya, ndokuti kwavari: Tarirai, kubudikidza nehasha dzaJehovha Mwari wemadzibaba enyu pamusoro paJudha, wakavapa muruoko rwenyu; uye imwi makavauraya nehasha dzinonanavira kumatenga.
\v	10	Zvino ikozvino imwi munoti munoisa pasi penyu vana vaJudha neveJerusarema, vave nhapwa nenhapwakadzi; asi imwi, hakuna mhosva here kwamuri dzinopokana naJehovha Mwari wenyu?
\v	11	Naizvozvo zvino ndinzwei, mudzosere vatapwa vamakatapa pahama dzenyu; nokuti kupfuta kwehasha dzaJehovha kuri pamusoro penyu.
\v	12	Zvino vamwe vemisoro yevana vaEfuremu: Azariya mwanakomana waJehohanani, Bherekia mwanakomana waMeshiremoti, naJehizikia mwanakomana waSharumi, naAmasa mwanakomana waHadhirai, vakamirisana neavo vaiuya kubva kuhondo,
\v	13	ndokuti kwavari: Hamungapinzi vatapwa pano, nokuti, kuti mhosva inobva kuna Jehovha ive pamusoro pedu, munoti tiwedzere pazvivi zvedu nepamhosva yedu; nokuti mhosva dzedu ihuru, uye kupfuta kwehasha kuri pamusoro paIsraeri.
\v	14	Naizvozvo vakashonga zvombo vakasiya vatapwa nezvakapambiwa pamberi pemachinda neungano yose.
\v	15	Zvino varume varehwa nemazita vakasimuka ndokubata vatapwa, ndokupfekedza vose vaiva vakashama vavo nezvakabva pane zvakapambiwa, hongu vakavapfekedza, ndokuvaisa manyatera, ndokuvadyisa nekuvanwisa, ndokuvazodza, ndokutakura vose vaiva vasina simba pamusoro pembongoro, ndokuvaunza paJeriko guta remichindwe pedo nehama dzavo; ivo ndokudzokera kuSamariya.
\p
\v	16	Panguva iyoyo mambo Ahazi wakatuma kumadzimambo eAsiriya kuti amubatsire.
\v	17	Nokuti vaEdhomu vaiva vauyazve, ndokurova Judha ndokutapa vatapwa.
\v	18	VaFirisitiawo vaiva vavamba maguta emupata, nechamhembe kwaJudha, uye vatapa Bheti-Shemeshi, neAjaroni, neGedheroti, neSoko nemisha yawo, neTimina nemisha yaro, neGimizo nemisha yaro; ndokugaramo.
\v	19	Nokuti Jehovha wakaninipisa Judha nekuda kwaAhazi mambo waIsraeri; nokuti waiva aitisa Judha zvisina maturo, ndokutadza zvikuru kuna Jehovha.
\v	20	Zvino Tirigati-Pirineseri mambo weAsiriya wakauya kwaari, ndokumutambudza, asi haana kumusimbisa.
\v	21	Nokuti Ahazi wakatora mugove weimba yaJehovha neweimba yamambo newemachinda, ndokupa kuna mambo weAsiriya; zvakadaro haana kumubatsira.
\p
\v	22	Zvino panguva yekutambudzika kwake wakawedzera kutadzira Jehovha; uyu mambo Ahazi.

\p
\v	20	Hezekia ndokuita saizvozvo kuna Judha wose, ndokuita zvakanaka nezvakarurama nechokwadi pamberi paJehovha Mwari wake.
\v	21	Nepabasa rimwe nerimwe raaiva atanga pashumiro yeimba yaMwari, nepamutemo nepamurairo, kutsvaka Mwari wake, wakaita nemoyo wake wose, ndokubudirira.
\c	32
\s	Seni-Akeribhi mambo weAsiriya anokomba Jerusarema, asi vaJudha vanorwirwa naJehovha
\p
\v	1	Shurekwezvinhu izvi nekutendeka uku, Seni-Akeribhi mambo weAsiriya wakasvika ndokupinda kwaJudha, ndokudzika musasa kurwisa maguta akashoshewa, ndokufunga kuzvipazira iwo.
\v	2	Hezekiapaakaona kuti Seni-Akeribhi wasvika, uye chiso chake chaiva pahondo kurwisa Jerusarema,
\v	3	wakarangananemachinda ake nevane simba vake kudzivira mvura yezvitubu zvaiva kunze kweguta; ivo ndokumubatsira.
\v	4	Naizvozvokwakaungana vanhu vazhinji, avo vakadzivira zvitubu zvose nerukova irwo rwaiyerera nepakati penyika, vachiti: Madzimambo eAsiriya achasvikirei akawana mvura zhinji?
\v	5	Zvinowakazvisimbisa, ndokuvaka rusvingo rwose rwaiva rwaputsika, ndokurwukwidza kusvika pashongwe, nerwumwe rusvingo kunze, ndokusimbisa Miro  paguta raDhavhidhi, ndokuita zvombo nenhovo zhinji.
\v	6	Zvinowakaisira vanhu vatungamiriri vehondo, ndokuvaunganidza kwaari panhandare yesuwo reguta, ndokutaura kumoyo yavo achiti:
\v	7	Simbaimutsunge, musatya kana kuodzwa moyo pachiso chamambo weAsiriya, kana pachiso chechaunga chose chaanacho, nokuti anesu anopfuura waanaye.
\v	8	Anayechanza chenyama, asi anesu ndiJehovha Mwari wedu kutibatsira nekurwa hondo dzedu. Vanhu ndokuzembera pamusoro pemashoko aHezekia mambo waJudha.
\p
\v	9	Shurekweizvi Seni-Akeribhi mambo weAsiriya wakatuma varanda vake kuJerusarema (asi iye waiva pamberi peRakishi nesimba rake rose rinaye) kuna Hezekia mambo waJudha nekuna Judha wose waiva paJerusarema achiti:
\v	10	Ndizvo zvinoreva Seni-Akeribhi mambo weAsiriya: Unovimba nei kuti ugare mukukombwa muJerusarema?
\v	11	Hezekia haakukwezvii kuti muzvipire kufa nenzara uye nenyota here achiti: Jehovha Mwari wedu achatinunura kubva paruoko rwamambo weAsiriya?
\v	12	Haasi iye Hezekia wakabvisa nzvimbo dzake dzakakwirira nearitari dzake, ndokuraira Judha neJerusarema achiti: Muchashumira pamberi pearitari imwe, mugopisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pairi here?
\v	13	Hamuzivi here ini nemadzibaba angu zvatakaitira vanhu vose venyika ino? Vamwari vendudzi dzenyika idzo vaigona kugona kununura nyika dzavo kubva paruoko rwangu here?
\v	14	Ndiani pakati pavamwari vose vendudzi idzo madzibaba angu dzavakaparadza wakagona kununura vanhu vake kubva paruoko rwangu, kuti Mwari wenyu agone kukununurai kubva paruoko rwangu?
\v	15	Zvino naizvozvo Hezekia ngaarege kukunyengerai, kana kukukwezvai nemutoo uyu; uye musamutenda, nokuti hakuna mwari werudzi rwupi nerwupi kana ushe wakagona kununura vanhu vake kubva paruoko rwangu nekubva paruoko rwemadzibaba angu. Naiye zvirokwazvo Mwari wenyu haangakununurii kubva paruoko rwangu here?
\v	16	Zvino varanda vake vakataurazve kupikisa Jehovha Mwari, uye kupikisa muranda wake Hezekia.
\v	17	Wakanyorawo tsamba dzekutuka Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri, nekutaura dzichimupikisa dzichiti: Sevamwari vendudzi dzenyika idzo vasina kununura vanhu vavo kubva paruoko rwangu, saizvozvo Mwari waHezekia haanganunuri vanhu vake kubva paruoko rwangu.
\v	18	Zvino vakadanidzira nenzwi guru nechiJudha kuvanhu veJerusarema vaiva pamusoro perusvingo, kuvatyisa, nekuvanetsa, kuti vatape guta.
\v	19	Zvino vakataura zvaMwari weJerusarema sezvana vamwari vevanhu venyika, basa remaoko emunhu.
\v	20	Asi Hezekia mambo nemuporofita Isaya mwanakomana waAmozi vakanyengetera nekuda kweichi, ndokuchemera kumatenga.
\p
\v	21	Zvino Jehovha wakatuma mutumwa uyo wakadzima vane simba veumhare vose nevatungamiriri nemisoro pamusasa wamambo weAsiriya. Naizvozvo wakadzoka panyika yake ane chiso chine nyadzi. Zvino apinde muimba yamwari wake, uye avo vakabuda muura hwake vakamuita kuti awe ipapo nemunondo.
\p
\v	22	Saizvozvo Jehovha wakaponesa Hezekia nevagari veJerusarema kubva paruoko rwaSeni-Akeribhi mambo weAsiriya uye kubva paruoko rwevose, ndokuvatungamirira kumativi ose.
\v	23	Zvino vazhinji vakauya nezvipo kuna Jehovha paJerusarema, nezvinhu zvakakosha kuna Hezekia mambo waJudha; iye ndokusimudzirwa pamberi pemeso emarudzi ose shure kweizvi.
\s	Kurwara kwaHezekia nekufa kwake
\p
\v	24	Pamazuva iwawo Hezekia wakarwarira kufa; iye ndokunyengetera kuna Jehovha, iye ndokutaura naye, ndokumupa chiratidzo.
\v	25	Asi Hezekia haana kudzorera sezvinoenderana nemibairo yaaiva aitirwa, nokuti moyo wake waiva pamusoro. Naizvozvo pakava nehasha pamusoro pake, nepamusoro paJudha neJerusarema.
\v	26	Asi Hezekia waka zozvininipisa pamusoro pekuzvikudza kwemoyo wake, iye nevagari veJerusarema, zvekuti hasha dzaJehovha hadzina kuuya pamusoro pavo pamazuva aHezekia.
\p
\v	27	Hezekia waiva nefuma nerukudzo zvizhinji; zvino wakazviitira matura esirivha negoridhe neezvibwe zvinokosha neezvinonhuhwira neenhovo neezvinhu zvose zvinoshuvika,
\v	28	nematura ezvibereko zvezviyo newaini itsva nemafuta, nematanga ezvipfuwo zvemarudzi ose, nezvidyiro zvemapoka.
\v	29	Uye wakazviitira maguta, nefuma yemakwai nemombe zhinji; nokuti Mwari waiva amupa fuma zhinji kwazvo.
\v	30	Uye ndiye Hezekia wakadzivira chitubu chemvura chekumusoro kweGihoni, ndokuinangisa ichiburuka kumavirira kweguta raDhavhidhi. Hezekia ndokubudirira pamabasa ake ose.
\p
\v	31	Asi zvaiva zvakadaro maererano nevatumwa vemisoro yeBhabhironi, avo vakatuma kwaari kubvunza zvechishamiso chakaitika panyika, Mwari ndokumusiya kuti amuedze kuti azive zvose zviri mumoyo make.
\p
\v	32	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaHezekia, nekunaka kwake, tarira, zvakanyorwa muchiratidzo chaIsaya muporofita mwanakomana waAmozi, mubhuku remadzimambo aJudha naIsraeri.
\v	33	Hezekia ndokurara nemadzibaba ake, ivo ndokumuviga panhongonya pemakuva evanakomana vaDhavhidhi; Judha wose nevagari veJerusarema vakamuitira rukudzo pakufa kwake. Manase mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	33
\s	Manase mambo waJudha
\p
\v	1	Manasewaiva nemakore gumi nemaviri paakava mambo, uye wakatonga makore makumi mashanu nemashanu paJerusarema.
\v	2	Asiwakaita zvakaipa pameso paJehovha, sezvinyangadzo zvevahedheni Jehovha vaaiva adzinga panhaka pamberi pevana vaIsraeri.
\v	3	Nokutiwakavakazve nzvimbo dzakakwirira baba vake Hezekia dzavaiva vaputsa, uye wakamisira vana Bhaari dziaritari, ndokuita matanda ekunamata nawo, ndokukotamira hondo yose yekumatenga, ndokuishumira.
\v	4	Zvinowakavaka dziaritari muimba yaJehovha, Jehovha yaaiva ati: PaJerusarema zita rangu richavapo rinhi narinhi.
\v	5	Akavakirawohondo yose yekumatenga dziaritari muzvivanze zviviri zveimba yaJehovha.
\v	6	Iyewakapfuudzawo vana vake nemumoto mumupata wemwanakomana waHinomi, ndokududzira mashura, nekuita mazango, akaita uroyi, uye wakashanda nemasvikiro nevauki; wakaita zvakaipa zvizhinji pameso paJehovha, kumutsamwisa.
\v	7	Zvinowakaisa muimba yaMwari mufananidzo wakavezwa wechifananidzo chaaiva aita, imo Mwari maakati kuna Dhavhidhi nekuna Soromoni mwanakomana wake: Muimba ino uye muJerusarema randakasarudza kumarudzi ose aIsraeri, ndinoisa zita rangu kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	8	Uyehandichabvisizve tsoka yaIsraeri ibude panyika yandakamisira madzibaba enyu; koga kana vachenjerere kuita zvose zvandakavaraira, zvichienderana nemurairo wose nezvimiso nemitemo neruoko rwaMozisi.
\v	9	NaizvozvoManase wakatsausa Judha nevagari veJerusarema, kuita zvakaipa kupfuura vemarudzi Jehovha vaakaparadza pamberi pevana vaIsraeri.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Jehovha wakataura kuna Manase nekuvanhu vake; asi havana kuteerera.
\v	11	Naizvozvo Jehovha wakauisa pamusoro pavo vakuru vehondo ivo vaiva vamambo weAsiriya, ivo vakabata Manase paminzwa, ndokumusunga nemaketani, vakamutakurira kuBhabhironi.
\v	12	Zvino paaiva pakumanikidzwa, wakanyengeterera chiso chaJehovha Mwari wake, ndokuzvininipisa zvikuru pamberi paMwari wemadzibaba ake.
\v	13	Ndokunyengetera kwaari; iye ndokunyengetedzeka naye, ndokunzwa chikumbiro chake, ndokumudzosa kuJerusarema paushe hwake. Naizvozvo Manase wakaziva kuti Jehovha ndiMwari.
\p
\v	14	Zvino shure kweizvi wakavaka rusvingo kunze kweguta raDhavhidhi, kumavirazuva kweGihoni, mumupata, kusvikawo panopinda pasuwo rehove; ndokukomberedza Oferi, ndokurwukwiridzira kwazvo; ndokuisa vatungamiriri vehondo mumaguta ose akashoshewa kwaJudha.
\v	15	Wakabvisawo vamwari vemarudzi nechifananidzo kubva muimba yaJehovha, nearitari dzose dzaaiva avaka mugomo reimba yaJehovha nemuJerusarema, ndokurasira kunze kweguta.
\v	16	Zvino wakavaka aritari yaJehovha, ndokubairapo zvibairo zvekuyananisa nezvipo zvekuvonga, ndokuraira Judha kushumira Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri.
\v	17	Kunyange zvakadaro vanhu vairamba vachibaira panzvimbo dzakakwirira, koga kuna Jehovha Mwari wavo.
\p
\v	18	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaManase, nemunyengetero wake kuna Mwari wake, nemashoko evaoni vakataura kwaari muzita raJehovha Mwari waIsraeri, tarirai, ari munyaya dzemadzimambo aIsraeri.
\v	19	Munyengetero wakewo, nekunyengetedza kwake, nechivi chake chose, nekudarika kwake, nemunzvimbo maakavaka nzvimbo dzakakwirira, nekumisa matanda ekunamata nawo, nemifananidzo, asati azvininipisa, tarirai zvakanyorwa pamashoko evaoni.
\p
\v	20	Zvino Manase wakarara nemadzibaba ake, ivo ndokumuviga muimba yake. Amoni mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\s	Amoni mambo waJudha
\p
\v	21	Amoni waiva nemakore makumi maviri nemaviri paakava mambo, ndokutonga makore maviri paJerusarema.
\v	22	Zvino wakaita zvakaipa pameso paJehovha, sezvakaita Manase baba vake; nokuti Amoni wakabaira mifananidzo yose yakavezwa Manase baba vake yavaiva vaita, ndokuishumira.
\v	23	Zvino haana kuzvininipisa pamberi paJehovha, Manase baba vake sezvavakazvininipisa; asi Amoni uyu wakawedzera kudarika.
\v	24	Zvino varanda vake vakarangana ndokumuuraya muimba yake.
\p
\v	25	Asi vanhu venyika vakarova avo vose vaiva varangana mambo Amoni; vanhu venyika ndokuita Josiya mwanakomana wake mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	34
\s	Josiya mambo waJudha anogadzira tembere nekudzosa vanhu kuna Jehovha
\p
\v	1	Josiyawaiva nemakore masere paakava mambo; ndokutonga paJerusarema makore makumi matatu nerimwe.
\v	2	Zvinowakaita zvakarurama pameso paJehovha, ndokufamba nenzira dzaDhavhidhi baba vake; uye haana kutsaukira kurudyi kana kuruboshwe.
\p
\v	3	Nokutipagore rerusere rekutonga kwake achiri mudiki, wakatanga kutsvaka Mwari waDhavhidhi baba vake; uye pagore regumi nemaviri wakatanga kunatsa Judha neJerusarema achibvisa nzvimbo dzakakwirira nematanda ekunamata nawo nemifananidzo yakavezwa nemifananidzo yakaumbwa nekunyungudusa.
\v	4	Vakaputsaaritari dzavana Bhaari pamberi pake; nearitari dzezuva dzaiva padziri pakakwirira wakadzitemera pasi; nematanda ekunamata nawo nemifananidzo yakavezwa nemifananidzo yakaumbwa nekunyungudusa wakaputsa-putsa ndokuita guruva ndokuzviparadzira pamusoro pemakuva eavo vaibaira kwazviri.
\v	5	Zvinowakapisa mapfupa evapristi pamusoro pearitari dzavo, ndokunatsa Judha neJerusarema.
\v	6	NemumagutaaManase naEfuremu naSimioni, kusvikawo paNafutari, mumatongo awo mativi ose,
\v	7	wakaputsadziaritari nematanda ekunamata nawo, nekukuya zvifananidzo zvakavezwa kusvika zvava upfu, akatemera pasi aritari dzose dzezuva panyika yose yaIsraeri, ndokudzokera kuJerusarema.
\p
\v	8	Zvinomugore regumi nesere rekutonga kwake, anatsa nyika neimba, wakatuma Shafani mwanakomana waAzariya, naMaaseya mubati weguta, naJoa mwanakomana waJoahazi munyori wenhoroondo, kugadzira imba yaJehovha Mwari wake.
\v	9	Pavakasvikakuna Hirikiya mupristi mukuru, vakapa mari yaiva yaiswa muimba yaMwari, iyo vaRevhi vaichengeta mikova yavaiva vaunganidza muruoko rwaManase naEfuremu nekuvakasara vose vaIsraeri nekuna Judha wose naBhenjamini, nevagari veJerusarema.
\v	10	Zvino vakapa muruoko rwevaiti vebasa vaiva vamisirwa imba yaJehovha; ivo ndokuipa kuvaiti vebasa vaishanda muimba yaJehovha kuti vagadzire uye vasimbise imba.
\v	11	Vakaipawo kuvavezi nevavaki kuti vatenge mabwe akavezwa nematanda ekubatanidza kuwadza mapuranga edzimba madzimambo aJudha dzaaiva aparadza.
\v	12	Zvino varume vakashanda nekuvimbika pabasa; uye vatariri pamusoro pavo vaiva Jahati naObhadhiya, vaRevhi vevanakomana vaMerari, naZekariya naMeshurami, vevanakomana vevaKohati, kuita sevarairidzi; nevaRevhi, vose vainzwisisa pamidziyo yerwiyo.
\v	13	Uye vaiva pamusoro pevatakuri vemitoro, nevatariri veupi neupi waiita basa reshumiro neshumiro; uye kuvaRevhi kwaiva nevanyori nevabati nevachengeti vemikova.
\p
\v	14	Zvino pakubudisa kwavo mari yakauiswa muimba yaJehovha, Hirikiya mupristi wakawana bhuku remurairo waJehovha,  wakapiwa neruoko rwaMozisi.
\v	15	Hirikiya ndokupindura ndokuti kuna Shafani munyori: Ndawana bhuku remurairo muimba yaJehovha. Hirikiya ndokupa bhuku kuna Shafani.
\v	16	Zvino Shafani wakauya nebhuku kuna mambo, ndokudzosawo shoko kuna mambo, achiti: Zvose zvapiwa muruoko rwevaranda venyu vari kuita.
\v	17	Uye vadurura mari yakawanikwa muimba yaJehovha, ndokuipa muruoko rweavo vakagadzwa nemuruoko rwevaiti vebasa.
\v	18	Zvino Shafani munyori wakaudza mambo achiti: Hirikiya mupristi wandipa bhuku. Shafani ndokuverenga mariri pamberi pamambo.
\v	19	Zvino zvakaitika pakunzwa kwamambo mashoko emurairo kuti wakabvarura nguvo dzake.
\v	20	Mambo ndokuraira Hirikiya, naAhikami mwanakomana waShafani, naAbhidhoni mwanakomana waMika, naShafani munyori, naAsaya muranda wamambo, achiti:
\v	21	Endai munobvunza pachinzvimbo changu kuna Jehovha uye pachinzvimbo chevakasara kwaIsraeri nekwaJudha maererano nemashoko ebhuku rawanikwa; nokuti ihuru hasha dzaMwari dzinodururwa pamusoro pedu, nekuda kwekuti madzibaba edu haana kuchengeta shoko raJehovha kuita zvinoenderana nezvose zvakanyorwa mubhuku iri.
\p
\v	22	Zvino Hirikiya nevaiva vamambo vakaenda kuna Huridha muporofitakadzi, mukadzi waSharumi mwanakomana waTokihati, mwanakomana waHazira, muchengeti wenguvo. Zvino iye waigara paJerusarema mumupanda wepiri. Zvino vakataura kwaari seizvi.
\v	23	Iye ndokuti kwavari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri: Udzai munhu wakakutumai kwandiri:
\v	24	Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Tarirai, ndichauisa chakaipa pamusoro penzvimbo iyi, nepamusoro pevagari vepo, kutuka kose kwakanyorwa mubhuku iri ravakaverenga pamberi pamambo waJudha.
\v	25	Nekuda kwekuti vakandisiya, ndokupisira zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira kune vamwe vamwari, kuti vanditsamwise nemabasa ose emaoko avo, naizvozvo hasha dzangu dzichadururwa pamusoro penzvimbo ino, uye hadzingadzimwi.
\v	26	Asi kuna mambo waJudha wakakutumai kuzobvunza Jehovha, muchareva seizvi kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri:  Maererano nemashoko awakanzwa,
\v	27	nokuti moyo wako munyoro, uye wazvininipisa pamberi paMwari pawanzwa mashoko ake anorwisa nzvimbo ino uye anorwisa vagari vayo, uye wazvininipisa pamberi pangu, ndokubvarura nguvo dzako, ndokuchema misodzi pamberi pangu, neniwo ndanzwa, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	28	Tarira, ndichakuunganidza kumadzibaba ako, uye uchaunganidzwa kuguva rako nerugare, uye meso ako haangaoni zvakaipa zvose zvandichauisa panzvimbo ino, nepamusoro pevagari vayo. Naizvozvo vakadzosa shoko kuna mambo.
\p
\v	29	Mambo ndokutuma  vanhu ndokuunganidza vakuru vose vaJudha neJerusarema.
\v	30	Zvino mambo wakakwira muimba yaJehovha, nevarume vose vaJudha, nevagari veJerusarema, nevapristi, nevaRevhi, nevanhu vose, kubva kumukuru kusvikawo kumudiki; iye ndokuverenga munzeve dzavo mashoko ose ebhuku resungano raiva rawanikwa muimba yaJehovha.
\v	31	Zvino mambo wakamira panzvimbo yake, ndokuita sungano pamberi paJehovha, kufamba achitevera Jehovha, nekuchengeta mirairo yake, nezvipupuriro zvake, nezvimiso zvake, nemoyo wake wose uye nemweya weupenyu wake wose, kuita mashoko esungano akanyorwa mubhuku iri.
\v	32	Zvino wakamisa vose vaivapo muJerusarema naBhenjamini. Vagari veJerusarema ndokuita zvichienderana nesungano yaMwari, Mwari wemadzibaba avo.
\v	33	Zvino Josiya wakabvisa zvinyangadzo zvose kubva munyika dzose dzaiva dzevana vaIsraeri, ndokuita kuti wose wakawanikwa kwaIsraeri ashumire, ashumire Jehovha Mwari wavo. Mazuva ake ose havana kutsauka pakutevera Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo.
\c	35
\s	Josiya anoitira Jehovha pasika
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJosiya wakaitira Jehovha pasika paJerusarema; zvino vakauraya pasika ne zuva regumi nemana remwedzi wekutanga.
\v	2	Zvinowakagadza vapristi pafaniro dzavo, ndokuvakurudzira pashumiro yeimba yaJehovha.
\v	3	NdokutikuvaRevhi vaidzidzisa Israeri wose, vaiva vatsvene kuna Jehovha: Isai areka tsvene muimba Soromoni mwanakomana waDhavhidhi mambo waIsraeri yaakavaka; hausisiri mutoro pamafudzi enyu. Ikozvino shumirai Jehovha Mwari wenyu, nevanhu vake Israeri.
\v	4	Zvino zvigadzirei, nedzimba dzemadzibaba enyu, zvichienderana nemapoka enyu, zvichienderana nechinyorwa chaDhavhidhi mambo waIsraeri, uye zvichienderana nechinyorwa chaSoromoni mwanakomana wake.
\v	5	Zvinomirai panzvimbo tsvene zvichienderana nezvikamu zveimba yemadzibaba ehama dzenyu, vanakomana vevanhu, nechikamu cheimba yababa vevaRevhi.
\v	6	Zvinourayai pasika, uye muzviite vatsvene, mugogadzirira hama dzenyu; kuti muite zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha neruoko rwaMozisi.
\p
\v	7	ZvinoJosiya wakapa kuvana vevanhu boka remakwayana nembudzana, zvose zviri zvezvipo zvepasika, zvevose vaivapo kuuwandu hwezvuru makumi matatu, uye nzombe zvuru zvitatu; izvi zvakabva pafuma yamambo.
\v	8	Machindaake akapawo nechido kuvanhu, kuvapristi, nekuvaRevhi. Hirikiya, naZekariya, naJehieri, vatungamiriri veimba yaMwari vakapa vapristi zviri zvezvipo zvepasika,  mbudzana zvuru zviviri nemazana matanhatu, nenzombe mazana matatu.
\v	9	NaKonaniya,naShemaya, naNetaneri, hama dzake, naHashabhia, naJeyieri, naJozabhadhi, vakuru vevaRevhi, vakapa kuvaRevhi zviri zvezvipo zvepasika,  mbudzana zvuru zvishanu, nenzombe mazana mashanu.
\p
\v	10	Naizvozvo shumiro yakagadzirwa, vapristi ndokumira panzvimbo dzavo, nevaRevhi pamapoka avo, zvichienderana nemurairo wamambo.

\v	28	Kana pane nzara panyika, kana pane denda, kana kune kupisa, nenyunje, mhashu, nemakonye; kana vavengi vavo vakavakomba panyika nesuwo ravo; nyatwa ipi neipi kana hosha ipi neipi.
\v	29	Munyengetero upi neupi, mukumbiro upi neupi uchaitwa nemunhu upi neupi, kana nevanhu venyu vose Israeri, apo vaziva uyo neuyo dambudziko rake nemarwadzo ake, zvino akatandavadzira maoko ake kuimba ino;
\v	30	zvino imwi inzwai muri kumatenga, nzvimbo yenyu yekugara, mugokanganwira, uye mupe umwe neumwe zvichienderana nenzira dzake dzose, sezvo muchiziva moyo wake, nokuti ndimwi moga munoziva moyo wevana vevanhu;
\v	31	kuti vakutyei, kufamba munzira dzenyu mazuva ose avanorarama pachiso chenyika yamakapa madzibaba edu.
\p
\v	32	Zvinowo maererano nemutorwa, asiri wevanhu venyu Israeri, asi wakabva kunyika iri kure nekuda kwezita renyu guru, nechanza chenyu chine simba, neruoko rwenyu rwakatandavadzwa; kana vakauya vakanyengetera vakatarira kuimba ino;
\v	33	zvino imwi inzwai muri kumatenga, muri kunzvimbo yenyu yekugara, mugoita zvinoenderana nezvose mutorwa zvaanodana kwamuri, kuti vanhu vose venyika vazive zita renyu, vagokutyai, sevanhu venyu Israeri, uye kuziva kuti zita renyu rinodanwa pamusoro peimba ino yandakavaka.
\p
\v	34	Kana vanhu venyu vakabudira kuhondo kurwisa vavengi vavo nenzira yamuchavatuma nayo, zvino vakanyengetera kwamuri vakatarisa kuguta iri ramakasarudza, nekuimba yandakavakira zita renyu;
\v	35	zvino inzwai muri kumatenga munyengetero wavo nemukumbiro wavo, mugoita mutongo wavo.
\p
\v	36	Kana vakakutadzirai; nokuti hakuna munhu asingatadzi; zvino mukavatsamwira, mukavapa pamberi pemuvengi, ivo ndokuvatapa kuvatapira kunyika iri kure kana pedo.
\v	37	Kana vakazozvifunga panyika kwavakatapirwa, zvino vakadzoka vakanyengetera kwamuri panyika yeutapwa hwavo, vachiti: Takatadza, takaita zvisakarurama, uye taita zvakaipa;
\v	38	kana vakadzokera kwamuri nemoyo wavo wose uye nemweya weupenyu wavo wose panyika yeutapwa hwavo kwavakavatapira, ndokunyengetera vakatarisa kunyika yavo, yamakapa kumadzibaba avo, nekuguta ramakasarudza, uye nekuimba yandakavakira zita renyu;
\v	39	zvino inzwai muri kumatenga, muri panzvimbo yenyu yekugara, munyengetero wavo nemikumbiro yavo, uye muite mutongo wavo mugokanganwira vanhu venyu vakakutadzirai.
\p
\v	40	Zvino Mwari wangu, meso enyu ngaasvinure hawo, nenzeve dzenyu dziteerere kumunyengetero wenzvimbo ino.
\v	41	Zvino ikozvino simukai, Jehovha Mwari, mupinde munzvimbo yenyu yekuzorora, imwi neareka yesimba renyu; vapristi venyu Jehovha Mwari ngavafukidzwe neruponeso, nevatsvene venyu ngavafare nezvakanaka.
\v	42	Jehovha Mwari, musafuratidza chiso chemuzodzwa wenyu; rangarirai kunaka kwaDhavhidhi muranda wenyu.
\c	7
\s	Jehovha anopinda mutembere
\p
\v	1	ZvinoSoromoni paakapedza kunyengetera, moto ndokuburuka kubva kumatenga ndokupedza chipo chinopiswa nezvibairo; kubwinya kwaJehovha ndokuzadza imba.
\v	2	Zvinovapristi vaiva vasingagoni kupinda muimba yaJehovha, nokuti kubwinya kwaJehovha kwaiva kwazadza imba yaJehovha.
\v	3	VanavaIsraeri vose pavakaona kuburuka kwemoto, uye kubwinya kwaJehovha pamusoro peimba, vakakotamira pasi nezviso pakarongerwa mabwe, ndokunamata nekurumbidza Jehovha  vachiti: Nokuti wakanaka. Nokuti kunaka kwake kuripo rinhi narinhi.
\p
\v	4	Mambonevanhu vose ndokupa zvibairo pamberi paJehovha.
\v	5	Zvinomambo Soromoni wakapa chibairo chenzombe zvuru makumi maviri nezviviri, nemakwai zvuru zana nemakumi maviri. Saizvozvo mambo nevanhu vose vakatsaura imba yaMwari.
\v	6	Zvinovapristi vakamira pazvinzvimbo zvavo, nevaRevhi vane midziyo yekuimbisa yaJehovha, Dhavhidhi mambo zvaaiva aita, kuvonga Jehovha  nazvo, nokuti kunaka kwake kuripo rinhi narinhi, Dhavhidhi paakarumbidza neshumiro yavo; uye vapristi vakaridza hwamanda pamberi pavo; Israeri wose ndokumira.
\v	7	Soromoniwakaitawo patsvene pakati pechivanze chaiva pamberi peimba yaJehovha; nokuti ndipo paakapa zvipo zvinopiswa, nemafuta ezvipo zvekuyananisa; nokuti aritari yendarira Soromoni yaakaita yaiva isingagoni kuringana chipo chinopiswa, nechipo chechidyiwa, nemafuta.
\p
\v	8	Uyepanguva iyoyo Soromoni wakachengetedza mabiko mazuva manomwe, naIsraeri wose anaye, ungano huru kwazvo, kubva pakupinda paHamati kusvika parukova rweEgipita.
\v	9	Zvinopazuva resere vakaita ungano yakadzama; nokuti vakaita kutsaurwa kwearitari mazuva manomwe, uye mabiko mazuva manomwe.
\v	10	Zvino pazuva remakumi maviri nematatu remwedzi wechinomwe wakaendesa vanhu kumatende avo, vachifara uye vachipemberera mumoyo nekuda kwekunaka Jehovha kwaaiva aita kuna Dhavhidhi nekuna Soromoni nekuna Israeri vanhu vake.
\s	Zvibairo zvaSoromoni, nemitambo
\p
\v	11	Saizvozvo Soromoni wakapedza imba yaJehovha, neimba yamambo. Nezvose zvakauya pamoyo paSoromoni kuita muimba yaJehovha nemuimba yake wakazvibudiririsa.
\p
\v	12	Zvino Jehovha wakaonekwa kuna Soromoni neusiku, ndokuti kwaari: Ndanzwa munyengetero wako, uye ndazvisarudzira nzvimbo ino kuva imba yechibairo.
\v	13	Kana ndikapfiga matenga kuti kusava nemvura, uye kana ndikaraira mhashu kupedza nyika, uye kana ndikatuma denda pakati pevanhu vangu,
\v	14	kana vanhu vangu avo zita rangu rinodanwa pamusoro pavo vakazvininipisa, uye vakanyengetera, nekutsvaka chiso changu, nekutendeuka kubva panzira dzavo dzakaipa, zvino ini ndichanzwa ndiri kumatenga uye ndichakanganwira chivi chavo, uye ndichaporesa nyika yavo.
\v	15	Zvino meso angu achazaruka, uye nzeve dzangu dzichateerera munyengetero wenzvimbo ino.
\v	16	Nokuti zvino ndasarudza nekuita tsvene imba ino, kuti zita rangu rivepo rinhi narinhi, hongu, meso angu nemoyo wangu zvichavapo mazuva ose.
\v	17	Zvino iwe, kana ukafamba pamberi pangu, Dhavhidhi baba vako sezvavakafamba, nekuita zvinoenderana nezvose zvandakakuraira, nekuchengeta zvimiso zvangu nemitongo yangu,
\v	18	ipapo ndichasimbisa chigaro cheushe cheumambo hwako, sekuita kwangu  sungano naDhavhidhi baba vako, ndichiti: Haungashaiwi munhu achabata ushe kwaIsraeri.
\v	19	Asi kana imwi mukatsauka, mukasiya zvimiso zvangu nemirairo yangu zvandakaisa pamberi penyu, uye mukaenda mukanoshumira vamwe vamwari ndokuvanamata;
\v	20	zvino ndichavadzura kubva panyika yangu yandakavapa; neimba iyi yandakatsvenesera zita rangu ndichairasa ibve pamberi pangu; uye ndichaiita tsumo nechibayo pakati pemarudzi ose.
\v	21	Neimba iyi yakakwirira ichava chishamiso kune uyo neuyo anopfuura pairi kusvika ati: Jehovha wakaitirei zvakadai kunyika iyi nekuimba iyi?
\v	22	Zvino vachati: Nokuti vakasiya Jehovha Mwari wemadzibaba avo, wakavabudisa panyika yeEgipita, vakanamatira vamwe vamwari, vakavanamata nekuvashumira; nekuda kweizvozvo wavawisira chakaipa chose ichi.
\c	8
\s	Zvimwe zvakavakwa naSoromoni
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pakupera kwemakore makumi maviri Soromoni aakavaka nawo imba yaJehovha neimba yake,
\v	2	kutimaguta Hurami aaiva apa Soromoni, Soromoni wakaavaka ndokugarisako vana vaIsraeri.
\v	3	Soromonindokuenda kuHamati-Zobha, ndokurikunda.
\v	4	Zvinowakavaka Tadhimori murenje, nemaguta ose ematura aaiva avaka paHamati.
\v	5	Zvinowakavaka Bheti-Horoni rekumusoro neBheti-Horoni rezasi, maguta akashoshewa nemasvingo, ane magonhi nemazariro;
\v	6	neBhaarati,nemaguta ose ematura Soromoni aaiva nawo, nemaguta ose engoro, nemaguta evatasvi vemabhiza, nechishuwo chose chaSoromoni chaakashuva nacho kuvaka paJerusarema nepaRebhanoni nepanyika yose yeushe hwake.
\p
\v	7	Vanhuvose vakasara pavaHiti, nevaAmori, nevaPerizi, nevaHivhi, nevaJebhusi, vaiva vasiri veIsraeri;
\v	8	pavanavavo vaiva vasara shure kwavo panyika, vana vaIsraeri vavasina kupedza, Soromoni wakavasimudza kuva chibharo kusvika nhasi.
\v	9	Asipavana vaIsraeri Soromoni haana kuvaita varanda vebasa rake; asi ivo vaiva varume vehondo nevakuru vevatungamiriri vehondo vake, nevarairi vengoro dzake, nevatasvi vemabhiza ake.
\v	10	Zvino ava vaiva misoro yemachinda amambo Soromoni, mazana maviri nemakumi mashanu, vaitonga vanhu.
\p
\v	11	Zvino Soromoni wakakwidza mukunda waFarao kubva muguta raDhavhidhi kumuisa kuimba yaaiva amuvakira; nokuti wakati: Mukadzi wangu haangagari muimba yaDhavhidhi mambo waIsraeri, nokuti i nzvimbo tsvene dzakasvika areka yaJehovha.
\p
\v	12	Ipapo Soromoni wakapa zvipo zvinopiswa kuna Jehovha paaritari yaJehovha, yaaiva avaka pamberi peberere.
\v	13	Izvo zvakatarirwa zuva nezuva, achipa zvichienderana nemurairo waMozisi, pamasabata, nepakugara kwemwedzi, nepamitambo yakadzama, katatu pagore, pamutambo wezvingwa zvisina mbiriso, nepamutambo wemavhiki, nepamutambo wematumba.
\p
\v	14	Zvino wakagadza zvichienderana nechimiso chaDhavhidhi baba vake, mapoka evapristi pamabasa avo, nevaRevhi pangava ravo, kurumbidza nekushumira pamberi pevapristi, zvichienderana nechinhu chezuva pazuva racho, nevachengeti vemikova pamapoka avo pasuwo rimwe nerimwe; nokuti saizvozvo waiva murairo waDhavhidhi munhu waMwari.
\v	15	Zvino havana kutsauka pamurairo wamambo kuvapristi nevaRevhi maererano nechinhu chose nemaererano nezvakakosha.
\v	16	Zvino basa rose raSoromoni rakagadzirwa kusvika pazuva rekuteiwa kweimba yaJehovha uye kusvika pakupera kwayo. Imba yaJehovha ikazopera.
\p
\v	17	Ipapo Soromoni wakaenda kuEziyoni-Gebheri nekuEroti, pamahombekombe egungwa panyika yaEdhomu.
\v	18	Hurami ndokumutumira neruoko rwevaranda vake zvikepe nevaranda vaiva neruzivo rwegungwa; ndokuenda nevaranda vaSoromoni kuOfiri, ndokutorapo matarenda mazana mana nemakumi mashanu egoridhe, ndokuunza kuna mambo Soromoni.
\c	9
\s	Mambokadzi weShebha anoshanyira Soromoni
\p
\v	1	MambokadziweShebha paakanzwa mbiri yaSoromoni, wakauya kuJerusarema kuzoedza Soromoni nemibvunzo mikukutu, nechaunga chikuru kwazvo, nemakamera akatakura zvinonhuhwira, negoridhe neuwandu, nemabwe anokosha; paakasvika kuna Soromoni, wakataura naye zvose zvaiva mumoyo make.
\v	2	Soromonindokumutsanangurira mashoko ake ose; uye kwaiva kusina chinhu chakavanda kuna Soromoni chaasina kumutsanangurira.
\v	3	MambokadziweShebha paakaona kuchenjera kwaSoromoni, neimba yaakavaka,
\v	4	nezvekudyazvetafura yake, nekugara kwevaranda vake, nekumira kwevashumiri vake, nezvipfeko zvavo, nevabati vemikombe vake, nezvipfeko zvavo, nechikwiriso chake chaakwira nacho kuimba yaJehovha, zvino mukasava nemweya maari.
\v	5	Zvinowakati kuna mambo: Guhu randakanzwa munyika yangu renyaya yenyu neuchenjeri hwenyu raiva rechokwadi.
\v	6	Asihandina kutenda mashoko avo kusvika ndauya, uye meso angu aona; zvino tarirai, hafu yeukuru hweuchenjeri hwenyu handina kuiudzwa. Munopfuura guhu randakanzwa.
\v	7	Varumevenyu vanofara, uye varanda venyu ava vanofara, vamire pamberi penyu nguva dzose, vachinzwa uchenjeri hwenyu.
\v	8	JehovhaMwari wenyu ngaarumbidzwe, iye wakafadzwa nemwi, kukuisai pachigaro chake cheushe, kuva mambo waJehovha Mwari wenyu; nokuti Mwari wenyu wakada Israeri, kumusimbisa kusvika rinhi narinhi, naizvozvo wakakuisai muve mambo pamusoro pavo kuti muite mutongo nekururama.
\v	9	Zvinowakapa mambo matarenda zana nemakumi maviri egoridhe, nezvinonhuhwira neuzhinji ukuru, nemabwe anokosha; uye hakuna kuvapo zvinonhuhwira zvakadai, mambokadzi weShebha zvaakapa mambo Soromoni.
\v	10	Nevaranda vaHuramiwo nevaranda vaSoromoni vakauisa goridhe kubva Ofiri, vakauya nemiti yemiarigumi nemabwe anokosha.
\v	11	Mambo ndokuita nematanda emiarigumi zvikwiriso zveimba yaJehovha nezveimba yamambo, nembira nemitengeranwa zvevaimbi; uye hakuna kumboonekwa nezvakadaro munyika yaJudha.
\v	12	Mambo Soromoni ndokupa mambokadzi weShebha chishuvo chake chose chaakakumbira, kunze kweizvo zvaaiva auya nazvo kuna mambo. Naizvozvo wakatendeuka ndokuenda kunyika yake, iye nevaranda vake.
\s	Fuma yaSoromoni nembiri yake
\p
\v	13	Zvino uremu hwegoridhe rakauya kuna Soromoni mugore rimwe chete hwaiva matarenda mazana matanhatu nemakumi matanhatu nematanhatu egoridhe;
\v	14	kunze kwezvavakauya nazvo kubva kuvatengi nekuvatengesi; nemadzimambo ose eArabhiya nevabati venyika vakauya negoridhe nesirivha kuna Soromoni.
\p
\v	15	Mambo Soromoni wakaita nhovo huru mazana maviri, goridhe rakapfurwa;  mashekeri mazana matatu egoridhe akaenda panhovo huru imwe.
\v	16	Nenhovo diki mazana matatu dzegoridhe rakapfurwa;  mashekeri mazana matatu egoridhe akaenda panhovo imwe; mambo ndokuzviisa muimba yesango reRebhanoni.
\v	17	Mambo wakaitawo chigaro cheushe chikuru chenyanga dzenzou, ndokuchidzudzura negoridhe rakazara.
\v	18	Zvino kwaiva nezvikwiriso zvitanhatu kuchigaro cheushe, nechitsiko chetsoka chegoridhe, zvakasunganidzwa nechigaro cheushe, uye zvizemberamaoko kuno neuko kwenzvimbo yekugara, uye shumba mbiri dzimire pedo nezvizemberamaoko.
\v	19	Zvino paiva pamire shumba gumi nembiri ipapo kuno neuko pazvikwiriso zvitanhatu; hakuna kuitwa chakadai kune humwe ushe.
\p
\v	20	Nemidziyo yose yekunwira yamambo Soromoni yaiva yegoridhe, nemidziyo yose yeimba yesango reRebhanoni yaiva yegoridhe rakazara; sirivha yaiva isingaverengwi sechinhu pamazuva aSoromoni.
\v	21	Nokuti zvikepe zvamambo zvaienda kuTarishishi pamwe nevaranda vaHurami; pamakore matatu oga-oga zvikepe zveTarishishi zvaiuya zvakatakura goridhe, nesirivha, nyanga dzenzou, nemakudo, nematendera.
\v	22	Mambo Soromoni ndokuva mukuru kumadzimambo ose enyika pafuma nekuchenjera.
\p
\v	23	Zvino madzimambo ose epanyika akatsvaka chiso chaSoromoni kunzwa uchenjeri hwake Mwari hwaakaisa mumoyo make.
\v	24	Zvino vakauya umwe neumwe nechipo chake, midziyo yesirivha, nemidziyo yegoridhe, nezvipfeko, zvombo, zvinonhuhwira, mabhiza, nemahesera, chinhu chegore pagore.
\p
\v	25	Zvino Soromoni waiva nezvidyiro zvemabhiza zvuru zvina, nengoro, nevatasvi vemabhiza zvuru gumi nezviviri, zvaakaisa pamaguta engoro, nekuna mambo paJerusarema.
\p
\v	26	Zvino wakatonga pamusoro pemadzimambo ose kubva parwizi kusvika panyika yevaFirisitia, nekusvika kumuganhu weEgipita.
\v	27	Mambo ndokuita sirivha paJerusarema semabwe, nemisidhari wakaita semionde yaiva pamapani pauwandu.
\v	28	Zvino vakabudisira Soromoni mabhiza kubva paEgipita, nekubva kunyika dzose.
\s	Kufa kwaSoromoni
\p
\v	29	Zvino dzimwe nyaya dzaSoromoni, dzekutanga nedzekupedzisira, hadzina kunyorwa here pamanzwi aNatani muporofita, nemuchiporofita chaAhiya muShiro, nemuzviratidzo zvaIdho muoni pamusoro paJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati?
\v	30	Soromoni wakatonga paJerusarema pamusoro paIsraeri wose makore makumi mana.
\v	31	Soromoni ndokurara nemadzibaba ake; uye vakamuviga muguta raDhavhidhi baba vake. Rehobhoamu mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
