\b	1 Madzimambo
\c	1
\s	Soromoni anotevera baba vake Dhavhidhi paushe
\p
\v	1	Zvinomambo Dhavhidhi waiva akwegura, apinda mumakore  mazhinji; zvino vakamufukidza nedzinguvo, asi haana kudziya.
\v	2	Naizvozvovaranda vake vakati kwaari: Ishe wangu mambo ngaatsvakirwe musikana mhandara, uye ngaamire pamberi pamambo, uye ave muongi kwaari, uye arare pachipfuva chenyu, kuti ishe wangu mambo adziye.
\v	3	Zvinovakatsvaka musikana wakanaka pamiganhu yose yaIsraeri, ndokuwana Abhishagi muShunami, ndokumuunza kuna mambo.
\v	4	Zvinomusikana waiva akanaka zvikuru kwazvo, iye ndokuva muongi wamambo, ndokumushandira, asi mambo haana kumuziva.
\p
\v	5	ZvinoAdhonia mwanakomana waHagiti wakazvikudza achiti: Ini ndichava mambo. Zvino wakazvigadzirira ngoro nevatasvi vemabhiza, nevarume makumi mashanu vaimhanya pamberi pake.
\v	6	Asibaba vake vaiva vasina kumutsamwisa kubva pamazuva avo vachiti: Unoitirei saizvozvo? Naiyewo waiva nechimiro chakanaka kwazvo, uye  mai vake vamubereka shure kwaAbhusaromu.
\v	7	Zvinomanzwi ake aiva pamwe naJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya uye pamwe naAbhiatari mupristi; ndokubatsira vachitevera Adhonia.
\v	8	AsiZadhoki mupristi, naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, naNatani muporofita, naShimei, naRei, nevarume vane simba Dhavhidhi vaaiva navo, vaiva vasiri kuna Adhonia.
\v	9	ZvinoAdhonia wakabaya makwai nenzombe nezvakakodzwa padombo reZohereti, riri paEni-Rogeri, ndokudana hama dzake dzose, vanakomana vamambo, nevarume vose vaJudha, varanda vamambo.
\v	10	Asi Natani muporofita, naBhenaya, nevarume vane simba, naSoromoni munin'ina wake, haana kuvadana.
\p
\v	11	Naizvozvo Natani wakataura kuna Bhati-Shebha mai vaSoromoni achiti: Hauna kunzwa here kuti Adhonia mwanakomana waHagiti anotonga, asi Dhavhidhi ishe wedu haazivi?
\v	12	Naizvozvo zvino, uya, rega hako ndikupe zano, kuti uponese upenyu hwako neupenyu hwemwanakomana wako Soromoni.
\v	13	Enda upinde kuna mambo Dhavhidhi uti kwaari: Ishe wangu mambo, imwi hamuna kupika here kumurandakadzi wenyu muchiti: Zvirokwazvo Soromoni mwanakomana wako achatonga shure kwangu, uye iye achagara pachigaro changu cheushe? Zvino Adhonia anotongerei?
\v	14	Tarira, uchiri kutaura ipapo namambo, iniwo ndichapinda shure kwako, ndigosimbisa mashoko ako.
\p
\v	15	Zvino Bhati-Shebha wakapinda kuna mambo mumba; zvino mambo waiva akwegura kwazvo, uye Abhishagi muShunami waishandira mambo.
\v	16	Bhati-Shebha wakakotama ndokunamata kuna mambo. Mambo ndokuti: Chii  chinodikanwa kwauri?
\v	17	Iye ndokuti kwaari: Ishe wangu, imwi makapika naJehovha Mwari wenyu kumurandakadzi wenyu,  muchiti: Zvirokwazvo, Soromoni mwanakomana wako achatonga shure kwangu, uye iye achagara pachigaro changu cheushe.
\v	18	Asi ikozvino tarirai, Adhonia anotonga; zvino ikozvino ishe wangu mambo, hamuzivi.
\v	19	Uye wabaya nzombe nezvakakodzwa nemakwai mazhinji, uye wadana vanakomana vose vamambo, naAbhiatari mupristi, naJoabhu mukuru wehondo, asi Soromoni muranda wenyu haana ku mudana.
\v	20	Zvino imwi ishe wangu mambo, meso aIsraeri wose ari pamusoro penyu, kuti muvaudze kuti ndiani achagara pachigaro cheushe chashe wangu mambo pashure pake.
\v	21	Zvimwe zvichaitika kuti apo ishe wangu mambo arara nemadzibaba ake, kuti ini nemwanakomana wangu Soromoni  tichava vatadzi.
\p
\v	22	Zvino tarira, achiri kutaura namambo, Natani muporofita wakapindawo.
\v	23	Zvino vakaudza mambo vachiti: Tarirai Natani muporofita. Uye wapinda pamberi pamambo, ndokukotamira pasi nechiso chake pamberi pamambo.
\v	24	Zvino Natani wakati: Ishe wangu, mambo, imwi makareva here  kuti: Adhonia achatonga pashure pangu uye iye achagara pachigaro changu cheushe?
\v	25	Nokuti waburuka nhasi, uye wabaya nzombe nezvakakodzwa nemakwai mazhinji, ndokudana vanakomana vose vamambo, nemisoro yehondo, naAbhiatari mupristi; zvino tarirai, vari kudya nekunwa pamberi pake, uye vanoti: Mambo Adhonia ngaararame!
\v	26	Asi ini, ini muranda wenyu, naZadhoki mupristi, naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, nemuranda wenyu Soromoni, haana ku tidana.
\v	27	Chinhu ichi chinoitwa nashe wangu mambo here, uye hamuna kuzivisa muranda wenyu kuti ndiani achagara pachigaro cheushe chashe wangu mambo pashure pake?
\p
\v	28	Zvino mambo Dhavhidhi wakapindura ndokuti: Ndidanire Bhati-Shebha. Iye ndokupinda pamberi pechiso chamambo, ndokumira pamberi pamambo.
\v	29	Zvino mambo wakapika ndokuti:  NaJehovha mupenyu, iye wakadzikinura mweya weupenyu wangu pakutambudzika kwese:
\v	30	Zvirokwazvo, sezvandakakupikira naJehovha Mwari waIsraeri ndichiti: Zvirokwazvo Soromoni mwanakomana wako achatonga pashure pangu, uye iye achagara pachigaro changu cheushe pachinzvimbo changu; zvirokwazvo ndichaita saizvozvo nhasi.
\v	31	Bhati-Shebha ndokukotamira pasi nechiso, ndokunamata kuna mambo, ndokuti: Ishe wangu mambo Dhavhidhi ngaararame kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\p
\v	32	Mambo Dhavhidhi ndokuti: Ndidanirei Zadhoki mupristi, naNatani muporofita, naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha. Ivo ndokupinda pamberi pamambo.
\v	33	Mambo ndokuti kwavari: Torai pamwe nemwi varanda vashe wenyu, mutasvise Soromoni mwanakomana wangu panyurusi riri rangu mugomuburusira paGihoni.
\v	34	Uye Zadhoki mupristi naNatani muporofita ngavamuzodze ipapo ave mambo pamusoro paIsraeri; zvino muridze hwamanda, uye muti: Mambo Soromoni ngaararame!
\v	35	Zvino muchakwira shure kwake, kuti auye agogara pachigaro changu cheushe, uye iye atonge pachinzvimbo changu; uye iye ndamugadza kuti ave mutongi pamusoro paIsraeri nepamusoro paJudha.
\v	36	Zvino Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha wakapindura mambo ndokuti: Ameni! Ngaareve saizvozvo Jehovha, Mwari washe wangu mambo.
\v	37	Jehovha sezvaaiva nashe wangu mambo, saizvozvo ngaave naSoromoni, uye aite chigaro chake cheushe chikuru pane chigaro cheushe chashe wangu mambo Dhavhidhi.
\v	38	Zvino Zadhoki mupristi, naNatani muporofita, naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, nevaKereti nevaPereti vakaburuka, ndokutasvisa Soromoni panyurusi ramambo Dhavhidhi, ndokumuendesa kuGihoni.
\v	39	Zvino Zadhoki mupristi wakatora nyanga yemafuta kubva mutende ndokuzodza Soromoni. Zvino vakaridza hwamanda, vanhu vose ndokuti: Mambo Soromoni ngaararame!
\v	40	Zvino vanhu vose vakakwira shure kwake; vanhu ndokuridza nyere, vakafara nemufaro mukuru; naizvozvo nyika yakatsemuka nenzwi ravo.
\p
\v	41	Zvino Adhonia nevakokwa vose vaiva naye vakanzwa ivo pavakapedza kudya. Joabhu paakanzwa kurira kwehwamanda wakati: Nderwei ruzha rweguta rwune mhere-mhere?
\v	42	Achiri kutaura, tarira, kwakasvika Jonatani mwanakomana waAbhiatari mupristi; Adhonia ndokuti: Pinda, nokuti uri mhare, unouya nemashoko akanaka.
\v	43	Zvino Jonatani wakapindura ndokuti kuna Adhonia: Zvirokwazvo ishe wedu mambo Dhavhidhi wakagadza Soromoni kuva mambo.
\v	44	Uye mambo watuma pamwe naye Zadhoki mupristi, naNatani muporofita, naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, nevaKereti nevaPereti, uye vamutasvisa panyurusi ramambo.
\v	45	Uye Zadhoki mupristi naNatani muporofita vamuzodza paGihoni kuva mambo; zvino vakwira kubvapo vachifara, naizvozvo guta rinounga. Ndirwo ruzha rwamanzwa.
\v	46	Uyewo Soromoni wagara pachigaro cheushe cheumambo.
\v	47	Uyewo varanda vamambo vasvika kuropafadza ishe wedu mambo Dhavhidhi vachiti: Mwari wenyu ngaaite zita raSoromoni rive nani pazita renyu, uye aite chigaro chake cheushe chikure kupfuura chigaro chenyu cheushe. Mambo ndokukotama pamubhedha.
\v	48	Uyewo saizvozvo mambo wati: Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri ngaakudzwe, wakapa nhasi anogara pachigaro changu cheushe, meso angu achi zviona.
\v	49	Zvino vakokwa vose vaiva naAdhonia vakatya, ndokusimuka, ndokuenda, umwe neumwe nenzira yake.
\p
\v	50	Zvino Adhonia wakatya nekuda kwaSoromoni, ndokusimuka, ndokuenda, ndokubata nyanga dzearitari.
\v	51	Zvino kwakaudzwa Soromoni zvichinzi: Tarirai, Adhonia anotya mambo Soromoni, nokuti tarirai wabata nyanga dzearitari achiti: Mambo Soromoni ngaapike kwandiri nhasi, kuti haangaurayi muranda wake nemunondo.
\v	52	Soromoni ndokuti: Kana akava murume chaiye, hakungawiri  chinhu pasi paruvhudzi rwake; asi kana kuipa kukawanikwa maari, achafa.
\v	53	Mambo Soromoni ndokutuma  nhume, zvino akamuburusa paaritari. Zvino wakauya ndokunamata kuna mambo Soromoni. Soromoni ndokuti kwaari: Enda kumba kwako.
\c	10
\s	Mambokadzi weShebha anoshanyira Soromoni
\p
\v	1	MambokadziweShebha paakanzwa mbiri yaSoromoni maererano nezita raJehovha, wakauya kuzomuedza nemibvunzo mikukutu.
\v	2	Zvinowakasvika kuJerusarema ane dungwe guru kwazvo, makamera akatakura zvinonhuhwira, negoridhe zhinji-zhinji, nemabwe anokosha. Paakasvika kuna Soromoni wakataura kwaari zvese zvaiva mumoyo make.
\v	3	Soromonindokududzira kwaari mibvunzo yake yese; hakuna chinhu chaiva chakavanzika kuna mambo, chaaiva asina kududzira kwaari.
\v	4	Zvinomambokadzi weShebha aona kuchenjera kwese kwaSoromoni, neimba yaaiva avaka,
\v	5	nezvekudyazvetafura yake, nekugara kwevaranda vake, nekumira kwevashandiri vake, nenguvo dzavo, nevabati vemikombe vake, nechikwiriso chake chaakwira nacho kuimba yaJehovha, akasazova nemweya maari.
\v	6	Zvinowakati kuna mambo: Shoko randakanzwa munyika yangu maererano nenyaya uye neuchenjeri hwenyu nderechokwadi.
\v	7	Asihandina kutenda mashoko iwawo kusvika ndauya, nemeso angu akaona; zvino tarira, handina kuudzwa hafu; makawedzera uchenjeri nekunaka pamashoko andakanzwa.
\v	8	Mufarowevanhu venyu, mufaro wevaranda venyu ava vanoramba vakamira pamberi penyu vachinzwa uchenjeri hwenyu.
\v	9	NgaarumbidzweJehovha Mwari wenyu wakafadzwa nemwi kukuisai pachigaro cheushe chaIsraeri. Nokuti Jehovha wakada Israeri nekusingaperi, naizvozvo wakakuitai mambo kuti muite mutongo nekururama.
\v	10	Zvino wakapa mambo matarenda zana nemakumi maviri egoridhe, nezvinonhuhwira zvizhinji kwazvo, nemabwe anokosha; hakuna kuzouyazve zvinonhuhwira zvakawanda seizvo mambokadzi weShebha zvaakapa mambo Soromoni.
\v	11	Uyezve zvikepe zvaHiramu zvaitakura goridhe kubva Ofiri zvaiuya nemiti yakawanda kwazvo yemiarimugi nemabwe anokosha kubva Ofiri.
\v	12	Mambo ndokuita nematanda emiarimugi mbiru dzeimba yaJehovha nedzeimba yamambo, uye mbira nemitengeranwa zvevaimbi; hakuna kumbouiswa kana kuonekwa miti yemiarimugi yakadai kusvika nhasi.
\v	13	Zvino mambo Soromoni wakapa mambokadzi weShebha zvishuwo zvake zvese zvaakakumbira kunze kweizvi wakamupa chipo chekupa chamambo Soromoni. Iye ndokutendeuka ndokuenda kunyika yake, iye nevaranda vake.
\s	Fuma yaSoromoni nembiri yake
\p
\v	14	Zvino uremu hwegoridhe hwakasvika kuna Soromoni negore rimwe hwaiva matarenda egoridhe mazana matanhatu nemakumi matanhatu nematanhatu;
\v	15	kunze kwerevashambadziri, nerevatengesi vezvirungiso, neremadzimambo ese eArabhiya, nerevatongi venyika.
\p
\v	16	Mambo Soromoni ndokuita nhovo huru mazana maviri negoridhe rakatakiwa;  mashekeri mazana matatu egoridhe akapinda munhovo imwe huru;
\v	17	nenhovo diki mazana matatu dzegoridhe rakavangiwa; mapaundi matatu egoridhe akapinda munhovo imwe diki; mambo ndokuzviisa mumba yedondo reRebhanoni.
\p
\v	18	Mambo wakaitawo chigaro chikuru cheushe chenyanga dzenzou, ndokuchidzudzura negoridhe rakaisvonaka.
\v	19	Chigaro cheushe chaiva nezvikwiriso zvitanhatu; uye musoro wechigaro cheushe waiva wakatenderedzwa shure; uye zvitsigiro kuno neuko kusvika panzvimbo yekugara; neshumba mbiri dzakamira panhivi dzezvitsigiro.
\v	20	Zvino shumba gumi nembiri dzaiva dzimirepo pazvikwiriso zvitanhatu, kuno neuko. Hakuna kumboitwa saizvozvo kune  humwe ushe hwose.
\p
\v	21	Zvino mikombe yese yekunwira yamambo Soromoni yaiva yegoridhe; uye midziyo yese yeimba yedondo reRebhanoni yaiva yegoridhe rakazara; kwaiva kusina sirivha  mazviri; iyo yaiva isingaverengwi sechinhu pamazuva aSoromoni.
\v	22	Nokuti mambo waiva nezvikepe zveTarishishi pagungwa pamwe nezvikepe zvaHiramu; kamwe pamakore matatu zvikepe zveTarishishi zvaiuya, zvakatakura goridhe, nesirivha, nyanga dzenzou, netsoko, nematendera.
\v	23	Naizvozvo mambo Soromoni waiva akakura pane madzimambo ese enyika pakufuma nepauchenjeri.
\p
\v	24	Nyika yese ndokutsvaka chiso chaSoromoni kunzwa uchenjeri hwake Mwari hwaakaisa mumoyo make.
\v	25	Zvino vakaunza umwe neumwe chipo chake: Midziyo yesirivha, nemidziyo yegoridhe, nenguvo, nezvombo, nezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira, mabhiza, nemanyurusi, chinhu chegore pagore.
\p
\v	26	Soromoni ndokuunganidza ngoro nevatasvi vemabhiza. Zvino waiva nengoro churu nemazana mana, nevatasvi vemabhiza zvuru gumi nezviviri; akazviisa mumaguta engoro, nekuna mambo paJerusarema.
\v	27	Mambo ndokuita sirivha kuita semabwe paJerusarema, uye wakaita misidhari kuita semionde yesikamori mumupata pakuwanda.
\p
\v	28	Zvino mabhiza aiva aSoromoni aibva kuEgipita, nemucheka wakapfava, vatengesi vamambo vaiutora mucheka wakapfava nemutengo.
\v	29	Ngoro ndokukwira ndokubuda kubva muEgipita ne mashekeri esirivha mazana matanhatu, uye bhiza ne mashekeri zana nemakumi mashanu; saizvozvowo kumadzimambo ese evaHiti, nekumadzimambo evaSiriya, vakaabudisa neruoko rwavo.
\c	11
\s	Soromoni anotsauka panzira dzaJehovha
\p
\v	1	Zvinomambo Soromoni waiva achida vakadzi vazhinji vatorwa pamwe nemukunda waFarao: Vakadzi vevaMoabhu, vakadzi vevaAmoni, vakadzi vevaEdhomu, vakadzi vevaSidhoni,  nevakadzi vevaHiti;
\v	2	vaibvakundudzi Jehovha dzaaiva ati kuvana vaIsraeri: Hamungapindi kwavari, uye ivo havangapindi kwamuri; zvirokwazvo vachashandura moyo wenyu utevere vamwari vavo. Ivavo Soromoni wakanamatira kwavari murudo.
\v	3	Zvinowaiva ane vakadzi mazana manomwe, vakunda vemadzimambo, nevarongo mazana matatu. Vakadzi vake ndokutsausa moyo wake.
\v	4	Nokutizvakaitika panguva yekukwegura kwaSoromoni,  kuti vakadzi vake vakatsausa moyo wake kutevera vamwe vamwari; uye moyo wake ukasava wakaperera kuna Jehovha Mwari wake, semoyo waDhavhidhi baba vake.
\v	5	NokutiSoromoni wakafamba shure kwaAshitoreti mwari wevaSidhoni, uye shure kwaMirikomi chinyangadzo chevaAmoni.
\v	6	Soromonindokuita zvakaipa pameso aJehovha, uye haana kutevera Jehovha zvakazara saDhavhidhi baba vake.
\v	7	Nenguvaiyoyo Soromoni wakavakira Kemoshi chinyangadzo chaMoabhu nzvimbo yakakwirira pagomo riri pamberi peJerusarema, naMoreki chinyangadzo chevana vaAmoni.
\v	8	Saizvozvowakaitira vakadzi vake vese vatorwa, vaipisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira uye vaibaira kuna vamwari vavo.
\p
\v	9	ZvinoJehovha wakatsamwira Soromoni, nokuti moyo wake wakatsauka kubva pana Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri, iye waiva aonekera kwaari kaviri,
\v	10	ndokumuraira maererano nechinhu ichi, kuti arege kutevera vamwe vamwari; asi haana kuchengeta izvo Jehovha zvaakaraira.
\v	11	Naizvozvo Jehovha wakati kuna Soromoni: Nekuda kwekuti izvi zvaitwa newe uye hauna kuchengeta sungano yangu nezvimiso zvangu zvandakakuraira, ndichabvarura zvirokwazvo ushe kubva kwauri ndigohupa kumuranda wako.
\v	12	Zvakadaro handingazviiti pamazuva ako nekuda kwaDhavhidhi baba vako; ndichahubvarura hubve muruoko rwemwanakomana wako.
\v	13	Zvakadaro handingabvaruri ushe hwose. Ndichapa mwanakomana wako rudzi rumwe nekuda kwaDhavhidhi muranda wangu, uye nekuda kweJerusarema randakasarudza.
\p
\v	14	Zvino Jehovha wakamutsira Soromoni muvengi, Hadhadhi muEdhomu; iye waiva wechizvarwa chamambo paEdhomu.
\v	15	Nokuti zvakaitika Dhavhidhi paaiva paEdhomu, Joabhu mukuru wehondo paakakwira kunoviga vakaurawa, wakarova varume vese paEdhomu.
\v	16	Nokuti Joabhu wakagarapo mwedzi mitanhatu, naIsraeri wose, kusvika agura murume umwe neumwe paEdhomu.
\v	17	Asi Hadhadhi wakatiza, iye nevamwe vaEdhomu vevaranda vababa vake vanaye, kupinda muEgipita; uye Hadhadhi waiva mwanana.
\v	18	Zvino vakasimuka kubva paMidhiani ndokusvika kuParani; ndokutora varume pamwe navo kubva paParani, ndokusvika kuEgipita kuna Farao mambo weEgipita, iye wakamupa imba ndokumugurira zvekudya, ndokumupa ivhu.
\v	19	Hadhadhi ndokuwana nyasha kwazvo pameso aFarao; iye ndokumupa mukadzi, muramu wake, munin'ina waTapenesi vahosi vamambo.
\v	20	Munin'ina waTapenesi ndokumuberekera Genubhati mwanakomana wake, Tapenesi waakarumurira mumba maFarao; Genubhati ndokuva mumba maFarao pakati pevanakomana vaFarao.
\v	21	Hadhadhi paakanzwa ari muEgipita kuti Dhavhidhi warara nemadzibaba ake, uye kuti Joabhu mukuru wehondo wafa, Hadhadhi wakati kuna Farao: Nditenderei ndiende kunyika yangu.
\v	22	Farao ndokuti kwaari: Asi washaiwei uneni kuti, tarira, utsvake kuenda kunyika yekwako? Iye ndokuti: Hapana; kunyange zvakadaro nditenderei kwazvo ndiende.
\p
\v	23	Zvino Mwari wakamumutsira muvengi, Rezonhi mwanakomana waEriadha, waiva atiza kubva kuna ishe wake Hadhadhezeri mambo weZobha,
\v	24	ndokuzviunganidzira varume, ndokuva mukuru weboka, Dhavhidhi paaiuraya. Zvino vakaenda Dhamasiko ndokugaramo, ndokutonga paDhamasiko.
\v	25	Zvino wakava muvengi kuna Israeri mazuva ese aSoromoni, uye pamusoro pezvakaipa Hadhadhi zvaa kaita; iye ndokusema Israeri, ndokutonga pamusoro peSiriya.
\p
\v	26	Zvino Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, muEfuremu weZeredha, muranda waSoromoni, zita ramai vake raiva Zerua, mukadzi chirikadzi, naiye wakasimudzira mambo ruoko.
\v	27	Iyi ndiyo nyaya yaakasimudzirira mambo ruoko: Soromoni wakavaka Miro, akavhara buri reguta raDhavhidhi baba vake.
\v	28	Zvino murume Jerobhoamu waiva murume wesimba weumhare. Soromoni aona jaya kuti waiva muiti webasa, wakamuisa pamusoro pemutoro wese weimba yaJosefa.
\v	29	Zvino zvakaitika panguva iyo Jerobhoamu paakabuda muJerusarema kuti muporofita Ahija muShiro wakamuwana panzira; zvino waiva akazvipfekedza nguvo itsva; uye vaiva vaviri vega kusango.
\v	30	Ahija ndokubata nguvo itsva yaiva paari, ndokuibvarura kuita mapandi gumi nemaviri.
\v	31	Zvino wakati kuna Jerobhoamu: Zvitorere mapandi gumi; nokuti anoreva seizvi Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri: Tarira, ndinobvarura ushe kubva muruoko rwaSoromoni, uye ndichapa kwauri marudzi gumi.
\v	32	Asi iye achava nerudzi rumwe nekuda kwemuranda wangu Dhavhidhi uye nekuda kweJerusarema guta randakasarudza kubva kumarudzi ese aIsraeri.
\v	33	Nokuti vakandisiya ndokunamata Ashitoreti mwari wevaSidhoni, Kemoshi mwari wevaMoabhu, naMirikomi mwari wevana vaAmoni; uye havana kufamba munzira dzangu kuita zvakarurama pameso angu uye  kuchengeta zvimiso zvangu nemitongo yangu, saDhavhidhi baba vake.
\v	34	Kunyange zvakadaro handingabvisi ushe hwese muruoko rwake, asi ndichamuita muchinda mazuva ese eupenyu hwake, nekuda kwaDhavhidhi muranda wangu wandakasarudza, wakachengeta mirairo yangu nezvimiso zvangu.
\v	35	Asi ndichabvisa ushe muruoko rwemwanakomana wake, ndigohupa iwe, marudzi gumi.
\v	36	Asi kumwanakomana wake ndichapa rudzi rumwe, kuti Dhavhidhi muranda wangu ave nemwenje mazuva ese pamberi pangu paJerusarema, guta randakazvisarudzira kuisa zita rangupo.
\v	37	Zvino ndichakutora uye uchatonga pamusoro pezvese mweya weupenyu wako zvaunoshuva; uye uve mambo pamusoro paIsraeri.
\v	38	Zvino kuchati kana ukateerera zvese zvandinokuraira, uye ukafamba munzira dzangu uye ukaita zvakarurama pameso angu, kuchengeta zvimiso zvangu nemirairo yangu sezvakaita Dhavhidhi muranda wangu, ndichava newe, ndichakuvakira imba yakasimba, sezvandakavakira Dhavhidhi, ndigopa Israeri kwauri.
\v	39	Uye nekuda kweizvi ndicharwadzisa mbeu yaDhavhidhi, asi kwete mazuva ose.
\v	40	Naizvozvo Soromoni wakatsvaka kuuraya Jerobhoamu; asi Jerobhoamu wakasimuka ndokutizira kuEgipita kuna Shishaki mambo weEgipita; ndokuva muEgipita kusvika pakufa kwaSoromoni.
\s	Kufa kwake
\p
\v	41	Zvino amwe mashoko aSoromoni, nezvese zvaakaita, neuchenjeri hwake, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku remashoko aSoromoni?
\v	42	Zvino nguva Soromoni yaakatonga paJerusarema pamusoro paIsraeri wese yakava makore makumi mana.
\v	43	Soromoni ndokurara nemadzibaba ake, ndokuvigwa muguta raDhavhidhi baba vake; Rehobhoamu mwanakomana wake ndokutonga pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	12
\s	Marudzi ane gumi anotsaura Jerobhoamu kuva mambo wavo
\p
\v	1	ZvinoRehobhoamu wakaenda kuShekemu, nokuti Israeri wese waiva asvika kuShekemu kumuisa ave mambo.
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati waiva achiri muEgipita paakazvinzwa (nokuti waiva atiza kubva pamberi paSoromoni mambo, Jerobhoamu ndokugara muEgipita),
\v	3	zvekutivakatuma  nhume ndokumudana; Jerobhoamu neungano yese yaIsraeri ndokuuya ndokutaura naRehobhoamu vachiti:
\v	4	Babavenyu vakaita joko redu riome; naizvozvo zvino imwi rerusai basa rakaoma rababa venyu, nejoko ravo rinorema ravakaisa pamusoro pedu, zvino tichakushumirai.
\v	5	Zvinowakati kwavari: Endaizve mazuva matatu mugodzokera kwandiri. Vanhu ndokuenda.
\p
\v	6	Zvinomambo Rehobhoamu wakapangana nevakuru vaimira pamberi paSoromoni baba vake vachiri vapenyu, vachiti: Munopanga zano sei kuti tidzosere mhinduro vanhu ava?
\v	7	Zvinovakataura kwaari vachiti: Kana muchava muranda kuvanhu ava nhasi, uye mukavashandira, muvapindure mutaure kwavari mashoko akanaka, ipapo vachava varanda venyu mazuva ose.
\v	8	Asiwakaramba zano revakuru ravaiva vamupanga, ndokupangana nemajaya akakura naye, aimira pamberi pake.
\v	9	Zvinowakati kwavari: Munopanga zano ripi kuti tidzosere mhinduro kuvanhu ava vakataura neni vachiti: Rerusai joko baba venyu ravakaisa pamusoro pedu?
\v	10	Zvino majaya akakura naye akataura kwaari achiti: Ndizvo zvamuchataura kuvanhu ava vakataura kwamuri vachiti: Baba venyu vakaita joko redu rireme, asi imwi itai rive rakareruka kwatiri; ndizvo zvamuchataura kwavari:  Munwe wangu mudiki mukobvu kupfuura chiuno chababa vangu.
\v	11	Zvino kana baba vangu vakakutakudzai joko rinorema, ini ndichawedzera pajoko renyu; baba vangu vaikurangai neshamhu, asi ini ndichakurangai nezvinyavada.
\p
\v	12	Naizvozvo Jerobhoamu nevanhu vese vakasvika kuna Rehobhoamu nezuva retatu, mambo sezvaaiva ataura achiti: Dzokai kwandiri nezuva retatu.
\v	13	Mambo ndokupindura vanhu nehasha, akasiya zano revakuru ravakamupanga;
\v	14	ndokutaura kwavari zvichienderana nezano remajaya, achiti: Baba vangu vakaita joko renyu rireme, asi ini ndichawedzera pajoko renyu; baba vangu vakakurangai neshamhu, asi ini ndichakurangai nezvinyavada.
\v	15	Naizvozvo mambo haana kuteerera kuvanhu; nokuti shanduko iyi yakabva kuna Jehovha kuti asimbise shoko rake Jehovha raakataura neruoko rwaAhija muShiro kuna Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati.
\p
\v	16	Israeri wese paakaona kuti mambo haana kuvateerera, vanhu vakadzosera mambo shoko vachiti: Tine mugove wei kuna Dhavhidhi? Hongu, ha tina nhaka kumwanakomana waJese. Kumatende enyu, Israeri. Ikozvino zvionere imba yako, Dhavhidhi. Naizvozvo Israeri wakaenda kumatende avo.
\v	17	Maererano nevana vaIsraeri vaigara mumaguta aJudha, Rehobhoamu waitonga pamusoro pavo.
\p
\v	18	Zvino mambo Rehobhoamu wakatuma Adhoramu waiva pamusoro pechibharo; Israeri wese ndokumutaka nemabwe kusvika afa. Naizvozvo mambo Rehobhoamu wakakurumidza kukwira mungoro kutizira kuJerusarema.
\v	19	Saizvozvo Israeri wakamukira imba yaDhavhidhi kusvika zuva rino.
\p
\v	20	Zvino zvakaitika Israeri wese paakanzwa kuti Jerobhoamu wadzoka, vakatuma  nhume ndokumudanira kuungano, ndokumuita mambo pamusoro paIsraeri wese; hakuna wakatevera imba yaDhavhidhi kunze kwerudzi rwaJudha chete.
\p
\v	21	Rehobhoamu paakasvika kuJerusarema, wakaunganidza imba yose yaJudha, nerudzi rwaBhenjamini, varume zvuru zana nemakumi masere vakasarudzwa, vairwa hondo, kurwa neimba yaIsraeri, kudzosera ushe kuna Rehobhoamu mwanakomana waSoromoni.
\v	22	Asi shoko raMwari rakasvika kuna Shemaya munhu waMwari richiti:
\v	23	Taura kuna Rehobhoamu, mwanakomana waSoromoni, mambo waJudha, nekuimba yose yaJudha ne yaBhenjamini, ne kumusiiwa wevanhu, uchiti:
\v	24	Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Hamungakwiri uye hamungarwi nehama dzenyu vana vaIsraeri; dzokerai umwe neumwe kumba kwake, nokuti chinhu ichi chinobva kwandiri. Zvino vakateerera kushoko raJehovha, ndokudzokera kuenda zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha.
\p
\v	25	Zvino Jerobhoamu wakavaka Shekemu pagomo raEfuremu ndokugaramo; ndokubudamo ndokuvaka Penueri.
\v	26	Zvino Jerobhoamu wakati mumoyo make: Ikozvino ushe huchadzokera kuimba yaDhavhidhi.
\v	27	Kana vanhu ava vakakwira kunoita zvibairo mumba maJehovha paJerusarema, ipapo moyo wevanhu ava uchadzokera kuna ishe wavo, kuna Rehobhoamu mambo waJudha; uye vachandiuraya vakadzokera kuna Rehobhoamu mambo waJudha.
\v	28	Zvino mambo wakatora zano ndokuita mhuru mbiri dzegoridhe, ndokuti kwavari: Zvakakunyanyirai zvikuru kukwira kuJerusarema; tarirai vamwari venyu, Israeri, vakakukwidzai kubva panyika yeEgipita.
\v	29	Zvino wakaisa imwe paBheteri, uye imwe wakaimisa paDhani.
\v	30	Chinhu ichi ndokuva chivi, nokuti vanhu vakaenda pamberi peimwe kusvika kuDhani.
\v	31	Wakaitawo imba yenzvimbo dzakakwirira, ndokuita vapristi kubva kumigumo yevanhu  vese vaiva vasiri veimba yaRevhi.
\v	32	Zvino Jerobhoamu wakaita mutambo pamwedzi werusere, nezuva regumi nemashanu remwedzi, semutambo waiva kwaJudha, ndokupa paaritari. Ndizvo zvaakaita paBheteri, kubaira mhuru dzaakaita; uye wakaisa paBheteri vapristi venzvimbo dzakakwirira dzaaiva aita.
\v	33	Naizvozvo wakapa paaritari yaaiva aita paBheteri nezuva regumi nemashanu mumwedzi werusere, mumwedzi waaiva afunga uchibva mumoyo make; ndokuitira vana vaIsraeri mutambo, ndokupa pamusoro pearitari kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\c	13
\s	Jerobhoamu anorohwa naJehovha paaritari
\p
\v	1	Zvinotarira, kwakauya munhu waMwari kubva kwaJudha neshoko raJehovha kuBheteri; zvino Jerobhoamu waiva amire pedo nearitari kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	2	Iyendokudanidzira kuaritari neshoko raJehovha ndokuti: Aritari, aritari, ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Tarira, mwanakomana achaberekerwa imba yaDhavhidhi, ndiJosiya zita rake; achabaira pamusoro pako vapristi venzvimbo dzakakwirira vanopisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pamusoro pako, uye mapfupa evanhu achapisirwa pamusoro pako.
\v	3	Zvinowakapa nezuva iro chiratidzo achiti: Ichi chiratidzo Jehovha chaataura: Tarira aritari inodamburwa uye madota ari pamusoro payo achadurukira  pasi.
\v	4	Zvinozvakaitika mambo paakanzwa shoko remunhu waMwari wakadanidzira kuaritari paBheteri, kuti Jerobhoamu wakatandavadza ruoko rwake akarubvisa paaritari achiti: Mubatei! Zvino ruoko rwake rwaakatandavadzira kwaari rwakaoma, zvekuti wakakonewa kurwudzosera kwaari.
\v	5	Aritariwoyakadamburwa, nemadota akadurukira  pasi kubva paaritari, zvichienderana nechiratidzo munhu waMwari chaaiva apa neshoko raJehovha.
\v	6	Zvinomambo wakapindura ndokuti kumunhu waMwari: Kumbira hako chiso chaJehovha Mwari wako, uye undinyengeterere, kuti ruoko rwangu rwudzokere kwandiri. Munhu waMwari ndokukumbira chiso chaJehovha, ruoko rwamambo ndokudzokera kwaari, rwukava sepakutanga.
\v	7	Zvinomambo wakati kumunhu waMwari: Enda neni tiende kumba, uzvivandudze, uye ndichakupa mubairo.
\v	8	Asimunhu waMwari wakati kuna mambo: Kunyange mukandipa hafu yeimba yenyu, handingaendi nemwi uye handingadyi chingwa kana kunwa mvura munzvimbo ino.
\v	9	Nokutisaizvozvo wakandiraira neshoko raJehovha achiti: Usadya chingwa uye usanwa mvura uye usadzoka nenzira yawaenda nayo.
\v	10	Naizvozvo wakaenda neimwe nzira akasadzokera nenzira yaakauya nayo kuBheteri.
\s	Muporofita asina kuteerera anourawa neshumba
\p
\v	11	Zvino kwaigara muporofita mutana paBheteri; mwanakomana wake ndokuuya ndokumuudza basa rese munhu waMwari raakaita zuva iroro paBheteri; mashoko aakataura kuna mambo, naiwo vakaaudza baba vavo.
\v	12	Baba vavo ndokuti kwavari: Waenda nenzira ipi? Nokuti vanakomana vake vaiva vaona nzira munhu waMwari wabva kwaJudha yaakafamba nayo.
\v	13	Zvino wakati kuvanakomana vake: Ndisungirei chigaro pambongoro; zvino vakamusungira chigaro pambongoro; iye ndokuitasva.
\v	14	Ndokutevera munhu waMwari, ndokumuwana agere pazasi pemuoki, ndokuti kwaari: Ndiwe munhu waMwari abva kwaJudha here? Iye ndokuti: Ndini.
\v	15	Iye ndokuti kwaari: Hendei neni tiende kumba, unodya chingwa.
\v	16	Iye ndokuti: Handigoni kudzokera nemwi kana kupinda nemwi, uye handingadyi chingwa uye handinganwi mvura nemwi panzvimbo iyi.
\v	17	Nokuti shoko kwandiri neshoko raJehovha  rakati: Haungadyi chingwa uye haunganwi mvurapo, haungadzokeri kuenda nenzira yawakauya nayo.
\v	18	Zvino wakati kwaari: Neni ndiri muporofita sewe; zvino mutumwa wataura kwandiri neshoko raJehovha, achiti: Mudzose kumba kwako newe kuti adye chingwa uye anwe mvura. Akataura nhema kwaari.
\v	19	Naizvozvo wakadzokera naye ndokudya chingwa mumba make ndokunwa mvura.
\p
\v	20	Zvino zvakaitika vagere patafura, kuti shoko raJehovha rikasvika kumuporofita wakamudzosa.
\v	21	Akadanidzira kumunhu waMwari, waiva abva kwaJudha, achiti: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Zvausina kuteerera muromo waJehovha, uye zvausina kuchengeta murairo Jehovha Mwari wako waakakuraira,
\v	22	asi wadzokera uye wadya chingwa nekunwa mvura munzvimbo yaakati nezvayo kwauri: Usadya chingwa uye usanwa mvura; chitunha chako hachingasviki kuguva remadzibaba ako.
\p
\v	23	Zvino zvakaitika shure kwekudya kwake chingwa uye shure kwekunwa kwake, kuti wakamusungirira chigaro pambongoro iye muporofita waiva amudzosa.
\v	24	Wakati aenda, shumba yakasangana naye panzira, ndokumuuraya; chitunha chake ndokukandirwa munzira, mbongoro ndokumira parutivi rwacho, shumba ndokumira parutivi rwechitunha.
\v	25	Zvino tarira, vanhu vakapfuura ndokuona chitunha chakakandirwa munzira, uye shumba imire parutivi rwechitunha. Zvino vakasvika ndokutaura muguta, maiva mugere muporofita mutana.
\v	26	Zvino muporofita wakamudzosa panzira paakanzwa, wakati: Ndiye munhu waMwari asina kuteerera muromo waJehovha; naizvozvo Jehovha wakamupa kushumba, yamubvambura ndokumuuraya, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha raakataura kwaari.
\v	27	Zvino wakataura kuvanakomana vake achiti: Ndisungirirei chigaro pambongoro; vaka isungira chigaro.
\v	28	Zvino wakaenda ndokuwana chitunha chake chakakandirwa munzira, uye mbongoro neshumba zvimire parutivi rwechitunha; shumba yaiva isina kudya chitunha uye yaiva isina kupwanya mbongoro.
\v	29	Zvino muporofita wakasimudza chitunha chemunhu waMwari ndokuchiisa pamusoro pembongoro ndokuchidzosa; muporofita mutana ndokusvika muguta ku muchema nekumuviga.
\v	30	Zvino wakaisa chitunha chake muguva rake ndokumuchema: Maiwee, munin'ina wangu!
\v	31	Zvino zvakaitika shure kwekumuviga, kuti wakataura kuvanakomana vake, achiti: Pakufa kwangu, mundivige muguva makavigwa munhu waMwari; muise mapfupa angu parutivi rwemapfupa ake.
\v	32	Nokuti shoko richaitika zvirokwazvo raakadanidzira neshoko raJehovha kuaritari paBheteri, nekudzimba dzose dzenzvimbo dzakakwirira ari pamaguta eSamariya.
\p
\v	33	Shure kwechinhu ichi Jerobhoamu haana kutendeuka panzira yake yakaipa, asi wakaitazve vapristi venzvimbo dzakakwirira pavanhu kubva kumigumo; anoda wakamutsaura, ndokuva  umwe wevapristi venzvimbo dzakakwirira.
\v	34	Zvino nechinhu ichi chakava chivi paimba yaJerobhoamu, kunyange ku igura neku iparadza ibve pachiso chenyika.
\c	14
\s	Zvivi zvaJerobhoamu naRehobhoamu nekurohwa kwavo
\p
\v	1	Panguvaiyo Abhija mwanakomana waJerobhoamu wakarwara.
\v	2	Jerobhoamundokuti kumukadzi wake: Simuka hako, ushandure chimiro, kuti vasaziva kuti uri mukadzi waJerobhoamu, ugoenda kuShiro; tarira, pana Ahija muporofita wakataura maererano neni kuva mambo pamusoro pevanhu ava.
\v	3	Zvinotora muruoko rwako zvingwa gumi nezvimedu zvakaoma nedende reuchi ugoenda kwaari; iye achakuudza zvichaitika kumwana.
\v	4	MukadziwaJerobhoamu ndokuita saizvozvo, ndokusimuka ndokuenda kuShiro, ndokusvika paimba yaAhija. Asi Ahija waiva asingagoni kuona nokuti meso ake aiva amira nekuda kwekukwegura kwake.
\p
\v	5	ZvinoJehovha wakati kuna Ahija: Tarira, mukadzi waJerobhoamu anouya kuzokubvunza chinhu pamusoro pemwanakomana wake, nokuti anorwara; uchamuudza zvekuti nezvekuti, nokuti zvichaitika pakupinda kwake, kuti achazviita umwe mukadzi.
\v	6	Zvinozvakaitika pakunzwa kwaAhija mutsindo wetsoka dzake, achipinda pamukova, kuti wakati: Pinda mukadzi waJerobhoamu, unozviidzirei umwe? Nokuti ndatumwa kwauri nechinhu chinorema.
\v	7	Enda,uudze Jerobhoamu: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri: Zvandakakusimudza pakati pevanhu, ndikakuita mutungamiriri pamusoro pevanhu vangu Israeri,
\v	8	ndokubvaruraushe kubva kuimba yaDhavhidhi, ndikahupa iwe; asi iwe hauna kuva semuranda wangu Dhavhidhi waichengeta mirairo yangu uye wakanditevera nemoyo wake wese kuita zvega zvakanaka pameso angu;
\v	9	asiwakaita zvakaipa kuita zvakapfuura vese vaiva mberi kwake, uye wakaenda ukazviitira vamwe vamwari nemifananidzo yakaumbwa nekunyungudusa, kuti unditsamwise; uye ini wakandirasira shure kwemusana wako.
\v	10	Naizvozvo, tarira, ndichauisa chakaipa paimba yaJerobhoamu; uye ndichagura kubva kuna Jerobhoamu anowetera pamadziro, wakavharirwa newakasununguka kwaIsraeri, uye ndidzime vanotevera veimba yaJerobhoamu, semunhu anodzima mupfudze kusvika wapera.
\v	11	Anofa kwaJerobhoamu mukati meguta imbwa dzichamudya; neanofira musango shiri dzematenga dzichamudya; nokuti Jehovha wakataura.
\v	12	Naizvozvo iwe simuka uende kumba kwako; pakupinda kwetsoka dzako muguta, mwana achafa.
\v	13	Zvino Israeri wese achamuchema ndokumuviga; nokuti ndiye ega waJerobhoamu achasvika paguva, nokuti maari makawanikwa chinhu chakanaka kuna Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri paimba yaJerobhoamu.
\p
\v	14	Uye Jehovha achazvimutsira mambo pamusoro paIsraeri, iye achagura imba yaJerobhoamu zuva iro. Asi chii? Kunyange ikozvino.
\v	15	Nokuti Jehovha acharova Israeri, serutsanga rwuchizununguswa mumvura; iye achadzura Israeri kubva panyika ino yakanaka, yaakapa kumadzibaba avo, nekuvaparadzira mhiri kwerwizi; nokuti vakaita matanda avo ekunamata nawo vachitsamwisa Jehovha.
\v	16	Zvino achapa Israeri nekuda kwezvivi zvaJerobhoamu zvaakatadza, uye zvaakatadzisa nazvo Israeri.
\p
\v	17	Zvino mukadzi waJerobhoamu wakasimuka, ndokuenda, ndokusvika paTiriza. Paakasvika pachikumbaridzo cheimba, mwana wakafa.
\v	18	Zvino vakamuviga, Israeri wese ndokumuchema, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha raakataura neruoko rwemuranda wake Ahija muporofita.
\p
\v	19	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaJerobhoamu, kuti wakarwa sei, uye kuti wakatonga sei, tarira, zvakanyorwa mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri.
\v	20	Zvino mazuva Jerobhoamu aakatonga aiva makore makumi maviri nemaviri; ndokurara nemadzibaba ake; Nadhabhi mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\p
\v	21	Zvino Rehobhoamu mwanakomana waSoromoni wakatonga kwaJudha. Rehobhoamu waiva wemakore makumi mana nerimwe paakava mambo, ndokutonga makore gumi nemanomwe paJerusarema, guta Jehovha raaiva asarudza pakati pemarudzi ese aIsraeri kuisa zita rakepo. Zvino zita ramai vake raiva Naama muAmoni.
\v	22	Zvino Judha wakaita chakaipa pameso aJehovha; ndokumumutsira godo nezvivi zvavo zvavakatadza, kupfuura zvese madzibaba avo zvaakaita, nezvivi zvavo zvavakatadza.
\v	23	Nokuti naivo vakazvivakira nzvimbo dzakakwirira, neshongwe dzakamira, nematanda ekunamata nawo, pamusoro pechikomo chese chakakwirira, nepazasi pemuti umwe neumwe munyoro.
\v	24	Zvino kwaivawo nengochani panyika; vakaita zvinoenderana nezvinyangadzo zvese zvemarudzi Jehovha aakadzingira kubva pamberi pevana vaIsraeri.
\p
\v	25	Zvino zvakaitika mugore rechishanu ramambo Rehobhoamu, Shishaki mambo weEgipita wakakwira kurwa neJerusarema.

\v	21	Zvino Eria wakaswedera kuvanhu vose ndokuti: Kusvika rinhi muchikamhina pamifungo miviri? Kana Jehovha ari Mwari, mutevere iye, asi kana ari Bhaari, mumutevere. Asi vanhu havana kumupindura shoko.
\v	22	Eria ndokuti kuvanhu: Ini ndoga ndini muporofita waJehovha wasara, asi vaporofita vaBhaari varume mazana mana nemakumi mashanu.
\v	23	Naizvozvo ngavatipe nzombe diki mbiri, uye ngavazvitsaurire nzombe diki imwe, vaigura kuita nhindi, vagoiisa pamusoro pehuni, vagorega kuisa moto; neni ndichagadzira imwe nzombe diki ndokuisa pamusoro pehuni, ndigosaisa moto.
\v	24	Zvino mudane zita ramwari wenyu, ini ndidanewo zita raJehovha; uye Mwari anopindura nemoto ndiye Mwari. Vanhu vose ndokupindura ndokuti: Shoko rakanaka.
\p
\v	25	Zvino Eria wakati kuvaporofita vaBhaari: Zvisarudzirei nzombe diki imwe mutange kugadzira, nokuti muri vazhinji; mugodana zita ramwari wenyu, asi musaisa moto.
\v	26	Zvino vakatora nzombe diki yavakapiwa, ndoku igadzira, ndokudana zita raBhaari kubva mangwanani kusvika masikati makuru, vachiti: Bhaari, tipindure! Asi hakuna kuva neinzwi, hakuna kuva neanopindura. Zvino vakakwakuka pamusoro pearitari yaiva yakaitwa.
\v	27	Zvino zvakaitika pamasikati makuru, kuti Eria wakavasveveredza, ndokuti: Danidzirai nenzwi guru; nokuti ndimwari, nokuti anofunganya, kana kuti wambosuduruka, kana kuti ari parwendo, zvimwe arere uye kuti amutswe.
\v	28	Zvino vakadanidzira nenzwi guru, ndokuzvicheka zvichitevera tsika yavo neminondo nemapfumo, kusvika ropa radzatuka pavari.
\v	29	Zvino zvakaitika masikati makuru apfuura, kuti vakaporofita kusvika  panguva yekupa yechipo; asi hakuna kuva nenzwi, uye hakuna kuva neanopindura, uye hakuna kuva neane hanya.
\p
\v	30	Zvino Eria wakati kuvanhu vese: Swederai kwandiri. Vanhu vose ndokuswedera kwaari. Zvino wakagadzirisa aritari yaJehovha yaiva yakaputsika.
\v	31	Eria ndokutora mabwe gumi nemaviri zvichienderana neuwandu hwemarudzi evanakomana vaJakove uyo kwaari kwakauya shoko raJehovha richiti: Zita rako richava Israeri.
\v	32	Zvino nemabwe awa wakavaka aritari nezita raJehovha; ndokuita mugero zvichienderana nechiyero chemaseya maviri embeu kupoteredza aritari.
\v	33	Zvino wakaronga huni, ndokugura nzombe diki kuita nhindi, ndokuisa pamusoro pehuni, ndokuti: Zadzai zvirongo zvina nemvura, mugodira pamusoro pechipo chinopiswa, nepamusoro pehuni.
\v	34	Zvino wakati: Itai rwechipiri; ivo ndokuita rwechipiri. Zvino wakati: Itai rwechitatu; vakaita rwechitatu.
\v	35	Mvura ndokufamba kupoteredza aritari; akazadzawo mugero nemvura.
\p
\v	36	Zvino zvakaitika pakupa chipo, kuti Eria muporofita wakaswedera, ndokuti: Jehovha Mwari waAbhurahama, Isaka, naIsraeri, ngazvizikanwe nhasi kuti muri Mwari kwaIsraeri, neni muranda wenyu, uye ndakaita zvinhu izvi zvose neshoko renyu.
\v	37	Ndipindurei Jehovha, ndipindurei, kuti vanhu ava vazive kuti imwi muri Jehovha Mwari, uye kuti imwi matendeuka kudzora moyo wavo.
\v	38	Zvino moto waJehovha wakawa, ndokupedza chipo chinopiswa, nehuni, nemabwe, neguruva, ukananzva mvura yaiva mumugero.
\v	39	Vanhu vose pavakaona vakawa nezviso zvavo ndokuti: Jehovha ndiye Mwari, Jehovha ndiye Mwari!
\v	40	Eria ndokuti kwavari: Batai vaporofita vaBhaari, ngakurege kuva neumwe wavo anopukunyuka. Zvino vakavabata; Eria ndokuvaburusira kurukova Kishoni, ndokuvaurairapo.
\s	Kupera kwenzara
\p
\v	41	Zvino Eria wakati kuna Ahabhi: Simuka udye unwe, nokuti kune ruzha rwekuunga kwemvura.
\v	42	Zvino Ahabhi wakakwira kunodya nekunwa. Eria ndokukwira kunhongonya yeKarimeri, akakotamira pasi, akaisa chiso chake pakati pemabvi ake.
\v	43	Ndokuti kumuranda wake: Kwira ikozvino, tarira nechekugungwa. Zvino wakakwira ndokutarira ndokuti: Hakuna chinhu. Iye ndokuti: Dzokera; kanomwe.
\v	44	Zvino zvakaitika kechinomwe kuti wakati: Tarirai, kakore kakaita sechanza chemunhu kanokwira kubva mugungwa. Iye ndokuti: Kwira unoudza Ahabhi: Sunga  ngoro uburuke, zvimwe mvura ingakudzivirira.
\v	45	Zvino zvakaitika munguva diki kuti matenga akasviba, makore makobvu nemhepo, uye kwaiva nemvura zhinji. Ahabhi ndokukwira  ngoro, ndokuenda kuna Jezireeri.
\v	46	Zvino ruoko rwaJehovha rwaiva pamusoro paEria; iye ndokusunga chiuno chake, ndokumhanya mberi kwaAhabhi kusvika pakupinda Jezireeri.
\c	19
\s	Eria pagomo reHorebhu
\p
\v	1	ZvinoAhabhi wakaudza Jezebheri zvose Eria zvaaiva aita, nevose vaakauraya, vaporofita vose nemunondo.
\v	2	ZvinoJezebheri wakatuma nhume kuna Eria achiti: Ngavaite saizvozvo vamwari, ngavawedzere saizvozvo, zvirokwazvo nenguva inenge ino mangwana ndikaita upenyu hwako huve seupenyu hweumwe wavo.
\v	3	Wakatiaona  izvozvo wakasimuka ndokuenda nekuda kweupenyu hwake, ndokusvika paBheeri-Shebha rinova raJudha, ndokusiyapo muranda wake.
\p
\v	4	Asiiye wakafamba rwendo rwezuva kurenje, ndokusvika ndokugara pazasi peumwe muti wemurotemu, ndokukumbira kuti mweya wake ufe, ndokuti: Zvaringana; ikozvino Jehovha torai henyu upenyu hwangu, nokuti handisi nani kumadzibaba angu.
\v	5	Zvinowakati akatsivama akarara pazasi peumwe muti wemurotemu; zvino tarira, ipapo mutumwa wakamugunzva ndokuti kwaari: Simuka, udye.
\v	6	Zvinowakacherekedza, zvino tarira, panzvimbo yemusoro wake bundu rekeke rakabhekewa pamazimbe uye guchu remvura. Zvino wakadya ndokunwa, ndokurarazve.
\v	7	Zvinomutumwa waJehovha wakasvika rwechipiri ndokumugunzva, ndokuti: Simuka udye, nokuti rwendo rukurusa kwauri.
\v	8	Zvinowakasimuka ndokudya ndokunwa, ndokufamba nesimba rezvekudya izvo mazuva makumi mana neusiku makumi mana kusvika paHorebhu, gomo raMwari.
\p
\v	9	Zvinowakapindapo mubako ndokuraramo usiku. Zvino tarira, inzwi raJehovha rakasvika kwaari rikati kwaari: Sei uri pano Eria?
\v	10	Zvino wakati: Ndanga ndichishingairira zvekushingairira Jehovha Mwari wehondo, nokuti vana vaIsraeri vakasiya sungano yenyu, vakaputsa aritari dzenyu, uye vakauraya vaporofita venyu nemunondo; uye ndini ndega ndasara; zvino vanotsvaka upenyu hwangu kuhutora.
\v	11	Iye ndokuti: Buda umire pagomo pamberi paJehovha. Zvino tarira, Jehovha anopfuura, uye mhepo huru ine simba yakabvarura makomo ndokuputsanya mabwe pamberi paJehovha; Jehovha waiva asiri mumhepo. Zvino shure kwemhepo kudengenyeka kwenyika; Jehovha waiva asiri mukudengenyeka kwenyika.
\v	12	Uye shure kwekudengenyeka kwenyika moto; Jehovha waiva asiri mumoto; zvino shure kwemoto inzwi diki rakadzikama.
\v	13	Zvino zvakaitika Eria paakanzwa, wakamonera chiso chake nenguvo yake ndokubuda ndokumira pamuromo webako. Zvino tarira, inzwi kwaari uye rakati: Sei uri pano, Eria?
\v	14	Iye ndokuti: Ndaishingairira zvekushingairira Jehovha Mwari wehondo, nokuti vana vaIsraeri vakasiya sungano yenyu, vakaputsa aritari dzenyu, uye vakauraya vaporofita venyu nemunondo; uye ndini ndega ndasara; zvino vanotsvaka upenyu hwangu kuhutora.
\v	15	Zvino Jehovha wakati kwaari: Enda, udzokere nenzira yako kurenje reDhamasiko; paunenge wasvika uzodze Hazaeri ave mambo pamusoro peSiriya.
\v	16	Uye Jehu mwanakomana waNimishi uchamuzodza kuva mambo pamusoro paIsraeri. Uye Erisha mwanakomana waShafati weAbheri-Mehora uchamuzodza kuva muporofita pachinzvimbo chako.
\v	17	Zvino zvichaitika kuti anopukunyuka pamunondo waHazaeri, Jehu achauraya; neanopukunyuka pamunondo waJehu, Erisha achauraya.
\v	18	Zvakadaro ndasiya kwaIsraeri zvuru zvinomwe, mabvi ose asina kufugamira Bhaari, nemuromo wose usina kumutsvoda.
\p
\v	19	Zvino wakabvapo ndokuwana Erisha mwanakomana waShafati, wairima nezvipani gumi nezviviri pamberi pake, iye ane chegumi nezviviri. Eria ndokupfuura nekwaari, ndokukanda nguvo yake pamusoro pake.
\v	20	Zvino wakasiya nzombe, ndokumhanya achitevera Eria, ndokuti: Regai henyu nditsvode baba vangu namai vangu ndigokuteverai. Iye ndokuti kwaari: Enda udzokere, nokuti ndaitei kwauri?
\v	21	Zvino wakadzoka pakumutevera, ndokutora chipani chenzombe, ndokuchibaya, akabika nenhumbi dzenzombe nyama yadzo, ndokupa vanhu, ivo ndokudya. Zvino wakasimuka, ndokutevera Eria, ndokumushandira.
\c	2
\s	Kufa kwaDhavhidhi
\p
\v	1	Zvinomazuva aDhavhidhi akaswedera kuti afe. Zvino wakaraira Soromoni mwanakomana wake achiti:
\v	2	Ndinoendanenzira yenyika yese; naizvozvo simba, uve murume.
\v	3	Uyechengeta zvawakarairwa naJehovha Mwari wako, kuti ufambe nenzira dzake, uchichengeta zvimiso zvake, mirairo yake, nemitongo yake, nezvipupuro zvake, sezvazvakanyorwa pamurairo waMozisi, kuti ubudirire pazvose zvaunoita, uye kose kwaunotendeukira.
\v	4	KutiJehovha asimbise shoko rake raakataura maererano neni, achiti: Kana vanakomana vako vakachenjera panzira yavo, kufamba pamberi pangu muchokwadi nemoyo wavo wose uye nemweya weupenyu wavo wose, achiti: Hakungagurwi kubva kwauri murume pachigaro cheushe chaIsraeri.
\v	5	Uyezveiwe unoziva Joabhu mwanakomana waZeruya zvaakandiitira, zvaakaita kuvakuru vaviri vehondo dzaIsraeri, kuna Abhineri mwanakomana waNeri, nekuna Amasa mwanakomana waJeteri, vaakauraya ndokuteura ropa rehondo parugare, wakaisa ropa rehondo pabhanhire rake raiva pachiuno chake, nepashangu dzake dzaiva patsoka dzake.
\v	6	Naizvozvoita zvinoenderana neuchenjeri hwako, uye usarega musoro wake wachena uchiburukira muguva nerugare.
\v	7	Asiuitire unyoro vanakomana vaBharizirai muGiriyadhi, uye ngavave pakati pevanodya patafura yako; nokuti saizvozvo vakaswedera kwandiri pakutiza kwangu pachiso chaAbhusaromu mukoma wako.
\v	8	Uyetarira, kwauri kuna Shimei mwanakomana waGera muBhenjamini weBhahurimi, wakandituka nekutuka kunorwadza pazuva randaienda kuMahanayimu; asi iye wakauya kundichingamidza paJoridhani, zvino ndakamupikira naJehovha ndichiti: Handingakuurayi nemunondo.
\v	9	Naizvozvozvino usamurega ari asina mhosva, nokuti uri munhu wakachenjera; uye unoziva zvauchamuitira, uye uchaburusira musoro wake wachena une ropa muguva.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakarara nemadzibaba ake, uye wakavigwa muguta raDhavhidhi.
\v	11	Zvino mazuva Dhavhidhi aakatonga pamusoro paIsraeri aiva makore makumi mana. Makore manomwe wakatonga paHebhuroni, uye makore makumi matatu nematatu wakatonga paJerusarema.
\s	Soromoni anosimbisa umambo hwake
\p
\v	12	Zvino Soromoni wakagara pachigaro cheushe chababa vake Dhavhidhi. Ushe hwake ndokusimbiswa kwazvo.
\p
\v	13	Zvino Adhonia mwanakomana waHagiti wakasvika kuna Bhati-Shebha mai vaSoromoni;  ivo ndokuti: Unouya  nerugare here? Iye ndokuti:  Nerugare.
\v	14	Iye ndokuti: Ndine shoko kwamuri. Ivo ndokuti: Taura.
\v	15	Zvino wakati: Imwi munoziva kuti ushe hwaiva hwangu, uye Israeri wose waiva akaisa zviso zvavo kwandiri kuti ndichatonga; asi ushe hwakashandurwa hukazova hwemunin'ina wangu; nokuti hwaiva hwake kubva kuna Jehovha.
\v	16	Zvino ikozvino ndinokumbira chikumbiro chimwe kwamuri; musafuratidza chiso changu. Ivo ndokuti kwaari: Taura.
\v	17	Iye ndokuti: Taurai henyu naSoromoni mambo (nokuti haangafuratidzi chiso chenyu) kuti andipe Abhishagi muShunami ave mukadzi  wangu.
\v	18	Bhati-Shebha ndokuti: Zvakanaka, ini ndichakutaurira kuna mambo.
\p
\v	19	Naizvozvo Bhati-Shebha wakaenda kuna mambo Soromoni kunotaurira Adhonia kwaari. Mambo ndokusimuka kumuchingamidza, ndokumukotamira ndokugara pachigaro chake cheushe, ndokuti kuisirwe mai vamambo chigaro; ivo vakagara kuruoko rwake rwerudyi.
\v	20	Zvino vakati: Ndinokumbira chikumbiro chimwe chidiki kwauri; usafuratidza chiso changu. Mambo ndokuti kwavari: Kumbirai, mai vangu, nokuti handingafuratidzi chiso chenyu.
\v	21	Zvino vakati: Abhishagi muShunami ngaapiwe Adhonia mukuru wako ave mukadzi  wake.
\v	22	Zvino mambo Soromoni wakapindura ndokuti kuna mai vake: Uye munokumbirirei Adhonia Abhishagi muShunami? Mumukumbirirewo ushe, nokuti iye mukoma wangu ari mukuru kwandiri; hongu, iye, naAbhiatari mupristi, naJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya.
\v	23	Mambo Soromoni ndokupika naJehovha achiti: Mwari ngaaite saizvozvo kwandiri uye awedzere saizvozvo, zvirokwazvo Adhonia wataura shoko iri kurwisana neupenyu hwake.
\v	24	Naizvozvo zvino,  naJehovha mupenyu, iye wakandisimbisa, ndokundigadza pachigaro cheushe chababa vangu Dhavhidhi, uye wakandiitira imba sezvaakataura, zvirokwazvo, Adhonia uchaurawa nhasi.
\v	25	Mambo Soromoni ndokutuma neruoko rwaBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, iye akamuwira, ndokufa.
\p
\v	26	Uye kuna Abhiatari mupristi, mambo wakati: Enda kuAnatoti kuminda yako, nokuti uri munhu wekufa; asi handingakuurayi nhasi, nokuti waitakura areka yaShe Jehovha pamberi pababa vangu Dhavhidhi, uye nokuti wakatambudzika pazvose baba vangu zvavakatambudzika nazvo.
\v	27	Naizvozvo Soromoni akadzinga Abhiatari pakuva mupristi waJehovha; kuzadzisa shoko raJehovha raakataura maererano neimba yaEri paShiro.
\p
\v	28	Zvino shoko rakasvika kuna Joabhu, nokuti Joabhu waiva atendeuka kutevera Adhonia, kunyange asina kutendeuka kutevera Abhusaromu. Joabhu ndokutizira kutende raJehovha, ndokubatirira panyanga dzearitari.
\v	29	Zvino kwakaudzwa mambo Soromoni kuti Joabhu watizira kutende raJehovha; zvino tarirai,  ari parutivi rwearitari. Soromoni ndokutuma Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha achiti: Enda unomuwira.
\v	30	Bhenaya ndokuenda kutende raJehovha, ndokuti kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva mambo: Budai! Iye ndokuti: Kwete, asi ndichafira pano. Bhenaya ndokudzosera shoko kuna mambo achiti: Ndizvo zvataura Joabhu, uye ndizvo zvaandipindura.
\v	31	Mambo ndokuti kwaari: Ita sezvaataura, umuwire, ugomuviga  muguva; kuti ubvise kwandiri nepaimba yababa vangu ropa risina mhosva Joabhu raakateura.
\v	32	Uye Jehovha achadzosera ropa rake pamusoro wake, nokuti wakawira varume vaviri vaiva vakarurama uye vari nani kumupfuura ndokuvauraya nemunondo, uye baba vangu Dhavhidhi vasingazvizivi: Abhineri mwanakomana waNeri mukuru wehondo yaIsraeri, naAmasa mwanakomana waJeteri mukuru wehondo yaJudha.
\v	33	Naizvozvo ropa ravo richadzokera pamusoro waJoabhu nepamusoro wembeu yake nekusingaperi; asi kuna Dhavhidhi nekumbeu yake nekuimba yake nekuchigaro chake cheushe kuchava nerugare kusvika rinhi narinhi rwunobva kuna Jehovha.
\v	34	Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha ndokukwira ndokumuwira ndokumuuraya. Zvino wakavigwa muimba yake murenje.
\v	35	Zvino mambo wakaisa Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha pachinzvimbo chake pamusoro pehondo; uye Zadhoki mupristi mambo wakamuisa pachinzvimbo chaAbhiatari.
\p
\v	36	Mambo ndokutuma  nhume ndokudana Shimei, ndokuti kwaari: Uzvivakire imba paJerusarema, uye ugareko, usabudapo kuenda kupi nekupi.
\v	37	Nokuti kuchati nezuva rekubuda kwako ndokuyambuka rukova Kidhironi, ziva zvirokwazvo kuti uchafa zvekufa; ropa rako richava pamusoro pemusoro wako.
\v	38	Shimei ndokuti kuna mambo: Shoko iri rakanaka; sezvareva ishe wangu mambo, muranda wenyu achaita saizvozvo. Shimei ndokugara paJerusarema mazuva mazhinji.
\v	39	Zvino zvakaitika pakupera kwemakore matatu kuti vaviri vevaranda vaShimei vakatizira kuna Akishi mwanakomana waMaaka mambo weGati. Zvino vakaudza Shimei vachiti: Tarira varanda vako vari paGati.
\v	40	Zvino Shimei wakasimuka ndokusungirira chigaro pambongoro yake, ndokuenda kuGati kuna Akishi kunotsvaka varanda vake. Shimei ndokuenda ndokuunza varanda vake kubva paGati.
\v	41	Zvino zvakaudzwa Soromoni kuti Shimei wakaenda kubva Jerusarema kuenda Gati, uye wakadzoka.
\v	42	Mambo ndokutuma  nhume ndokudana Shimei, ndokuti kwaari: Handina kukupikisa naJehovha here, ndikapupura kwauri ndichiti: Nezuva rekubuda kwako ukaenda kupi nekupi, uzive zvirokwazvo kuti uchafa zvekufa? Uye iwe wakati kwandiri: Shoko randanzwa rakanaka.
\v	43	Zvino sei usina kuchengeta mhiko yaJehovha, nemurairo wandakaraira pamusoro pako?
\v	44	Mambo wakatiwo kuna Shimei: Iwe unoziva kuipa kose moyo wako kwaunoziva kwawakaita kuna Dhavhidhi baba vangu; naizvozvo Jehovha achadzosera kuipa kwako pamusoro wako.
\v	45	Asi mambo Soromoni acharopafadzwa, nechigaro cheushe chaDhavhidhi chichasimbiswa pamberi paJehovha kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	46	Naizvozvo mambo wakaraira Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha; iye wakabuda ndokumuwira zvekuti wakafa. Naizvozvo ushe hwakasimbiswa muruoko rwaSoromoni.

\s	Kurwa pakati paAhabhi naBheni-Hadhadhi, mambo weSiriya
\p
\v	1	ZvinoBheni-Hadhadhi, mambo weSiriya, wakaunganidza hondo yake yose. Zvino kwaiva nemadzimambo makumi matatu nemaviri pamwe naye, nemabhiza nengoro; ndokukwira ndokukomba Samariya, ndokurwa naro.
\v	2	Zvinowakatuma nhume kuna Ahabhi mambo waIsraeri muguta, ndokuti kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Bheni-Hadhadhi:
\v	3	Sirivhayako negoridhe rako ndezvangu, nevakadzi vako nevana vako vakanakisisa ndevangu.
\v	4	Zvinomambo waIsraeri akapindura ndokuti: Zvichienderana neshoko renyu, ishe wangu mambo; ndiri wenyu, nezvose zvandinazvo.
\v	5	Nhumedzikadzoka ndokuti: Ndizvo zvinoreva Bheni-Hadhadhi achiti: Kunyange ndakatuma kwauri ndichiti: Sirivha yako negoridhe rako, nevakadzi vako nevana vako, uzvipe kwandiri;
\v	6	zvakadarondichatuma varanda vangu kwauri mangwana nenguva inenge ino; vachanzvera imba yako nedzimba dzevaranda vako; uye zvichaitika kuti chinhu chose chinodikanwa pameso ako vachaisa muruoko rwavo ndokutora.
\v	7	Zvinomambo waIsraeri wakadana vakuru vose venyika, ndokuti: Cherekedzai henyu muone kuti munhu uyu anotsvaka chakaipa; nokuti wakatuma kwandiri zvevakadzi vangu nezvevana vangu nezvesirivha yangu nezvegoridhe rangu, ndikasamunyima.
\v	8	Zvinovakuru vose nevanhu vose vakati kwaari: Usateerera uye usabvuma.
\v	9	Naizvozvowakati kunhume dzaBheni-Hadhadhi: Udzai ishe wangu mambo: Zvose zvamakatuma kumuranda wenyu pakutanga ndichaita, asi chinhu ichi handikwanisi kuita. Zvino nhume dzakaenda, ndokumudzosera shoko.
\v	10	Zvino Bheni-Hadhadhi wakatuma kwaari ndokuti: Vamwari ngavaite saizvozvo kwandiri, vawedzere saizvozvo, kana guruva reSamariya rikaringana maoko azere aivo vanhu vose vanonditevera.
\v	11	Zvino mambo waIsraeri wakapindura ndokuti:  Muudzei: Anosungira  zvombo muchiuno ngaarege kuzvikudza seanozvisunungura.

\v	34	Zvino  Bheni-Hadhadhi wakati kwaari: Maguta baba vangu avakatora kuna baba vako ndichadzosera, uye muchazviitira mugwagwa muDhamasiko, baba vangu seyavakaita muSamariya.  Ahabhi ndoku ti: Ini ndichakurega uende nesungano iyi. Zvino wakaita sungano naye akamurega achienda.

\v	36	Zvino wakati kwaari: Nekuda kwekuti hauna kuteerera kuinzwi raJehovha, tarira, uchangobva kwandiri, shumba ichakuuraya. Wakati achangobva kwaari, shumba yakamuwana, ndokumuuraya.
\v	37	Zvino wakawana umwe murume ndokuti: Ndirove hako. Murume ndokumurova, kurova nekukuvadza.
\v	38	Naizvozvo muporofita wakabva, ndokumirira mambo panzira, ndokuzvivanza chimiro nemucheka pameso ake.
\v	39	Zvino zvakaitika mambo paaipfuura kuti iye wakadanidzira kuna mambo, ndokuti: Muranda wenyu wakabudira mukati mehondo, zvino tarira, munhu wakatsauka ndokuunza munhu kwandiri, ndokuti: Chengeta munhu uyu. Kana akarasika zvekurasika, ipapo upenyu hwako huchava pachinzvimbo cheupenyu hwake, kana uripe tarenda resirivha.
\v	40	Muranda wenyu paaiva akabatikana pano nepapo, iye wakava asipo. Mambo waIsraeri ndokuti kwaari: Mutongo wako wakadaro, wautema iwe.
\v	41	Zvino wakakurumidza ndokubvisa mucheka kubva pameso ake; mambo waIsraeri ndokumuziva kuti ndeumwe wevaporofita.
\v	42	Zvino iye wakati kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Nokuti warega kuti abude muruoko  rwako munhu wandakamisira kumuparadza, naizvozvo upenyu hwako huchava pachinzvimbo cheupenyu hwake, nevanhu vako pachinzvimbo chevanhu vake.

\s	Ahabhi anotora munda waNabhoti nesimba
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kwezvinhu izvi, Nabhoti muJezireeri waiva ane munda wemizambiringa waiva paJezireeri pedo neimba yeushe yaAhabhi mambo weSamariya.
\v	2	ZvinoAhabhi wakataura kuna Nabhoti achiti: Ndipe munda wako wemizambiringa kuti uve kwandiri munda wemiriwo, nokuti uri pedo parutivi rweimba yangu; ndigokupa munda wemizambiringa uri nani pane uyu pachinzvimbo chawo; kana zvakanaka pameso ako, ndichakupa mari yemutengo wawo.
\v	3	ZvinoNabhoti wakati kuna Ahabhi: Ngazvive kure neni kuna Jehovha, kuti ndikupei nhaka yemadzibaba angu.
\v	4	ZvinoAhabhi wakapinda mumba make akaremerwa uye akatsamwa nekuda kweshoko Nabhoti muJezireeri raakataura kwaari; nokuti wakati: Handingakupii nhaka yemadzibaba angu. Zvino wakatsivama pamubhedha wake, ndokutendeusa chiso chake, uye haana kudya zvekudya.
\p
\v	5	ZvinoJezebheri mukadzi wake wakapinda kwaari ndokutaura kwaari: Sei mweya wenyu wakanuva kuti musadya zvekudya?
\v	6	Zvinowakati kwaari: Nokuti ndataura kuna Nabhoti muJezireeri ndokuti kwaari: Ndipe munda wako wemizambiringa  ndiutenge nemari, kana kuti kana uchida ndichakupa munda wemizambiringa pachinzvimbo chawo; iye ndokuti: Handingakupii munda wangu wemizambiringa.
\v	7	ZvinoJezebheri mukadzi wake wakati kwaari: Ikozvino ndimwi munotonga ushe pamusoro paIsraeri? Mukai, mudye zvekudya, uye moyo wenyu ufare; ndichakupai ini munda wemizambiringa waNabhoti muJezireeri.
\v	8	Naizvozvowakanyora tsamba muzita raAhabhi, ndokudzidhinda nechidhindo chake, ndokutuma tsamba kuvakuru nekumachinda vaiva muguta rake, vagere naNabhoti.

\v	10	Zvino muise varume vaviri vanakomana vaBheriari pakatarisana naye kuti vapupure kupokana naye vachiti: Wakatuka Mwari namambo. Zvino vachamubudisa vamutake nemabwe kusvika afa.

\v	13	Zvino kwakapinda varume vaviri vanakomana vaBheriari, ndokugara pamberi pake; vanakomana vaBheriari ndokumupupurira, kurwisa Nabhoti, pamberi pevanhu, vachiti: Nabhoti wanyomba Mwari namambo. Zvino vakamubudisira kunze kweguta, ndokumutaka nemabwe kusvika afa.

\v	16	Zvino zvakaitika pakunzwa kwaAhabhi kuti Nabhoti wafa, kuti Ahabhi wakasimuka kuburukira kumunda wemizambiringa waNabhoti muJezireeri, kuuita wake.

\v	18	Simuka uburukire kunosangana naAhabhi mambo waIsraeri ari muSamariya; tarira, ari mumunda wemizambiringa waNabhoti, kwaaburukira kunouita wake.

\v	20	Zvino Ahabhi wakati kuna Eria: Wandiwana here, iwe muvengi wangu? Iye ndokuti: Nda kuwana; nekuda kwekuzvitengesa kwako kuita chakaipa pameso aJehovha.
\v	21	Tarira, ndichauisa chakaipa pamusoro pako, uye ndichatsvaira chizvarwa chako, uye ndichagura kubva kuna Ahabhi anowetera pamadziro newakavharirwa newakasununguka kwaIsraeri.
\v	22	Zvino ndichaita imba yako seimba yaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, uye seimba yaBhaasha mwanakomana waAhija, nekuda kwekutsamwisa kwawakatsamwisa nako, uye ukaita kuti Israeri atadze.
\v	23	Zvino maererano naJezebheri Jehovha wakataurawo achiti: Imbwa dzichadya Jezebheri parusvingo rweJezireeri.

\p
\v	25	Asi hakuna kuva newakaita saAhabhi, wakazvitengesa kuita chakaipa pameso aJehovha, Jezebheri mukadzi wake wakamukurudzira.
\v	26	Ndokuita zvinyangadzo kwazvo pakutevera zvifananidzo maererano nezvose izvo vaAmori zvavakaita, ivo Jehovha vaakadzinga panhaka pamberi pevana vaIsraeri.

\v	28	Zvino shoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Eria muTishibhi richiti:
\v	29	Waona here kuti Ahabhi wazvininipisa pamberi pangu? Nekuda kwekuti wazvininipisa pamberi pangu, handingauisi chakaipa pamazuva ake; pamazuva emwanakomana wake ndichauisa chakaipa ichi paimba yake.

\c	3
\s	Kurota kwaSoromoni
\p
\v	1	ZvinoSoromoni wakasangana pawanano naFarao mambo weEgipita, ndokutora mukunda waFarao ndokumuunza kuguta raDhavhidhi kusvika apedza kuvaka imba yake neimba yaJehovha nerusvingo reJerusarema rwunopoteredza.
\v	2	Zvakadarovanhu vaipa zvibairo panzvimbo dzakakwirira, nokuti kwaiva kusina kuvakirwa zita raJehovha imba kusvika pamazuva iwawo.
\v	3	ZvinoSoromoni wakada Jehovha, achifamba muzvimiso zvaDhavhidhi baba vake; zvakadaro waibaira zvibairo nekupisa zvinonhuhwira panzvimbo dzakakwirira.
\v	4	Zvinomambo wakaenda kuGibhiyoni kunobairapo, nokuti yaiva nzvimbo huru yakakwirira; Soromoni ndokupa zvipo zvinopiswa churu paaritari iyo.
\p
\v	5	PaGibhiyoniJehovha wakazviratidza kuna Soromoni muchiroto usiku. Mwari ndokuti: Kumbira chandingakupa.
\v	6	Soromonindokuti: Imwi makaitira muranda wenyu Dhavhidhi baba vangu tsitsi huru, sezvaakafamba pamberi penyu muchokwadi uye mukururama, uye pakuvimbika kwemoyo kwamuri; mukamuchengeterawo tsitsi idzi huru, uye makamupa mwanakomana agere pachigaro chake cheushe, se zvazviri nhasi.
\v	7	Naizvozvozvino Jehovha Mwari wangu, imwi makaita muranda wenyu mambo pachinzvimbo chababa vangu Dhavhidhi, asi ndiri mwanana; handizivi kubuda kana kupinda.
\v	8	Zvinomuranda wenyu ari pakati pevanhu venyu vamakatsaura, vanhu vazhinji, vasingagoni kuverengwa kana kurahwa neuwandu.
\v	9	Naizvozvoipai muranda wenyu moyo unonzwisisa, kutonga vanhu venyu, kuti ndizive kuparadzanisa pakati pezvakanaka nezvakaipa; nokuti ndiani anogona kutonga rudzi rwenyu urwu rwunorema?
\v	10	Zvino chinhu ichi chaiva chakanaka pameso aIshe, kuti Soromoni wakakumbira chinhu ichi.
\v	11	Mwari ndokuti kwaari: Nekuda kwekuti wakumbira chinhu ichi, uye hauna kuzvikumbirira mazuva mazhinji, uye hauna kuzvikumbirira fuma, uye hauna kukumbira mweya weupenyu wevavengi vako; asi wazvikumbirira kunzwisisa kunzwa mutongo;
\v	12	tarira, ndaita zvinoenderana nemashoko ako; tarira ndakupa moyo wakachenjera uye unonzwisisa, zvekuti hakuna newakaita sewe mberi kwako, uye shure kwako hakungamuki wakaita sewe.
\v	13	Uye ndakupawo zvausina kukumbira, zvose fuma nerukudzo, zvekuti hakungazovi neumwe pakati pemadzimambo wakaita sewe mazuva ako ose.
\v	14	Zvino kana ukafamba nenzira dzangu, kuchengeta zvimiso zvangu nemirairo yangu, sezvakafamba baba vako Dhavhidhi, ipapo ndicharebesa mazuva ako.
\v	15	Zvino Soromoni wakamuka, zvino tarira, chaiva chiroto. Zvino wakauya kuJerusarema ndokumira pamberi peareka yesungano yaIshe, ndokupa zvipo zvinopiswa, ndokupa zvipo zvekuyananisa, ndokuitira varanda vake vose mabiko.
\p
\v	16	Ipapo kwakasvikapo vakadzi vaviri vaiva zvifeve kuna mambo ndokumira pamberi pake.
\v	17	Umwe mukadzi ndokuti: Haiwa ishe wangu, ini nemukadzi uyu tinogara muimba imwe; zvino ndikapona ndinaye mumba.
\v	18	Zvino zvakaitika pazuva retatu shure kwekunge ndapona kuti mukadzi uyu wakaponawo, uye taiva pamwe, pasina mweni nesu mumba, kunze kwedu tiri vaviri mumba.
\p
\v	19	Zvino mwana wemukadzi uyu wakafa usiku, nokuti wakarara pamusoro pake.
\v	20	Zvino wakamuka pakati peusiku ndokutora mwanakomana wangu kubva parutivi rwangu, murandakadzi wenyu achakarara, ndokumuradzika pachipfuva chake, ndokuradzika mwana wake wakafa pachipfuva changu.
\v	21	Pandamuka mangwanani kuyamwisa mwana wangu, zvino tarirai, wakafa; asi ndati ndichimucherekedza mangwanani, zvino tarirai, haasi mwanakomana wangu wandakabereka.
\v	22	Zvino umwe mukadzi wakati: Kwete, asi mupenyu mwanakomana wangu, uye wakafa mwanakomana wako; uyu ndokuti: Kwete, asi wakafa mwanakomana wako, mupenyu mwanakomana wangu. Vakataura saizvozvo pamberi pamambo.
\p
\v	23	Zvino mambo wakati: Uyu anoti: Uyu mwanakomana wangu mupenyu uye mwanakomana wako ndewakafa; uye umwe anoti: Kwete, asi mwanakomana wako ndewakafa uye mwanakomana wangu mupenyu.
\v	24	Mambo ndokuti: Nditorerei munondo. Zvino vakaunza munondo pamberi pamambo.
\v	25	Mambo ndokuti: Pamurai mwana mupenyu kuita  mapandi maviri, uye mupe umwe hafu, neumwe hafu.
\v	26	Zvino mukadzi waiva wemwanakomana mupenyu wakataura kuna mambo, nokuti ura hwake hwakamunzwira pamusoro pemwanakomana wake, zvino wakati: Haiwa, ishe wangu, mupei mwana mupenyu, uye musatongomuuraya. Asi umwe wakati: Ngaarege kuva wangu kana wako, pamurai.
\v	27	Zvino mambo wakapindura ndokuti: Ipai iye mwana mupenyu, musatongomuuraya; ndiye mai vake.
\v	28	Zvino Israeri wose wakanzwa mutongo mambo waakatonga, ndokutya pamberi pamambo; nokuti vakaona kuti uchenjeri hwaMwari hwaiva maari kuita mutongo.

\p
\v	1	Zvinomambo Soromoni wakava mambo pamusoro paIsraeri wose.
\v	2	Zvinoawa ndiwo machinda aaiva nawo: Azariya mwanakomana waZadhoki waiva mupristi;
\v	3	ErihorefinaAhija vanakomana vaShisha vaiva vanyori; Jehoshafati mwanakomana waAhirudhi waiva munyori wenhoroondo;
\v	4	naBhenayamwanakomana waJehoyadha waiva pamusoro pehondo; naZadhoki naAbhiatari vaiva vapristi;
\v	5	naAzariyamwanakomana waNatani waiva pamusoro pevatariri; naZabhudhi mwanakomana waNatani waiva mupristi, shamwari yamambo.
\v	6	Abhishariwaiva pamusoro peimba; naAdhoniramu mwanakomana waAbhidha waiva pamusoro pemutero.
\p
\v	7	ZvinoSoromoni waiva nevatariri gumi nevaviri pamusoro paIsraeri wose, vaitsvakira mambo neimba yake zvekudya; mwedzi pagore waiva paumwe neumwe kutsvaka zvekudya.
\v	8	Zvinoawa mazita avo: Bheni-Huri pamakomo aEfuremu;
\v	9	Bheni-DhekeripaMakazi, nepaSharibhimi, neBheti-Shemeshi, neEroni-Bheti-Hanani;
\v	10	Bheni-Hesedhi paArubhoti; iye waiva neSokoho, nenyika yose yeHeferi;
\v	11	Bheni-Abhinadhabhi  panyika yose yeDhori; Tafati mukunda waSoromoni waiva mukadzi wake;
\v	12	Bhaana mwanakomana waAhirudhi  paTaanaki, neMegidho, neBheti-Sheani rose, iri paZaretani pazasi peJezireeri, kubva paBheti-Sheani kusvika paAbheri-Mehora kusvika mhiri kweJokimeami;
\v	13	Bheni-Gebheri paRamoti-Giriyadhi; waiva nemisha yaJairi mwanakomana waManase, yaiva paGiriyadhi; iye waiva nedunhu reArigobhi iri paBhashani, maguta makuru makumi matanhatu aiva nemasvingo ane mazariro endarira;
\v	14	Ahinadhabhi mwanakomana waIdho  paMahanayimu;
\v	15	Ahimaazi paNafutari; iyewo wakatora Bhasemati mukunda waSoromoni kuva mukadzi;
\v	16	Bhaana mwanakomana waHushai paAsheri nepaAroti;
\v	17	Josafati mwanakomana waParua paIsasikari;
\v	18	Shimei mwanakomana waEra paBhenjamini;
\v	19	Gebheri mwanakomana waUri panyika yeGiriyadhi, nyika yaSihoni mambo wevaAmori neyaOgi mambo weBashani; uye waiva iye ega mutariri waiva panyika iyo.
\p
\v	20	Judha naIsraeri vaiva vazhinji sejecha riri pagungwa pauwandu, vachidya, vachinwa, vachifara.
\v	21	Zvino Soromoni wakatonga pamusoro peushe hwose kubva parwizi  kusvika kunyika yevaFirisitia nekusvika kumuganhu weEgipita; vakauya nezvipo uye vaishandira Soromoni mazuva ose eupenyu hwake.
\p
\v	22	Zvino zvekudya zvaSoromoni zvezuva rimwe zvaiva zviyero makumi matatu zveupfu hwakatsetseka, nezviyero makumi matanhatu zveupfu,
\v	23	nzombe gumi dzakakodzwa, nemombe makumi maviri dzekumafuro, nemakwai zana, kunze kwenondo, nemhara, nemhembwe, neshiri dzakakodzwa.
\v	24	Nokuti wakatonga pamusoro pechekuno kose kwerwizi, kubva paTifisa kusvika paGaza, pamusoro pemadzimambo ose nechekuno kwerwizi; uye wakava nerugare kunhivi dzake dzose dzaiva dzakapoteredza.
\v	25	Zvino Judha naIsraeri vakagara pakadzivirirwa, umwe neumwe pazasi pemuzambiringa wake nepazasi pemuonde wake, kubva paDhani kusvika paBheeri-Shebha, mazuva ose aSoromoni.
\p
\v	26	Soromoni waiva nezvidyiro zvuru makumi mana zvemabhiza engoro dzake, nevatasvi vemabhiza zvuru gumi nezviviri.
\v	27	Zvino vatariri ava vaitsvakira mambo Soromoni zvekudya, newose waiswedera patafura yamambo Soromoni, umwe neumwe pamwedzi wake; havana kusiya kuchishaikwa chinhu.
\v	28	Bhariwo nemajanga emabhiza nemabhiza aimhanyisa vakauisa panzvimbo ya zvaifanira kuva, umwe neumwe sezvaakatongerwa.
\p
\v	29	Mwari ndokupa Soromoni njere nekunzwisisa kukuru kwazvo, neupamhi hwemoyo, sejecha riri pamahombekombe egungwa.
\v	30	Zvino njere dzaSoromoni dzakapfuura njere dzevose vanakomana vemabvazuva, nedzakapfuura njere dzose dzevaEgipita.
\v	31	Nokuti waiva akachenjera kupfuura vanhu vese; kupfuura Etani muEzirahi, naHemani, naKarikori, naDharidha, vanakomana vaMahori; uye zita rake rakava kundudzi dzose dzaiva dzakapoteredza.
\v	32	Zvino wakataura tsumo zvuru zvitatu, uye nziyo dzake dzaiva churu neshanu.
\v	33	Zvino wakataura zvemiti, kubva pamusidhari uri paRebhanoni kusvika pamuhisopi unobuda parusvingo; akataurawo zvemhuka, nezveshiri, nezvezvinokambaira, nezvehove.
\v	34	Zvino kwakasvika kubva kumarudzi ose kuzonzwa kuchenjera kwaSoromoni, kubva kumadzimambo ose enyika vakanzwa zvekuchenjera kwake.
\c	5
\s	Soromoni anogadzira zvose kuti tembere ivakwe
\p
\v	1	ZvinoHiramu mambo weTire wakatuma varanda vake kuna Soromoni, nokuti waiva anzwa kuti vakamuzodza kuva mambo pachinzvimbo chababa vake; nokuti Hiramu waida Dhavhidhi mazuva ose.
\v	2	ZvinoSoromoni wakatuma  nhume kuna Hiramu achiti:
\v	3	Imwimunoziva kuti Dhavhidhi baba vangu vaiva vasingagoni kuvakira zita raJehovha Mwari wavo imba nekuda kwehondo mavaiva vakamukomba madziri, kusvika Jehovha avaisa pasi pechitsiko chetsoka dzake.
\v	4	Asiikozvino Jehovha Mwari wangu wakandipa zororo kumativi ose. Hakuna muvengi uye hakuna chakaipa chinoitika.
\v	5	Zvinotarirai, ndakazvipira kuvakira zita raJehovha Mwari wangu imba, Jehovha sezvaakataura kuna baba vangu Dhavhidhi, achiti: Mwanakomana wako wandichaisa pachigaro chako cheushe pachinzvimbo chako ndiye achavakira zita rangu imba.
\v	6	Zvinoikozvino, rairai kuti vanditemere misidhari kubva kuRebhanoni. Zvino varanda vangu vachava pamwe nevaranda venyu; uye kwamuri ndichapa mubairo wevaranda venyu sezvose zvamuchareva; nokuti imwi munoziva kuti pakati pedu hapana munhu anoziva kutema matanda sevaSidhoni.
\p
\v	7	Zvinozvakaitika Hiramu paakanzwa mashoko aSoromoni, kuti wakafara kwazvo, ndokuti: Jehovha ngaarumbidzwe nhasi, iye wakapa Dhavhidhi mwanakomana wakachenjera pamusoro perudzi urwu rukuru.
\v	8	Hiramundokutuma  nhume kuna Soromoni achiti: Ndanzwa zvamanditumira; ini ndichaita zvishuwo zvenyu zvose maererano nematanda emisidhari nemaererano nematanda emisipiresi.
\v	9	Varandavangu vacha aburusa kubva kuRebhanoni kuenda kugungwa; uye ini ndichaaisa mumakwande ekuyeredza negungwa kuenda kunzvimbo yamuchatuma kwandiri, uye ndichaasunungurira ipapo; uye imwi muchatorera po, imwi muchaita chishuwo changu nekupa veimba yangu zvekudya.
\v	10	Naizvozvo Hiramu wakapa Soromoni matanda emisidhari nematanda emisipiresi, chishuwo chake chose.
\v	11	Zvino Soromoni wakapawo kuna Hiramu zviyero zvuru makumi maviri zvezviyo zvive zvekudya zveveimba yake, nezviyero makumi maviri zvemafuta akatakiwa. Saizvozvo Soromoni wakapa Hiramu gore negore.
\v	12	Zvino Jehovha wakapa Soromoni njere sezvaaiva ataura kwaari; uye pakava nerugare pakati paHiramu naSoromoni; vakaita sungano vari vaviri.
\p
\v	13	Mambo Soromoni akasimudza vanhu vechibharo pakati paIsraeri wese; zvino vanhu vechibharo vaiva vanhu zvuru makumi matatu.
\v	14	Akavatumira Rebhanoni, mwedzi umwe neumwe zvuru zvine gumi, vachiravana; vaiva paRebhanoni mwedzi umwe, kumusha mwedzi miviri; Adhoniramu ndiye waiva pamusoro pevanhu vechibharo.
\v	15	Uye Soromoni waiva nevatakuri vemitoro zvuru makumi manomwe, nevatemi zvuru makumi masere pamakomo;
\v	16	vasingaverengwi madzishe evatariri vaSoromoni, vaiva pamusoro pebasa, zvuru zvitatu nemazana matatu, vaiva pamusoro pevanhu vaibata pabasa.
\v	17	Mambo akaraira, vakaunza mabwe makuru, mabwe aikosha, kuti vateye nheyo dzeimba  nemabwe akavezwa.
\v	18	Vavaki vaSoromoni nevavaki vaHiramu nevaGhebhari vakaaveza, vakagadzira matanda nemabwe kuzovaka imba.
\c	6
\s	Kuvakwa kwetembere
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pagore remazana mana nemakumi masere shure kwekubuda kwevana vaIsraeri panyika yeEgipita, pagore rechina rekutonga kwaSoromoni pamusoro paIsraeri, mumwedzi waZifi unova mwedzi wechipiri, wakavaka imba yaJehovha.
\v	2	Zvinoimba mambo Soromoni yaakavakira Jehovha, kureba kwayo kwaiva makubhiti makumi matanhatu, uye upamhi hwayo maviri, nekukwirira kwayo makubhiti makumi matatu.
\v	3	Uyeberere pamberi petembere yeimba, kureba kwaro kwaiva makubhiti makumi maviri zvichienderana neupamhi hweimba; upamhi hwaro hwaiva makubhiti gumi pamberi peimba.
\v	4	Zvinowakaitira imba mahwindo matete akavharwa ebasa rezvirukwa zvezvitanda.
\p
\v	5	Zvinowakavaka makamuri pachidziro cheimba kumativi ose, madziro eimba kunhivi dzose, etembere uye enzvimbo yeshoko; ndokuita makamuri parutivi kupoteredza.
\v	6	Zvinokamuri raiva pasi peose, upamhi hwaro hwaiva makubhiti mashanu, uye riri pakati, upamhi hwaro hwaiva makubhiti matanhatu, neretatu, upamhi hwaro makubhiti manomwe. Nokuti wakaita zvitakuriso zveimba kupoteredza kunze, kuti  matanda arege kupfekera mumadziro eimba.
\v	7	Zvinoimba ichiri kuvakwa, yakavakwa nemabwe akaperedzerwa pakuvezwa, kuti kusanzwikwa nyundo kana sanhu  kana nhumbi ipi neipi yedare muimba ichiri kuvakwa.
\v	8	Mukovawekamuri riri kurutivi rwepakati waiva kurutivi rwerudyi rweimba. Zvino vaikwira nezvikwiriso zvinomonereka kuenda ku kamuri repakati, uye kubva pane repakati kuenda kune retatu.
\v	9	Zvinowakavaka imba ndokuipedza; ndokufukidza imba nematanda uye nemapuranga emisidhari.
\v	10	Zvino wakavaka makamuri kurutivi rweimba yese; makubhiti mashanu kukwirira kwawo, neekupfekera paimba nematanda emisidhari.
\p
\v	11	Zvino shoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Soromoni richiti:
\v	12	 Kana iri imba iyi yaunovaka, kana ukafamba nezvimiso zvangu, uye ukaita mitongo yangu, uye ukachengeta mirairo yangu yose kufamba nayo, ndichaita shoko rangu kwauri, randakataura kuna Dhavhidhi baba vako;
\v	13	uye ndichagara pakati pevana vaIsraeri, uye handingarasi vanhu vangu Israeri.
\p
\v	14	Naizvozvo Soromoni wakavaka imba ndokuipedza.
\v	15	Zvino wakavaka madziro eimba mukati nemapuranga emusidhari; kubva pasi peimba kusvika pamadziro edenga wakawaridza mukati nemapuranga, ndokuwaridza pasi peimba nemapuranga emusipiresi.
\v	16	Zvino wakavaka makubhiti makumi maviri kumativi eimba nemapuranga emusidhari kubva pasi kusvika pamadziro; akaivakawo nechemukati, achiitira nzvimbo yeshoko, achiitira nzvimbo tsvene-tsvene.
\v	17	Zvino imba, iyo tembere yaiva pamberi, yaiva makubhiti makumi mana.
\v	18	Zvino musidhari weimba mukati waiva wakavezwa nemafundo nemaruva akazaruka; zvose zviri musidhari, kwaiva kusingaonekwi ibwe.
\p
\v	19	Zvino wakagadzira nzvimbo yeshoko mumba mukati, kuisapo areka yesungano yaJehovha.
\v	20	Zvino nzvimbo yeshoko yaiva pamberi yaiva makubhiti makumi maviri pakureba uye makubhiti makumi maviri paupamhi uye makubhiti makumi maviri pakukwirira kwayo; zvino wakaiputira negoridhe rakaisvonaka; nearitari wakaifukidza nemusidhari.
\v	21	Zvino Soromoni wakadzudzura imba nechemukati mayo negoridhe rakaisvonaka; ndokupfuudza maketani egoridhe pamberi penzvimbo yeshoko, ndokuidzudzura negoridhe.
\v	22	Zvino wakadzudzura imba yose negoridhe kusvika apedza imba yese; akadzudzurawo negoridhe aritari yose yaiva panzvimbo yeshoko.
\p
\v	23	Zvino wakaita mukati menzvimbo yeshoko makerubhi maviri emuti wemuorivhi, kukwirira kwerimwe nerimwe makubhiti gumi.
\v	24	Rimwe bapiro rekerubhi raiva makubhiti mashanu, uye rimwe bapiro rekerubhi raiva makubhiti mashanu; kubva pamicheto yebapiro raro  rimwe kusvika pamicheto yebapiro raro  rimwe aiva makubhiti gumi.
\v	25	Uye rimwe kerubhi raiva makubhiti gumi; makerubhi ari maviri aiva nechiyero chimwe nechimiro chimwe.
\v	26	Kukwirira kwerimwe kerubhi kwaiva makubhiti gumi, uye saizvozvo rimwe kerubhi.
\v	27	Zvino wakaisa makerubhi mukati meimba yemukati; uye makerubhi akatandavadza mapapiro zvekuti bapiro rerimwe rakabata madziro, uye bapiro rerimwe rekerubhi rakabata amwe madziro; uye mapapiro awo ndokugunzvana bapiro nebapiro pakati peimba.
\v	28	Zvino wakadzudzura makerubhi negoridhe.
\v	29	Uye wakafombora madziro ose eimba kunhivi dzose mifananidzo yakafomborwa yemakerubhi nemiti yemichindwe nemaruva akazaruka, mukati nekunze.

\v	32	Uye magonhi maviri aiva emuti wemuorivhi, ndokuafombora mifomborwa yemakerubhi, nemiti yemichindwe, nemaruva akazaruka, ndokuzvidzudzura negoridhe; ndokupambadzira goridhe pamusoro pemakerubhi nepamusoro pemiti yemichindwe.
\v	33	Saizvozvowo wakaitira mukova wetembere magwatidziro emuti wemuorivhi, pamativi mana.
\v	34	Nemagonhi maviri emuti wemusipiresi; mativi maviri egonhi rimwe aipeteka, uye mapenga maviri egonhi repiri aipeteka.
\v	35	Zvino wakafomborera po makerubhi nemiti yemichindwe nemaruva akazaruka; akazvidzudzura negoridhe rakapambadzirwa zvakanaka pamusoro pezvakavezwa.

\v	36	Zvino wakavaka chivanze chemukati nemitsara mitatu yemabwe akavezwa, nemusara wematanda emusidhari.

\v	6	Wakaitaberere rembiru; urefu hwaro hwaiva makubhiti makumi mashanu, uye upamhi hwaro hwaiva makubhiti makumi matatu; neberere pamberi padzo, nembiru nechikumbaridzo pamberi padzo.

\v	8	Neimbayake yaaigara, chimwe chivanze mukati meberere, yaiva yakafanana nebasa iri. Wakaitirawo mukunda waFarao, Soromoni waaiva awana, imba, yakafanana neberere iri.

\v	11	Zvino kumusoro kwaiva nemabwe anokosha akavezwa zvinoenderana nezviyero, nemusidhari.
\v	12	Zvino chivanze chikuru chakapoteredza chaiva mitsara mitatu yemabwe akavezwa, nemusara wematanda emisidhari. Zvaiva zvakadaro nechivanze chemukati cheimba yaJehovha, uye neberere reimba.
\p
\v	13	Zvino mambo Soromoni wakatuma  nhume ndokutora Hiramu kubva kuTire.
\v	14	Iye waiva mwanakomana wemukadzi chirikadzi yerudzi rwaNafutari, uye baba vake vaiva munhu weTire, mupfuri wendarira; uye waiva akazadzwa nenjere nekunzwisisa neruzivo rwekuita basa ripi neripi nendarira. Zvino wakauya kuna mambo Soromoni, ndokuita basa rake rose.

\v	16	Zvino wakaita misoro miviri yendarira yakanyunguduswa kuisa pamusoro wembiru; kukwirira kwemusoro umwe kwaiva makubhiti mashanu, uye kukwirira kweumwe musoro kwaiva makubhiti mashanu.
\v	17	Mimbure iri basa rakarukwa, tambo dzakarukwa dzaiva basa remaketani, kuitira misoro iri pamusoro wembiru; minomwe pamusoro umwe uye minomwe pane umwe musoro.
\v	18	Zvino wakaita mbiru mbiri nemitsara miviri kupoteredza pachirukwa chimwe kufukidza misoro yaiva pamusoro wemapomegiranati. Zvino saizvozvo wakaitira umwe musoro.
\v	19	Zvino misoro yaiva pamusoro wembiru yaiva basa remaruva emusipiti muberere, makubhiti mana.
\v	20	Zvino misoro yaiva pambiru mbiri nepamusoro pakatarisana nedumbu raiva pedo nemumbure; nemapomegiranati aiva mazana maviri aiva  pamitsara yakapoteredza, pamusoro peumwe musoro.
\v	21	Zvino wakamisa mbiru paberere retembere; ndokumisa mbiru yerudyi, ndokutumidza zita rayo Jakini; ndokumisa mbiru yeruboshwe, ndokutumidza zita rayo Bhowasi.
\v	22	Zvino pamusoro wembiru paiva nebasa remaruva emusipiti; zvino basa rembiru rakapedzwa.
\p
\v	23	Zvino wakaita gungwa rakanyunguduswa; makubhiti gumi kubva kumupendero waro kusvika kumupendero waro, kutenderera kupoteredza, uye urefu hwaro hwaiva makubhiti mashanu; uye rwonzi rwemakubhiti makumi matatu rwakakomba kupoteredza.
\v	24	Zvino pasi pemupendero waro paiva nemafundo akaripoteredza kuritenderedza, ari gumi pakubhiti, kupoteredza gungwa kuritenderedza; mitsara miviri yemafundo yakaumbwa nekunyungudusa pakuumbwa kwawo nekunyungudusa.
\v	25	Rakamira pamusoro penzombe gumi nembiri, nhatu dzaiva dzakatarisa kumaodzanyemba, uye nhatu dzakatarisa kumavirazuva, uye nhatu dzakatarisa kuchamhembe, uye nhatu dzakatarisa kumabvazuva, uye gungwa raiva pamusoro padzo pamusoro, uye shure kwadzo kose kwaiva nechemukati.
\v	26	Zvino ukobvu hwaro hwaiva upamhi hwechanza, uye mupendero waro waiva wakafanana nebasa remupendero wemukombe,  seruva remusipiti; raiva rine mabhati zvuru zviviri mukati.

\v	28	Zvino iri raiva basa rezvigadziko: Zvaiva nemipendero; uye mipendero yaiva pakati pezvibatanidzi.

\v	30	Zvino chigadziko chimwe nechimwe chaiva nemavhiri mana endarira nemaekisera endarira; uye makona acho mana aiva nemafudzi; pasi pedhishi rekugezera paiva nemafudzi akaumbwa nekunyunguduswa kurutivi rwechirukwa chemaruva ichi neichi.
\v	31	Zvino muromo wawo pakati pemusoro nechekumusoro waiva kubhiti; nemuromo wawo waiva wakapoteredza, basa rechigadziko, kubhiti nehafu yekubhiti; uyewo pamusoro pemuromo wawo paiva nezvakavezwa, uye mipendero yawo yaiva mativi mana akaenzana, kwete denderedzwa.
\v	32	Uye mavhiri mana aiva pasi pemipendero, uye maekisera emavhiri aiva pachigadziko; uye kukwirira kwevhiri rimwe kwaiva kubhiti nehafu yekubhiti.
\v	33	Zvino basa remavhiri raiva sebasa revhiri rengoro; maekisera awo nemarimu awo nemasipokisi awo nemahabhu awo zvaiva zvakaumbwa nekunyungudusa.

\v	34	Zvino paiva nemafudzi mana pamakona ari mana echigadziko chimwe; mafudzi acho achibva pachigadziko.
\v	35	Zvino pamusoro wechigadziko paiva nekukwirira hafu yekubhiti kutenderedza kupoteredza; uye pamusoro pechigadziko zvitsigiro zvacho nemipendero yacho zvichibva pachiri.
\v	36	Zvino pamusoro pemapuranga ezvitsigiro zvayo uye pamusoro pemipendero yazvo wakaveza makerubhi, shumba, nemiti yemichindwe, zvakaringana nenzvimbo yazvo yose isina chinhu, nezvirukwa zvemaruva kunhivi dzose.
\v	37	Wakaita saizvozvo zvigadziko gumi; zvose zvaiva zvine kuumbwa kumwe, chiyero chimwe, chimiro chimwe.
\p
\v	38	Zvino wakaita madhishi ekugezera endarira gumi; dhishi rekugezera rimwe nerimwe raipinda mabhati makumi mana; dhishi rekugezera rimwe nerimwe raiva makubhiti mana; dhishi rekugezera rimwe raiva pamusoro pechimwe nechimwe chezvigadziko gumi.
\v	39	Zvino wakaisa zvigadziko zvishanu kurutivi rwerudyi rweimba, uye zvishanu kurutivi rweruboshwe rweimba; ndokuisa gungwa kurutivi rwerudyi rweimba nechekumabvazuva pakatarisana nekuchamhembe.

\v	40	Hiramu ndokuita madhishi ekugezera nemafoshoro nembiya. Hiramu ndokupedza kuita mabasa ose aakaitira mambo Soromoni paimba yaJehovha:

\v	42	nemapomegiranati mazana mana emimbure miviri, mitsara miviri yemapomegiranati pamumbure umwe, kufukidza mbiya mbiri dzemisoro yaiva pamusoro pembiru;

\v	44	negungwa rimwe, nenzombe gumi nembiri pasi pegungwa;

\v	50	nemikombe, nembato dzemwenje, nembiya, nezvirongo, nezvaenga zvemoto zvegoridhe rakazara; nezvibato zvegoridhe zvemagonhi eimba yemukati, zvenzvimbo tsvene-tsvene, zvemagonhi eimba yetembere.

\v	29	Kuti meso enyu azarurikire imba iyi siku nesikati, nzvimbo yamakati nezvayo: Zita rangu richavapo; kuteerera kumunyengetero muranda wenyu waanonyengetera akatarisa kunzvimbo ino.

\v	35	Kana matenga akavharwa, zvino kukasava nemvura, nokuti vakakutadzirai, kana vakanyengetera vakatarisa kunzvimbo ino, vakatenda zita renyu, vakatendeuka pazvivi zvavo, pamunovatambudza,

\v	37	Kana pakava nenzara panyika, kana pane denda, kuvhuvha, kufunuka, mhashu, kana kune mhondoro, kana muvengi wavo akavakomba panyika yemaguta avo, dambudziko ripi, hosha ipi;

\v	41	Zvinowo maererano nemutorwa, asiri wevanhu venyu Israeri, asi anobva kunyika iri kure nekuda kwezita renyu;
\v	42	nokuti vachanzwa zvezita renyu guru uye zveruoko rwenyu rwakasimba uye zvechanza chenyu chakatandavadzwa; kana akauya ndokunyengetera akatarira kuimba ino;
\v	43	inzwai imwi muri kumatenga, nzvimbo yenyu yekugara yakamiswa, mugoita zvinoenderana nezvose mutorwa zvaanokudanirai, kuti marudzi ose epanyika azive zita renyu, kukutyai, sevanhu venyu Israeri, uye kuti vazive kuti zita renyu rinodanwa pamusoro peimba ino yandakavaka.

\v	45	ipapo inzwai imwi muri kumatenga munyengetero wavo nemukumbiro wavo, uye muite kururamisirwa kwavo.

\v	47	kana vakazozvifunga kunyika kwavakatapirwa, vakadzokazve, vakakumbira kwamuri munyika yevatapi vavo, vachiti: Tatadza, uye tatsauka, taita zvakaipa;

\v	56	Jehovha ngaakudzwe wakapa vanhu vake Israeri zororo zvichienderana nezvose zvaakataura. Hakuna kuwa shoko rimwe pachivimbiso chake chose chakanaka chaakataura neruoko rwaMozisi muranda wake.
\v	57	Jehovha Mwari wedu ngaave nesu, sezvaaiva nemadzibaba edu; ngaarege kutisiya, kana kutirega;
\v	58	kuti arerekerese moyo yedu kwaari, kufamba munzira dzake dzose, uye kuchengeta mirairo yake, nezvimiso zvake, nemitongo yake, zvaakaraira madzibaba edu.
\v	59	Zvino mashoko awa andakakumbira nawo pamberi paJehovha, ngaave pedo naJehovha Mwari wedu masikati neusiku, kuti aite kururamisirwa kwemuranda wake, nekururama kwevanhu vake Israeri, nyaya yezuva pazuva rayo;
\v	60	kuti vanhu vose venyika vazive kuti Jehovha ndiMwari; hakuna umwe.
\v	61	Naizvozvo moyo wenyu ngauve wakaperedzerwa kuna Jehovha Mwari wedu, kufamba muzvimiso zvake nekuchengeta mirairo yake, sanhasi.
\p
\v	62	Zvino mambo naIsraeri wose anaye ndokupa chibairo pamberi paJehovha.
\v	63	Zvino Soromoni wakapa chibairo chezvipo zvekuyananisa, zvaakapa kuna Jehovha, nzombe zvuru makumi maviri nezviviri, nemakwai zvuru zana nemakumi maviri. Mambo nevana vaIsraeri vose ndokutsaura imba yaJehovha.
\v	64	Nezuva iroro mambo wakatsvenesa pakati pechivanze chaiva pamberi peimba yaJehovha, nokuti ipapo wakapa chipo chinopiswa nechipo chechidyiwa nemafuta ezvipo zvekuyananisa; nokuti aritari yendarira yaiva pamberi paJehovha yaiva iri diki zvikuru kuringana chipo chinopiswa nechipo chechidyiwa nemafuta ezvipo zvekuyananisa.
\p
\v	65	Zvino nenguva iyo Soromoni wakaita mabiko, naIsraeri wose pamwe naye, ungano huru, kubva pakupinda paHamati kusvika parukova rweEgipita, pamberi paJehovha Mwari wedu, mazuva manomwe nemazuva manomwe, mazuva gumi nemana.
\v	66	Pazuva rerusere wakaendesa vanhu, ivo ndokuropafadza mambo, ndokuenda kumatende avo nerufaro uye kunaka kwemoyo nezvose Jehovha zvaakaitira Dhavhidhi muranda wake naIsraeri vanhu vake.
\c	9
\s	Jehovha anozviratidzazve kuna Soromoni
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pakupedza kwaSoromoni kuvaka imba yaJehovha neimba yamambo nechishuwo chese chaSoromoni chaaifarira kuita,
\v	2	kutiJehovha wakaonekwa kuna Soromoni rwechipiri sezvaaiva aonekwa kwaari paGibhiyoni.
\v	3	Jehovhandokuti kwaari: Ndanzwa munyengetero wako nemukumbiro wako zvawakumbira mberi kwangu. Ndaita imba ino tsvene yawakavaka kuisa zita rangupo rinhi narinhi; uye meso angu nemoyo wangu zvichavapo mazuva ose.
\v	4	Zvinoiwe, kana uchifamba pamberi pangu saDhavhidhi baba vako vakafamba nemoyo unorevesa, nekururama, kuita maererano nezvese zvandakakuraira, uchichengeta zvimiso zvangu nemitongo yangu,
\v	5	ipapondichasimbisa chigaro cheushe cheumambo hwako pamusoro paIsraeri rinhi narinhi, sezvandakataura kuna Dhavhidhi baba vako, ndichiti: Hakungashaikwi munhu pamusoro pechigaro cheushe chaIsraeri.
\v	6	Kanamukatendeuka zvekutendeuka imwi nevana venyu pakunditevera uye mukasachengeta mirairo yangu, zvimiso zvangu zvandakaisa pamberi penyu, asi mukaenda mukashumira vamwe vamwari ndokuvanamata;
\v	7	ipapondichagura Israeri kubva panyika yandakavapa; neimba iyi yandakaitira tsvene zita rangu, ndichairasira kure nechiso changu; uye Israeri achava shumo nechiseko pakati pevanhu vese;
\v	8	uyeimba iyi yakakwirira, wese anopfuura nepairi achashamiswa uye acharidza muridzo, uye vachati: Jehovha wakaitirei kudai kunyika ino uye kuimba ino?
\v	9	Zvinovachiti: Nokuti vakasiya Jehovha Mwari wavo, wakabudisa madzibaba avo munyika yeEgipita, ndokubatirira vamwe vamwari ndokuvashumira, ndokuvabatira; naizvozvo Jehovha wavaburusira chakaipa chese ichi.
\s	Maguta akavakwa naSoromoni
\p
\v	10	Zvino zvakaitika pakupera kwemakore makumi maviri Soromoni avaka dzimba idzi mbiri, imba yaJehovha neimba yamambo;
\v	11	( nokuti Hiramu mambo weTire waiva atsigira Soromoni nematanda emisidhari uye nematanda emisipiresi uye negoridhe zvichienderana nechishuwo chake chese); nenguva iyo mambo Soromoni wakapa Hiramu maguta makumi maviri panyika yeGarirea.
\v	12	Hiramu ndokubuda akabva Tire kuzoona maguta Soromoni aaiva amupa; asi aiva asina kunaka pameso ake.
\v	13	Ndokuti: Magutai awa awandipa, hama yangu? Zvino wakaatumidza nyika yeKabhuri kusvika nhasi.
\v	14	Zvino Hiramu wakatumira mambo matarenda egoridhe zana nemakumi maviri.
\p
\v	15	Zvino iyi inyaya yechibharo mambo Soromoni chaakasimudzira kuvaka imba yaJehovha neimba yake neMiro nerusvingo rweJerusarema neHazori neMegidho neGezeri.
\v	16	Farao mambo weEgipita waiva akwira ndokutapa Gezeri, ndokuripisa nemoto, ndokuuraya vaKenani vaigara muguta, ndokupemberera naro mukunda wake, mukadzi waSoromoni.
\v	17	Soromoni ndokuvaka Gezeri, neBheti-Horoni rezasi,
\v	18	neBharati, neTamari murenje, munyika iyo,
\v	19	nemaguta ese ematura Soromoni aaiva nawo, nemaguta engoro, nemaguta evatasvi vemabhiza, nechishuwo chaSoromoni chaakashuva kuvaka muJerusarema, nemuRebhanoni, nepanyika yese yaaiva nesimba pamusoro payo.
\v	20	Vanhu vese vaiva vasara vevaAmori, vaHiti, vaPerezi, vaHivhi, nevaJebhusi, vaiva vasi vevana vaIsraeri;
\v	21	vana vavo vaiva vasara shure kwavo panyika, avo vana vaIsraeri vavaiva vasingagoni kuparadza zvachose, Soromoni wakavasimudza kuita chibharo chevaranda kusvika nhasi.
\v	22	Asi pavana vaIsraeri, Soromoni haana kuita varanda; asi vaiva varume vehondo nevabatsiri vake nemachinda ake nevakuru vake nevarairi vengoro dzake nevatasvi vemabhiza vake.
\v	23	Ava vaiva vabati vevatariri vaiva pamusoro pebasa raSoromoni, mazana mashanu nemakumi mashanu, vaitonga vanhu vaibata pabasa.
\p
\v	24	Zvino mukunda waFarao wakakwira kubuda muguta raDhavhidhi kuenda kuimba yake yaaiva amuvakira; ipapo akavaka Miro.
\p
\v	25	Zvino katatu pagore Soromoni wakapa zvipo zvinopiswa nezvipo zvekuyananisa pamusoro pearitari yaakavakira Jehovha, ndokupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pairi zvaiva pamberi paJehovha. Saizvozvo wakapedza imba.
\p
\v	26	Mambo Soromoni wakaitawo zvikepe paEziyoni-Gebheri riri pedo neEroti pamahombekombe eGungwa Dzvuku panyika yeEdhomu.
\v	27	Hiramu ndokutuma muzvikepe izvo varanda vake, varume vezvikepe vaiva neruzivo rwegungwa, pamwe nevaranda vaSoromoni.
\v	28	Zvino vakasvika Ofiri, ndokutorapo goridhe, matarenda mazana mana nemakumi maviri, ndokuunza kuna mambo Soromoni.
