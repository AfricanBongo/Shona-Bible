\b	Bhuku rechina raMozisi rinonzi Numeri
\c	1
\s	Kuverengwa kwevana vaIsraeri
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJehovha wakataura kuna Mozisi murenje reSinai, mutende rekusangana, nerekutanga remwedzi wechipiri, mugore rechipiri shure kwekubuda kwavo kubva munyika yeEgipita, achiti:
\v	2	Toraiuwandu hweungano yose yevana vaIsraeri zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba avo, neuwandu hwemazita, murume umwe neumwe, nemisoro yavo.
\v	3	Kubvapamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vano gona kubudira kuhondo kwaIsraeri, muchavaverenga nemauto avo, iwe naAroni.
\v	4	Pachavanemwi murume werudzi rumwe nerumwe, musoro umwe neumwe weimba yemadzibaba ake.
\p
\v	5	Zvinoawa mazita evarume vachamira nemwi: WekwaRubheni, Erizuri, mwanakomana waShedheuri.
\v	6	WekwaSimioni,Sherumieri, mwanakomana waZurishadhai.
\v	7	WekwaJudha,Nashoni, mwanakomana waAminadhabhi.
\v	8	WekwaIsasikari,Netaneri, mwanakomana waZuwari.
\v	9	WekwaZebhuruni,Eriabhu, mwanakomana waHeroni.
\v	10	Vekuvana vaJosefa: WekwaEfuremu, Erishama, mwanakomana waAmihudhi; wekwaManase, Gamarieri, mwanakomana waPedhazuri.
\v	11	WekwaBhenjamini, Abhidhani, mwanakomana waGidhioni.
\v	12	WekwaDhani, Ahiezeri, mwanakomana waAmishadhai.
\v	13	WekwaAsheri, Pagieri, mwanakomana waOkirani.
\v	14	WekwaGadhi, Eriasafi, mwanakomana waDheueri.
\v	15	WekwaNafutari, Ahira, mwanakomana waEnani.
\v	16	Ndivo vakadanwa veungano, ari machinda emarudzi emadzibaba avo; yaiva misoro yezvuru kwaIsraeri.
\p
\v	17	Zvino Mozisi naAroni vakatora varume ava vadomewa nemazita.
\v	18	Zvino vakaunganidza ungano yose nerekutanga remwedzi wechipiri, ndokurondedzera marudzi avo pamhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, nemisoro yavo.
\v	19	Jehovha sezvaakaraira Mozisi, saizvozvo wakavaverenga murenje reSinai.
\p
\v	20	Zvino vana vaRubheni dangwe raIsraeri,  nemazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, nemisoro yavo, murume umwe neumwe kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	21	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaRubheni, zvuru makumi mana nezvitanhatu nemazana mashanu.
\p
\v	22	Vekuvana vaSimioni, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, vakaverengwa vavo zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, nemisoro yavo, murume umwe neumwe kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	23	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaSimioni, zvuru makumi mashanu nezvipfumbamwe nemazana matatu.
\p
\v	24	Vekuvana vaGadhi, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	25	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaGadhi, zvuru makumi mana nezvishanu nemazana matanhatu nemakumi mashanu.
\p
\v	26	Vekuvana vaJudha, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	27	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaJudha, zvuru makumi manomwe nezvina nemazana matanhatu.
\p
\v	28	Vekuvana vaIsasikari, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	29	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaIsasikari, zvuru makumi mashanu nezvina nemazana mana.
\p
\v	30	Vekuvana vaZebhuruni, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	31	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaZebhuruni, zvuru makumi mashanu nezvinomwe nemazana mana.
\p
\v	32	Vekuvana vaJosefa: Vekuvana vaEfuremu, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	33	vakaverengwa vavo verudzi rwaEfuremu, zvuru makumi mana nemazana mashanu.
\p
\v	34	Vekuvana vaManase, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	35	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaManase, zvuru makumi matatu nezviviri nemazana maviri.
\p
\v	36	Vekuvana vaBhenjamini, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	37	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaBhenjamini, zvuru makumi matatu nezvishanu nemazana mana.
\p
\v	38	Vekuvana vaDhani, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	39	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaDhani, zvuru makumi matanhatu nezviviri nemazana manomwe.
\p
\v	40	Vekuvana vaAsheri, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	41	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaAsheri, zvuru makumi mana nechimwe nemazana mashanu.
\p
\v	42	Vana vaNafutari, mazera avo zvichienderana nemhuri dzavo, neimba yemadzibaba avo, zvichienderana neuwandu hwemazita, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo,
\v	43	vakaverengwa vavo, verudzi rwaNafutari, zvuru makumi mashanu nezvitatu nemazana mana.
\p
\v	44	Ava ndivo vakaverengwa, Mozisi naAroni vavakaverenga, nemachinda aIsraeri, vari varume gumi nevaviri, umwe neumwe weimba yababa vake.
\v	45	Vaiva vakadaro vose vakaverengwa vevana vaIsraeri, neimba yemadzibaba avo, kubva pamwanakomana wemakore makumi maviri zvichikwira, vose vai gona kubudira kuhondo kwaIsraeri.
\v	46	Zvino vose vakaverengwa vaiva zvuru mazana matanhatu nezvitatu nemazana mashanu nemakumi mashanu.
\p
\v	47	Asi vaRevhi nerudzi rwemadzibaba avo havana kuverengwa pakati pavo.
\v	48	Nokuti Jehovha waiva ataura kuna Mozisi achiti:
\v	49	Koga usaverenga rudzi rwaRevhi uye usatora uwandu hwavo pakati pevana vaIsraeri.
\v	50	Asi iwe, gadza vaRevhi pamusoro petabhenakeri yechipupuriro, uye pamusoro penhumbi dzayo dzose, nepamusoro pezvinhu zvose zvayo; vachatakura tabhenakeri nenhumbi dzayo dzose, uye vachaishumira, nekudzika misasa kupoteredza tabhenakeri.
\v	51	Nepakusimuka kwetabhenakeri, vaRevhi vachaiwisira pasi; uye kana tabhenakeri ichidzikwa, vaRevhi vachaimisa; uye mweni akaswedera achaurawa.
\v	52	Zvino vana vaIsraeri vachadzika matende, umwe neumwe pamusasa wake, uye umwe neumwe pamureza wake, zvichienderana nehondo dzavo.
\v	53	Asi vaRevhi vachadzika matende kupoteredza tabhenakeri yechipupuriro, kuti kusava nehasha pamusoro peungano yevana vaIsraeri; uye vaRevhi vachachengetedza chirindo chetabhenakeri yechipupuriro.
\v	54	Zvino vana vaIsraeri vakaita zvinoenderana nezvose Jehovha zvaakaraira Mozisi; vakaita saizvozvo.
\c	10
\s	Vanorairwa kuita hwamanda mbiri dzesirivha
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJehovha wakataura kuna Mozisi achiti:
\v	2	Zviitirehwamanda dzesirivha mbiri; uchadziita dzive basa rakapfurwa; uye dzichava dzako pakudana ungano, nepakusimuka kwemusasa.
\v	3	Kanavachidziridza ungano yose ichaunganira kwauri pamukova wetende rekusangana.
\v	4	Asikana vachiridza imwe, ipapo machinda, misoro yezvuru zvaIsraeri, vachaungana kwauri.
\v	5	Pamunoridzaruzha, ipapo misasa yakadzikwa kumabvazuva ichasimuka.
\v	6	Pamunoridzaruzha rwechipiri, ipapo misasa yakadzikwa kuchamhembe ichasimuka; vacharidza ruzha rwenzendo dzavo.
\v	7	Asipakuunganidza ungano mucharidza, asi musaridza ruzha.
\v	8	Zvinovanakomana vaAroni, vapristi, vacharidza nehwamanda; uye vachava kwamuri chimiso nekusingaperi pamazera enyu.
\v	9	Zvinokana muchienda kuhondo munyika menyu makatarisana nemuvengi anokudzvinyirirai, ipapo mucharidza ruzha nehwamanda; zvino mucharangarirwa pamberi paJehovha Mwari wenyu, mugoponeswa pavavengi venyu.
\v	10	Uye nezuva rekufara kwenyu uye nemitambo yenyu yakatarwa nepakutanga kwemwedzi yenyu mucharidza nehwamanda pamusoro pezvipo zvenyu zvinopiswa nepamusoro pezvibairo zvezvipo zvenyu zvekuyananisa, kuti zvive kwamuri chirangaridzo pamberi paMwari wenyu; ndini Jehovha Mwari wenyu.
\s	VaIsraeri vanosimuka paSinai
\p
\v	11	Zvino zvakaitika negore rechipiri nemwedzi wechipiri neremakumi maviri remwedzi, gore rakasimudzwa rikabva pamusoro petabhenakeri yeuchapupu.
\v	12	Zvino vana vaIsraeri vakafamba kubva murenje reSinai, nenzendo dzavo; gore ndokumira murenje reParani.
\v	13	Zvino vakatanga kusimuka sekuraira kwaJehovha neruoko rwaMozisi.
\v	14	Kwakasimuka mureza wemusasa wevana vaJudha pakutanga nehondo dzavo; nepamusoro pehondo yake paiva naNaashoni mwanakomana waAminadhabhu.
\v	15	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaIsasikari paiva naNetaneri mwanakomana waZuwari.
\v	16	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaZebhuruni paiva naEriabhu mwanakomana waHeroni.
\v	17	Zvino tabhenakeri yakawiswa; vanakomana vaGerishoni nevanakomana vaMerari ndokusimuka vakatakura tabhenakeri.
\p
\v	18	Zvino kwakasimuka mureza wemusasa waRubheni nehondo dzavo; nepamusoro pehondo yake paiva naErizuri mwanakomana waShedheuri.
\v	19	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaSimioni paiva naSherumieri mwanakomana waZurishadhai.
\v	20	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaGadhi paiva naEriasafi mwanakomana waDheueri.
\v	21	Zvino kwakasimuka VaKohati, vakatakura imba tsvene; ne vamwe vakamisa tabhenakeri vasati vasvika.
\p
\v	22	Zvino kwakasimuka mureza wemusasa wevana vaEfuremu nehondo dzavo; nepamusoro pehondo yake paiva naErishama mwanakomana waAmihudhi.
\v	23	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaManase paiva naGamarieri mwanakomana waPedhazuri.
\v	24	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaBhenjamini paiva naAbhidhani mwanakomana waGidheoni.
\p
\v	25	Zvino kwakasimuka mureza wemusasa wevana vaDhani, vaiva vekupedzisira pamisasa yose nehondo dzavo; nepamusoro pehondo yake paiva naAhiezeri mwanakomana waAmishadhai.
\v	26	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaAsheri paiva naPagieri mwanakomana waOkirani.
\v	27	Nepamusoro pehondo yerudzi rwevana vaNafutari paiva naAhira mwanakomana waEnani.
\v	28	Uku kusimuka kwevana vaIsraeri nehondo dzavo kana vachifamba.
\p
\v	29	Zvino Mozisi wakati kuna Hobhabhu, mwanakomana waReueri, muMidhiani, mukarahwa waMozisi: Isu tobva toenda kunzvimbo Jehovha yaakati pamusoro payo: Ndichakupai iyo. Handei nesu, tigokuitirai zvakanaka; nokuti Jehovha wakataura zvakanaka pamusoro paIsraeri.
\v	30	Iye akati kwaari: Handingaendi, asi ndichaenda kunyika yekwedu nekuhama dzangu.
\v	31	Iye ndokuti: Musatisiya henyu, nokuti sezvamunoziva kuti tinodzika  sei matende edu murenje, muchava semeso edu.
\v	32	Zvichaitika kuti kana muchifamba nesu, hongu zvichaitika izvo zvakanaka Jehovha zvaachatiitira zvakanaka nazvo, tichakuitirai zvakanaka.
\v	33	Zvino vakabva pagomo raJehovha, rwendo rwemazuva matatu; areka yesungano yaJehovha ikafamba pamberi pavo rwendo rwemazuva matatu, kuvatsvakira nzvimbo yekuzorora.
\v	34	Zvino gore raJehovha raiva pamusoro pavo masikati pakusimuka kwavo pamusasa.
\v	35	Zvino zvakaitika pakusimuka kweareka, Mozisi wakati: Mukai, Jehovha, kuchaparara vavengi venyu, nevanokuvengai ngavatize pamberi penyu.
\v	36	Zvino pakuzorora kwayo, akati: Dzokai Jehovha,  kuzvuru gumi zvezvuru zvaIsraeri.
\c	11
\s	Vanhu vanonyunyuta paTabhera
\p
\v	1	Zvinovanhu vakati vachinyunyuta, zvaiva zvakaipa munzeve dzaJehovha; zvino Jehovha wakati azvinzwa, hasha dzake dzikapfuta; moto waJehovha ndokupfuta pakati pavo, ndokuparadza pamucheto wemusasa.
\v	2	Zvinovanhu vakachema kuna Mozisi; Mozisi ndokunyengetera kuna Jehovha; moto ukadzimwa.
\v	3	Zvinowakatumidza zita renzvimbo iyo Tabhera, nokuti moto waJehovha wakapfuta pakati pavo.
\s	Vanhu vanonyunyutazve paKibhuroti-Hatava; vanopiwa zvihuta
\p
\v	4	Zvinozvaunga zvakasangana zvaiva zviri pakati pavo vakashuva chishuvo; naizvozvo vana vaIsraeri vakachemawo zvekare misodzi ndokuti: Ndiani achatipa nyama kuti tidye?
\v	5	Tinorangarirahove dzatakadya muEgipita pachena, magaka, nemavisi, nerikisi, nehanyanisi, negariki.
\v	6	Asiikozvino mweya weupenyu wedu unooma, hapana chinhu zvachose, kunze kwemana iyi pamberi pemeso edu.
\v	7	Zvinomana yaiva yakaita sembeu yekorianderi, nekutarisika kwayo  kwakaita sekutarisika kwebhedhora.
\v	8	Vanhuvaifamba-famba ndokuunganidza ndokukuya pamakuyo kana kutswa mumaturi, ndokubika muhari, ndokuita makeke nayo; uye kunaka kwayo kwaiita sekunaka kwemuto wemafuta.
\v	9	Kanadova richiwira pamusasa usiku, mana yaiwa pamusoro pawo.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Mozisi wakanzwa vanhu vachichema misodzi padzimba dzavo, umwe neumwe pamukova wetende rake; zvino hasha dzaJehovha dzikapfuta zvikuru; ndokuva zvakaipa pameso aMozisi.
\v	11	Mozisi ndokuti kuna Jehovha: Makarwadzisirei muranda wenyu, uye ndakaregerei kuwana nyasha pameso enyu, kuti muise mutoro wevanhu ava vose pamusoro pangu?
\v	12	Ini ndakatora mimba yevanhu vose ava here? Ndini ndakavabereka here, kuti muti kwandiri: Vatakure pachipfuva chako, semureri wechirume anotakura mwana anoyamwa, uvaise kunyika yamakapikira kumadzibaba avo?
\v	13	Ndichawanepi nyama yekupa vanhu ava vose? Nokuti vanondichemera vachiti: Tipe nyama kuti tidye.
\v	14	Ini handigoni kutakura vanhu ava vose ndoga, nokuti zvinondiremera kwazvo.
\v	15	Zvino kana mandiitira saizvozvo, ndiurayei zvekundiuraya henyu, kana ndawana nyasha pameso enyu, ndigorega kuona zvakaipa zvangu.
\p
\v	16	Zvino Jehovha wakati kuna Mozisi: Ndiunganidzire varume makumi manomwe vevakuru vaIsraeri, vaunoziva kuti vakuru vevanhu, nevatariri vavo; ugouya navo kutende rekusangana, kuti vamirepo newe.
\v	17	Zvino ndichaburuka ndigotaura newe ipapo; zvino ndichatsaura kubva pamweya uri pamusoro pako, ndigoisa pamusoro pavo; zvino vachatakura mutoro wevanhu pamwe newe, kuti iwe urege kutakura woga.
\v	18	Zvino uti kuvanhu: Zviitirei vatsvene mangwana, nokuti muchadya nyama; nokuti makachema munzeve dzaJehovha muchiti: Ndiani achatipa nyama tidye? Nokuti zvaiva zvakatinakira muEgipita. Naizvozvo Jehovha achakupai nyama, uye muchadya.
\v	19	Hamungadyi zuva rimwe, kwetewo mazuva maviri, kwetewo mazuva mashanu, kwetewo mazuva gumi, kwetewo mazuva makumi maviri.
\v	20	Kusvika pamwedzi wose, kusvika yabuda kumhino dzenyu, ndokusemesa kwamuri; nokuti makaramba Jehovha ari pakati penyu, mukachema pamberi pake muchiti: Takabudirei kubva muEgipita?
\v	21	Asi Mozisi wakati: Vanhu vetsoka vandiri pakati pavo zvuru mazana matanhatu, zvino moti: Ndichavapa nyama, kuti vadye mwedzi wose!
\v	22	Makwai nemombe zvichabairwa ivo kuti zvivaringane here? Kana vachaunganidzirwa hove dzose dzegungwa kuti dzivaringane here?
\v	23	Zvino Jehovha wakati kuna Mozisi: Ikozvino ruoko rwaJehovha rwakafupiswa here? Ikozvino uchaona kana shoko rangu richiitika kwauri kana kwete.
\p
\v	24	Zvino Mozisi wakabuda ndokuudza vanhu mashoko aJehovha; ndokuunganidza varume makumi manomwe vevakuru vevanhu, ndokuvamisa vakakomba tende.
\v	25	Zvino Jehovha wakaburuka mugore ndokutaura kwaari, ndokutsaura pamweya waiva pamusoro pake ndokuupa kuvakuru makumi manomwe; zvino zvakaitika mweya wakati achigara pamusoro pavo vakaporofita, asi havana kuzoitazve.
\v	26	Asi kwakasara varume vaviri pamusasa; zita reumwe raiva Eridhadhi, nezita reumwe Medhadhi; mweya ndokugara pamusoro pavo, nokuti vaiva vari pakati pevakanyorwa, asi havana kubudira kutende; ndokuporofita pamusasa.
\v	27	Zvino kwakamhanya jaya, ndokuudza Mozisi ndokuti: Eridhadhi naMedhadhi vanoporofita pamusasa.
\v	28	Zvino Joshua mwanakomana waNuni muranda waMozisi weujaya hwake wakapindura ndokuti: Ishe wangu, Mozisi, vadzivisei.
\v	29	Mozisi ndokuti kwaari: Une godo nekuda kwangu here? Dai vanhu vose vaJehovha vaiva vaporofita, kuti Jehovha aise mweya wake pamusoro pavo!
\v	30	Zvino Mozisi wakadzokera kumusasa, iye nevakuru vaIsraeri.
\p
\v	31	Zvino kwakabuda mhepo kubva kuna Jehovha, ndokuunza zvihuta kubva kugungwa, ndokuwisira pamusasa, panenge rwendo rwezuva kurutivi  rumwe, nerwendo rwezuva kune rumwe, kupoteredza musasa; uye zvaiva makubhiti maviri pamusoro penyika.
\v	32	Zvino vanhu vakasimuka zuva iro rose, neusiku hwose, nezuva rose raitevera, zvino vakaunganidza zvihuta. Uyo waiva nezvishomasa wakaunganidza mahomeri gumi; vakazvinikira-nikira kumativi ose emusasa.
\v	33	Nyama ichiri pakati pemazino avo, isati yatsengwa, kutsamwa kwaJehovha kwakapfutira vanhu, Jehovha ndokurova vanhu nehosha huru kwazvo.
\v	34	Naizvozvo wakatumidza zita renzvimbo iyo Kibhuroti-Hatava, nokuti ipapo vakaviga vanhu vakachiva.
\v	35	Vanhu vakasimuka kubva Kibhuroti-Hatava kuenda kuHazeroti; ndokugara paHazeroti.
\c	12
\s	Miriamu anorohwa nemaperembudzi nokuti iye naAroni vakamukira Mozisi
\p
\v	1	ZvinoMiriamu naAroni vakataura vachipokana naMozisi nekuda kwemukadzi muItiyopiya waaiva awana; nokuti waiva awana mukadzi muItiyopiya.
\v	2	Zvinovakati: Zvirokwazvo Jehovha wakataura naMozisi oga here? Haana kutaurawo nesu here? Jehovha ndoku zvinzwa.
\v	3	Zvinomurume Mozisi waiva munyoro kwazvo kupfuura vanhu vose vaiva pachiso chenyika.
\v	4	ZvinoJehovha wakataura pakarepo kuna Mozisi nekuna Aroni nekuna Miriamu: Budirai imwi vatatu kutende rekusangana. Vatatu ndokubuda.
\v	5	ZvinoJehovha wakaburuka mushongwe yegore, ndokumira  pamukova wetende, ndokudana Aroni naMiriamu; ivo vaviri vakabuda.

\s	Mvura yeMeribha
\p
\v	1	Ipapovana vaIsraeri, ungano yose, vakasvika murenje reZini nemwedzi wekutanga; vanhu ndokugara muKadheshi; Miriamu ndokufirapo, ndokuvigwapo.
\v	2	Zvinopaiva pasina mvura yeungano; zvino vakaunganira Mozisi naAroni.
\v	3	Vanhundokupikisana naMozisi, ndokutaura, vachiti: Dai takafa panguva yakafa hama dzedu pamberi paJehovha!
\v	4	Makauisireiungano yaJehovha murenje rino, kuti isu nezvipfuwo zvedu tifiremo?
\v	5	Zvinomakatikwidzirei kubva muEgipita, kutiunza panzvimbo iyi yakaipa? Haizi nzvimbo yembeu kana yemaonde kana yemizambiringa kana yemapomegiranati; uye hapana mvura yekunwa.
\v	6	ZvinoMozisi naAroni vakabva pamberi peungano kuenda kumukova wetende rekusangana ndokuwira pasi nezviso zvavo; kubwinya kwaJehovha ndokuonekwa kwavari.
\p
\v	7	ZvinoJehovha wakataura kuna Mozisi achiti:
\v	8	Toratsvimbo, ugounganidza ungano, iwe naAroni mukuru wako, mugotaura kudombo pamberi pemeso avo, zvino richapa mvura yaro, uye iwe uchavabudisira mvura kubva padombo, kuti ugopa ungano nezvipfuwo zvavo zvinwe.
\v	9	ZvinoMozisi wakatora tsvimbo kubva pamberi paJehovha sezvaakamuraira.
\v	10	Zvino Mozisi naAroni vakaunganidza ungano pamberi pedombo, ndokuti kwavari: Inzwai ikozvino, imwi vapanduki, tichakubudisirai mvura kubva padombo iri here?
\v	11	Zvino Mozisi wakasimudza ruoko rwake, ndokurova dombo kaviri netsvimbo yake; mvura zhinji ndokubuda, ungano ikanwa, nezvipfuwo zvavo.
\p
\v	12	Zvino Jehovha wakataura kuna Mozisi nekuna Aroni  achiti: Nokuti hamuna kunditenda, kundiita mutsvene pameso evana vaIsraeri, naizvozvo hamungapinzi ungano iyi munyika yandakavapa.
\v	13	Iyi imvura yeMeribha, nokuti vana vaIsraeri vakapikisana naJehovha; zvino akaitwa mutsvene mavari.
\p
\v	14	Zvino Mozisi wakatuma nhume kuna mambo weEdhomu kubva kuKadheshi  achiti: Ndizvo zvinoreva munin'ina wako Israeri: Iwe unoziva nhamo dzose dzakatiwira;
\v	15	kuti madzibaba edu akaburukira Egipita, uye takagara muEgipita mazuva mazhinji, vaEgipita ndokutiitira zvakaipa isu nemadzibaba edu.
\v	16	Patakachema kuna Jehovha, wakanzwa inzwi redu, ndokutuma mutumwa ndokutibudisa paEgipita; zvino tarirai, tiri muKadheshi, guta riri pamucheto wemuganhu wenyika yako.
\v	17	Tirege hako tipfuure nepanyika yako; hatingaguri nepaminda, kana nepaminda yemizambiringa; uye hatinganwi mvura yematsime; tichafamba nenzira yamambo; hatingatsaukiri kurudyi kana kuruboshwe, kusvika tapfuura muganhu wako.
\v	18	Zvino Edhomu wakati kwaari: Haungapfuuri nekwandiri, zvimwe ndingakubudira nemunondo.
\v	19	Vana vaIsraeri ndokuti kwaari: Tichafamba nenzira huru; uye kana ini nezvipfuwo zvangu tikanwa pamvura yako, ipapo ndichapa mutengo wayo; koga ndichapfuura nemakumbo angu pasina chimwe.
\v	20	Iye ndokuti: Haungapfuuri. Zvino Edhomu wakamubudira nevanhu vazhinji uye neruoko rwune simba.
\v	21	Naizvozvo Edhomu wakaramba kutendera Israeri kupfuura nepamuganhu wake. Ipapo Israeri wakatsauka kwaari.

\v	24	Aroni achaunganidzwa kuvanhu vake; nokuti haangapindi munyika yandakapa vana vaIsraeri, nokuti makapandukira inzwi rangu pamvura yeMeribha.

\v	8	ZvinoJehovha wakati kuna Mozisi: Uzviitire nyoka inopfuta, ugoiisa padanda; zvino zvichaitika kuti umwe neumwe anorumwa paanoitarisa achararama.
\v	9	ZvinoMozisi wakaita nyoka yendarira, ndokuiisa padanda; zvino zvakaitika kuti kana nyoka ikaruma munhu, paanotarisa nyoka yendarira, wakararama.

\v	11	Zvino vakabva Obhoti ndokudzika matende paIje-Abharimi murenje rakanangana neMoabhu nechekumabvazuva.

\v	18	Machinda akachera tsime iri, makurukota evanhu akarichera, nemupi wemurairo, netsvimbo dzawo. Zvino kubva murenje  vakaenda Matana;
\v	19	ndokubva Matana kuenda Naharieri; ndokubva Naharieri kuenda Bhamoti;
\v	20	zvino kubva Bhamoti kumupata uri kubani raMoabhu, kunhongonya yePisiga rakatarisa pasi pachiso cherenje.

\v	21	Zvino Israeri wakatuma nhume kuna Sihoni mambo wevaAmori achiti:
\v	22	Rega ndipfuure panyika yako; hatingatsaukiri kuminda kana kuminda yemizambiringa; hatinganwi mvura yetsime; tichafamba nenzira yamambo kusvika tapfuura pamiganhu yako.

\v	24	Zvino Israeri wakamurova nekwakapinza kwemunondo ndokuva mwene wenyika yake, kubva paArinoni kusvika paJabhoki kusvika pavana vaAmoni; nokuti muganhu wevana vaAmoni waiva wakasimba.

\v	26	Nokuti Heshibhoni raiva guta raSihoni mambo wevaAmori; iye waiva arwa namambo wekutanga waMoabhu, ndokutora nyika yake yose kubva muruoko rwake kusvika paArinoni.
\v	27	Naizvozvo vanoshandisa tsumo vanoti: Uyai muHeshibhoni, guta raSihoni ngarivakwe, risimbiswe.
\v	28	Nokuti moto wakabuda muHeshibhoni, murazvo paguta raSihoni, wakaparadza Ari weMoabhu, madzishe enzvimbo dzakakwirira dzaArinoni.
\v	29	Une nhamo iwe Moabhu, maparadzwa, haiwa vanhu veKemoshi! Wakapa vanakomana kuva vatizi, nevanasikana vake muutapwa, kuna Sihoni mambo wevaAmori.
\v	30	Takavapfura; Heshibhoni rakaparara kusvika kuDhibhoni; zvino takaaparadza kusvika kuNofa rinosvika kuMedhebha.
\p
\v	31	Israeri ndokugara munyika yevaAmori.
\v	32	Zvino Mozisi wakatuma kunoshora Jazeri; zvino vakatapa misha yaro, ndokudzingira kunze vaAmori vaivapo.

\v	2	ZvinoBharaki, mwanakomana waZipori, wakaona zvose Israeri zvaakaitira vaAmori.
\v	3	Moabhundokutya kwazvo chiso chevanhu ava, nokuti vaiva vazhinji; Moabhu ndokuvhunduka nekuda kwevana vaIsraeri.
\v	4	ZvinoMoabhu wakati kuvakuru veMidhiani: Zvino boka iri richananzva zvose zvakatipoteredza, semombe inonanzva uswa hwemafuro. Zvino Bharaki, mwanakomana waZipori waiva ari mambo waMoabhu panguva iyo.
\v	5	Naizvozvowakatuma nhume kuna Bharamu mwanakomana waBheori kuPetori iri parwizi rwenyika yevana vevanhu vekwake kunomudana achiti: Tarira kune vanhu vakabuda kubva Egipita, tarira, vakafukidzira chiso chenyika, uye vogara pakanangana neni.

\v	7	Zvinovakuru vaMoabhu nevakuru vaMidhiani vakabva vane  mubairo wekuvuka muruoko rwavo, ndokusvika kuna Bharamu, ndokutaura kwaari mashoko aBharaki.

\v	11	Tarira, vanhu vakabuda kubva Egipita vakafukidza chiso chenyika; uya ikozvino unditukire ivo; zvimwe ndingagona kurwa navo, nekuvadzinga.

\v	14	Zvino machinda aMoabhu akasimuka, ndokuenda kuna Bharaki ndokuti: Bharamu waramba kuenda nesu.

\v	16	Zvino vakasvika kuna Bharamu ndokuti kwaari: Saizvozvo Bharaki mwanakomana waZipori anoti: Usadziviswa hako kuuya kwandiri.

\v	20	Zvino Mwari wakauya kuna Bharamu usiku ndokuti kwaari: Kana varume vakauya kuzokudana, simuka uende navo, asi koga shoko randichataura kwauri, iro uchariita.

\p

\v	32	Mutumwa waJehovha ndokuti kwaari: Waroverei mbongoro hadzi yako katatu aka? Tarira, ini ndabuda kuva mudzivisi, nokuti nzira yako yakatsauka kwandiri.
\v	33	Zvino mbongoro hadzi yandiona ndokutsauka mberi kwangu katatu aka; kunze kwekuti yatsauka mberi kwangu, zvirokwazvo ikozvino ndingadai ndakuurayawo, uye ndaichengeta iri mhenyu.
\v	34	Zvino Bharamu wakati kumutumwa waJehovha: Ndatadza, nokuti ndange ndisingazivi kuti umire munzira uchindipikisa; naizvozvo ikozvino, kana zvakaipa pameso ako, ndichadzokera.
\v	35	Zvino mutumwa waJehovha wakati kuna Bharamu: Enda nevarume ava, asi koga shoko randichataura kwauri, ndiro rauchataura. Naizvozvo Bharamu akaenda nemachinda aBharaki.

\v	37	Zvino Bharaki wakati kuna Bharamu: Handina kutuma kwazvo  nhume kwauri ndichikudana here? Sei usina kusvika kwandiri? Handigoni kwazvo kukukudza here?
\v	38	Bharamu ndokuti kuna Bharaki: Tarira, ndasvika kwauri. Ikozvino ndinogona kwazvo kutaura chipi nechipi here? Shoko Mwari raanoisa mumuromo mangu ndiro randichataura.
\v	39	Zvino Bharamu wakaenda naBharaki ndokusvika paKiriati-Huzoti.
\v	40	Bharaki ndokubaya nzombe nemakwai ndokutumira kuna Bharamu nekumachinda aiva naye.
\v	41	Zvino zvakaitika mangwanani kuti Bharaki wakatora Bharamu ndokumukwidza panzvimbo dzakakwirira dzaBhaari, kuti aone aripo magumo evanhu.
