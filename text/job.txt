\b	Jobho
\c	1
\s	Jobho munhu wakarurama
\p
\v	1	Kwaivakune murume munyika yeUzi, zita rake raiva Jobho. Zvino murume uyu waiva akaperera, uye akarurama, uye waitya Mwari, achinyenyeredza chakaipa.
\v	2	Zvinowakaberekerwa vanakomana vanomwe nevakunda vatatu.
\v	3	Uyezvipfuwo zvake zvaiva makwai zvuru zvinomwe, nemakamera zvuru zvitatu, nezvipani zvemombe mazana mashanu, nembongoro hadzi mazana mashanu, nevaranda vazhinji kwazvo. Naizvozvo munhu uyu waiva mukuru pane vanhu vose vekumabvazuva.
\p
\v	4	Zvinovanakomana vake vaienda ndokuita mabiko muimba yeuyo neuyo nezuva rake, uye vaituma ndokudana hanzvadzi dzavo nhatu kuti vadye nekunwa navo.
\v	5	Zvinozvakaitika paanenge atenderera mazuva emabiko kuti Jobho waituma ndokuvatsvenesa, ndokumuka mangwanani-ngwanani, ndokupa zvipo zvinopiswa pauwandu hwavo vose; nokuti Jobho wakati: Zvimwe vanakomana vangu vakatadza ndokutuka Mwari pamoyo yavo. Jobho waiita saizvozvo mazuva ose.
\s	Satani anotenderwa kuedza Jobho
\p
\v	6	Zvinopakava nezuva vanakomana vaMwari pavakauya kuzvimisa pamberi paJehovha, Satani ndokusvikawo pakati pavo.
\v	7	Jehovhandokuti kuna Satani: Wabvepi? Satani ndokupindura Jehovha, ndokuti: Pakupota-pota nenyika, nepakufamba-famba pairi.
\v	8	Jehovhandokuti kuna Satani: Waisa here moyo wako pamuranda wangu Jobho? Nokuti hakuna wakaita saiye panyika, munhu wakaperera uye wakarurama, uye anotya Mwari, uye anonyenyeredza chakaipa.
\v	9	Satanindokupindura Jehovha, ndokuti: Jobho anotya Mwari pasina here?
\v	10	Hamuna kumushoshera here iye neimba yake nezvose zvaanazvo kupoteredza? Makaropafadza basa remaoko ake, uye zvipfuwo zvake zvakawanda kwazvo panyika.
\v	11	Asi zvirokwazvo tandavadzai ruoko rwenyu ikozvino, mugobata zvose zvaanazvo; zvirokwazvo achakutukai pachiso chenyu.
\v	12	Zvino Jehovha wakati kuna Satani: Tarira, zvose zvaanazvo zviri paruoko rwako; koga kwaari usatandavadzira ruoko rwako. Zvino Satani wakabuda kubva pamberi paJehovha.
\p
\v	13	Zvino kwakava nezuva vanakomana vake nevakunda vake pavaidya nekunwa waini paimba yemukoma vavo, dangwe.
\v	14	Zvino kwakauya nhume kuna Jobho, ndokuti: Mombe dzanga dzichirima, nembongoro hadzi dzichifura parutivi rwadzo;
\v	15	VaShebha ndokuvamba, ndokudzitora, ndokurova varanda nekwakapinza kwemunondo, zvino koga ini ndoga ndapukunyuka kuti ndikuzivisei.
\v	16	Achiri kutaura, kwakasvikawo umwe, ndokuti: Moto waMwari wawa kubva kumatenga ndokupisa makwai nevaranda, ndokuvapedza; uye koga ini ndoga ndapukunyuka kuti ndikuzivisei.
\v	17	Achiri kutaura, umwewo wakasvika ndokuti: VaKaradhiya vaita mapoka matatu, ndokuwira pamakamera, ndokumatora, ndokurova varanda nekwakapinza kwemunondo; uye koga ini ndoga ndapukunyuka kuti ndikuzivisei.
\v	18	Achiri kutaura umwewo wakasvika ndokuti: Vanakomana venyu nevakunda venyu vanga vachidya nekunwa waini muimba yemukoma wavo, dangwe.
\v	19	Zvino tarirai, kwasvika mhepo huru ichibva mhiri kwerenje, ndokurova makona mana eimba, ndokuwira pamusoro pemajaya, ivo ndokufa; zvino koga ini ndoga ndapukunyuka kuti ndikuzivisei.
\v	20	Zvino Jobho wakasimuka, akabvarura nguvo yake, ndokuveura musoro wake, ndokuwira pasi ndokunamata,
\v	21	ndokuti: Ndakabuda ndiri musvi mudumbu ramai vangu, zvino ndinodzokerako ndiri musvi; Jehovha wakapa, Jehovha wakatora, zita raJehovha ngarikudzwe.
\p
\v	22	Pane izvi zvose Jobho haana kutadza, uye haana kuverengera upenzi kuna Mwari.
\c	10
\s	Jobho anochema neupenyu hwake
\p
\v	1	Mweyaweupenyu wangu waneta neupenyu hwangu; ndichasiya kunyunyuta kwangu pamusoro pangu; ndichataura mukurwadza kwemweya weupenyu wangu.
\v	2	Ndichatikuna Mwari: Musanditi ndine mhosva. Ndizivisei kuti sei muchipikisana neni.
\v	3	Zvakanakakwamuri here kuti mumanikidze, kuti muzvidze basa remaoko enyu, mugovhenekera pamusoro pezano revakaipa?
\v	4	Munemeso enyama here? Munoona sekuona kwemunhu here?
\v	5	Mazuvaenyu akafanana nemazuva emunhu here? Makore enyu akafanana nemazuva emunhu here;
\v	6	zvamunobvunzazveuipi hwangu, nekunzvera zvechivi changu?
\v	7	Zvichienderananeruzivo rwenyu kuti handisi mutadzi; asi hakuna anonunura paruoko rwenyu.
\p
\v	8	Maokoenyu akandiumba nekundiita pamwechete nhivi dzose, zvakadaro munondimedza.
\v	9	Rangariraihenyu kuti makandiita sevhu; uye muchandidzosera muguruva here?
\v	10	Hamuna kundidurura semukaka here, nekundikodzeka semukaka wakakora?
\v	11	Makandifukidza neganda nenyama, ndokundisunganidza nemapfupa nemarunda.
\v	12	Makandiitira upenyu netsitsi, uye hanya dzenyu dzakachengeta mweya wangu.
\p
\v	13	Asi izvi makazvivanza mumoyo menyu; ndinoziva kuti ichi chiri mamuri.
\v	14	Kana ndichitadza munondicherekedza, uye hamungandiregi ndisina mhosva pachivi changu.
\v	15	Kana ndiri wakaipa, ndine nhamo; kana ndakarurama, handingasimudzi musoro wangu. Ndizere nenyadzi, naizvozvo onai kurwadziwa kwangu.
\v	16	Nokuti kunozvikudza; seshumba munondivhima. Modzoka mozviita anoshamisa kwandiri.
\v	17	Munovandudza uchapupu hwenyu kurwisana neni, uye munowedzera kutsamwa kwenyu kundipikisa; shanduko nehondo zvineni.
\p
\v	18	Zvino makandibudisirei pachizvaro? Dai ndaiva ndapera, uye ziso risina kundiona.
\v	19	Ndaizova sekuti ndaiva ndisipo; ndingadai ndakatakurirwa kuguva kubva muchizvaro.
\v	20	Mazuva angu haasi mashoma here? Zvino gumai, uye ndiregei, kuti ndifare zvishoma;
\v	21	ndisati ndaenda ndikasazodzoka, kunyika yerima nemumvuri werufu;
\v	22	nyika yerima guru, serima; mumvuri werufu, pasina kurongeka, uye inobwinya serima.
\c	11
\s	Zofari anoti Jobho wakatadza
\p
\v	1	ZvinoZofari muNaama wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Uwanduhwemashoko hahupindurwi here? Uye mutaurisi anganzi haana mhosva here?
\v	3	Kurotomokakwako kunganyaradza vanhu here? Paunohomera, pasinawo ano kunyadzisa.
\v	4	Nokutiwakati: Dzidziso yangu yakarurama, uye ndakachena pameso enyu.
\v	5	Asizvakadaro dai Mwari ataura, ndokuzarura miromo yake kwauri;
\v	6	kutiakuzivise zvakavanzika zveuchenjeri, kuti zvakapetwa kaviri neuchenjeri hwechokwadi. Zvino ziva kuti Mwari anodana kwauri zvishoma pamhosva yako.
\p
\v	7	Ungawanakudzama kwaMwari here? Ungawana Wemasimbaose kusvika pakuperera here?
\v	8	Zvakakwirira sekumatenga; ungaitei? Zvakadzika kupfuura gehena; unozivei?
\v	9	Chiyerochazvo chakareba kupfuura nyika, uye chakapamhama kupfuura gungwa.
\v	10	Kana akapfuura, ndokupfigira kana kuunganidza, ndiyani angamudzivisa?
\v	11	Nokuti iye anoziva vanhu pasina, uye anoona uipi; naizvozvo haangachenjereri here?
\v	12	Nokuti munhu dununu angava wakachenjera, kunyange munhu angaberekwa  semhuru yembongoro yesango.
\p
\v	13	Kana iwe wakagadzirira moyo wako, uye wakatandavadzira maoko ako kwaari;
\v	14	kana uipi huri muruoko rwako, huise kure, uye usarega kudarika kuchigara mumatende ako.
\v	15	Nokuti ipapo uchasimudza chiso chako usina gwapa, uye wakasimba, uye hautyi.
\v	16	Nokuti iwe uchakanganwa dambudziko, unorangarira semvura dzapfuura.
\v	17	Kureba kweupenyu  hwako huchajeka kupfuura masikati makuru;  kunyange kwasviba, kuchava semangwanani.
\v	18	Zvino uchava nechivimbo, nokuti kune tariro; uchatsvaka, ucharara mukuchengetedzeka.
\v	19	Ucharara, uye hakuna anga kubvundisa; nevazhinji vachagombedzera  pachiso chako.
\v	20	Asi meso evakaipa achapera; neutiziro huchaparara kubva kwavari; netariro yavo ichava kupera kwemweya.
\c	12
\s	Jobho anozvidavirira
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJobho wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Nokutizvirokwazvo muri vanhu, uye uchenjeri huchafa nemwi.
\v	3	Iniwondine moyo semwi; handisi mudiki kwamuri. Zvino ndiyani kwaari kusina zvinhu seizvi?
\v	4	Ndiriari chiseko kushamwari yake, anodana kuna Mwari achimupindura; uyo asina mhosva  uye wakarurama chiseko.
\v	5	Mwenjeunozvidzwa mumirangariro yewakachengetedzeka; wakagadzirira vanotedzemuka netsoka.
\v	6	Matendeemakororo ane rugare, uye vanotsamwisa Mwari vakachengetedzeka, uyo muruoko rwake Mwari anouisa.
\p
\v	7	Asizvirokwazvo bvunzai henyu mhuka, zvino dzichakudzidzisai, neshiri dzematenga, zvino dzichakuudzai.
\v	8	Kanataurai kunyika, zvino ichakudzidzisai; nehove dzegungwa dzichakurondedzerai.
\v	9	Ndianiasingazivi pazvinhu izvi zvose kuti ruoko rwaJehovha rwakaita izvi?
\v	10	Uyo muruoko rwake mune mweya weupenyu wechipenyu chimwe nechimwe, nemweya wenyama yose yemunhu.
\p
\v	11	Nzeve haiidzi mashoko, semukanwa unozviravirira zvekudya here?
\v	12	Kuvakwegura kune uchenjeri, ne pakureba kwemazuva pane kunzwisisa.
\v	13	Kwaari kune uchenjeri nesimba; ane zano nekunzwisisa.
\v	14	Tarirai, anokoromora, uye hapangavakwi zve; anopfigira munhu, uye hapangazarurwi.
\v	15	Tarirai, anodzivisa dzimvura idzo ndokuoma; uye anodzituma, idzo ndokupidigura nyika.
\v	16	Kwaari kune simba neuchenjeri; wakanyengerwa uye munyengeri ndevake.
\v	17	Anoendesa vapi vemazano vapambiwa, agoita vatongi vave mapenzi.
\v	18	Anosunungura chisungo chemadzimambo, ndokusunga bhanhire pazviuno zvavo.
\v	19	Anoendesa vapristi vakapambiwa, uye anowisa vakasimba.
\v	20	Anokamura mutauro wevakatendeka, nekubvisa kunzwisisa kwevakura.
\v	21	Anodurura kushora pamusoro pemachinda, nekutembenudza bhanhire revane simba.
\v	22	Anofukura zvinhu zvakadzika kubva parima, uye anobudisira kuchiedza mumvuri werufu.
\v	23	Anokurisa marudzi, ndokuaparadza; awanze marudzi, ndokuabvisa.
\v	24	Anobvisa moyo wemisoro yevanhu venyika, ndokuvadzungairisa murenje musina nzira.
\v	25	Vanotsvangadzira murima pasina chiedza, anovadzedzeresa sechidhakwa.
\c	13
\s	Jobho anofunga kuti wakarurama
\p
\v	1	Tarirai,zvose ziso rangu raona, nzeve yangu yanzwa uye yazvinzwisisa.
\v	2	Zvichienderananeruzivo rwenyu, iniwo ndinoziva; handisi pasi penyu.
\v	3	Asiini ndichataura kune Wemasimbaose, uye ndinoshuva kukakavadzana naMwari.
\v	4	Asizvirokwazvo imwi vadzudzuri venhema, mose muri varapi pasina.
\v	5	Haiwa!Dai muchinyarara zvachose! Zvino zvichava kwamuri uchenjeri!
\p
\v	6	Inzwaihenyu kuzvidavira kwangu, uye teererai kugakava remiromo yangu.
\v	7	MuchatauriraMwari nezvakaipa here, nekumutaurira nekunyengera?
\v	8	Muchagamuchirachiso chake here? Muchamukakavadzira Mwari here?
\v	9	Zvichavazvakanaka here paanokunzverai? Kana muchamunyengera here sekunyengera munhu?
\v	10	Zvirokwazvo achakutsiurai, kana pakavanda muchigamuchira zviso.
\v	11	Ukuru hwake hahungakutyityidzirii, kutyisa kwake ndokukuwirai here?
\v	12	Zvirangaridzo zvenyu itsumo dzemadota, zvitaviro zvenyu zvitaviro zvevhu.
\p
\v	13	Nyararai, ndiregei, kuti nditaure ini, uye chipi nechipi chigondipfuura.
\v	14	Ndichatorerei nyama yangu mumazino angu, nemweya wangu ndiise muruoko rwangu?
\v	15	Kunyange akandiuraya, ndichatarisira iye. Asi ndichamiririra nzira dzangu pamberi pake.
\v	16	Ndiyewo achava ruponeso rwangu, nokuti munyepedzeri haangauyi pamberi pake.
\p
\v	17	Nzwisisai shoko rangu, netsananguro yangu nenzeve dzenyu.
\v	18	Tarirai henyu, ndaronga nyaya  yangu zvakanaka; ndinoziva kuti ini ndichanzi handina mhosva.
\v	19	Ndiani achapikisana neni? Nokuti ikozvino ndichinyarara ndichapera.
\v	20	Koga usaita zviviri kwandiri; ipapo handingazvivanzi kubva pachiso chenyu.
\v	21	Bvisirai ruoko rwenyu kure neni; uye kutyisa kwenyu kusandivhundusa.
\v	22	Zvino muchadana, uye ini ndichapindura; kana nditaure mugondipindura.
\v	23	Zvingani zvakaipa nezvivi zvangu? Ndizivisei chitadzo changu nechivi changu.
\v	24	Munovanzirei chiso chenyu, mondibata semuvengi wenyu?
\v	25	Munovhundusa shizha rinopepereswa here? Zvino muchadzingirirana nehwindi yakaoma here?
\v	26	Nokuti manyora zvinhu zvinorwadza kurwisana neni, mukandiita ndigare nhaka yezvakaipa zveujaya hwangu.
\v	27	Munoisawo tsoka dzangu mumatanda, ndokucherekedza nzira dzangu dzose; ndokuisa mutaro panotsika tsoka dzangu.
\v	28	Zvino iye sechinhu chakaora anopera, senguvo zvipfukuto zvayakadya.
\c	14
\s	Jobho anotaura pamusoro pekutambudzika kwemunhu
\p
\v	1	Munhuwakazvarwa nemukadzi ane mazuva mapfupi, uye azere dambudziko.
\v	2	Anobudaseruva, ndokusvava; anotizawo semumvuri, uye haamiri.
\v	3	Kowakadai mungamuzarurira meso enyu; ndokundipinza pakutonga pamwe nemwi.
\v	4	Ndianiangapa chinhu chakachena kubva pane chakasviba? Hakuna neumwe.
\v	5	Mazuvaake zvaakatarwa, uwandu hwemwedzi yake huri kwamuri, imwi makatara miganhu yake kuti asapfuura.
\v	6	Regaikumucherekedza, kuti azorore, kusvika afadzwa nezuva rake semushandi wakahaiwa.
\v	7	Nokutipane tariro pamuti kana uchitemwa, kuti uchatungazve, kuti bukira rawo haringaperi.
\v	8	Kunyangemudzi wawo ukakwegurira muvhu, nehunde yawo ikafa muguruva,
\v	9	nekunhuhwirakwemvura unotunga, ndokuita matavi semuti mudiki.
\v	10	Asi murume anofa ndokuradzikwa pasi; zvirokwazvo munhu anopera, zvino aripi?
\v	11	Mvura inoenda kubva mugungwa, uye rwizi runopwa ndokuoma.
\v	12	Naizvozvo munhu anorara pasi, akasamuka; kusvika matenga asisiko havangamuki, kana kusimudzwa kubva pahope dzavo.
\p
\v	13	Haiwa dai maindivanza paSheori, kuti mundichengete pakavanda kusvika kutsamwa kwenyu kwadzokera, munditemere nguva yakatarwa, mundirangarire.
\v	14	Kana munhu afa angararama zve here? Nguva dzose dzehondo dzangu ndichamirira, kusvika kusunungurwa kwangu kwasvika.
\v	15	Muchadana, ini ndichakupindurai, muchashuva basa remaoko enyu.
\v	16	Asi ikozvino munoverenga nhambwe dzangu; hamuchenjereri pachivi changu.
\v	17	Chitadzo changu chakasungirwa muhombodo, munodzudzura pamusoro peuipi hwangu.
\v	18	Zvirokwazvo gomo rinowa rinopfupfunyuka, uye dombo rinofamba kubva panzvimbo yaro.
\v	19	Mvura inochera mabwe; nhuruka dzayo dzinokukura guruva renyika. Zvino munoparadza tariro yemunhu.
\v	20	Munomukunda nekusingaperi, agoenda; munoshandura chiso chake, uye munomuendesa.
\v	21	Vana vake vanokudzwa iye asingazivi; kana vanoderedzwa, asi asingazvitarisi.
\v	22	Asi nyama yake iri pamusoro pake iri pamarwadzo, nemweya weupenyu wake unochema mukati make.
\c	15
\s	Erifazi anoti Jobho anozvikudza
\p
\v	1	ZvinoErifazi muTemani wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Wakachenjeraangapindura here ruzivo rwemhepo, ndokuzadza dumbu rake nemhepo yekumabvazuva here?
\v	3	Angakakavaraneshoko raasingabatsiriki mariri, nenhaurwa dzisina mubairo here?
\v	4	Zvirokwazvoiwe unogura kutya, nekudzivisa munyengetero pamberi paMwari.
\v	5	Nokutimuromo wako unodzidzisa uipi hwako, uye unosarudza rurimi rwevanyepedzeri.
\v	6	Muromowako unokupa mhosva, uye handisi ini; uye miromo yako inokupupurira.
\p
\v	7	Ndiwewakatanga kuzvarwa kanhi? Kana wakaberekwa makomo asati avapo here?
\v	8	Wakanzwahere nezvezano rakavanzika raMwari? Kana kuti unogumisira uchenjeri kwauri  woga?
\v	9	Chiichaunoziva chatisingazivi? Chaunonzwisisa chisiri kwatiri?
\v	10	Patiri pane vakachena musorowo nevakwegura, vakuru kuna baba vako  pamazuva.
\v	11	Nyaradzo dzaMwari idiki kwauri here, neshoko reunyoro kwauri?
\v	12	Moyo wako unokutorerei? Uye sei meso ako achibwaira?
\v	13	Kuti ushandure mweya wako kurwisana naMwari uye ubudise mashoko mumuromo wako.
\p
\v	14	Munhu chii kuti ave wakachena, newakaberekwa nemukadzi kuti ave wakarurama?
\v	15	Tarira, haavimbi nevatsvene vake, uye matenga haana kuchena pameso ake.
\v	16	Kuzoti munhu anosemesa uye akaora, anonwa zvakaipa semvura.
\p
\v	17	Ndichakuratidza, nditeerere; nezvandakaona ndicharondedzera.
\v	18	Vachenjeri zvavakataura kubva kumadzibaba avo, uye vakasavanza.
\v	19	Kwavari chete kwakapiwa nyika, uye mutorwa kwaasingapfuuri nepakati pavo.
\v	20	Mazuva ose ewakaipa iye anozvirwadzisa, uye uwandu hwemakore hwakaisirwa mumanikidzi.
\v	21	Inzwi rezvityiso riri munzeve dzake; pakubudirira muparadzi achauya pamusoro pake.
\v	22	Haatendi kuti achadzoka kubva parima, asi kuti wakamirirwa pamunondo.
\v	23	Anombeyera zvekudya. Zviripi? Anoziva kuti zuva rerima rakagadzirirwa paruoko rwake.
\v	24	Dambudziko nekushungurudzika zvinomutyisa; zvichamukunda, samambo akagadzirira kuvamba.
\v	25	Nokuti wakatandavadzira ruoko rwake kurwisa Mwari, nekuzviita simba kurwisa Wemasimbaose.
\v	26	Anomumhanyira nemutsipa  mukukutu, nemakwande makobvu enhovo dzake.
\v	27	Nokuti wakafukidza chiso chake nemafuta ake, uye wakafutisa chiuno chake nemafuta.
\v	28	Anogara maguta ava matongo, dzimba dzisina anogaramo, dzakagadzirira kuva zvuru.
\v	29	Haangafumi, nefuma yake haingamiri, nezvibereko zvazvo hazvingatandavari pasi.
\v	30	Haangabvi parima; murazvo uchaomesa bukira rake, uye achabva nekufema kwemuromo wake.
\p
\v	31	Anonyengerwa ngaarege kuvimba zvisina maturo, nokuti zvisina maturo ndiwo mubairo wake.
\v	32	Zvichapedzwa risati rava zuva rake; nedavi rake haringavi nyoro.
\v	33	Achazuza mazambiringa ake asina kuibva semuzambiringa, ndokuzuka maruva ake semuorivhi.
\v	34	Nokuti ungano yevanyepedzeri ichava isina zvibereko, uye moto uchapedza matende evafufuri.
\v	35	Vanobata mimba yedambudziko, ndokubereka uipi, nedumbu ravo rinogadzirira unyengeri.
\c	16
\s	Jobho anochema njodzi yaanopiwa naMwari
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJobho wakapindura, ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Ndanzwazvinhu zvizhinji zvakadai; vanyaradzi vanotambudza imwi mose.

\v	2	Nokutikutsamwa kunoponda benzi, uye ruchiva rwunouraya dununu.
\v	3	Ndakaonabenzi richidzika mudzi; asi pakarepo ndakatuka nzvimbo yaro yekugara.
\v	4	Vanavaro vari kure neruponeso, uye vanopwanyiwa pasuwo; uye hapana mununuri.
\v	5	Gohoraro ane nzara anodya, uyewo anoritora muminzwa; nerudzingi rwunomedza fuma yaro.
\v	6	Nokutikusuruvara hakubudi muguruva, nedambudziko haritungi kubva muvhu.
\v	7	Zvakadaromunhu anoberekerwa dambudziko, sezvo baribari dzinodaukira kumusoro.
\p
\v	8	Asiini ndanga ndichatsvaka kuna Mwari, uye kuna Mwari ndanga ndichaisa nyaya yangu.
\v	9	Anoitazvinhu zvikuru uye zvisinganzvereki, zvinhu zvinoshamisa zvisingaverengeki.
\v	10	Anopa mvura inonaya pachiso chenyika, uye anotuma mvura pachiso che nzvimbo dzekunze.
\v	11	Kumisa pamusoro avo vari pasi, kuti vanochema vakwiridzirwe  kuchengetedzeka.
\v	12	Anokonesa mazano evanyengeri, kuti maoko avo asaita uchenjeri.
\v	13	Anobata vakachenjera muunyengeri hwavo, nezano revasakarurama rakakurumidzirwa.
\v	14	Pamasikati vanosangana nerima; uye vanotsvanzvadzira pamasikati makuru sepausiku.
\v	15	Asi anorwira murombo kubva pamunondo, kubva pamuromo wavo, uye kubva paruoko rweane simba.
\v	16	Saka murombo ane tariro, uye uipi huchadzivira muromo wahwo.
\p
\v	17	Tarirai, wakaropafadzwa munhu Mwari waanotsiura; naizvozvo kuranga kweWemasimbaose usazvidza.
\v	18	Nokuti iye anorwadzisa ndokusunga, anorova, maoko ake ndokupodza.
\v	19	Achakununura pamatambudziko matanhatu, nepane rechinomwe chakaipa hachingakubati.
\v	20	Panzara achakudzikinura kubva parufu; nepahondo kubva pasimba remunondo.
\v	21	Uchavanzwa kubva pashamhu yerurimi, uye haungatyi kuparara pakunosvika.
\v	22	Pakuparara nepanzara uchaseka, uye chikara chenyika haungatyi.
\v	23	Nokuti uchava muchitenderano nemabwe esango, nechikara chesango chichava nerugare newe.
\v	24	Zvino uchaziva kuti tende rako rine rugare, uye uchashanyira nzvimbo yako yekugara, uye haungatadzi.
\v	25	Uchazivawo kuti mbeu yako ichawanda, nezvizvarwa zvako seuswa hwenyika.
\v	26	Uchasvika paguva wakwegura, sedutu rezviyo richikwira panguva yaro.
\v	27	Tarira izvi, takazvinzvera, ndizvo; inzwa, uzvizivire iwe.
\c	6
\s	Jobho anoshuva kufa nokuti shamwari dzake hadzina hanya naye
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJobho wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Haiwa,dai kurwadziwa kwangu kwaiyerwa zvekuyerwa, nedambudziko rangu raiiswa pamwe pachikero!
\v	3	Nokutiikozvino zvingarema kupfuura jecha remakungwa. Naizvozvo mashoko angu ndeekumhanyirira.
\v	4	Nokutimiseve yeWemasimbaose iri mandiri, uturu hwawo mweya wangu unohunwa; zvityiso zvaMwari zvinozvirongedzera kundirwisa.
\v	5	Mbongoroyesango inokuma here pabumhudza? Nzombe ingaomba pazvekudya zvayo here?
\v	6	Kutichisinganaki chingadyiwa chisina munyu here? Pane kuzipa pazvichena zvezai here?
\v	7	Mweyaweupenyu wangu unoramba ku zvibata; zvakaita sezvekudya zvangu zvinosemesa.
\p
\v	8	Haiwa,dai chikumbiro changu chasvika, uye Mwari ape tariro yangu;
\v	9	kutizvifadze Mwari kundipwanya, aregedzere ruoko rwake, uye andigure.
\v	10	Ipapo zvichagara zvive nyaradzo yangu, ndaizofara pamarwadzo asingaregi; nokuti handina kuviga mashoko eMutsvene.
\v	11	Simba rangu chii kuti ndivimbe? Uye mugumo wangu chii kuti ndiredzve upenyu hwangu?
\v	12	Simba rangu isimba remabwe here? Nyama yangu indarira kanhi?
\v	13	Rubatsiro rwangu harwusi mandiri here? Uye uchenjeri hwadzingwa kwandiri here?
\p
\v	14	Anotambudzika achi nzwirwa tsitsi neshamwari dzake, asi anosiya kutyisa kweWemasimbaose.
\v	15	Hama dzangu dzakaita nekusatendeka serukova; sekuyerera kwehova vanopfuura;
\v	16	dzakasviba nekuda kwemabwe echando, panovanda magada echando;
\v	17	panguva yekudziya kwadzo dzinonyangarika; pakunenge kuchipisa dzinodzima kubva panzvimbo yadzo.
\v	18	Nzira dzerwendo rwavo dzinomonyoroka; vanokwira kurenje ndokuparara.
\v	19	Vafambi veTema vakatarira; midungwe yeShebha yakadzimirira.
\v	20	Vakanyadziswa nokuti vaiva netariro; vakasvikapo ndokunyara.
\v	21	Nokuti ikozvino hamusi chinhu; munoona chityiso, ndokutya.
\v	22	Ndareva here kuti: Uisai kwandiri? Kana: Ipai chipo nekuda kwangu kubva pafuma yenyu?
\v	23	Kana: Ndinunurei paruoko rwemuvengi? Kana: Ndidzikunurei paruoko rwevanotyisa?
\v	24	Ndidzidzisei, ipapo ini ndichanyarara; mundiite ndinzwisise pandakanganisa.
\v	25	Ane simba zvakadii mashoko akarurama; asi kutsiura kunobva kwamuri kunotsiurei?
\v	26	Munorangarira mashoko ekutsiura here, nemashoko euyo wapererwa achava mhepo?
\v	27	Hongu, munokanda  mijenya pamusoro penherera, uye munotsinhanisa shamwari yenyu.
\v	28	Naizvozvo ikozvino, zvipirei, kunditarira, nokuti zviri pamberi pechiso chenyu kana ndichireva nhema.
\v	29	Dzokai henyu, kusava kuipa; hongu dzokaizve; kururama kwangu kuri mazviri.
\v	30	Kuti pane chakaipa parurimi rwangu? Kuravira kwangu hakusiyanisi zvinhu zvakaipa here?
\c	7
\s	Pakuchema kwake Jobho anobvunza kuti Mwari anomuisirei mumatambudziko
\p
\v	1	Hakunahondo kumunhu panyika here? Uye mazuva ake  haana kuita semazuva ewakahaiwa here?
\v	2	Semurandaanoshuva mumvuri, uye sewakahaiwa anotarisira muhoro wake,
\v	3	saizvozvoini ndakagariswa nhaka yemwedzi isina maturo, uye usiku hwedambudziko hwakaverengerwa ini.
\v	4	Pandinorarapasi ndinoti: Ndichamuka rinhi? Nokuti usiku hwakareba; uye ndizere kubidirika kusvika koedza.
\v	5	Nyamayangu yakafukidzwa nehonye nemavhinga eguruva. Ganda rangu rinofunuka, uye rinoyerera  urwa.
\v	6	Mazuvaangu anokurumidza pane chirukiso chemuruki, uye anopera pasina tariro.
\v	7	Rangarirakuti upenyu hwangu imhepo; ziso rangu harichazoonizve zvakanaka.
\v	8	Zisorewakandiona harichazondioni; meso ako ari pamusoro pangu, asi handichazovipo.
\v	9	Gorerinopera ndokunyangarika; saizvozvo anoburukira kuguva haangakwiri.
\v	10	Haangadzokerizve kumba kwake; nenzvimbo yake haingazomuzivizve.
\p
\v	11	Saka handingabati muromo wangu; ndichataura pakutambudzika kwemweya weupenyu wangu; ndichanyunyuta pakurwadziwa kweupenyu hwangu.
\v	12	Ndiri gungwa kanhi, kana bukanana regungwa, kuti mundiisire murindi?
\v	13	Kana ndikati: Mubhedha wangu uchandinyaradza, nhovo yangu ichazorodza kunyunyuta kwangu;
\v	14	zvino munondityityidzira nehope, uye munondivhundusa nezviratidzo;
\v	15	kuti mweya weupenyu wangu usarudze kudzipwa, rufu kupfuura mapfupa angu.
\v	16	Ndino usema; handingararami kusvika rinhi narinhi; ndiregei; nokuti mazuva angu haana maturo.
\v	17	Munhu chii kuti mumukudze, uye kuti muise moyo wenyu kwaari,
\v	18	uye kuti mumushanyire mangwanani oga-oga, mumuedze nguva imwe neimwe?
\v	19	Kusvika rinhi musingaregi kundicherechedza, musingandiregi kusvika ndamedza mate angu?
\v	20	Ndakatadza, ndichaitei kwamuri, imwi murindi wevanhu? Mandiisirei sechakanangwa kwamuri, kuti ndive mutoro kwandiri?
\v	21	Zvino munoregerei kukanganwira chitadzo changu, nekubvisa uipi hwangu? Nokuti ikozvino ndicharara pasi muguruva; muchanditsvaka zvikuru, asi handingavipo.
\c	8
\s	Bhiridhadhi anoraira Jobho kuti adzokere kuna Mwari
\p
\v	1	ZvinoBhiridhadhi muShuhi wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Kusvikarinhi uchitaura zvinhu izvi, uye mashoko emuromo wako ave mhepo ine simba?
\v	3	Mwariangaminamisa mutongo here? Kana Wemasimbaose angaminamisa zvakarurama here?
\v	4	Kanavana vako vakamutadzira, iye ndokuvarasira muruoko rwechitadzo chavo.
\v	5	Kanaiwe ukashingairira kutsvaka Mwari, kuita mukumbiro wako kuna Wemasimbaose;
\v	6	kanawakachena uye wakarurama, zvirokwazvo ikozvino waimuka nekuda kwako, ndokumutsidzira nzvimbo yekugara yekururama kwako.
\v	7	Kunyangekutanga kwako kwaiva kudiki, zvakadaro kuguma kwako kuchakura kwazvo.
\v	8	Nokutibvunza hako zvezera rekutanga, ugogadzirira tsvakurudzo yemadzibaba avo.
\v	9	Nokutitiri vazuro, uye hatizivi, nokuti mazuva edu panyika mumvuri.
\v	10	Havangakudzidzisi here, vakuudze, vabudise mashoko kubva mumoyo wavo?
\v	11	Ko nhokwe ingakura pasina nhope here? Rutsanga rwungakura pasina mvura here?
\v	12	Rwuchiri paunyoro hwaro, rwu sati rwadzurwa, zvakadaro rwunosvava kutangira mirivo yose.
\v	13	Ndizvo zvakaita nzira dzevose vanokanganwa Mwari; uye tariro yevanyepedzeri ichaparara.
\v	14	Tariro yake ndiyo ichagurwa, uye chivimbo chake mambure edandemutande.
\v	15	Achasendama paimba yake, asi haingamiri; achaibatirira, asi haingamuki.
\v	16	Iye mutema pamberi pezuva, uye bukira rake rinobudira pamusoro pebindu rake.
\v	17	Midzi yake yakapomberwa pamurwi, anoona nzvimbo yemabwe.
\v	18	Kana achimuparadza panzvimbo yake, zvino ichamuramba  ichiti: Handina kukuona.
\v	19	Tarira, uyu mufaro wenzira yake, uye kubva muguruva vamwe vanokura.
\v	20	Tarira, Mwari haangarasi wakaperera, uye haabati ruoko rwevaiti vezvakaipa.
\v	21	Kusvika azadza muromo wako nekuseka, nemiromo yako nekudanidzira kwemufaro.
\v	22	Avo vanokuvenga vachafukidzwa nenyadzi; uye tende revakaipa haringazovipo.
\c	9
\s	Jobho anopindura: Hakuna munhu angazviruramisa pamberi paMwari, nokuti iye anoita sezvaanoda
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJobho wakapindura ndokuti:
\p
\v	2	Zvirokwazvondinoziva kuti zvakadaro. Asi munhu angarurama sei kuna Mwari?
\v	3	Kanaachishuva kupikisana naye, haazomupinduri chimwe pa zvinhu churu.
\v	4	Wakachenjerapamoyo, uye wakagwinya pasimba; ndiyani wakazvisimbisa akamirisana naye uye akava parunyararo?
\v	5	Anobvisamakomo uye asingazvizivi, anoapidigura pakutsamwa kwake.
\v	6	Anozungunusanyika kubva panzvimbo yayo, nembiru dzayo dzinodengenyeka.
\v	7	Anorairazuva kuti risabuda, nekudzitira nyeredzi.
\v	8	Iyeoga anotatamura matenga, nekutsika pamusoro pemafungu egungwa.
\v	9	Anoita nyeredzi dzeAkituro, Oriyoni, nePireadhesi, nemakamuri ekuchamhembe.
\v	10	Anoita zvinhu zvikuru zvisinganzvereki, uye zvinoshamisa zvisingaverengeki.
\p
\v	11	Tarira, anopfuura nepandiri asi handioni, anopfuurirawo asi handimunzwi.
\v	12	Tarira, anobvuta, ndiani angamudzivisa? Ndiani angati kwaari: Unoitei?
\v	13	Mwari haangadzori kutsamwa kwake; vabatsiri vaRahabhi vanokotama pasi pake.
\v	14	Ko zvino ini ndichamupindura ndichitii, ndisarudze mashoko angu kunangana naye?
\v	15	Iye, kana ndaiva ndakarurama, handina kupindura; ndichakumbira nyasha kumutongi wangu.
\p
\v	16	Kana ndaiva ndadana, wakandipindurawo, handizaitenda kuti wakaisa nzeve painzwi rangu.
\v	17	Nokuti anondiputsa nedutu, nekuwanza mavanga angu pasina chikonzero.
\v	18	Haanditenderi kudzosa mweya wangu, asi anondizadza nezvinovava.
\v	19	Kana zviri zvesimba, tarirai, wakasimba; uye kana zviri zvemutongo, ndiyani achandidana?
\v	20	Kana ndakarurama, muromo wangu uchandipa mhosva;  kana ndaiva ndakaperera, iye aiti ndakatadza.
\v	21	Ndakaperera; handizivi mweya weupenyu wangu; ndinozvidza upenyu hwangu.
\v	22	Chinhu chimwe ichi, naizvozvo ndinoti: Anoparadza wakaperera newakaipa.
\v	23	Kana shamhu ichiuraya nekukurumidza, achaseka pakurasa tariro kwevasina mhosva.
\v	24	Nyika yakapiwa muruoko rwewakaipa; anofukidza zviso zvevatongi vayo; kana zvisizvo, zvino ndiani?
\v	25	Zvino mazuva angu anokurumidza kupfuura mumhanyi; anotiza, haaoni zvakanaka.
\v	26	Apfuura sezvikepe zverutsanga, segondo rinohatura chekudya.
\p
\v	27	Kana ndikati: Ndichakanganwa kunyunyuta kwangu, ndichasiya chiso changu  chinopunyaira, ndifare:
\v	28	Ndinotya marwadzo angu ose; ndinoziva kuti hamunganditi handina mhosva.
\v	29	Ndine mhosva; zvino ndinoshingairirei pasina?
\v	30	Kana ndikashamba nemvura yemagada echando, uye ndikachenesa maoko angu nesipo,
\v	31	ipapo muchandinyudza mugomba, nenguvo dzangu dzichandisema.
\v	32	Nokuti haasi munhu wakafanana neni,  kuti ndimupindure,  kuti tisvike pamwe pamutongo.
\v	33	Hakuna mutongi pakati pedu, angaisa ruoko rwake pamusoro pedu tiri vaviri.
\v	34	Ngaabvise shamhu yake kwandiri, nekuvhundusa kwake kusandityisa.
\v	35	 Zvino ndaitaura ndisingamutyi; asi handina kudaro pachangu.
