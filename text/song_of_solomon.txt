\b	Rwiyo Rukuru rwaSoromoni
\c	1
\s	Kudanana kwemwenga nechikomba
\p
\v	1	Rwiyorwenziyo rwuri rwaSoromoni.
\p
\v	2	Ngaanditsvodenetsvodo dzemuromo wake; nokuti rudo rukuru rwako rwuri nani pane waini.
\p
\v	3	Mafutaako akanakira kunhuhwira; zita rako chifafaidzo chinonhuhwira chakadururwa; naizvozvo mhandara dzinokuda.
\v	4	Ndikweve;tichamhanya shure kwako; mambo wakandipinza mudzimba dzake dzemukati; tichafara nekufarisisa mauri; ticharangarira rudo rukuru rwenyu kupfuura waini; vakakuda zvakarurama.
\v	5	Ndakasviba,asi ndakanaka, vakunda veJerusarema, sematende eKedhari, semaketeni aSoromoni.
\v	6	Musandicherekedzanokuti ndakasviba, nokuti zuva rakandipisa. Vanakomana vamai vangu vakanditsamwira; vakandiita murindikadzi weminda yemizambiringa. Munda wangu wemuzambiringa wangu handina kuurinda.
\v	7	Ndiudze iwe, mweya wangu wawakada, kuti unofudzira kupi, unorarisa kupi boka masikati; nokuti ndichaitirei sewakafukidzwa kumeso pamapoka eshamwari dzako?
\p
\v	8	Kanausingazivi, iwe wakanaka zvikuru pakati pevakadzi, buda iwe nematsimba eboka, ufudzire makwayana ako parutivi rwematende evafudzi.
\v	9	Ndakakufananidzaiwe nebhizagadzi pangoro dzaFarao, shamwarikadzi yangu.
\v	10	Matama ako akanaka pakati pezvirukwa zvevhudzi; mutsipa wako nezvuma zvezvishongo.
\v	11	Tichakuitira zvirukwa zvegoridhe pamwe nemhete dzesirivha.
\p
\v	12	Mambo achiri padenderedzwa retafura yake, nadho yangu yakabudisa kunhuhwira kwayo.
\v	13	Mudiwa wangu kwandiri ihombodo yemura, ichagara pakati pemazamu angu usiku.
\v	14	Mudiwa wangu kwandiri isumbu remaruva ehena paminda yemizambiringa yeEni-Gedhi.
\v	15	Tarira, wakanaka, shamwarikadzi yangu; tarira, wakanaka; meso ako  akafanana neenjiva.
\v	16	Tarira, wakanaka, mudiwa wangu, zvirokwazvo unofadza. Nemubhedha wedu ibumhudza.
\v	17	Matanda edzimba dzedu misidhari; mbariro dzedu misiperesi.
\c	2
\v	1	Ndirifurauzi reSharoni, ruva remumapani.
\p
\v	2	Seruvapakati peminzwa, wakadaro mudiwasikana wangu pakati pevakunda.
\p
\v	3	Semutiwemuapuri pakati pemiti yemudondo, wakadaro mudiwa wangu pakati pevanakomana. Ndakagara mumumvuri wake ndikafara kwazvo; uye chibereko chake chinotapira pamukanwa wangu.
\v	4	Wakandiisakuimba yewaini; uye mureza wake pamusoro pangu rudo.
\v	5	Nditsigireinezvingwa zvezvibereko zvemuzambiringa zvakaomeswa, ndisimbisei nemaapuri; nokuti ndinorwara nerudo.
\v	6	Ruokorwake rweruboshwe rwuri pasi pemusoro wangu; uye ruoko rwake rwerudyi rwunondimbundikira.
\v	7	Ndinokupikisai,vakunda veJerusarema, nemharapara kana nenondo dzesango, kuti musabvongodza kana kumutsa rudo kusvika rwada.
\p
\v	8	Inzwiremudiwa wangu! Tarirai iye anouya, achikwakuka pamusoro pemakomo, achiuruka pamusoro pezvikomo.
\v	9	Mudiwawangu wakaita semharapara kana mhuru yenondo; tarirai, wakamira seri kwerusvingo rwedu; achidongorera pamahwindo; anozviratidza pazvitanda zvemahwindo.
\v	10	Mudiwa wangu wakapindura akati kwandiri: Simuka iwe, shamwarikadzi yangu, wakanaka wangu, iwe hande!
\v	11	Nokuti, tarira, chando chapfuura; mvura yapera, iyo yaenda.
\v	12	Maruva oonekwa panyika; nguva yekuimba yasvika, neinzwi renjiva rinonzwikwa panyika yedu.
\v	13	Muti wemuonde waibvisa maonde awo mambishi, uye mizambiringa  ine maruva yobudisa kunhuhwira. Simuka iwe, shamwarikadzi yangu, wakanaka wangu, iwe hande!
\p
\v	14	Njiva yangu, mumikaha yedombo, pakavanda pemawere; ndionese chiso chako, ndinzwise inzwi rako; nokuti inzwi rako rinotapira, uye chiso chako chakanaka.
\v	15	Tibatirei makava, makava madiki, mharadzi dzeminda yemizambiringa; nokuti minda yedu yemizambiringa yava nemaruva.
\v	16	Mudiwa wangu ndewangu, neni ndiri wake; anofudza pakati pemaruva.
\v	17	Kusvika ava mambakwedza, nemimvuri yatiza, dzoka, mudiwa wangu, iwe uve semharapara kana semhuru yenondo pamusoro pemakomo eBheteri.
\c	3
\s	Mwenga anotsvaka chikomba chake
\p
\v	1	Pamubhedhawangu pausiku uzhinji, ndakamutsvaka iye mweya wangu waunoda; ndakamutsvaka, asi handina kumuwana.
\v	2	Ndichasimukaikozvino nditenderere muguta; panzira dzemumusha, nepamapani, ndakamutsvaka iye mweya wangu waunoda; ndakamutsvaka asi handina kumuwana.
\v	3	Varindi,vanotenderera muguta vakandiwana;  ndikati: Mamuona here iye mweya wangu waunoda?
\v	4	Ndakatindapfuura zvishoma pavari, ndokumuwana iye mweya weupenyu wangu waunoda; ndakamubata, uye handina kumurega achienda kusvika ndamupinza muimba yamai vangu, nemukamuri raiye wakandigamuchira muchizvaro.
\v	5	Ndinokupikisai,vakunda veJerusarema, nemharapara kana nenondo dzesango, kuti musabvongodza kana kumutsa rudo, kusvika rwada.
\s	Kuuya kwechikomba
\p
\v	6	Ndiyaniuyo anokwira achibva kurenje seshongwe dzeutsi, akafafaidzwa zvinonhuhwira zvemura nezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira, nezvakakuyiwa zvose zvemutengesi?
\v	7	Tariraimubhedha wake uri waSoromoni; mhare makumi matanhatu dzakaukomberedza, vemhare dzekwaIsraeri.
\v	8	Vosevakabata munondo, vakadzidziswa hondo; umwe neumwe munondo wake pahudyu yake, nekuda kwekutya pausiku uzhinji.
\v	9	MamboSoromoni wakazviitira chigaro chinotakurwa chematanda eRebhanoni.
\v	10	Wakaita mbiru dzacho  nesirivha, pasi pacho  negoridhe, chigaro chacho  nezveruvara rwehute. Mukati macho makarukirirwa rudo nevakunda veJerusarema.
\v	11	Budai, imwi vakunda veZiyoni, muone mambo Soromoni, ane korona mai vake yavakamudzika pazuva rekuwana kwake, nepazuva remufaro wemoyo wake.
\c	4
\s	Chikomba chinorumbidza mwenga
\p
\v	1	Tarira,wakanaka, shamwarikadzi yangu; tarira, wakanaka. Meso ako seenjiva mukati mechifukidzo chako; vhudzi rako rakaita seboka rembudzi, dzinoburuka mugomo reGiriyadhi.
\v	2	Mazinoako akaita seboka  remakwai akaveurwa, anokwira kubva pakushambidzwa; rimwe nerimwe rawo rinobereka mapatya, uye hakuna rakafirwa pakati pawo.
\v	3	Serwonzirushava ndizvo zvakaita miromo yako, uye mutauro wako wakanaka; sebandi remupomegiranati ndizvo zvakaita zvavovo zvako mukati mechifukidzo chako.
\v	4	Mutsipawako wakaita seshongwe yaDhavhidhi, yakavakwa kuva imba yezvombo, kwakaremberera pairi nhovo churu, dzose dziri nhovo dzemhare.
\v	5	Mazamuako maviri akaita sevana vaviri vemharapara, vari mapatya, vanofura pakati pemaruva.
\v	6	Kusvikamambakwedza, nemimvuri yatiza, ini ndichaenda kugomo remura, nekuchikomo chezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	7	Iwewakanaka wose, shamwarikadzi yangu, uye hamuna chipomerwa mauri.
\p
\v	8	Handeneni kubva kuRebhanoni, mwenga, neni kubva kuRebhanoni. Tarira uri panhongonya yeAmana, panhongonya yeSeniri neHerimoni, pamagara edzishumba, pamakomo eingwe zhinji.
\v	9	Wanditoreramoyo wangu, hanzvadzi yangu, mwenga; wanditorera moyo wangu nerimwe remaziso ako, neruketani rwumwe rwemutsipa wako.
\v	10	Rudo rwako rukuru rwakanaka sei, hanzvadzi yangu, mwenga! Rwuri nani sei rudo rwako rukuru pane waini; nekunhuhwira kwezvifafaidzo zvako zvinonhuhwira kupfuura zvinonhuhwira zvose!
\v	11	Miromo yako, mwenga, inodonha zinga reuchi; uchi nemukaka zviri pasi perurimi rwako; uye kunhuhwira kwenguvo dzako kwakaita sekunhuhwira kweRebhanoni.
\v	12	Uri bindu rakapfigwa, hanzvadzi yangu, mwenga, chitubu chakapfigirwa, tsime rakaiswa chiratidzo.
\v	13	Mabukira ako ibindu remapomegiranati, rine zvibereko zvakaisvonaka; hena nenaridho.
\v	14	Naridho nesafironi, karamusi nekinamoni, nemiti yose inonhuhwira, mura negavakava, nezvose zvinonhuhwira zvinokosha.
\v	15	Chitubu chemabindu, tsime remvura zhinji mhenyu, nedzinoyerera kubva Rebhanoni.
\s	Kupindura kwemwenga
\p
\v	16	Muka, iwe mhepo yekumaodzanyemba; uye uya, iwe mhepo yekuchamhembe; furidzira pabindu rangu, kuti zvinonhuhwira zvaro zviyerere zvibude. Mudiwa wangu ngaapinde mubindu rake, ngaadye zvibereko zvake zvakaisvonaka.
\c	5
\s	Kupindura kwechikomba
\p
\v	1	Ndapindamubindu rangu, hanzvadzi yangu, mwenga; ndatanha mura yangu pamwe nezvinonhuhwira zvangu; ndadya zinga reuchi hwangu pamwe neuchi hwangu; ndanwa waini yangu pamwe nemukaka wangu. Idyai, shamwari dzangu; inwai, zvirokwazvo inwai zvikuru, vadiwa.
\p
\v	2	Ndakarara,asi moyo wangu wakamuka; inzwi remudiwa wangu rinogogodza  richiti: Ndizarurire, hanzvadzi yangu, shamwarikadzi yangu, njiva yangu, wakakwana wangu; nokuti musoro wangu uzere dova, mhotsi dzangu nemadonhedza eusiku.
\v	3	Ndabvisanguvo refu yangu; ndingaipfeka sei? Ndashambidza tsoka dzangu; ndingadzisvibisa sei?
\v	4	Mudiwawangu wakapinza ruoko rwake nepaburi  regonhi, zvino ura hwangu hwaiva mukusagadzikana nekuda kwake.
\v	5	Inindakasimuka kuzarurira mudiwa wangu; uye maoko angu akadonha mura, neminwe yangu mura inoyerera, pazvibato zvechizarira.
\v	6	Inindakazarurira mudiwa wangu; asi mudiwa wangu waiva atendeuka akapfuura. Mweya wangu wakabuda paakataura; ndakamutsvaka asi handina kumuwana; ndakamudana asi haana kundipindura.
\v	7	Varindivanotenderera muguta vakandiwana; vakandirova, vakandikuvadza; vachengeti vemasvingo vakasimudza kwiriti yangu kubva pandiri.
\v	8	Ndinokupikisai,imwi vakunda veJerusarema, kana mukawana mudiwa wangu, muchamuudzei? Kuti ndinorwara rudo.
\p
\v	9	Mudiwawako anopfuura  umwe mudiwa nei, iwe, wakanaka pakati pevakadzi? Mudiwa wako anopfuura  umwe mudiwa nei, zvaunotipikisa saizvozvo?
\p
\v	10	Mudiwa wangu wakachena uye mutsvuku, anoonekwa pakati pemazana gumi.
\v	11	Musoro wake igoridhe rakaisvonaka; mhotsi dzake mapazi, itema segunguvo.
\v	12	Meso ake akaita senjiva pahova dzemvura, akashambidzwa mumukaka, akarongedzwa zvakanaka.
\v	13	Matama ake akaita sendima yemiti inonhuhwira,  sematutu ezvifafaidzo zvinobudisa mweya unonhuhwira. Miromo yake maruva anodonha mura inoyerera.
\v	14	Maoko ake akaita  sepombi dzegoridhe dzakazadzwa nebheriri; dumbu rake inyanga yenzou inoonekwa, yakafukidzwa nesafiri.
\v	15	Makumbo ake imbiru dzemabwe machena, dzakateiwa pazvigadziko zvegoridhe rakaisvonaka; kuonekwa kwake kwakaita seRebhanoni, rakasanangurwa semisidhari.
\v	16	Muromo wake unotapira kwazvo. Zvirokwazvo zvose zvake zvinoshuvika kwazvo. Uyu mudiwa wangu, uye uyu ishamwari yangu, vakunda veJerusarema.
\c	6
\p
\v	1	Wakaendepimudiwa wako, iwe wakanaka pakati pevakadzi? Wakatendeukirepi mudiwa wako, kuti timutsvake pamwe newe?
\v	2	Mudiwawangu wakaburukira kubindu rake, kundima dzezvinonhuhwira, kunofura mumabindu, nekuunganidza maruva.
\v	3	Inindiri wemudiwa wangu; uye mudiwa wangu ndewangu; anofura pakati pemaruva.
\p
\v	4	Wakanaka,shamwarikadzi yangu, seTiriza, unoyevedza seJerusarema, unotyisa se hondo ine mireza.
\v	5	Bvisameso ako kwandiri, nokuti iwo anondikunda. Vhudzi rako rakaita seboka rembudzi dzinoburuka kubva kuGiriyadhi.
\v	6	Mazinoako akaita seboka remakwayana anokwira kubva pakushambidzwa, nokuti rimwe nerimwe rawo rinobereka mapatya, uye hapana rakafirwa pakati pawo.
\v	7	Zvavovozvako zvakaita sechimedu chepomegiranati mukati mechifukidzo chako.
\v	8	Panemadzihosi makumi matanhatu, nevarongo makumi masere, nemhandara dzisingaverengeki.
\v	9	Njivayangu ndeimwe, wakakwana wangu, ndiye umwe oga wamai vake; ndiye wakachena wewakamubereka. Vakunda vakamuona, vakamuti wakaropafadzwa; madzihosi nevarongo, vakamurumbidza.
\p
\v	10	Ndiyani uyu anotsikitsira semambakwedza, wakanaka semwedzi, wakachena sezuva, anotyisa se hondo ine mireza?
\p
\v	11	Ndakaburukira kubindu renzungu, kuona mabukira mumupata, kuona kana muzambiringa uchibuka, mapomegiranati anotunga.
\v	12	Handina kuziva, mweya wangu wakandimisa  pangoro dzeumambo dzevanhu vekwedu.
\p
\v	13	Dzoka, dzoka, muShuramiti; dzoka, dzoka, zvino tichakuyeva. Munoyeverei muShuramiti, sekutamba kwehondo mbiri?
\c	7
\p
\v	1	Tsokadzako dzakanaka sei mushangu, iwe mukunda wemuchinda! Hudyu dzezvidya zvako dzakaita sezvishongo, basa remaoko emhizha.
\v	2	Guvhurako rakaita semukombe wakatendereka, usingashaiwi waini yakavhenganiswa; dumbu rako  rakaita sedutu rezviyo rakashoshewa nemaruva.
\v	3	Mazamuako maviri akaita setsvana mbiri, mapatya emharapara.
\v	4	Mutsipawako wakaita seshongwe yenyanga yenzou; meso ako  semadziva muHeshibhoni, pasuwo reBhati-Rabhimi. Mhino yako yakaita seshongwe yeRebhanoni yakatarisana neDhamasiko.
\v	5	Musorowako pamusoro pako seKarimeri; nemhotsi dzemusoro wako sezveruvara rwehute; mambo wakasungwa mukumonana kwevhudzi.
\v	6	Wakanakasei, uye unofadza sei, rudo, pane zvinofadza.
\v	7	Chimirochako ichi chakaita semuchindwe, nemazamu ako semasumbu.
\v	8	Ndakati:Ndichakwira mumuchindwe, ndibate matavi awo; mazamu ako ngaave hawo semasumbu emuzambiringa, nekunhuhwira kwemhino yako semaapuri.
\v	9	Nematadzaemuromo wako sewaini yakanaka, inoenda kumudiwa wangu zvakanaka, ichitaudza miromo yevarere.
\p
\v	10	Ndiri wemudiwa wangu; nechishuwo chake chiri kwandiri.
\v	11	Uya mudiwa wangu, ngatibudire kusango, ngatirare usiku mumisha.
\v	12	Ngatifumire kuminda yemizambiringa; tinoona kuti muzambiringa watunga, wabudisa maruva,  nekuti mapomegiranati atunga maruva here; ndipapo pandichakupa rudo rwangu rukuru.
\v	13	Mamandarakisi anobudisa kunhuhwira, nepamikova yedu pane mhando dzose dzezvinonaka kwazvo, zvitsva nezvitsaru, zvandakachengetera iwe, mudiwa wangu.
\c	8
\p
\v	1	Daiiwe wakaita sehanzvadzi yangu, yakanwa mazamu amai vangu! Dai ndaikuwana panze, ndaikutsvoda; zvirokwazvo havazaindishora.
\v	2	Ndaikutungamirira,ndikakupinza mumba mamai vangu; vaindidzidzisa; ndaikunwisa zvewaini yakaiswa zvinonhuhwira, zvemuto wemapomegiranati angu.
\v	3	Ruokorwake rweruboshwe rwuri pasi pemusoro wangu, uye rudyi rwake rwunondimbundikira.
\v	4	Ndinokupikisai,vakunda veJerusarema, kuti musabvongodza kana kumutsa rudo kusvika rwada.
\p
\v	5	Ndiyaniuyu anokwira kubva kurenje, akasendama pamudiwa wake? Ndakakumutsa pasi pemiti wemuapuri; ndipo mai vako pavakakutamburira, ndipo pavaka kutamburira ivo vakakubereka.
\p
\v	6	Ndiisesechisimbiso pamoyo wako, sechisimbiso paruoko rwako; nokuti rudo rune simba serufu; godo rine utsinye seguva; mazimbe aro mazimbe emoto, murazvo une simba.
\v	7	Mvurazhinji haigoni kudzima rudo; uye mafashame haangarwukukuri. Kana munhu akapa fuma yose yeimba yake nekuda kwerudo, vangaizvidza zvekuizvidza.
\p
\v	8	Tinehanzvadzi diki, uye isina mazamu; tichaiitirei hanzvadzi yedu pazuva richataurwa nezvake?
\v	9	Kanaari rusvingo, tichavaka pamusoro pake nhare yesirivha; uye kana ari mukova, tichamukomberedza nemapuranga emusidhari.
\p
\v	10	Ini ndaiva rusvingo, uye mazamu angu akaita sedzishongwe; panguva iyo ndakava pameso ake seawana rugare.
\v	11	Soromoni waiva nemunda wemizambiringa paBhaari-Hamoni; wakapa munda wemizambiringa kuvachengeti; umwe neumwe waiuya nezvezvibereko zvawo, makobiri esirivha gumi remazana.
\v	12	Munda wangu wemizambiringa, iwo wangu, uri pamberi pangu; iwe Soromoni, gumi remazana nderako; nemazana maviri ndeevachengeti vezvibereko zvawo.
\p
\v	13	Iwe unogara mumabindu, shamwari dzinoteerera inzwi rako; ndinzwise wo iro.
\p
\v	14	Kurumidza, mudiwa wangu, uve iwe semharapara kana mhuru yenondo pamusoro pemakomo ezvinonhuhwira.
