\b	Evhangeri yakanyorwa naRuka
\c	1
\s	Shoko rekuvamba
\p
\v	1	Sezvovazhinji vakabata kuronga nhoroondo yezvinhu zvakavimbika zvizere pakati pedu,
\v	2	sezvavakapakwatiri ivo kubva pakutanga vakazvionera, uye vari varanda veshoko,
\v	3	zvakaonekwazvakanaka kwandiriwo, ndanyatsoongorora zvinhu zvose kubva pakutanga, kuti ndikunyorerei nekutevedzanisa, imwi Teofiro changamire,
\v	4	kutimuzive chokwadi chezvinhu zvamakadzidziswa.
\s	Zakaria anoziviswa kuberekwa kwaJohwani Mubhabhatidzi
\p
\v	5	Kwaivaponemazuva aHerodhi mambo weJudhiya umwe mupristi wainzi Zakaria, weboka raAbhiya; nemukadzi wake waibva kuvanasikana vaAroni, nezita rake riri Erizabheti.
\v	6	Uyevari vaviri vaiva vakarurama pamberi paMwari, vachifamba nemirairo yose nezviga zvaIshe, vasina chavangapomerwa.
\v	7	Uyevaiva vasina mwana, nokuti Erizabheti waiva ari mhanje, uye vaiva vaenda pamakore vari vaviri.
\p
\v	8	Zvinozvakaitika kuti wakati achiita ushumiri hweupristi pamberi paMwari, pachidanho cheboka rake,
\v	9	maereranonetsika yeushumiri hweupristi, wakabatwa nemujenya kuti apise zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira apinda mutembere yaIshe.
\v	10	Zvino chaunga chose chevanhu chaiva chichinyengetera kunze nenguva yezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	11	Zvino kwakaonekwa kwaari mutumwa waIshe, amire kurutivi rwerudyi rwearitari yezvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira.
\v	12	Zvino Zakaria wakati achi muona akanetseka, uye kutya kukamuwira.
\v	13	Asi mutumwa wakati kwaari: Usatya Zakaria; nokuti munyengetero wako wanzwikwa, nemukadzi wako Erizabheti achakuberekera mwanakomana, ugotumidza zita rake Johwani.
\v	14	Uye uchava nemufaro nekupembera, nevazhinji vachafarira kuberekwa kwake.
\v	15	Nokuti achava mukuru pamberi paIshe, haangatongonwi waini kana zvinodhaka; uye achazadzwa neMweya Mutsvene kunyange kubva padumbu ramai vake.
\v	16	Nevazhinji vevana vaIsraeri achatendeusira kuna Ishe Mwari wavo;
\v	17	uye iye achafamba pamberi pake mumweya nesimba raEria, kutendeusira moyo yemadzibaba kuvana, nevasingateereri kuuchenjeri hwevakarurama, kugadzirira Ishe vanhu vakagadzirwa.
\v	18	Zakaria akati kumutumwa: Izvozvi ndichazviziva nei? Nokuti ini ndava mukweguru, nemukadzi wangu waenda pamazuva ake.
\v	19	Mutumwa achipindura akati kwaari: Ini ndini Gabhirieri, anomira pamberi paMwari; ndatumwa kuti nditaure kwauri, ndikuparidzire mashoko awa akanaka.
\v	20	Zvino tarira, uchava chimumumu, usingagoni kutaura, kusvika zuva rinoitika zvinhu izvi, nokuti hauna kutenda mashoko angu, achazadziswa nenguva yawo.
\p
\v	21	Vanhu vaiva vakamirira Zakaria, vakashamisika nekunonoka kwake mutembere.
\v	22	Zvino wakati abuda, akakonewa kutaura kwavari; vakaziva kuti aiva aona chiratidzo mutembere; iye akavaninira nemaoko, akaramba ari chimumumu.
\v	23	Zvino zvakaitika kuti mazuva ekushumira kwake akati achangopera, akaenda kumba kwake.
\p
\v	24	Zvino shure kwemazuva iwayo, mukadzi wake Erizabheti akatora mimba, akazvivanza mwedzi mishanu, achiti:
\v	25	Ishe wandiitira saizvozvo nemazuva aaka nditarira, kuti abvise ruzvidzo rwangu pakati pevanhu.
\s	Maria anoziviswa kuti achabereka Jesu
\p
\v	26	Zvino nemwedzi wechitanhatu mutumwa Gabhirieri wakatumwa naMwari kuguta reGarirea rinonzi Nazareta,
\v	27	kumhandara, yaiva yatsidza kuwanikwa nemurume zita rake raiva Josefa, weimba yaDhavhidhi; uye zita remhandara riri Maria.
\v	28	Mutumwa akapinda kwaari akati: Hekanhi, iwe une nyasha! Ishe anewe, wakaropafadzwa iwe pakati pevakadzi.
\v	29	Iye wakati achi muona akanetseka neshoko rake, akafunganya  kuti uku kukwazisa rudzii kwakadai.
\v	30	Zvino mutumwa akati kwaari: Usatya Maria; nokuti wawana nyasha kuna Mwari.
\v	31	Zvino tarira, uchava nemimba, ugozvara mwanakomana, uchatumidza zita rake  kuti Jesu.
\v	32	Iye achava mukuru, achanzi Mwanakomana weWekumusoro-soro; Ishe Mwari achapa kwaari chigaro cheushe chababa vake Dhavhidhi.
\v	33	Achatonga pamusoro peimba yaJakobho kusvika narinhi, neushe hwake hahungavi nemugumo.
\v	34	Maria ndokuti kumutumwa: Izvi zvingava sei, nokuti handizivi murume?
\v	35	Mutumwa akapindura akati kwaari: Mweya Mutsvene uchauya pamusoro pako, nesimba reWekumusoro-soro richakudzikatira; naizvozvo icho chitsvene chichaberekwa newe chichanzi Mwanakomana waMwari.
\v	36	Uye tarira, Erizabheti hama yako, iyewo wava nemimba yemwanakomana pakuchembera kwake; nemwedzi uno wava wechitanhatu kuna iye wainzi mhanje.
\v	37	Nokuti naMwari hakuna chinhu chisingazogoneki.
\v	38	Maria ndokuti: Tarirai, murandakadzi waIshe; ngazvive kwandiri seshoko renyu. Mutumwa ndokubva kwaari.
\s	Maria anondoshanyira Erizabheti
\p
\v	39	Zvino Maria akasimuka nemazuva iwayo akaenda kunyika yezvikomo nekukurumidza, kuguta raJudha;
\v	40	akapinda mumba maZakaria, akakwazisa Erizabheti.
\v	41	Zvino zvakaitika kuti Erizabheti wakati achinzwa kukwazisa kwaMaria, mwana akatamba mudumbu rake; naErizabheti akazadzwa neMweya Mutsvene,
\v	42	akadanidzira nenzwi guru, akati: Wakaropafadzwa iwe pakati pevakadzi, uye chakaropafadzwa chibereko chedumbu rako!
\v	43	Ko zvandibvira nepi, kuti mai vaIshe wangu vauye kwandiri?
\v	44	Nokuti tarira, inzwi rekukwazisa kwako rangoti richisvika munzeve dzangu, mwana watamba mudumbu rangu nemufaro.
\v	45	Uye wakaropafadzwa anotenda, nokuti zvakataurwa naIshe kwaari zvichazadziswa.
\s	Rwiyo rwaMaria rwekurumbidza
\p
\v	46	Maria akati: Moyo wangu unokudza Ishe,
\v	47	nemweya wangu unofara muna Mwari Muponesi wangu;
\v	48	nokuti wakatarira kuninipiswa kwemurandakadzi wake; nokuti tarira, kubva zvino mazera ose achati ndakaropafadzwa.
\v	49	Nokuti ane masimba wakandiitira zvinhu zvikuru; nezita rake idzvene.
\v	50	Netsitsi dzake dziri kune avo vanomutya kubva kumazera kusvika kumazera.
\v	51	Wakaita zvine simba neruoko rwake; wakaparadzira vanozvikudza mundangariro dzemoyo yavo.
\v	52	Wakaburusa vane masimba pazvigaro zveushe, uye akasimudza vakaderera.
\v	53	Vane nzara wakavagutsa nezvinhu zvakanaka, asi vafumi wakavaendesa vasina chinhu.
\v	54	Wakabatsira Israeri muranda wake kuti arangarire tsitsi,
\v	55	sezvaakataura kumadzibaba edu, kuna Abhurahama nekumbeu yake kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	56	Maria akagara naye mwedzi inenge mitatu, uye akadzokera kumba kwake.
\s	Kuberekwa kwaJohwani Mubhabhatidzi
\p
\v	57	Zvino nguva yaErizabheti yekusununguka kwake yakazara; akabereka mwanakomana.
\v	58	Zvino vavakidzani nehama dzake vakati vanzwa kuti Ishe waiva amuitira tsitsi dzake huru, vakafara naye.
\v	59	Zvino zvakaitika kuti nezuva rerusere vakauya kuzodzingisa mucheche, vakamutumidza Zakaria, zvichienderana nezita rababa vake.
\v	60	Asi mai vake vakapindura vakati: Kwete, asi achanzi Johwani.
\v	61	Vakati kwaari: Hakuna munhu kuhama dzako anodaidzwa nezita iri.
\v	62	Vakataura nemaoko kuna baba vake, kuti vanoda kuti anzi ani.
\v	63	Vakakumbira chinyorero, vakanyora vachiti: NdiJohwani zita rake; vakashamisika vose.
\v	64	Muromo wake ukazarurwa pakarepo, nerurimi rwake  rukasununguka, akataura, achirumbidza Mwari.
\v	65	Kutya kukawira vose vaigara vakavakomba; nemashoko awa ose akataurwa kunyika yose yezvikomo yeJudhiya.
\v	66	Zvino vose vakanzwa, vaka zviisa mumoyo mavo, vachiti: Mucheche uyu achazova ani? Uye ruoko rwaIshe rwakava naye.
\s	Kuporofita kwaZakaria
\p
\v	67	Zvino Zakaria baba vake vakazadzwa neMweya Mutsvene, vakaporofita, vachiti:
\v	68	Ngaarumbidzwe Ishe Mwari waIsraeri, nokuti wakashanyira nekuita rudzikunuro kuvanhu vake;
\v	69	akatisimudzira runyanga rweruponeso mumba maDhavhidhi muranda wake;
\v	70	sezvaakataura nemuromo wevaporofita vake vatsvene, vaiva varipo kubva pasichigare;
\v	71	ruponeso pamhandu dzedu, neparuoko rwavose vanotivenga;
\v	72	kuitira madzibaba edu tsitsi, nekurangarira sungano yake tsvene,
\v	73	mhiko yaakapika kuna Abhurahama baba vedu;
\v	74	kutipa, kuti tasunungurwa muruoko rwevavengi vedu, timushumire tisingatyi;
\v	75	muutsvene nekururama pamberi pake mazuva ose eupenyu hwedu.
\v	76	Newe mucheche, uchanzi muporofita weWekumusoro-soro, nokuti uchatungamira pamberi pechiso chaIshe, kugadzirira nzira dzake;
\v	77	kupa ruzivo rweruponeso kuvanhu vake mukanganwiro yezvivi zvavo;
\v	78	netsitsi dzemoyo dzaMwari wedu, marwuri mambakwedza anobva kumusoro anotishanyira;
\v	79	kuvhenekera vagere murima nemumvuri werufu,  nekururamisa tsoka dzedu munzira yerugare.
\p
\v	80	Mucheche akakura, akasimba mumweya, akava mumarenje, kusvika zuva rekuratidzwa kwake kuna Israeri.
\c	10
\s	Vanhu makumi manomwe vanotumwa kunoparidza nekuporesa
\p
\v	1	Shurekwezvinhu izvi, Ishe akagadza vamwe makumi manomwewo, akavatuma vaviri vaviri pamberi pechiso chake muguta rimwe nerimwe nenzvimbo  imwe neimwe iye kwaaiva achizoenda.
\v	2	Naizvozvowakati kwavari: Goho zvirokwazvo iguru, asi vashandi vashoma. Naizvozvo kumbirai Ishe wegoho kuti abudisire vashandi pagoho rake.
\v	3	Endai;tarirai, ini ndinokutumai semakwayana pakati pemapumhi.
\v	4	Musatakurachikwama, kana nhava, kana manyatera; uye musakwazisa munhu panzira.
\v	5	Zvinopaimba ipi neipi yamunopinda, tangai kuti: Rugare kuimba ino.
\v	6	Uyekana mwanakomana werugare ari ipapo, rugare rwenyu ruchazorora pamusoro payo; uye kana zvisakadaro, ruchadzokera kwamuri.
\v	7	Garaimumba iyo, muchidya nekunwa zvinobva kwavari; nokuti mushandi wakafanirwa nemubairo wake; musapota-pota nedzimba.
\v	8	Mugutaripi neripi mamunopinda, uye vakakugamuchirai, idyai zvinoiswa mberi kwenyu,
\v	9	muporesevarwere varimo, muchiti kwavari: Ushe hwaMwari hwaswedera kwamuri.
\v	10	Asi muguta ripi neripi ramunopinda, vakasakugamuchirayi, budirai munzira dzaro, muti:
\v	11	Kunyange guruva reguta renyu rakatinamatira, tinorikuhumurira kwamuri; asi muzive chinhu ichi, kuti ushe hwaMwari hwaswedera kwamuri.
\v	12	Asi ndinoti kwamuri: Zvichava nani kuSodhoma nezuva iro, kupfuura guta iro.
\s	Kutukwa kwemaguta asina kutendeuka
\p
\v	13	Une nhamo, Korazini! Une nhamo, Bhetisaidha! Nokuti dai akaitwa muTire neSidhoni mabasa esimba akaitwa mamuri, vangadai vatendeuka kare, vakagara vakapfeka masaga nedota.
\v	14	Asi zvichava nani kuTire neSidhoni pakutongwa, kupfuura imwi.
\v	15	Newe Kapenaume, wakasimudzirwa kudenga, uchawisirwa pasi kuhadhesi.
\p
\v	16	Anokunzwai imwi anondinzwa ini; neanokurambai imwi anondiramba ini; neanondiramba ini anomuramba wakandituma.
\s	Kudzoka kwevanhu makumi manomwe
\p
\v	17	Vane makumi manomwe vakadzoka nemufaro, vachiti: Ishe, nemadhimoni anozviisa pasi pedu muzita renyu.
\v	18	Zvino akati kwavari: Ndakaona Satani semheni achiwa achibva kudenga.
\v	19	Tarirai, ndinopa kwamuri simba rekutsika pamusoro penyoka nezvinyavada, nepamusoro pesimba rose remuvengi; uye hakuna chinhu chingatongokukuvadzai.
\v	20	Asi musafara nechinhu ichi kuti mweya inozviisa pasi penyu; asi zvikuru mufare kuti mazita enyu akanyorwa kumatenga.
\s	Jesu anovonga Baba
\p
\v	21	Neawa iro Jesu wakafara mumweya, akati: Ndinokuvongai, Baba, Ishe wekudenga nenyika, kuti makavanza zvinhu izvi kune vakachenjera nevanonzwisisa, mukazvizarurira vacheche; hongu, Baba, nokuti saizvozvo zvakava zvakanaka pamberi penyu.
\v	22	Zvose zvakakumikidzwa kwandiri naBaba vangu; uye hakuna anoziva kuti Mwanakomana ndiani, kunze kwaBaba, uye kuti Baba ndiani, kunze kweMwanakomana, uye ani nani Mwanakomana waanoda kuzarurira.
\p
\v	23	Zvino akatendeukira kuvadzidzi vari vega akati: Akaropafadzwa meso anoona zvamunoona.
\v	24	Nokuti ndinoti kwamuri: Vaporofita vazhinji nemadzimambo vaishuva kuona zvinhu zvamunoona imwi, asi havana ku zviona, nekunzwa zvinhu zvamunonzwa, asi havana ku zvinzwa.
\s	Murairo mukuru
\p
\v	25	Zvino tarira, imwe nyanzvi yemutemo yakasimuka, ichimuidza, ichiti: Mudzidzisi, ndingaitei kuti ndigare nhaka yeupenyu husingaperi?
\v	26	Ndokuti kwaari: Pamurairo pakanyorwei? Unoverenga sei?
\v	27	Uye achipindura, akati: Ude Ishe Mwari wako, nemoyo wako wose, uye nemweya weupenyu wako wose, uye nesimba rako rose, uye nepfungwa dzako dzose; neumwe wako sezvaunozvi da iwe.
\v	28	Akati kwaari: Wapindura zvakarurama; ita izvi, ugorarama.
\s	Mufananidzo wemuSamariya ane tsitsi
\p
\v	29	Iye, achida kuzviruramisa, wakati kuna Jesu: Zvino ndiani umwe wangu?
\v	30	Jesu akapindura akati: Umwe munhu wakaburuka kubva kuJerusarema achienda kuJeriko; akawira pakati pemakororo, akamukururawo akamupa mavanga akaenda, ndokusiya oda kufa.
\v	31	Zvakangoitikawo kuti umwe mupristi waiva achiburuka nenzira iyo; akati achimuona, akapfuura neparutivi rwakapesa.
\v	32	Saizvozvowo muRevhi asvika panzvimbo iyo, aka muona, akapfuura neparutivi rwakapesa.
\v	33	Zvino umwe muSamariya, waiva achifamba wakasvika paaiva, akati achimuona akanzwa tsitsi,
\v	34	akaswedera ndokupomba mavanga ake, achidiramo mafuta newaini; akamugadza pachipfuwo chake pachake, ndokumuisa kuimba yevafambi, ndokumuchengeta.
\v	35	Zvino pamangwana oenda, wakabudisa madhenario maviri akapa kumwene weimba, akati kwaari: Muchengete; uye zvose zvaunoshandisa kupfuura  izvi, ini ndichakupazve kana ndichidzoka.
\v	36	Naizvozvo ndeupi wevatatu ava waunofunga kuti waiva umwe waiye wakawira pakati pemakororo?
\v	37	Akati: Ndiye wakamuitira tsitsi. Ipapo Jesu akati kwaari: Enda, newe unoita saizvozvo.
\s	Marita naMaria
\p
\v	38	Zvino zvakaitika pakufamba kwavo, kuti iye wakapinda mune umwe musha; uye umwe mukadzi wainzi Marita akamugamuchira mumba make.
\v	39	Uye iye waiva ane munin'inawo wainzi Maria, wakagarawo pamakumbo aJesu, akanzwa shoko rake.
\v	40	Asi Marita waiva akabatikana kwazvo nekushanda kukuru, akaswedera akati: Ishe, hamuna hanya here nokuti munin'ina wangu wandisiya ndega ndichishanda? Naizvozvo muudzei kuti andibatsire.
\v	41	Jesu akapindura akati kwaari: Marita, Marita, unozvidya moyo nekutambudzika nezvinhu zvizhinji;
\v	42	asi chinhu chimwe chinodikanwa; asi Maria wasanangura mugove wakanaka, waasingazotorerwi.
\c	11
\s	Jesu anodzidzisa kunyengetera
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika achinyengetera pane imwe nzvimbo, apo amira, umwe wevadzidzi vake akati kwaari: Ishe, tidzidzisei kunyengetera, Johwani sezvaakadzidzisawo vadzidzi vake.
\v	2	Ndokutikwavari: Kana muchinyengetera, muti: Baba vedu vari kumatenga, zita renyu ngariitwe dzvene. Ushe hwenyu ngahuuye. Chido chenyu ngachiitwe, panyikawo se zvachinoitwa kudenga.
\v	3	Mutipeizuva rimwe nerimwe kudya kwedu kwemazuva ose.
\v	4	Uyemutikanganwire zvivi zvedu, nokuti isuwo tinokanganwira ani nani ane ngava kwatiri. Uye musatipinza pakuedzwa, asi mutisunungure pakuipa.
\s	Mufananidzo weshamwari yakatsungirira
\p
\v	5	Akatikwavari: Ndeupi wenyu ane shamwari, uye anoenda kwaari pakati peusiku, akati kwaari: Shamwari, ndikwerete zvingwa zvitatu,
\v	6	nokutishamwari yangu yasvika kwandiri ichibva parwendo, asi handina chinhu chandingamuperekera;
\v	7	uyeuyo ari mukati akapindura akati: Usandinetsa; ikozvino mukova wapfigwa, nevana vangu vadiki vaneni pamubhedha; handigoni kumuka ndikakupa.
\v	8	Ndinotikwamuri: Kunyange akasamuka akamupa, nekuda kwekuva shamwari yake, asi nekuda kwekutsungirira kwake achamuka akamupa zvose zvaanoshaiwa.
\v	9	Zvinoini ndinoti kwamuri: Kumbirai, muchapiwa; tsvakai, muchawana; gogodzai, muchazarurirwa.
\v	10	Nokuti wose anokumbira anogamuchira; neanotsvaka anowana; neanogogodza anozarurirwa.
\v	11	Kuti ndeupi wenyu ari baba, kana mwanakomana achikumbira chingwa, angazomupa ibwe? Kanawo hove, angazomupa nyoka panzvimbo yehove here?
\v	12	Kanawo kuti akakumbira zai, angazomupa chinyavada here?
\v	13	Naizvozvo kana imwi makaipa muchiziva kupa zvipo zvakanaka vana venyu, Baba vari kudenga vachapa zvikuru sei Mweya Mutsvene vanovakumbira.
\s	Jesu anobudisa dhimoni
\p
\v	14	Zvino waiva achibudisa dhimoni iro raiva chimumumu. Zvino zvakaitika kuti dhimoni rabuda, chimumumu chikataura; zvaunga zvikashamisika.
\v	15	Asi vamwe vavo vakati: Anobudisa madhimoni naBheerizebhuri mukuru wemadhimoni.
\v	16	Uye vamwe vachiidza, vakatsvaka kwaari chiratidzo chinobva kudenga.
\v	17	Asi iye achiziva mifungo yavo wakati kwavari: Ushe hwose hunopesana huchizvipikisa hunoparadzwa; neimba inopikisana neimba inowa.
\v	18	Kana Sataniwo achipesana achizvipikisa, ushe hwake hungazomira sei? Nokuti munoti, ndinobudisa madhimoni naBheerizebhuri.
\v	19	Asi kana ini ndichibudisa madhimoni naBheerizebhuri, vanakomana venyu vanobudisa nani? Naizvozvo ivo vachava vatongi venyu.
\v	20	Asi kana ndichibudisa madhimoni nemunwe waMwari, naizvozvo ushe hwaMwari hwasvika kwamuri.
\v	21	Kana chikakarara chashonga zvombo chichirinda chivanze chacho, nhumbi dzacho dziri murugare;
\v	22	asi kana wakasimba kuchipfuura aka chirwisa, akachikunda, anotora nhumbi dzacho dzose dzekurwa dzachange chichivimba nadzo, ndokugovera zvaapamba zvacho.
\v	23	Asina ini anopesana neni; neasingaunganidzi neni anoparadzira.
\s	Kutendeuka kusingaperi
\p
\v	24	Kana mweya wetsvina wabuda mumunhu, unofamba uchigura nemunzvimbo dzakaoma, uchitsvaka zororo; asi uchishaiwa unoti: Ndichadzokera kuimba yangu kwandakabuda.
\v	25	Zvino kana wasvika unowana yatsvairwa nekurongedzwa.
\v	26	Ipapo unoenda ndokutora pamwe naye imwe mweya minomwe yakaipa kuupfuura, igopinda ndokugaramo; uye kuguma kwemunhu uyo kwakaipa kupfuura kutanga.
\p
\v	27	Zvino zvakaitika pakutaura kwake zvinhu izvozvi, umwe mukadzi pakati pechaunga akasimudza inzwi, akati kwaari: Rakaropafadzwa dumbu rakakutakurai, nemazamu amakayamwa.
\v	28	Asi iye akati: Hongu, asi zvikuru vakaropafadzwa vanonzwa shoko raMwari vachirichengeta.

\v	2	akaonazvikepe zviviri zvimire parutivi rwegungwa; asi varedzi vaiva vabuda mazviri vachisuka mimbure.
\v	3	Akapindamune chimwe chezvikepe chaiva chaSimoni, akakumbira kwaari kuti abve pavhu zvishoma. Ndokubva agara pasi, akadzidzisa zvaunga ari muchikepe.
\p
\v	4	Zvinowakati amira kutaura, akati kuna Simoni: Pinda kwakadzika udzikise mimbure yenyu kuti ubate.
\v	5	Simoniakapindura, akati kwaari: Tenzi, tafondoka usiku hwose, tikasabata chinhu, asi zvamadaro, ndichadzikisa mumbure.
\v	6	Vakativaita izvozvi, vakakomba dutu rehove zhinji; mumbure wavo ukange wobvaruka.
\v	7	Vakaniniravamwe vavo vaiva vari mune chimwe chikepe, kuti vauye vazovabatsira; vakauya vakazadza zvikepe zviri zviviri, zvekuti zvakatanga kunyura.
\v	8	ZvinoSimoni Petro wakati achiona, akawira pasi pamabvi aJesu, achiti: Ibvai kwandiri, Ishe, nokuti ndiri munhu mutadzi.
\v	9	Nokutikushamisika kwaiva kwamubata, nevose vaiva vanaye, nekubatwa kwehove dzavaiva vabata;
\v	10	saizvozvowo Jakobho naJohwani, vanakomana vaZebhedhi, vaiva vadyidzani naSimoni. Jesu ndokuti kuna Simoni: Usatya, kubva zvino ucharedza vanhu.
\v	11	Zvino, vakati vaisa zvikepe kunyika, vakasiya zvose vakamutevera.
\s	Jesu anoporesa munhu unamaperembudzi
\p
\v	12	Zvino zvakaitika, ari kune rimwe remaguta, tarira, murume azere maperembudzi; wakati achiona Jesu, akawa nechiso, ndokumugombetedza, achiti: Ishe, kana muchida, munogona kundinatsa.
\v	13	Ndokubva atandavadza ruoko, akamubata, achiti: Ndinoda, chinatswa. Pakarepo maperembudzi akabva kwaari.
\v	14	Iye akamuraira kuti asaudza munhu;  akati: Asi enda unozviratidza kumupristi, ubaire pakunatswa kwako, Mozisi sezvaakaraira, chive chapupu kwavari.
\v	15	Asi shoko pamusoro pake rakanyanya kupararira; zvaunga zvikuru zvikaungana kuzonzwa, nekuporeswa naye pazvifo zvavo.
\v	16	Asi iye waienda kumarenje achinonyengetera.
\s	Jesu anoporesa munhu wakafa mitezo
\p
\v	17	Zvino zvakaitika nerimwe remazuva, iye paaiva achidzidzisa; zvino vaFarisi nevadzidzisi vemurairo vaiva vagere po, vaibva kumisha yose yeGarirea neJudhiya neJerusarema; nesimba raIshe raiva riri po kuvaporesa.
\v	18	Uye tarira, varume vaiva vakatakura munhu panhovo waiva akafa mitezo; vakatsvaka kumupinza vaise pamberi pake.
\v	19	Zvino, vakati vachishaiwa pavangamupinza napo nekuda kwechaunga, vakakwira padenga peimba, vakamuburusira pasi nemumapfuriro pamwe nenhovo  yake, pakati pamberi paJesu.
\v	20	Iye wakati aona rutendo rwavo, akati kwaari: Munhu, zvivi zvako wazvikanganwirwa.
\v	21	Zvino vanyori nevaFarisi vakatanga kufunga, vachiti: Ndiani uyu anotaura kunyomba? Ndiani anogona kukanganwira zvivi, kunze kwaMwari oga?
\v	22	Zvino Jesu paakaziva mifungo yavo, akapindura, akati kwavari: Munofungei mumoyo yenyu?
\v	23	Chii chakareruka, kuti: Zvivi zvako wazviregererwa; kana kuti: Simuka, ufambe?
\v	24	Asi kuti muzive kuti Mwanakomana wemunhu ane simba panyika rekuregerera zvivi (akati kune waiva akafa mitezo): Ndinoti kwauri: Simuka, utore nhovo yako, uende kumba kwako.
\v	25	Pakarepo akasimuka pamberi pavo, akatora chaaiva arere pachiri, akaenda kumba kwake, achirumbidza Mwari.
\v	26	Kuvhiringidzika kukabata vose, vakarumbidza Mwari, vakazadzwa nekutya, vachiti: Taona chisionekwe nhasi.
\s	Kudanwa kwaRevhi
\p
\v	27	Zvino shure kwezvinhu izvi wakabuda, akaona muteresi, wainzi Revhi, agere paimba yemutero, akati kwaari: Nditevere.
\v	28	Ndokubva asiya zvose, akasimuka akamutevera.
\v	29	Revhi ndokumuitira mabiko makuru mumba make; kukavapo chaunga chikuru chevateresi nevamwe vaiva vagere navo pakudya.
\v	30	Asi vanyori vavo nevaFarisi vakanyunyutira vadzidzi vake, vachiti: Nemhaka yei muchidya nekunwa nevateresi nevatadzi?
\v	31	Jesu achipindura akati kwavari: Vatano havadi murapi, asi avo vanorwara.
\v	32	Handina kuuya kuzodana vakarurama, asi vatadzi vauye mukutendeuka.
\s	Kutsanya
\p
\v	33	Zvino vakati kwaari: Nemhaka yei vadzidzi vaJohwani vachitsanya kazhinji, vachiita minyengetero, uye saizvozvo vevaFarisi, asi venyu vanodya nekunwa?
\v	34	Asi wakati kwavari: Munogona kutsanyisa vana veimba yemuchato muwani achiri kwavari here?
\v	35	Asi mazuva achasvika, apo muwani achabviswa kwavari, ipapo vachatsanya nemazuva iwayo.
\p
\v	36	Akataurawo mufananidzo kwavari, akati: Hakuna munhu anoisa chigamba chemucheka mutsva panguvo sharu; kana zvikasadaro naichowo chitsva chinobvarura, nechigamba che mucheka mutsva hachipindirani nesharu.
\v	37	Uye hakuna munhu anodira waini itsva muhombodo sharu; kana zvikasadaro, waini itsva ichaparusa hombodo iyo ikateuka, uye hombodo dzikaparara.
\v	38	Asi waini itsva inofanira kuiswa muhombodo itsva, zvichengetedzeke zviri zviviri.
\v	39	Uye hakuna munhu anoti anwa  waini sharu pakarepo akashuva itsva; nokuti anoti: Sharu iri nani.
\c	6
\s	Jesu Ishe wesabata
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika nesabata rechipiri shure kwerekutanga, achigura nemuminda yezviyo, vadzidzi vake vatanha hura, vakadya, vachi dzipukuta nemaoko.
\v	2	Asivamwe vevaFarisi vakati kwavari: Munoitirei zvisiri pamutemo kuita nemasabata?
\v	3	ZvinoJesu achivapindura, wakati: Hamuna kuverenga izvowo Dhavhidhi zvaakaita, paaiva ava nenzara iye nevaiva vanaye,
\v	4	kutiwakapinda sei mumba maMwari, akatora akadya zvingwa zvekuratidza, akapa nevaiva vanaye; izvo zvisiri pamutemo kudyiwa asi nevapristi vega?
\v	5	Ndokutikwavari: Mwanakomana wemunhu ndiIshe wesabatawo.
\s	Jesu anoporesa munhu waiva neruoko rwakawonyana
\p
\v	6	Zvinozvakaitikawo nerimwe sabata kuti wakapinda musinagoge, akadzidzisa; zvino kwaiva kune munhu ipapo uye ruoko rwake rwerudyi rwaiva rwakawonyana.
\v	7	VanyorinevaFarisi vakamutarisisa,  kuona kana angaporesa nesabata, kuti vawane mhosva yavangamupomera.
\v	8	Asiiye wakaziva mifungo yavo, akati kumunhu waiva ane ruoko rwakawonyana: Simuka, uende unomira pakati. Akasimuka akamira.
\v	9	ZvinoJesu akati kwavari: Ndichakubvunzai  chimwe chinhu: Zviri pamutemo here nemasabata kuita zvakanaka kana kuita zvakaipa? Kuponesa upenyu kana kuparadza?
\v	10	Zvino akaringa-ringa kwavari vose, akati kumunhu: Tandavadza ruoko rwako. Akaita saizvozvo. Ruoko rwake rwukaporeswa ndokugwinya serumwe.
\v	11	Ivo vakazara neupengo; vakataurirana umwe neumwe, kuti vangamuitei Jesu.
\s	Jesu anodana vadzidzi vake
\p
\v	12	Zvino zvakaitika nemazuva iwayo kuti wakabuda akaenda kugomo kunonyengetera; agara usiku hwose mukunyengetera kuna Mwari.
\v	13	Zvino kwakati kwaedza, akadanira vadzidzi vake kwaari; akasanangura kwavari gumi nevaviri, vaakatumidzawo  kuti vaapositori;
\v	14	Simoni, waakatumidzawo Petro, naAndiriya munin'ina wake, Jakobho naJohwani, Firipi naBhatoromiyo,
\v	15	Matewu naTomasi, Jakobho waArifiyosi, naSimoni wainzi Ziroti;
\v	16	Judhasi waJakobho, naJudhasi Isikariyoti, wakavawo mutengesi.
\p
\v	17	Akaburuka navo, akamira panzvimbo pakaenzana, nechaunga chevadzidzi vake, nechaunga chikuru chevanhu vaibva Judhiya rose neJerusarema, nepagungwa reTire neSidhoni, vakauya kuzomunzwa, nekuporeswa pazvifo zvavo;
\v	18	nevaiva vachitambudziwa nemweya yetsvina, vakaporeswa.
\v	19	Chaunga chose chikatsvaka kumubata; nokuti kwakabuda simba kwaari rikaporesa vose.
\s	Mharidzo yaJesu
\p
\v	20	Ndokusimudzira meso ake kuvadzidzi vake, akati: Makaropafadzwa varombo, nokuti ushe hwaMwari ndehwenyu.
\v	21	Makaropafadzwa imwi mune nzara zvino, nokuti muchagutiswa. Makaropafadzwa imwi munochema ikozvino, nokuti muchaseka.
\v	22	Makaropafadzwa, kana vanhu vachikuvengai, uye kana vachikusarurai, vachikunyombai, vachirasa zita renyu serakashata, nekuda kweMwanakomana wemunhu.
\v	23	Farai nezuva iro, muuruke nemufaro; nokuti tarirai, mubairo wenyu mukuru kudenga; nokuti saizvozvo zvakaita madzibaba avo kuvaporofita.
\v	24	Asi mune nhamo imwi vakafuma! Nokuti magamuchira nyaradzo yenyu.
\v	25	Mune nhamo imwi munoguta! Nokuti muchanzwa nzara. Mune nhamo imwi munoseka zvino! Nokuti muchachema nekuwungudza.
\v	26	Mune nhamo kana vanhu vose vachitaura zvakanaka pamusoro penyu! Nokuti madzibaba avo akaita saizvozvo kuvaporofita venhema.
\v	27	Asi ndinoti kwamuri munonzwa: Idai mhandu dzenyu, itai zvakanaka kune vanokuvengai,
\v	28	ropafadzai vanokutukai, munyengeterere vanokubatai zvakaipa.
\v	29	Kune anokurova padama, umupe rimwewo; anokutorera nguvo, usarambidza neshati.
\v	30	Asi upe umwe neumwe anokumbira kwauri; neanokutorera zvako, usazvirevazve.
\v	31	Uye sezvamunoda kuti vanhu vaite kwamuri, imwi muvaitirewo saizvozvo.
\v	32	Uye kana muchida avo vanokudai, mune kuvongwa kwakadini? Nokuti vatadziwo vanoda vanovada.
\v	33	Uye kana muchiitira zvakanaka kune avo vanokuitirai zvakanaka, mune kuvongwa kwakadini? Nokuti vatadzi vanoitawo izvozvo.
\v	34	Uye kana muchikweretesa vamunotarisira kugamuchira kwavari, mune kuvongwa kwakadini? Nokuti vatadzi vanokweretesawo vatadzi, kuti vagamuchirezve zvakaenzana nazvo.
\v	35	Asi idai vavengi venyu, muite zvakanaka, mukweretese musingatarisirizve chinhu; zvino mubairo wenyu uchava mukuru, mugova vana veWekumusoro-soro; nokuti iye ane moyo munyoro kune vasingavongi nevakaipa.
\v	36	Naizvozvo ivai netsitsi, saBaba venyuwo vane tsitsi.
\v	37	Musatonga, uye hamungazotongwi; musapa mhosva, uye hamungazopiwi mhosva; regererai, mugoregererwawo.
\v	38	Ipai, nemwi muchapiwa; chiyero chakanaka, chakatsikirirwa, nekuzunguzirwa, chinopfachukira vachakupai pachifuva chenyu. Nokuti nechiyero ichocho chamunoyera nacho muchayerwa nacho zvekare.
\p
\v	39	Akataura mufananidzo kwavari  akati: Ko bofu ringatungamirira bofu here? Ko haangawiri mugomba ari maviri here?
\v	40	Mudzidzi haasi pamusoro pemudzidzisi wake; asi umwe neumwe kana apedzeredzwa achava semudzidzisi wake.
\p
\v	41	Uye unotarirei rubanzu rwuri muziso reumwe wako, asi danda riri muziso rako pachako haurinanzereri?
\v	42	Kana unogona kureva sei kune umwe wako,  uchiti: Umwe wangu, rega ndibudise rubanzu rwuri muziso rako, iwe pachako usingaoni danda riri muziso rako? Iwe munyepedzeri, budisa pakutanga danda kubva muziso rako, zvino ipapo uchaona zvakanaka kubudisa rubanzu rwuri muziso reumwe wako.
\p
\v	43	Nokuti hausi muti wakanaka unobereka chibereko chakaipa; kana muti wakaipa unobereka chibereko chakanaka.
\v	44	Nokuti muti umwe neumwe unozikanwa nechibereko chawo pachawo. Nokuti paminzwa havatanhi maonde, uye parukato havatanhi mazambiringa.
\v	45	Munhu wakanaka kubva pafuma yakanaka yemoyo wake anobudisa zvakanaka, nemunhu wakaipa kubva pafuma yakaipa yemoyo wake anobudisa zvakaipa; nokuti kubva pazvizere mumoyo muromo wake anotaura.
\s	Mufananidzo weimba yakavakwa paruware
\p
\v	46	Zvino munondiidzirei Ishe, Ishe, musingaiti zvinhu zvandinoreva?
\v	47	Ani nani anouya kwandiri akanzwa mashoko angu akaaita, ndichakuratidzai kuti wakafanana nani:
\v	48	Wakafanana nemunhu wakavaka imba, akachera akadzikisa, akateya nheyo paruware; zvino mafashame akati auya, rukova rukarova zvine simba paimba iyo, rukasagona kuizunungusa; nokuti yaiva yakateyiwa paruware.
\v	49	Asi anonzwa asingaiti, wakafanana nemunhu wakavaka imba pavhu isina nheyo; ipo rukova rwakarova nesimba, pakarepo ikawa; uye kuwa kweimba iyo kukava kukuru.
\c	7
\s	Jesu anoporesa muranda wemukuru wezana
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakati apedza mashoko ake ose munzeve dzevanhu, akapinda muKapenaume.
\v	2	Zvinomuranda weumwe mukuru wezana, waiva akakosha kwaari, waiva achirwara, achitandadza.
\v	3	Zvinowakati anzwa zvaJesu, akatuma kwaari vakuru vevaJudha, achimukumbira kuti auye aporese muranda wake.
\v	4	Zvinoivo vakati vachisvika kuna Jesu, vakamukumbirisa zvikuru, vachiti: Wakafanira kuti mumuitire izvozvi;
\v	5	nokutianoda rudzi rwedu, uye ndiye wakativakira sinagoge.
\v	6	ZvinoJesu akaenda navo. Zvino apo aiva asisiri kure neimba, mukuru wezana akatuma shamwari kwaari, achiti kwaari: Ishe, musazvitambudza; nokuti handina kufanira kuti mupinde pasi pedenga reimba yangu;
\v	7	naizvozvohandina kutongofunga ini pachangu ndakafanira kuuya kwamuri; asi taurai neshoko, muranda wangu achaporeswa.
\v	8	Nokutiiniwo ndiri munhu wakaiswa pasi pesimba, ndine mauto pasi pangu; ndinoti kune uyu: Enda, anoenda; uye kune umwe: Uya, anouya; nekumuranda wangu: Ita ichi, achiita.
\v	9	Jesuwakati anzwa zvinhu izvi, akashamisika naye, akatendeuka, akati kuchaunga chaimutevera: Ndinoti kwamuri: Handina kuwana rutendo rukuru rwakadai kunyange pakati paIsraeri.
\v	10	Zvino avo vaiva vatumwa vodzokera kumba vakawana muranda airwara ava mutano.
\s	Jesu anomutsa mwanakomana wechirikadzi paNaini
\p
\v	11	Zvino zvakaitika  zuva raitevera, kuti wakaenda kuguta rainzi Naini; zvino vazhinji vevadzidzi vake nechaunga chikuru vakaenda naye.
\v	12	Zvino, wakati achiswedera pasuwo reguta, zvino tarira, mushakabvu achibudiswa kunze, mwanakomana umwe woga wamai vake, uye ivo vaiva chirikadzi; nechaunga chikuru cheguta chaiva chinaye.
\v	13	Zvino Ishe wakati amuona, akamunzwira tsitsi, akati kwaari: Usachema.
\v	14	Zvino akaswedera akabata nhovo; zvino vatakuri vakamira; akati: Jaya, ndinoti kwauri: Muka!
\v	15	Mushakabvu akamuka ndokugara, akatanga kutaura; akamupa kuna mai vake.
\v	16	Kutya ndokubata vose; vakarumbidza Mwari, vachiti: Muporofita mukuru wamuka pakati pedu; nekuti: Mwari washanyira vanhu vake.
\v	17	Zvino iri shoko maererano naye rikabudira muJudhiya rose nenharaunda yose yakapoteredza.
\s	Johwani Mubhabhatidzi anotuma vadzidzi kunobvunza Jesu
\p
\v	18	Vadzidzi vaJohwani ndokumupira zvezvinhu izvozvi zvose.
\v	19	Johwani akadanira kwaari vamwe vaviri vevadzidzi vake, aka vatuma kuna Jesu achiti: Imwi ndimwi anouya here, kana tichakataririra umwe?
\v	20	Zvino varume vakati vasvika kwaari, vakati: Johwani Mubhabhatidzi watituma kwamuri achiti: Imwi ndimwi anouya here, kana titaririre umwe?
\v	21	Zvino munguva iyoyo akaporesa vazhinji pazvifo neurwere, nepamweya yakaipa; nekumapofu mazhinji akapa kuona.
\v	22	Zvino Jesu achipindura akati kwavari: Endai munoudza Johwani zvamakaona nezvamakanzwa; zvekuti, mapofu anoonazve, vanokamhina vanofamba, vane maperembudzi vanonatsva, matsi dzinonzwa, vakafa vanomutswa, varombo vanoparidzirwa evhangeri.
\v	23	Uye wakaropafadzwa ani nani asingagumbuswi neni.
\p
\v	24	Zvino vatumwa vaJohwani vakati vabva, wakatanga kutaura kuzvaunga pamusoro paJohwani,  achiti: Makabudira kurenje kunoonei? Rutsanga rwunozununguswa nemhepo here?
\v	25	Asi maiva mabuda kunoonei? Munhu wakapfeka nguvo dzakapfava here? Tarirai, vari munguvo dzinobwinya, vachirarama zvakanaka, vari mudzimba dzemadzimambo.
\v	26	Asi maiva mabuda kunoonei? Muporofita here? Hongu, ndinoti kwamuri: Kunyange anopfuura zvikuru muporofita.
\v	27	Uyu ndiye wakanyorwa pamusoro pake,  zvichinzi: Tarira, ini ndinotuma mutumwa wangu pamberi pechiso chako, achagadzira nzira yako pamberi pako.
\p
\v	28	Nokuti ndinoti kwamuri: Pakati pevakaberekwa nevakadzi hakuna muporofita mukuru kuna Johwani Mubhabhatidzi; asi mudikisa muushe hwaMwari mukuru kwaari.
\p
\v	29	Zvino vanhu vose nevateresi vakati vachizvinzwa vakati Mwari wakarurama, zvavaiva vabhabhatidzwa nerubhabhatidzo rwaJohwani.
\v	30	Asi vaFarisi nenyanzvi dzemutemo vakaramba zano raMwari vachizvipikisa, vakasabhabhatidzwa naye.
\p
\v	31	Asi Ishe akati: Zvino vanhu vezera iri ndichavafananidza nei? Uye vakafanana nei?
\v	32	Vakafanana nevana vadiki vagere pamisika vachidanidzirana vachiti: Takakuridzirai nyere asi hamuna kutamba; takakuririrai asi hamuna kuchema.
\v	33	Nokuti Johwani Mubhabhatidzi akauya asingadyi chingwa kana kunwa waini, zvino moti: Ane dhimoni;
\v	34	Mwanakomana wemunhu wauya achidya, achinwa, zvino moti: Tarirai munhu wemakaro nemunwi wewaini, shamwari yevateresi nevatadzi.
\v	35	Asi uchenjeri hunonzi hwakarurama nevana vahwo vose.
\s	Mukadzi mutadzi anozodza makumbo aJesu
\p
\v	36	Zvino umwe wevaFarisi wakamukumbira kuti adye naye; akapinda mumba memuFarisi, akagara pakudya.
\v	37	Zvino tarira, mukadzi muguta iro, waiva mutadzi, wakati aziva kuti agere pakudya mumba memuFarisi, akauya nechinu chearibhasiteri chechizoro.
\v	38	Akamira patsoka dzake mushure  make achichema, akatanga kunyorovesa tsoka dzake nemisodzi, akapukuta nevhudzi remusoro wake, ndokutsvoda tsoka dzake ndoku dzizodza nechizoro.
\v	39	Zvino muFarisi waiva amudana wakati achi zviona, akataura mukati make, achiti: Uyu dai aiva muporofita, angadai aiziva kuti ndiani, uye ndewakadini mukadzi anomubata, nokuti mutadzi.
\v	40	Jesu ndokupindura akati kwaari: Simoni, ndine chekutaura kwauri. Iye ndokuti: Mudzidzisi, revai.
\v	41	Kwaiva kune vakwereti vaviri kune umwe mukweretesi; umwe waiva ane chikwereti chamadhenario mazana mashanu, uye umwe makumi mashanu.
\v	42	Vakati vasina chekuripisa, akavakanganwira vari vaviri. Naizvozvo ndeupi wavo, taura, achamuda kupfuura  umwe?
\v	43	Simoni akapindura akati: Ndinofunga ndouyo waakakanganwira kupfuura  umwe. Akati kwaari: Watonga zvakarurama.
\v	44	Zvino akatendeukira kumukadzi, akati kuna Simoni: Unoona mukadzi uyu here? Ndapinda mumba mako, mvura yetsoka dzangu hauna kundipa; asi uyu wanyorevesa tsoka dzangu nemisodzi, akapusika nevhudzi remusoro wake.
\v	45	Hauna kunditsvoda, asi iye chinguri chandapinda, haana kumira kutsvoda tsoka dzangu.
\v	46	Nemafuta musoro wangu hauna kuuzodza; asi iye wazodza tsoka dzangu nechizoro.
\v	47	Naizvozvo, ndinoti kwauri: Zvivi zvake zvizhinji zvakanganwirwa, nokuti wada zvikuru; asi anokanganwirwa zvishoma, anoda zvishoma.
\v	48	Zvino akati kwaari: Zvivi zvako zvakanganwirwa.
\v	49	Zvino avo vaiva vagere pakudya naye vakatanga kureva mukati mavo,  vachiti: Ndiani uyu anokanganwirawo zvivi?
\v	50	Akati kumukadzi: Rutendo rwako rwakuponesa; enda nerugare.
\c	8
\s	Vakadzi vaishandira Jesu nenhumbi dzavo
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kweizvozvo, kuti iye wakagura nemuguta nemusha umwe neumwe, achidanidzira nekuparidza mashoko akanaka eushe hwaMwari; nevanegumi nevaviri vanaye,
\v	2	nevamwevakadzi vaiva vaporeswa pamweya yakaipa neurwere, Maria anonzi Magidharini, maari makabuda madhimoni manomwe,
\v	3	naJohwanamukadzi waKuza mutariri waHerodhi, naSusana, nevamwe vazhinji, vaimubatsira nenhumbi dzavo.
\s	Mufananidzo wemukushi
\p
\v	4	Zvinochaunga chikuru chakati chaungana, uye vaibva kuguta ripi neripi vachiuya kwaari, akataura nemufananidzo  akati:
\v	5	Mukushiwakabuda kunokusha mbeu yake; zvino pakukusha kwake, imwe yakawira kurutivi rwenzira, ndokutsikwa netsoka, uye shiri dzekudenga dzikaidya dzikaipedza.
\v	6	Uyeimwe ikawira paruware; zvino yakamera, ikaoma, nokuti yakashaiwa unyoro.
\v	7	Neimweikawira pakati peminzwa, minzwa ikakura nayo, ikaivhunga.
\v	8	Uyeimwe yakawira muvhu rakanaka, ikamera, ikabereka zvibereko zvine zana. Wakati ataura zvinhu izvi, akadanidzira  achiti: Ane nzeve dzekunzwa, ngaanzwe.
\p
\v	9	Vadzidzivake ndokumubvunza vachiti: Mufananidzo uyu ungavei?
\v	10	Ndokubva ati: Kwamuri kwakapiwa kuziva zvakavanzika zveushe hwaMwari; asi kune vamwe  zviri mumifananidzo, kuti vachiona vasaona, uye vachinzwa vasanzwisisa.
\p
\v	11	Zvino mufananidzo ndiwoyu: Mbeu ishoko raMwari.
\v	12	Avo veparutivi rwenzira ndivo vanonzwa, ipapo dhiabhorosi anouya ndokubvisa shoko pamoyo wavo, kuti varege kutenda, vaponeswe.
\v	13	Neveparuware ndevaya, vanoti kana vanzwa, vanogamuchira shoko nemufaro; asi ava havana mudzi, vanotenda kwenguva, asi nenguva yemuedzo vanowa.
\v	14	Neyakawira paminzwa, ndevaya kana vanzwa, zvino vanoenda ndokuvhungwa nekufunganya nefuma nemafaro eupenyu, vakasasvitsa zvibereko.
\v	15	Asi iri muvhu rakanaka, ndevaya vanoti vanzwa shoko, vano richengeta mumoyo wakarurama newakanaka, vachibereka zvibereko nekutsungirira.
\s	Mufananidzo wemwenje
\p
\v	16	Hakuna munhu anoti kana atungidza mwenje, anoufukidzira nemudziyo, kana kuisa pasi pemubhedha; asi anouisa pachigadziko chemwenje, kuti avo vanopinda vaone chiedza.
\v	17	Nokuti hakuna chakavanzika, chisingazovi pachena, kana chakavigwa, chisingazozikanwi nekubuda pachena.
\p
\v	18	Naizvozvo chenjerai, kuti munonzwa sei; nokuti ani nani anazvo, achapiwa; asi ani nani asina, achatorerwa kunyange icho chaanofunga kuti anacho.
\s	Ukama naJesu
\p
\v	19	Zvino kwakauya kwaari mai nevanin'ina vake, vakasagona kusvika kwaari nekuda kwechaunga.
\v	20	Akaudzwa  nevamwe vakati: Mai venyu nevanin'ina venyu vamire panze, vanoda kukuonai.
\v	21	Asi wakapindura akati kwavari: Mai vangu nevanin'ina vangu ndeava vanonzwa shoko raMwari vachiriita.
\s	Jesu anonyaradza dutu remhepo
\p
\v	22	Zvino zvakaitika nerimwe remazuva kuti wakapinda muchikepe iye nevadzidzi vake, akati kwavari: Ngatiyambukire kune rumwe rutivi rwegungwa; vakasimuka nechikepe.
\v	23	Zvino vakati vachienda nechikepe, akarara hope; kukauya dutu guru remhepo padziva; vakazarirwa  nemvura, vakava munjodzi.
\v	24	Vakaswedera vakamumutsa, vachiti: Tenzi, tenzi, toparara! Ipapo akamuka, akatsiura mhepo nemafungu emvura; zvikaguma, kukava nekudzikama.
\v	25	Ndokubva ati kwavari: Rutendo rwenyu rwuripi? Asi vachitya vakashamisika, vakataurirana  vachiti: Ko ndeupi uyu, kuti anoraira kunyange nemhepo nemvura, zvichimuteerera?
\s	Jesu anobudisa mweya yetsvina panyika yevaGadharini
\p
\v	26	Zvino vakaenda nechikepe kunyika yevaGadharini, yakatarisana neGarirea.
\v	27	Wakati achibudira panyika, akamuchingamidza umwe murume achibva muguta, waiva ane madhimoni kwenguva refu, uye asingapfeki nguvo, asingagari mumba, asi kumarinda.
\v	28	Wakati achiona Jesu, akadanidzira ndokuwira pasi pamberi pake, akati nenzwi guru: Ndinei nemwi, Jesu, Mwanakomana waMwari Wekumusoro-soro? Ndinokukumbirisai, musanditambudza.
\v	29	Nokuti waiva araira mweya wetsvina kuti ubude kumunhu; nokuti waiva wamubata nguva zhinji uye waichengetwa akasungwa nemaketani nemabote; asi akadambura zvisungo, achitinhirwa kumarenje nedhimoni.
\v	30	Jesu ndokumubvunza achiti: Zita rako ndiani? Akati: Regiyoni; nokuti madhimoni mazhinji aiva apinda maari.
\v	31	Akamukumbirisa kuti arege kumaraira kuti aende kugomba risina chigadziko.
\v	32	Zvino kwaiva kuripo ipapo boka renguruve zhinji dzaifura mugomo; akamukumbirisa kuti amatendere kupinda madziri. Akamatendera.
\v	33	Zvino madhimoni akabuda kumunhu, akapinda munguruve; boka ndokupitirikira kumawere richienda kudziva, rikabitirirwa.
\v	34	Zvino vai dzifudza vachiona zvaitika, vakatiza, vakaenda kunotaura muguta nemumaruwa.
\v	35	Zvino vakabuda kunoona zvaitika; vakasvika kuna Jesu, vakawana munhu waiva abuda madhimoni maari, agere patsoka dzaJesu, akapfeka uye akakwana mupfungwa; vakatya.
\v	36	Vaiva va zvionawo vakavaudza kuti akaponeswa sei aiva nemadhimoni.
\v	37	Zvino chaunga chose chedunhu rakapoteredza vaGadharini chakakumbirisa kwaari kuti abve kwavari, nokuti vabatwa nekutya kukuru; akapinda muchikepe, akadzokera.
\v	38	Asi murume waiva abuda madhimoni maari, wakakumbirisa kwaari kuti ave naye. Asi Jesu wakamurega achienda, achiti:
\v	39	Dzokera kumba kwako, unotaura kuti zvikuru sei Mwari zvaaita kwauri. Akaenda, akaparidza muguta rose kuti zvikuru sei Jesu zvaakamuitira.
\s	Jesu anoporesa mukadzi waiva nekubuda ropa, uye anomutsa mukunda waJairosi
\p
\v	40	Zvino zvakaitika kuti pakudzoka kwaJesu, chaunga chakamugamuchira; nokuti vose vaiva vakamurindira.
\v	41	Zvino tarira, kwakasvika murume, zita rake  rainzi Jairosi, iye waiva ari mukuru wesinagoge, ndokuwira patsoka dzaJesu, akamukumbirisa kuti apinde mumba make;
\v	42	nokuti waiva ane mukunda wakaberekwa umwe woga wemakore anenge gumi nemaviri, uye iye waiva achitandadza. Zvino wakati achaenda, zvaunga zvikamumbandidzira.
\v	43	Uye mukadzi, waiva nekubuda ropa kwemakore gumi nemaviri, waiva apedzera zvose zveupenyu hwake kuvarapi, asingagoni kurapwa kwete neumwe.
\v	44	Akauya mushure make, akabata mupendero wenguvo yake; pakarepo kubuda kweropa rake kukaguma.
\v	45	Jesu ndokuti: Ndiani wandibata? Zvino vose vakati vachiramba, Petro nevaiva naye vakati: Tenzi, zvaunga zvinokumbandidzirai neku kutsikirira i, zvino moti: Ndiani wandibata?
\v	46	Asi Jesu wakati: Aripo wandibata; nokuti ini ndaziva kuti simba rabuda kwandiri.
\v	47	Zvino mukadzi wakati achiona kuti haana kuvanzika, akauya achibvunda, akawira pasi pamberi pake, akarondedzera kwaari pamberi pevanhu vose kuti wakamubata nechikonzero chipi, uye kuti wakaporeswa pakarepo sei.
\v	48	Zvino akati kwaari: Mukunda, tsunga moyo, rutendo rwako rwakuponesa; enda nerugare.
\v	49	Wakati achataura, kwakauya umwe achibva  kumba kwemukuru wesinagoge, achiti kwaari: Mukunda wenyu wafa; musatambudza Mudzidzisi.
\v	50	Asi Jesu wakati achinzwa, akamupindura, achiti: Usatya, tenda chete, uye achaponeswa.
\v	51	Zvino wakati apinda mumba, haana kutendera munhu kupinda, kunze kwaPetro, naJakobho, naJohwani, nababa vemusikana, namai.
\v	52	Uye vose vakachema, vachimuririra. Asi wakati: Musachema; haana kufa, asi arere.
\v	53	Vakamuseka nekumhura, vachiziva kuti wafa.
\v	54	Asi wakavabudisira vose panze, akamubata ruoko, akadanidzira, achiti: Musikana, muka!
\v	55	Mweya wake ukadzoka, akamuka pakarepo; akaraira kuti apiwe kudya.
\v	56	Zvino vabereki vake vakavhiringidzika; asi wakavaraira kuti varege kuudza munhu zvakaitika.
\c	9
\s	Gumi nevaviri vanotumwa kunoparidza nekuporesa
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakadanira pamwe vadzidzi vake gumi nevaviri, akavapa simba nechikuriri pamusoro pemadhimoni ose, nekuporesa zvirwere.
\v	2	Akavatumakuparidza ushe hwaMwari nekuporesa varwere.
\v	3	Zvinoakati kwavari: Musatakura chinhu cherwendo, kana mudonzvo, kana nhava, kana chingwa, kana mari; uye musava nenguvo refu mbiri umwe neumwe.
\v	4	Uyeimba ipi neipi yamunopinda mairi, garaimo, mugobvamo.
\v	5	Uyeani nani asingakugamuchirii, kana mobuda paguta iro, zuzai kunyange guruva patsoka dzenyu, huve uchapupu hunopikisana navo.
\v	6	Zvinovakabva vakaenda vachigura nemumisha yose, vachiparidza evhangeri, nekuporesa pose-pose.
\s	Herodhi anokanganiswa pamusoro paJohwani Mubhabhatidzi
\p
\v	7	ZvinoHerodhi, umwe wevatongi vana, wakanzwa zvose zvakaitwa naye; akakanganisika, nokuti zvairehwa nevamwe kuti Johwani waiva amuka kubva kuvakafa;
\v	8	nevamwekuti Eria waonekwa; nevamwe kuti umwe muporofita wevekare wamuka.
\v	9	AsiHerodhi wakati: Johwani ndakagura musoro ini; asi ndiani uyu, ini wandinonzwa zvinhu zvakadai pamusoro pake? Akatsvaka kumuona.
\s	Vanhu zvuru zvishanu vanopiwa kudya naJesu
\p
\v	10	Zvino vaapositori vakati vadzoka, vakamurondedzera zvose zvavaiva vaita. Akavatora, akaenda navo vari voga kunzvimbo yerenje reguta rinonzi Bhetisaidha.
\v	11	Zvino zvaunga zvakati zva zviziva, zvikamutevera; akavagamuchira, akataura kwavari zveushe hwaMwari, akaporesa vaiva vachida kuporeswa.
\p
\v	12	Zuva raiva rotanga kuvira; vanegumi nevaviri vakaswedera vakati kwaari: Regai chaunga chiende, kuti vaende kumisha nekuruwa rwakapoteredza, vagare, vawane kudya; nokuti pano patiri panzvimbo yerenje.
\v	13	Asi wakati kwavari: Vapei imwi kuti vadye. Vakati: Hatina zvinopfuura zvingwa zvishanu, nehove mbiri, kunze kwekuti isu taenda kunotengera vanhu ava vose kudya.
\v	14	Nokuti vaiva varume vanenge zvuru zvishanu. Asi akati kuvadzidzi vake: Vagarisei pasi mumapoka, rimwe nerimwe makumi mashanu.
\v	15	Vakaita saizvozvo, vakavagarisa vose pasi.
\v	16	Zvino akatora zvingwa zvishanu nehove mbiri, akatarira kumusoro kudenga, akazviropafadza, aka zvimedura, ndokupa vadzidzi kuti vagadzike pamberi pechaunga.
\v	17	Vakadya, vakaguta vese; kukanongwa zvimedu zvakasara kwavari, matengu gumi nemaviri.
\s	Petro anopupura kuti Jesu ndiye Kristu
\p
\v	18	Zvino zvakaitika ari oga achinyengetera, vadzidzi vaiva vanaye; akavabvunza, achiti: Zvaunga zvinoti ndini ani?
\v	19	Ivo vakapindura vakati: Johwani Mubhabhatidzi; asi vamwe: Eria; nevamwe vakati: Umwe muporofita wevekare wamuka.
\v	20	Akati kwavari: Ko imwi munoti ndini ani? Petro akapindura akati: Kristu waMwari.
\v	21	Akavayambirisa, akaraira kuti varege kuudza munhu izvi;
\v	22	achiti: Mwanakomana wemunhu anofanira kutambudzika zvikuru, nekurambwa nevakuru nevapristi vakuru nevanyori, nekuurawa, nekumutswa nezuva retatu.
\s	Kutevera Jesu
\p
\v	23	Zvino wakati kune vese: Kana munhu achida kuuya shure kwangu, ngaazvirambe, atakure muchinjikwa wake zuva rimwe nerimwe, anditevere.
\v	24	Nokuti ani nani anoda kuponesa upenyu hwake, acharasikirwa nahwo; asi ani nani anorasikirwa neupenyu hwake nekuda kwangu, iye achahuponesa.
\v	25	Nokuti munhu angabatsirwa nei, kana akawana nyika yose, asi iye pachake azvitadzire kana kurasikirwa?
\v	26	Nokuti ani nani anonyara pamusoro pangu nepamashoko angu, Mwanakomana wemunhu achanyara pamusoro pake paanouya mukubwinya kwake nekwaBaba nekwevatumwa vatsvene.
\v	27	Asi ndinokuudzai zvechokwadi: Varipo vamwe vevamire pano vasingazoraviri rufu, kusvika vaona ushe hwaMwari.
\s	Kubwinya kwaJesu pagomo
\p
\v	28	Zvino zvakaitika mazuva anenge masere mushure memashoko awa, kuti wakatora Petro naJohwani naJakobho, akakwira mugomo kunonyengetera.
\v	29	Zvino zvakaitika mukunyengetera kwake, chimiro chechiso chake chikashanduka, uye nguvo yake  ikachena,  ikapenya.
\v	30	Zvino tarira, varume vaviri vakataura naye, vaiva Mozisi naEria.
\v	31	Vakaonekwa mukubwinya, vakataura zvekubva kwake kwaaiva achinopedzisa paJerusarema.
\v	32	Asi Petro nevaiva naye vaiva varemerwa nehope; asi vakati vapepuka, vakaona kubwinya kwake, nevarume vaviri vaiva vamire naye.
\v	33	Zvino zvakaitika pakubva kwavo paari, Petro akati kuna Jesu: Tenzi, zvakanaka kuti tive pano; zvino ngatiite matende matatu, rimwe renyu, nerimwe raMozisi, nerimwe raEria; asingazivi zvaanoreva.
\v	34	Wakati achitaura izvi, kwakauya gore rikavadzikatira; vakatya pakupinda kwavo mugore.
\v	35	Zvino kwakabuda inzwi mugore, richiti: Uyu Mwanakomana wangu anodikanwa; inzwai iye.
\v	36	Zvino inzwi rakati raitika, Jesu akawanikwa ari ega. Asi ivo vakanyarara, vakasaudza munhu mumazuva iwayo chimwe chezvinhu zvavaiva vaona.
\s	Jesu anoporesa mukomana ane zvipusha
\p
\v	37	Zvino zvakaitika kuti nezuva raitevera, vaburuka mugomo, chaunga chikuru chikamuchingamidza.
\v	38	Zvino tarira, murume muchaunga wakadanidzira, achiti: Mudzidzisi, ndinokukumbirisai, tarirai mwanakomana wangu nokuti  ndiye wakaberekwa umwe woga wangu;
\v	39	zvino tarirai, mweya unomubata, pakarepo woridza mhere, uye unomugwinhisa achipupuma furo, unorema kubva paari, asi uchimusvuura.
\v	40	Zvino ndakakumbirisa vadzidzi venyu kuti vaubudise, asi vakasagona.
\v	41	Jesu achipindura akati: Haiwa zera risina rutendo rakatsauka, ndichava nemwi kusvika rinhi, ndichikuitirai moyo murefu? Uisa mwanakomana wako pano.
\v	42	Zvino wakati achaswedera, dhimoni rikamudambura, rikamugwinhisa zvikuru; asi Jesu akatsiura mweya wetsvina, akaporesa mwana, akamudzosera kuna baba vake.
\s	Jesu anotaura zverufu rwake nekumuka kwake
\p
\v	43	Vose ndokushamiswa zvikuru neukuru hwaMwari. Zvino vose va chakashamisika nezvose Jesu zvaakaita, akati kuvadzidzi vake:
\v	44	Imwi isai mashoko awa munzeve dzenyu: Nokuti Mwanakomana wemunhu achakumikidzwa mumaoko evanhu.
\v	45	Asi havana kuziva chirevo ichi, uye chaiva chakavanzika kwavari, kuti vasachinzwisisa; vakatya kumubvunza pamusoro pechirevo ichi.
\s	Mukuru paushe hwekudenga
\p
\v	46	Zvino kwakamuka nharo pakati pavo, kuti ndiani angava mukurusa kwavari.
\v	47	Asi Jesu wakati achiona kufunga kwemoyo wavo, akatora mucheche, akamuisa parutivi rwake,
\v	48	akati kwavari: Ani nani anogamuchira mucheche uyu muzita rangu, anogamuchira ini, uye ani nani anogamuchira ini anogamuchira wakandituma; nokuti mudikisa pakati penyu mose, ndiye achava mukuru.
\p
\v	49	Johwani akapindura akati: Tenzi, taona umwe achibudisa madhimoni muzita renyu, tikamudzivisa, nokuti haateverani nesu.
\v	50	Asi Jesu akati kwaari: Musa mudzivisa, nokuti uyo asingatipikisi, ndewedu.
\s	VaSamariya vanoramba kugamuchira Jesu
\p
\v	51	Zvino zvakaitika pakupera kwemazuva ekukwidzwa kwake, kuti iye akarinzira chiso chake kuenda kuJerusarema,
\v	52	akatuma vatumwa pamberi pechiso chake; vakaenda, vakapinda mumusha wevaSamariya, kunomugadzirira.
\v	53	Asi havana kumugamuchira, nokuti chiso chake chaienda kuJerusarema.
\v	54	Zvino vadzidzi vake, Jakobho naJohwani, vakati vachi zviona vakati: Ishe, munoda kuti tiraire moto uburuke kubva kudenga, uvapedze, Eria sezvaakaitawo here?
\v	55	Asi wakatendeuka akavatsiura akati: Hamuzivi kuti imwi muri vemweya wakadii.
\v	56	Nokuti Mwanakomana wemunhu haana kuuya kuzoparadza upenyu hwevanhu asi ku huponesa. Zvino vakaenda kune umwe musha.
\s	Kutevera Jesu
\p
\v	57	Zvino zvakaitika vachifamba munzira, umwe wakati kwaari: Ishe, ndichakuteverai kupi zvako kwamunoenda.
\v	58	Jesu akati kwaari: Makava ane mwena, neshiri dzedenga matendere; asi Mwanakomana wemunhu haana paanotsamhidza musoro.
\v	59	Akati kune umwe: Nditevere. Asi iye akati: Ishe, nditenderei ndiende kunoviga baba vangu pakutanga.
\v	60	Asi Jesu wakati kwaari: Rega vakafa vavige vakafa vavo; asi iwe enda unoparidza ushe hwaMwari.
\v	61	Umwezve akati: Ndichakuteverai Ishe, asi nditenderei kutanga kuti ndinovawoneka ivo vari mumba mangu.
\v	62	Asi Jesu akati kwaari: Hakuna ungati kana aisa ruoko rwake pagejo, akacheukira kuzvinhu zviri shure, wakafanira ushe hwaMwari.
