\b	Esiteri
\c	1
\s	Vahosi Vhashiti vanoramba kuteerera mambo Ahasuwerusi
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pamazuva aAhasuwerusi, Ahasuwerusi uyu waitonga kubva paIndia kusvikawo paItiyopiya, matunhu zana nemakumi maviri nemanomwe;
\v	2	pamazuvaiwawo mambo Ahasuwerusi paakagara pachigaro cheushe cheumambo hwake chaiva paShushani muzinda,
\v	3	pagorerechitatu rekutonga kwake, wakaitira misoro yake yose nevaranda vake mabiko. Simba rePerisiya neMedhiya, vakuru nemachinda ematunhu vaiva pamberi pake,
\v	4	paakaratidzafuma yekubwinya kweumambo hwake nerukudzo rwekunaka kweukuru hwake mazuva akawanda, mazuva zana nemakumi masere.
\v	5	Zvinomazuva awa paakazadzisika, mambo wakaitira mabiko vanhu vose vaivapo paShushani muzinda, kubva kumukuru kusvika kumudiki, mazuva manomwe, muchivanze chebindu remuzinda wamambo.
\v	6	Paivanemicheka michena, micheka yakapfava, neyeruvara rwedenga, yakasungwa netambo dzemucheka wakapfava neyeruvara rwehute, pazvindori zvesirivha nembiru dzemabwe earibhasiteri. Mibhedha yaiva yegoridhe nesirivha pamusoro penzvimbo yakarongwa mabwe matsvuku, neearibhasiteri, neemaparera, nematema.
\v	7	Zvinovakapa kunwa mumidziyo yegoridhe, uye midziyo yaiva midziyo yakasiyana-siyana, newaini yeumambo yaiva yakawanda zvichienderana neruoko rwamambo.
\v	8	Zvinokunwa kwaienderana nemutemo, hakuna wakamanikidza; nokuti saizvozvo mambo waiva atarira vakuru vose veimba yake, kuti vaite zvichienderana nechido chemunhu nemunhu.
\v	9	Navahosivamambo Vhashiti vakaitira vakadzi mabiko paimba yeumambo, mambo Ahasuwerusi yaaiva nayo.
\p
\v	10	Pazuva rechinomwe moyo wamambo pawaiva wafara newaini, wakaraira Mehumani, Bhizita, Haribhona, Bhigita, naAbhagata, Zetari, naKarikasi, vatenwa vanomwe vaishanda pamberi pamambo Ahasuwerusi,
\v	11	kuti vauye navahosi vamambo Vhashiti pamberi pamambo, vane korona yeumambo, kutaridza vanhu nemachinda kunaka kwavo; nokuti vaiva vakanaka pakuonekwa.
\v	12	Asi vahosi vamambo Vhashiti vakaramba kuuya pakuraira kwamambo kwaiva neruoko rwevatenwa. Naizvozvo mambo wakatsamwa kwazvo, hasha dzake ndokupfuta mukati make.
\p
\v	13	Zvino mambo wakati kuvachenjeri vaiziva dzinguva (nokuti shoko ramambo raiva rakadaro pamberi peavo vose vaiziva mutemo nemutongo,
\v	14	nevaiva pedo naye vaiva Karishena, Shetari, Adhimata, Tarishishi, Mereesi, Marisena, Memukani, machinda manomwe ePerisiya neMedhiya vaiona chiso chamambo, vaigara pamberi paushe):
\v	15	Zvichienderana nemutemo kuchaitwei navahosi vamambo Vhashiti, nokuti havana kuita murairo wamambo Ahasuwerusi neruoko rwevatenwa?
\v	16	Zvino Memukani wakati pamberi pamambo nemachinda: Vhashiti, vahosi vamambo vatadza kwete kuna mambo koga, asi kumachinda ose nekuvanhu vose vari pamatunhu ose amambo Ahasuwerusi.
\v	17	Nokuti chiito chavahosi vamambo chichabudira kuvakadzi vose zvekuti vachazvidza varume vavo pameso avo, pavachati: Mambo Ahasuwerusi vakaraira vahosi vamambo Vhashiti kuti vauiwe navo pamberi pake, asi havana kuuya.
\v	18	Nhasi vakadzi vanoremekedzwa vePerisiya neMedhiya vachataura saizvozvo pavachanzwa chiito chavahosi vamambo kumachinda ose amambo; kuchava nekuzvidza nekutsamwa kwakapfuurisa.
\v	19	Kana zvakanaka kuna mambo ngakubude mutemo weumambo kubva kwaari, uye ngaunyorwe pakati pemitemo yevaPerisiya nevaMedhi, kuti usashandurwa, kuti Vhashiti arege kuzouya pamberi pamambo Ahasuwerusi; uye mambo ngaape uhosi hwake kune umwe ari nani kumupfuura.
\v	20	Kana mutemo wamambo wavachaita wanzwikwa paushe hwavo hwose (nokuti hwakakura), vakadzi vose vachapa rukudzo kuvarume vavo, kubva kumukuru kusvika kumudiki.
\v	21	Zvino nyaya yakava yakanaka pameso amambo nemachinda; mambo ndokuita zvichienderana neshoko raMemukani.
\v	22	Nokuti wakatuma tsamba kumatunhu ose amambo, kudunhu rimwe nerimwe zvichienderana nekunyorwa kwaro, uye kurudzi rumwe nerumwe zvichienderana nemutauro wavo, kuti murume umwe neumwe ave mutongi muimba yake, uye achitaura zvinoenderana nemutauro wevanhu vake.
\c	10
\s	Ukuru hwaModhekai
\p
\v	1	Zvinomambo Ahasuwerusi wakaisa mutero panyika nezviwi zvegungwa.
\v	2	Zvinobasa rose rekugwinya kwake neresimba rake, nedudziro yeukuru hwaModhekai, mambo hwaakamukurisa nahwo, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku remakoronike emadzimambo eMedhiya nePerisiya?
\v	3	NokutiModhekai muJudha wakava wechipiri pana mambo Ahasuwerusi, uye mukuru pakati pevaJudha, uye achigamuchirwa nechaunga chehama dzake, achitsvakira vanhu vake zvakanaka, uye achitaura rugare kumbeu yake yose.
\c	2
\s	Esiteri anoitwa vahosi panzvimbo yaVhashiti
\p
\v	1	Shurekwezvinhu izvi, pakumira kwekutsamwa kwamambo Ahasuwerusi, wakarangarira Vhashiti, nezvaakaita, nechaakatemerwa.
\v	2	Zvinovaranda vamambo vaimushandira vakati: Ngakutsvakirwe mambo vasikana, mhandara dzakanaka pakuonekwa.
\v	3	Zvinomambo ngaagadze vatariri pamatunhu ose eumambo hwake, kuti vaunganidze pamwe vasikana vose, mhandara dzakanaka pakuonekwa, paShushani muzinda, paimba yevanhukadzi, muruoko rwaHegai mutenwa wamambo, muchengetedzi wevanhukadzi, nekupa zvekuzvinatsa zvavo.
\v	4	Zvinomusikana wakanaka pameso amambo ngaave vahosi vamambo pachinzvimbo chaVhashiti. Chinhu ichi ndokuva chakanaka pameso amambo; iye ndokuita saizvozvo.
\p
\v	5	Kwaivanemurume muJudha paShushani muzinda, uye zita rake raiva Modhekai, mwanakomana waJairi, mwanakomana waShimei, mwanakomana waKishi, muBhenjamini,
\v	6	waivaatapwa kubva Jerusarema pamwe nevatapwa vaiva vatapwa naJekoniya mambo waJudha, Nebhukadhinezari mambo weBhabhironi waaiva atapa.
\v	7	Zvinowairera Hadhasa, ndiye Esiteri, mukunda wababamunini wake; nokuti waiva asina baba kana mai; zvino musikana waiva akanaka pachimiro uye akanaka pakuonekwa; uye baba vake namai vake pavaiva vafa, Modhekai wamutora kuva mukunda wake.
\p
\v	8	Zvinozvakaitika pakwakanzwikwa murairo wamambo nechirevo chake, uye vasikana vakawanda vaungana pamwe paShushani muzinda muruoko rwaHegai, kuti Esiteri wakatorwa kuiswa muimba yamambo muruoko rwaHegai muchengetedzi wevanhukadzi.
\v	9	Zvinomusikana waiva akanaka pameso ake, iye ndokuwana nyasha kubva kwaari, ndokukurumidza kumupa zvekuzvinatsa zvake nemigove yake, nevasikana vaionekera vanomwe kumupa vaibva muimba yamambo; akamubvisa ndokuisa iye nevasikana vake pakanaka peimba yevanhukadzi.
\v	10	Esiteri waiva asina kutaura vanhu vekwake kana rudzi rwake, nokuti Modhekai waiva amuraira kuti asataura.
\v	11	Zvino Modhekai waifamba-famba zuva nezuva pamberi pechivanze cheimba yevanhukadzi, kuziva upenyu hwaEsiteri, uye kuti chii chichaitika kwaari.
\p
\v	12	Zvino pakusvika kwedzoro remusikana umwe neumwe kupinda kuna mambo Ahasuwerusi, pakupedzisira zvaitwa kwaari zvichienderana nemutemo wevanhukadzi, mwedzi gumi nemiviri (nokuti saizvozvo kwaizadzisika mazuva ezvekunatsa zvavo, mwedzi mitanhatu nemafuta emura, uye mwedzi mitanhatu nezvinonhuhwira zvinotapira, uye ne zvimwe zvekunatsa zvevanhukadzi).
\v	13	Zvino neizvozvi musikana waipinda kuna mambo; chose chaakareva chakapiwa kwaari kuti aende nacho achibva paimba yevanhukadzi achienda kuimba yamambo.
\v	14	Madekwani waipinda, uye mangwanani waidzokera muimba yechipiri yevanhukadzi, muruoko rwaShaashigazi, mutenwa wamambo muchengetedzi wevarongo; waiva asingachapindizve kuna mambo, kunze kwekuti mambo afadzwa naye, ndokudanwa nezita.
\p
\v	15	Zvino parakasvika dzoro raEsiteri mukunda waAbhihairi babamunini vaModhekai waiva amutora kuva mukunda wake kuti apinde kuna mambo, haana kukumbira chinhu kunze kweizvo Hegai mutenwa wamambo zvaakareva, muchengetedzi wevanhukadzi. Zvino Esiteri waiwana nyasha pameso evose vaimuona.
\v	16	Naizvozvo Esiteri wakaiswa kuna mambo Ahasuwerusi muimba yake yeushe mumwedzi wegumi unova mwedzi weTebheti, mugore rechinomwe rekutonga kwake.
\v	17	Mambo ndokuda Esiteri kupfuura vanhukadzi vose; iye ndokugamuchira nyasha netsitsi pamberi pake kupfuura mhandara dzose, zvekuti wakaisa korona yeumambo mumusoro wake, ndokumuita vahosi vamambo pachinzvimbo chaVhashiti.
\v	18	Zvino mambo wakaita mabiko makuru kumachinda ake ose nevaranda vake, mabiko aEsiteri. Akaitirawo matunhu zororo, ndokupa zvipo zvichienderana nesimba ramambo.
\v	19	Zvino pakuunganidzwa kwemhandara rwechipiri, Modhekai waiva agere pasuwo ramambo.
\v	20	Esiteri waiva asina kutaura rudzi rwake kana vanhu vekwake, sezvo Modhekai aiva amuraira, nokuti Esiteri wakaita murairo waModhekai sepaaiva achirerwa anaye.
\p
\v	21	Pamazuva iwawo Modhekai agere pasuwo ramambo, Bhigitani naTereshi, vatenwa vaviri vamambo, pavarindi vemukova, vakatsamwa ndokutsvaka kuisa ruoko kuna mambo Ahasuwerusi.
\v	22	Zvino nyaya iyi ikazikanwa kuna Modhekai; uyo wakazivisawo Esiteri vahosi vamambo; Esiteri ndokuudza mambo nezita raModhekai.
\v	23	Nyaya yakati ichiongororwa, zvakawanikwa kuti  ndizvo. Naizvozvo vari vaviri vakaremberedzwa pamuti. Zvino zvakanyorwa mubhuku remakoronike pamberi pamambo.
\c	3
\s	Hamani anoronga kuparadza vaJudha
\p
\v	1	Shurekwezvinhu izvi mambo Ahasuwerusi wakakwiridzira Hamani mwanakomana waHamedhata muAgagi, ndokumusimudzira, ndokuisa chigaro chake pamusoro pemachinda ose aiva naye.
\v	2	Zvinovaranda vose vamambo vaiva pasuwo ramambo vakakotama ndokufugamira Hamani; nokuti mambo waiva araira saizvozvo maererano naye. Asi Modhekai haana kukotama uye haana kufugama.
\v	3	Zvinovaranda vamambo vaiva pasuwo ramambo vakati kuna Modhekai: Unodarikirei murairo wamambo?
\v	4	Zvinozvakaitika pavaitaura zuva nezuva kwaari, kuti haana kuteerera kwavari, zvekuti vakaudza Hamani kuti vaone kuti mashoko aModhekai angamira here; nokuti waiva avaudza kuti muJudha.
\v	5	Hamanipaakaona kuti Modhekai haakotami kana kumufugamira, Hamani ndokuzara nekutsamwa.
\v	6	Asiwakashora pameso ake kuisa ruoko kuna Modhekai oga, nokuti vaiva vamuzivisa rudzi rwaModhekai; naizvozvo Hamani wakatsvaka kuparadza vaJudha vose vaiva paushe hwose hwaAhasuwerusi, rudzi rwaModhekai.
\p
\v	7	Mumwedziwekutanga, ndiwo mwedzi waNisani, mugore regumi nemaviri ramambo Ahasuwerusi, vakakanda Puri, ndiwo mujenya, pamberi paHamani kubva pazuva kusvika pazuva uye kubva pamwedzi kusvika pamwedzi wegumi nemiviri, ndiwo mwedzi waAdhari.
\p
\v	8	ZvinoHamani wakati kuna mambo Ahasuwerusi: Kune rumwe rudzi, rwakapararira nekutekeshera pakati pevanhu kumatunhu ose eumambo hwenyu; uye mirairo yavo yakasiyana neyevanhu vose, uye mirairo yamambo havasi kuita; zvino kuna mambo hazvibatsiri kuvarega.
\v	9	Kanazvakanaka kuna mambo, ngakunyorwe kuti vaparadzwe; uye ndichayerera matarenda zvuru gumi esirivha mumaoko evaiti vebasa, kuisa mune zvakakosha zvamambo.
\v	10	Zvino mambo wakabvisa chindori chake paruoko rwake, ndokuchipa kuna Hamani mwanakomana waHamedhata muAgagi, muvengi wevaJudha.
\v	11	Zvino mambo wakati kuna Hamani: Sirivha yakapiwa kwauri, nevanhu, kuita navo sezvakanaka pameso ako.
\v	12	Zvino kwakadanwa vanyori vamambo mumwedzi wekutanga pazuva regumi nematatu rawo; zvino kwakanyorwa zvichienderana nezvose Hamani zvaaiva araira kumachinda amambo, nekuvatongi vaiva pamusoro pedunhu nedunhu, nekumisoro yerudzi nerudzi; dunhu nedunhu zvichienderana nechinyorwa chavo uye rudzi nerudzi zvichienderana nemutauro wavo; dzakanyorwa muzita ramambo Ahasuwerusi, ndokudhindwa nechindori chamambo.
\v	13	Zvino tsamba dzakatumwa neruoko rwevamhanyi kumatunhu ose amambo, kuparadza, kuuraya, nekutsakatisa vaJudha vose, kubva kune wechidiki kusvika kune wakwegura, vanana nevanhukadzi, pazuva rimwe, neregumi nematatu remwedzi wegumi nemiviri, uri mwedzi weAdhari, uye zvakatapiwa zvavo zvipambwe.
\v	14	Chikoponorwa chechinyorwa chekuti murairo upiwe kudunhu rimwe nerimwe, chikava pachena kuvanhu vose kuti vagadzirire zuva iro.
\v	15	Vamhanyi vakabuda, vakakurumidza neshoko ramambo; mutemo ndokupiwa paShushani muzinda; mambo naHamani ndokugara kunwa, asi guta reShushani rakadziyaniswa.
\c	4
\s	Esiteri anoreverera vaJudha pana mambo Ahasuwerusi
\p
\v	1	Modhekaipaakaziva zvose zvaitwa, Modhekai ndokubvarura nguvo dzake, ndokupfeka masaga nemadota, ndokubudira pakati peguta, ndokuchema nekuchema kukuru kunorwadza.
\v	2	Akauyakusvika pamberi pesuwo ramambo; nokuti kwaiva kusina wakapfeka masaga waipinda pasuwo ramambo.
\v	3	Nepadunhurimwe nerimwe nedunhu, nzvimbo shoko ramambo nemutemo wake pazvakasvika, kwakava nekuchema kukuru kuvaJudha, nekutsanya, nekuchema misodzi, nekurira; uye masaga nemadota zvakaparadzirwa pavazhinji.
\p
\v	4	Zvinokwakasvika vasikana vaEsiteri nevatenwa vake ndokumuudza; vahosi vamambo ndokushungurudzika zvikuru, ndokutumira nguvo kupfekedza Modhekai nekubvisa masaga ake paari, asi haana kugamuchira.
\v	5	ZvinoEsiteri wakadana Hataki,  umwe wevatenwa vamambo waaiva azvimisira pamberi pake, ndokumuraira zvaModhekai, kuti azive kuti chii, uye kuti imhaka yei.
\v	6	Hatakindokubudira kuna Modhekai pabani reguta raiva pamberi pesuwo ramambo.
\v	7	ZvinoModhekai wakamuudza zvose zvakaitika kwaari, nezvetsananguro chaiyo yemari Hamani yaakareva kuyerera pazvinokosha zvamambo pamusoro paJudha, kuvaparadza.
\v	8	Zvinowakamupa chikoponorwa chechinyorwa chemutemo chakapiwa paShushani kuvaparadza, kuti aratidze Esiteri, nekumuudza, nekumuraira kuti apinde kuna mambo, kunoita chikumbiro kwaari, nekukumbirira vanhu vekwake pamberi pake.
\v	9	ZvinoHataki wakauya ndokuudza Esiteri mashoko aModhekai.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Esiteri wakataura kuna Hataki, ndokumupa murairo kuenda kuna Modhekai,  akati:
\v	11	Varanda vose vamambo nevanhu vematunhu amambo vanoziva kuti, ani nani, munhurume kana munhukadzi anopinda kuenda kuna mambo muchivanze chemukati asina kudanwa, mutemo umwe kwaari kuuraya, kunze kwekuti mambo amutandavadzira tsvimbo yeushe yegoridhe, kuti ararame; asi ini handina kudanwa kupinda kuna mambo mazuva awa makumi matatu.
\v	12	Zvino vakaudza Modhekai mashoko aEsiteri.
\v	13	Zvino Modhekai wakati pakudzosera  shoko kuna Esiteri: Usafunga mumoyo mako kuti uchapukunyuka muimba yamambo kupfuura vaJudha vose.
\v	14	Nokuti kana ukanyarara zvekunyarara nenguva ino, rubatsiro nerusununguko zvichamirira vaJudha kubva kune imwe nzvimbo. Asi iwe neimba yababa vako muchaparadzwa. Zvino ndiyani anoziva kuti wakasvika paumambo nekuda kwenguva yakadai?
\p
\v	15	Zvino Esiteri wakati achipindura Modhekai:
\v	16	Endai muunganidze vaJudha vose varipo paShushani, uye munditsanyire, uye musadya kana kunwa mazuva matatu, usiku nemasikati, uye ini nevasikana vangu tichatsanya saizvozvo; zvino saizvozvo ndichapinda kuna mambo, izvo zvisingaenderani nemutemo; uye kana ndaparara, ndaparara.
\v	17	Zvino Modhekai wakapfuurira ndokuita zvinoenderana nezvose Esiteri zvaakamuraira.
\c	5
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pazuva retatu kuti Esiteri wakapfeka  nguvo dzeuhosi, ndokumira pachivanze chemukati cheimba yamambo, pakatarisana neimba yamambo; mambo waiva agere pachigaro cheushe cheumambo hwake muimba yeumambo, pakatarisana nemukova weimba.
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika mambo paakaona Esiteri vahosi vamambo amire pachivanze, wakawana nyasha pameso ake; mambo ndokutandavadzira Esiteri tsvimbo yeushe yegoridhe yaiva muruoko rwake. Esiteri ndokuswedera, ndokubata musoro wetsvimbo.
\v	3	Zvinomambo wakati kwaari: Unei, vahosi Esiteri? Uye chikumbiro chako chii? Uchachipiwa, kunyange kusvika pahafu yeushe.
\v	4	ZvinoEsiteri wakati: Kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, mambo naHamani ngavauye nhasi kumabiko andakamugadzirira.
\v	5	Zvinomambo wakati: Kurumidzisai Hamani, kuti aite shoko raEsiteri. Naizvozvo vakauya mambo naHamani kumabiko Esiteri aaiva aita.
\p
\v	6	Zvinomambo wakati kuna Esiteri pamabiko ewaini: Chikumbiro chako chii? Zvino uchachipiwa. Uye chii chishuvo chenyu? Zvichaitwa kunyange kusvika pahafu yeushe.
\v	7	ZvinoEsiteri wakapindura ndokuti: Chikumbiro changu nechishuvo changu  ndeichi:
\v	8	Kanandawana nyasha pameso amambo, uye kana zvakanaka kuna mambo kupa chikumbiro changu, nekuita chishuvo changu, mambo naHamani ngavauye pamabiko andichavaitira; zvino mangwana ndichaita zvinoenderana neshoko ramambo.
\s	Hamani anoronga kuuraya Modhekai
\p
\v	9	ZvinoHamani wakabuda nezuva iro nemufaro, nemoyo wakanaka; asi Hamani paakaona Modhekai pasuwo ramambo, kuti haana kusimuka, uye kuti haana kumudederera, Hamani wakazara nehasha nekuda kwaModhekai.
\v	10	Asi Hamani wakazvidzora; paakasvika muimba yake, wakatuma ndokupinza shamwari dzake, naZereshi mukadzi wake.
\v	11	Zvino Hamani wakavarondedzera kubwinya kwefuma yake, nekuwanda kwevana vake, nezvose mambo zvaakamukwiridzira pazviri, uye kuti wakamusimudzira pamusoro pemachinda nevaranda vamambo.
\v	12	Hamani ndokuti: Hongu, Esiteri vahosi vamambo havana kupinza munhu pamwe namambo pamabiko aaita kunze kwangu; uye namangwana ndakokwa kwaari, pamwe namambo.
\v	13	Asi zvose hazvindifadzi panguva yose yandinoona Modhekai muJudha agere pasuwo ramambo.
\p
\v	14	Zvino Zereshi mukadzi wake neshamwari dzake dzose vakati kwaari: Ngakuitwe danda remakubhiti makumi mashanu kureba, zvino mangwana taurai kuna mambo kuti Modhekai aremberedzwe pariri. Zvino pindai makafara namambo pamabiko. Zvino nyaya iyi yaiva yakanaka pamberi paHamani. Zvino akaita danda.
\c	6
\s	Modhekai anokudzwa
\p
\v	1	Pausikuuhwo hope dzamambo dzakatiza; ndokuti kuuiswe bhuku rezvirangaridzo, makoronike. Zvino zvakaverengwa pamberi pamambo.
\v	2	Zvinopakawanikwa pakanyorwa kuti Modhekai wakazivisa zvaBhigitana naTereshi, vatenwa vaviri vamambo, kubva kuvachengetedzi vemukova, avo vakatsvaka kuisa ruoko pana mambo Ahasuwerusi.
\v	3	Zvinomambo wakati: Rukudzo neukuru rudzii zvakaitirwa Modhekai nekuda kweizvi? Zvino varanda vamambo vaimushandira vakati: Hapana chaakaitirwa.
\p
\v	4	Mambondokuti: Ndiani ari pachivanze? Zvino Hamani waiva apinda pachivanze chekunze cheimba yamambo kutaura kuna mambo kuti aremberedze Modhekai padanda raaiva amugadzirira.
\v	5	Zvinovaranda vamambo vakati kwaari: Tarirai Hamani amire pachivanze. Mambo ndokuti: Ngaapinde.
\v	6	ZvinoHamani wakapinda. Mambo ndokuti kwaari: Kuitwei kumunhu mambo waanofarira rukudzo rwake? Zvino Hamani wakati mumoyo make: Ndiani mambo wavangafarira kuita rukudzo kupfuura ini?
\v	7	Hamanindokuti kuna mambo: Munhu mambo waanofarira rukudzo rwake,
\v	8	ngakuuiswenguvo dzeushe mambo dzaanopfeka, nebhiza mambo raanotasva, uye kuti korona yeumambo iiswe pamusoro wake.
\v	9	Zvinonguvo nebhiza ngazvipiwe muruoko rweumwe wemachinda makuru amambo, uye vapfekedze munhu uyu mambo waanofarira rukudzo rwake, vamutasvise bhiza apfuure nepanhandare yeguta, vadanidzire pamberi pake  vachiti: Ngakuitwe seizvi kumunhu mambo waanofarira rukudzo rwake.
\v	10	Zvino mambo wakati kuna Hamani: Kurumidza, tora nguvo nebhiza sezvawataura ugoita saizvozvo kuna Modhekai muJudha uyo agere pasuwo ramambo; kusawa shoko pane zvose zvawataura.
\v	11	Zvino Hamani wakatora nguvo nebhiza, ndokupfekedza Modhekai, ndokumutasvisa akapfuura nepanhandare yeguta, akadanidzira pamberi pake  achiti: Kuchaitwa seizvi kumunhu mambo waanofarira rukudzo rwake.
\p
\v	12	Zvino Modhekai wakadzokera kusuwo ramambo. Asi Hamani wakakurumidza kuenda kumba kwake achichema uye nemusoro wakafukidzwa.
\v	13	Zvino Hamani wakaudza Zereshi mukadzi wake neshamwari dzake dzose zvose zvakasangana naye. Zvino vachenjeri vake naZereshi mukadzi wake vakati kwaari: Kana Modhekai ari werudzi rwevaJudha, wawatanga kuwa pamberi pake, haungamukundi, asi uchawa zvirokwazvo pamberi pake.
\v	14	Zvino vachiri kutaura naye, vatenwa vamambo ndokuswedera, ndokukurumidza kupinza Hamani kumabiko Esiteri aaiva aita.
\c	7
\s	Hamani anosungirirwa
\p
\v	1	Zvinomambo naHamani vakasvika ku zova nemabiko navahosi vamambo Esiteri.
\v	2	Mambondokutizve kuna Esiteri pazuva rechipiri pamabiko ewaini: Chii chikumbiro chako, vahosi vamambo Esiteri? Zvino uchachipiwa. Uye chishuvo chako chii? Chichaitwa kunyange kusvika pahafu yeushe.
\v	3	ZvinoEsiteri vahosi vamambo wakapindura ndokuti: Kana ndawana nyasha pameso enyu, mambo, uye kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, upenyu hwangu ngahupihwe kwandiri pachikumbiro changu, nevanhu vekwangu pachishuvo changu.
\v	4	Nokutitatengeserwa kuparadzwa, ini nevanhu vekwangu, kuurawa, nekutsakatiswa. Asi kana tanga tatengeswa kuvanhurume vatapwa nekuvanhukadzi vatapwa, ndingadai ndanyarara; asi muvengi haana kuenzana nekutadzirwa kwamambo.
\p
\v	5	Zvinomambo Ahasuwerusi wakapindura ndokuti kuna Esiteri vahosi vamambo: Ndiani, uye aripi, uyo wakazadza moyo wake kuita saizvozvo?
\v	6	Esiterindokuti: Munhu, mudzivisi nemuvengi ndiHamani uyu wakaipa! Zvino Hamani wakatya pamberi pamambo navahosi vamambo.
\p
\v	7	Mambondokusimuka pamabiko ewaini mukutsamwa kwake kuenda kubindu remuzinda. Hamani akasimukawo kukumbirira upenyu hwake kuna Esiteri vahosi vamambo, nokuti wakaona kuti chakaipa chatemwa namambo pamusoro pake.
\v	8	Zvinomambo wakadzoka kubva pabindu remuzinda kupinda muimba yemabiko ewaini. Zvino Hamani wakawira pamusoro pemubhedha Esiteri paaiva ari. Mambo ndokuti: Kumanikidzawo vahosi vamambo vaneni mumba here? Shoko rakabuda mumuromo mamambo; zvino vakafukidza chiso chaHamani.
\v	9	ZvinoHaribhona, umwe wevatenwa wakati pamberi pamambo: Tariraiwo, danda Hamani raakaitira Modhekai, iye wakataura zvakanaka pana mambo, rimire muimba yaHamani, kureba makubhiti makumi mashanu. Mambo ndokuti: Muremberedzei pariri.
\v	10	Zvino vakaremberedza Hamani padanda raaiva agadzirira Modhekai. Zvino kutsamwa kwamambo kwakadzikira.
\c	8
\s	VaJudha vanotenderwa kuzvirwira
\p
\v	1	Pazuvairo mambo Ahasuwerusi wakapa imba yaHamani muvengi wevaJudha kuna Esiteri vahosi vamambo. Modhekai ndokuuya pamberi pamambo, nokuti Esiteri waiva arondedzera kuti waiva chii chake.
\v	2	Zvinomambo wakabvisa chindori chechidhindo chake chaaiva atorera Hamani, ndokuchipa kuna Modhekai. Esiteri ndokuisa Modhekai pamusoro peimba yaHamani.
\p
\v	3	ZvinoEsiteri wakataurazve pamberi pamambo, ndokuwira pasi petsoka dzake, ndokuchema misodzi, ndokuita chikumbiro kwaari, kuti abvise kuipa kwaHamani muAgagi, nezano rake raaiva arongera vaJudha.
\v	4	Zvinomambo wakatandavadzira kuna Esiteri tsvimbo yeushe yegoridhe. Naizvozvo Esiteri wakasimuka ndokumira pamberi pamambo.
\v	5	Zvinowakati: Kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, uye kana ndawana nyasha pamberi pake, uye chiri chinhu chakanaka pamberi pamambo, uye kana ndichifadza pameso ake, ngakunyorwe kudzosera shure tsamba  nezano raHamani mwanakomana waHamedhata, muAgagi, dzaakanyora kuparadza vaJudha vari pamatunhu ose amambo.
\v	6	Nokutindingagona sei kuona zvakaipa zvichawana vanhu vekwangu? Uye ndingagona sei kuona kuparadzwa kwerudzi rwangu?
\p
\v	7	Zvinomambo Ahasuwerusi wakati kuna Esiteri vahosi vamambo nekuna Modhekai muJudha: Tarirai ndapa Esiteri imba yaHamani, uye iye vakamuremberedza padanda, nokuti wakaisa ruoko rwake pavaJudha.
\v	8	Zvinoimwi nyorerai vaJudha muzita ramambo sezvakanaka pameso enyu, mugosimbisa nechidhindo chechindori chamambo, nokuti chinyorwa chakanyorwa muzita ramambo uye chakasimbiswa nechidhindo chechindori chamambo, hakuna angachidzosera shure.
\v	9	Zvinovanyori vamambo vakadanwa panguva iyo, pamwedzi wechitatu, unova mwedzi weShivhani, pa zuva remakumi maviri nematatu rawo; zvino kukanyorwa zvichienderana nezvose Modhekai zvaakaraira kuvaJudha, nekumachinda, nevatungamiriri, nevatongi vematunhu, iko kubva paIndiya kusvikawo paItiyopiya, matunhu zana nemakumi maviri nemanomwe, dunhu nedunhu zvichienderana nerunyoro rwaro, nerudzi nerudzi zvichienderana nerurimi rwarwo, nekuvaJudha zvichienderana nerunyoro rwavo uye zvichienderana nerurimi rwavo.
\v	10	Zvino wakanyora muzita ramambo Ahasuwerusi, ndokusimbisa nechidhindo chechindori chamambo, ndokutuma tsamba neruoko rwevamhanyi vanotasva mabhiza, vakatasva mabhiza anomhanyisa eumambo, mabhiza echidiki,
\v	11	kuti mambo wakatendera vaJudha vaiva paguta rimwe nerimwe neguta kuti vaungane pamwe kuti vamiririre upenyu hwavo, kuparadza nekuuraya nekutsakatisa masimba ose evanhu nedunhu anovashungurudza, vanana nevanhukadzi, nekupamba zvakapambiwa zvavo,
\v	12	pazuva rimwe pamatunhu ose amambo Ahasuwerusi, pane regumi nematatu remwedzi wegumi nemiviri, ndiwo mwedzi weAdhari.
\v	13	Chikoponorwa chechinyorwa chemurairo uchapiwa kudunhu rimwe nerimwe nedunhu wakaiswa pachena kuvanhu vose, uye kuti vaJudha vagadzirire zuva iri kuzvitsivira pavavengi vavo.
\v	14	Vamhanyi vaiva vakatasva mabhiza eumambo aimhanyisa vakabuda, vakakurumidziswa nekumanikidzwa neshoko ramambo. Zvino mutemo ukapiwa paShushani muzinda.
\p
\v	15	Zvino Modhekai wakabuda kubva pamberi pamambo ari munguvo dzeumambo zveruvara rwedenga nezvichena, uye nekorona huru yegoridhe, uye nenguvo yemucheka wakapfava nezveruvara rwehute. Zvino guta reShushani rakafara ndokupembera.
\v	16	VaJudha vakava nechiedza nemufaro nekupembera nerukudzo.
\v	17	Zvino padunhu rimwe nerimwe nedunhu nepaguta rimwe nerimwe neguta, nzvimbo pakasvika shoko ramambo nemutemo wake, mufaro nekupembera zvakava kuvaJudha, mabiko nezuva rakanaka. Zvino vazhinji vevanhu venyika vakava vaJudha, nokuti kutya vaJudha kwakavawira.
\c	9
\s	VaJudha vanouraya vavengi vavo
\p
\v	1	Zvinomumwedzi wegumi nemiviri, ndiwo mwedzi weAdhari, nezuva regumi nematatu rawo, kwaswedera shoko ramambo nemutemo wake kuti zviitwe, pazuva vavengi vevaJudha pavaitarisira kuvatonga naro, zvino zvikashanduka, nokuti vaJudha pachavo vakatonga vaivavenga.
\v	2	VaJudhavakaungana pamaguta avo pamatunhu ose amambo Ahasuwerusi, kuisa ruoko kune avo vaitsvaka kukuvadzwa kwavo; uye kwaiva kusina munhu waimira pamberi pavo, nokuti kuvatya kwakawira vanhu vose.
\v	3	Zvinovatongi vose vematunhu, nemachinda, nevatungamiriri, nevaiti vebasa mambo vavaiva navo, vakabatsira vaJudha, nokuti kutya Modhekai kwakavawira.
\v	4	NokutiModhekai waiva mukuru muimba yamambo, uye mbiri yake yakaenda nemumatunhu ose. Nokuti murume uyu Modhekai wakaramba achikura.
\v	5	SaizvozvovaJudha vakarova pavavengi vavo vose nekurova kwemunondo nekuuraya nekuparadza, ndokuita zvinoenderana nechido chavo nevaivavenga.
\v	6	NepaShushanimuzinda vaJudha vakauraya nekuparadza varume mazana mashanu.
\v	7	NaParishandata,naDharifoni, naAsipata,
\v	8	naPorata,naAdhariya, naAridhata,
\v	9	naParimashita,naArisai, naAridhai, naVayizata,
\v	10	vanakomana gumi vaHamani mwanakomana waHamedhata, muvengi wevaJudha, vakavauraya; asi pazvakapambiwa havana kutandavadzira ruoko rwavo.
\p
\v	11	Pazuva iro uwandu hwevakaurawa paShushani muzinda hwakasvika pamberi pamambo.
\v	12	Zvino mambo wakati kuna Esiteri vahosi vamambo: VaJudha vauraya nekuparadza varume mazana mashanu paShushani muzinda, nevanakomana gumi vaHamani; vaitei kune amwe matunhu amambo! Zvino chikumbiro chako chii? Zvino chichapiwa kwauri. Uye chishuvo chakozve chii? Zvino chichaitwa.
\v	13	Zvino Esiteri wakati: Kana zvakanaka kuna mambo, ngakutenderwe vaJudha vari paShushani kuita mangwanawo zvinoenderana nemutemo wanhasi, uye vanakomana gumi vaHamani ngavaremberedzwe padanda.
\v	14	Zvino mambo wakati zviitwe saizvozvo; zvino kukapiwa mutemo paShushani, uye vanakomana gumi vaHamani vakaremberedzwa.
\v	15	Zvino vaJudha vaiva paShushani vakaunganazve nezuva regumi nemana remwedzi weAdhari, zvino vakauraya varume mazana matatu paShushani, asi pazvakapambiwa havana kutandavadzira ruoko rwavo.
\p
\v	16	Zvino vamwe vaJudha vaiva pamatunhu amambo vakaungana ndokumiririra upenyu hwavo, ndokuva nezororo kubva pavavengi vavo, ndokuuraya pane avo vaivavenga zvuru makumi manomwe nezvishanu; asi pane zvakapambiwa havana kutandavadzira ruoko rwavo.
\v	17	Pazuva regumi nematatu remwedzi weAdhari, zvino vakazorora nezuva regumi nemana rawo, ndokuriita zuva remabiko nemufaro.
\v	18	Asi vaJudha vaiva paShushani vakaungana pane regumi nematatu rawo, uye pane regumi nemana rawo; uye pane regumi nemashanu rawo vakazorora, ndokuriita zuva remabiko nemufaro.
\v	19	Naizvozvo vaJudha vemaruwa vanogara pamaguta emaruwa vanoita zuva regumi nemana remwedzi weAdhari rive remufaro nemabiko, uye zuva rakanaka, nerekutumirana migove.
\s	Mutambo wePurimi
\p
\v	20	Zvino Modhekai wakanyora zvinhu izvi, ndokutumira tsamba kuvaJudha vose vaiva pamatunhu ose amambo Ahasuwerusi, epedo neekure,
\v	21	kusimbisa pakati pavo kuti vanofanira kuchengetedza zuva regumi nemana remwedzi weAdhari, nezuva regumi nemashanu rawo, mugore negore rimwe nerimwe,
\v	22	semazuva vaJudha avakazorora nawo kubva pavavengi vavo, uye mwedzi wakashandurwa kwavari kubva pakusuwa kuenda pamufaro, uye kubva pakuchema kuenda pazuva rakanaka, kuaita mazuva emabiko neemufaro, neekutumirana migove, neezvipo kuvarombo.
\v	23	Zvino vaJudha vakagamuchira icho chavakatanga kuita, uye icho Modhekai chaakavanyorera.
\v	24	Nokuti Hamani mwanakomana waHamedhata muAgagi, muvengi wevaJudha vose, waiva arongera vaJudha kuvaparadza, uye waiva akanda Puri, ndiwo mujenya, kuvatambudza nekuvaparadza.
\v	25	Asi pakasvika  Esiteri pamberi pamambo, iye wakaraira netsamba kuti zano rake rakaipa raakarongera vaJudha ridzokere pamusoro wake, uye kuti vamuremberedze iye nevanakomana vake padanda.
\v	26	Naizvozvo vakatumidza mazuva iwawo Purimi zvichitevera zita raPuri. Naizvozvo nekuda kwemashoko ose etsamba iyi, nezvavaiva vaona maererano nenyaya iyi, nezvaiva zvavawira,
\v	27	vaJudha vakatema ndokugamuchira pamusoro pavo, nepamusoro pembeu yavo, nepamusoro pevose vaizozvibatanidza kwavari, kuti vasadarika, kuchengetedza mazuva awa maviri, zvichienderana nechinyorwa chawo, uye zvichienderana nenguva yawo yakatarwa, mugore negore rimwe nerimwe;
\v	28	uye mazuva awa arangarirwe nekuchengetedzwa mumazera nemazera ose, mhuri nemhuri, dunhu nedunhu, uye guta neguta, uye mazuva awa ePurimi arege kudarikwa kubva pakati pevaJudha, uye chirangaridzo chawo chisapera kubva pambeu yavo.
\p
\v	29	Zvino Esiteri vahosi vamambo, mukunda waAbhihairi, naModhekai muJudha, vakanyora nesimba rose kusimbisa tsamba iyi yepiri yePurimi.
\v	30	Zvino wakatuma tsamba kuvaJudha vose kumatunhu zana nemakumi maviri nemanomwe eumambo hwaAhasuwerusi, mashoko erugare nechokwadi,
\v	31	kusimbisa mazuva awa ePurimi panguva dzawo dzakatarwa, Modhekai muJudha naEsiteri vahosi vamambo sezvavaiva vasimbisa pamusoro pavo, uye sekuzvisimbisa kwavo nepamusoro pembeu yavo, nyaya dzekutsanya nekuchema kwavo.
\v	32	Zvino mutemo waEsiteri wakasimbisa nyaya idzi dzePurimi; uye zvakanyorwa mubhuku.
