\b	Zekariya
\c	1
\s	Vanhu vanoyambirwa kuti vatendeukire kuna Jehovha
\p
\v	1	Mumwedziwerusere, mugore rechipiri raDhariyasi, shoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Zekariya, mwanakomana waBherekiya, mwanakomana wemuporofita Idho, richiti:
\v	2	Jehovhawakatsamwira madzibaba enyu zvekutsamwa.
\v	3	Naizvozvouti kwavari: Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Dzokerai kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, neni ndichadzokera kwamuri, anoreva Jehovha wehondo.
\v	4	Regaikuva semadzibaba enyu, vaporofita vekutanga vakadanidzira kwavari, vachiti: Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Dzokai henyu panzira dzenyu dzakaipa nezviito zvenyu zvakaipa. Asi havana kunzwa, uye havana kuteerera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	5	Madzibabaenyu aripi? Nevaporofita, vanorarama nekusingaperi here?
\v	6	Asimashoko angu nemitemo yangu, zvandakaraira varanda vangu vaporofita, hazvina kubata madzibaba enyu here? Zvino vakatendeuka vakati: Jehovha wehondo sezvaakafunga kutiitira, zvinoenderana nenzira dzedu uye zvinoenderana nezviito zvedu, saizvozvo wakaita kwatiri.
\s	Chiratidzo chemabhiza
\p
\v	7	Pazuvaremakumi maviri nemana remwedzi wegumi neumwe, uri mwedzi weShebhati, mugore rechipiri raDhariyasi, rakasvika shoko raJehovha kuna Zekariya, mwanakomana waBherekiya, mwanakomana wemuporofita Idho, richiti:
\v	8	Ndakaonausiku, zvino tarira, murume wakatasva pabhiza dzvuku, zvino wakamira pakati pemiti yemimirite yaiva mumukaha; uye shure kwake kune mabhiza matsvuku, akatsvukuruka, nemachena.
\v	9	Zvinondakati: Ishe wangu, zvii izvi? Mutumwa waitaura neni ndokuti kwandiri: Ini ndichakuratidza kuti zvii izvi.
\v	10	Zvino munhu waiva amire pakati pemiti yemimirite wakapindura ndokuti: Ava ndivo Jehovha vaakatuma kufamba kuenda uko neuko panyika.
\v	11	Zvino vakapindura mutumwa waJehovha waiva amire pakati pemiti yemimirite, ndokuti: Takafamba kuenda uko neuko panyika, zvino tarira, nyika yose igere murunyararo.
\p
\v	12	Zvino mutumwa waJehovha wakapindura ndokuti: Jehovha wehondo, kusvika rinhi imwi musinganzwiri ngoni Jerusarema nemaguta aJudha, amakatsamwira makore awa makumi manomwe?
\v	13	Zvino Jehovha wakapindura mutumwa waiva achitaura neni  nemashoko akanaka, mashoko anonyaradza.
\v	14	Saizvozvo mutumwa waitaura neni wakati kwandiri: Danidzira uti: Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Ndine godo neJerusarema uye neZiyoni, godo guru.
\v	15	Uye nekutsamwa kukuru ndakatsamwira vechirudzi vakatambarara; nokuti ini ndaiva ndakatsamwa zvishoma, asi ivo vakabatsira kuti kuve nekuipa.
\v	16	Naizvozvo anoreva seizvi Jehovha: Ndinodzokera kuJerusarema nengoni zhinji; imba yangu ichavakwa mariri, anoreva Jehovha wehondo; uye rwonzi rwekuyera rwuchatambanudzirwa pamusoro peJerusarema.
\v	17	Danidzirazve, uti: Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Maguta angu achapfachukirazve nezvakanaka; uye Jehovha achangonyaradzazve Ziyoni, uye achasarudzazve Jerusarema.
\s	Chiratidzo chenyanga nevapfuri
\p
\v	18	Zvino ndakasimudza meso angu ndokuona, zvino tarira, nyanga ina.
\v	19	Zvino ndakati kumutumwa waitaura neni: Zvii izvi? Iye ndokuti kwandiri: Idzi inyanga dzakaparadzira Judha, Israeri, neJerusarema.
\v	20	Zvino Jehovha wakandiratidza mhizha ina.
\v	21	Zvino ndakati: Ava vauya kuzoitei? Iye ndokutaura achiti: Idzi inyanga dzakaparadzira Judha, zvekuti hakuna munhu anosimudza musoro wake; asi idzi dzauya kudzityityidzira, kurasa nyanga dzevechirudzi, vakasimudza runyanga pamusoro penyika yaJudha kuiparadzira.
\c	10
\s	Jehovha achadzikinura Israeri
\p
\v	1	Kumbiraikuna Jehovha mvura nenguva yemvura yekupedzisira; Jehovha anoita mheni zhinji; uye anovapa mvura inonaya, kune umwe neumwe chinomera mumunda.
\v	2	Nokutizvifananidzo zvinotaura zvisina maturo, uye vavuki vakaona nhema, uye vanotaura hope dzezvisina maturo, vanonyaradza zvisina maturo; naizvozvo vakafamba semakwai; vanotambudzika, nokuti hakuna mufudzi.
\p
\v	3	Kutsamwakwangu kwakapfutira vafudzi, uye ndakaranga mbudzi, nokuti Jehovha wehondo wakashanyira boka rake rezvipfuwo, imba yaJudha, uye wakazviita sebhiza rake rakanakisisa pahondo.
\v	4	Kwakabudakwaari kona, mbambo kubva kwaari, uta hwehondo kubva kwaari, mumanikidzi umwe neumwe pamwe chete kubva kwaari.
\v	5	Zvinovachava semhare, vachitsika-tsika mumatope enzira dzemumusha pahondo, uye vacharwa, nokuti Jehovha anavo, uye vachanyadzisa vatasvi vemabhiza.
\p
\v	6	Zvinondichasimbisa imba yaJudha, uye ndichaponesa imba yaJosefa, uye ndichavagadzikazve; nokuti ndinovanzwira ngoni, uye vachava sezvinonzi handina kumbovarasa; nokuti ndiri Jehovha Mwari wavo, uye ndichavanzwa.
\v	7	ZvinovaEfuremu vachava semhare; nemoyo wavo uchafara sewaini; uye vanakomana vavo vachaona, uye vachafara; moyo wavo uchafara muna Jehovha.
\v	8	Ndichavaridziramuridzo, uye ndichavaunganidza, nokuti ndakavadzikinura, uye vachawedzerwa sezvavaka mbowedzerwa.
\v	9	Zvinondichavadzvara pakati pemarudzi, uye vachandirangarira munyika dziri kure, uye vachararama nevana vavo ndokudzoka.
\v	10	Zvino ndichavadzosa kubva kunyika yeEgipita, ndigovaunganidza kubva muAsiriya. Zvino ndichavaunza munyika yeGiriyadhi neRebhanoni, asi havangawanirwi  nzvimbo.
\v	11	Zvino achapfuura nemugungwa nekutambudzika; uye acharova mafungu mugungwa; neudziku hwose hwerwizi huchapwa; nekuzvikudza kweAsiriya kuchaderedzwa; netsvimbo yeushe yeEgipita ichabva.
\v	12	Zvino ndichavasimbisa muna Jehovha, uye vachafamba-famba muzita rangu, anoreva Jehovha.
\c	11
\s	Nyika yaJehovha ichaparadzwa
\p
\v	1	Zaruramikova yako, iwe Rebhanoni, kuti moto upedze misidhari yako.
\v	2	Ungudza,iwe muti wemufiri, nokuti musidhari wawa, nokuti  miti yeumambo yaparadzwa; ungudzai, imwi miouki yeBhashani, nokuti sango risingapindiki rawa.
\v	3	Kuneinzwi rekuungudza kwevafudzi, nokuti rukudzo rwavo rwaparadzwa; inzwi rekuomba kweshumba diki, nokuti kuzvikudza kwaJoridhani kunoparadzwa.
\s	Vafudzi vaviri
\p
\v	4	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha Mwari wangu: Fudza makwai ekuuraiwa;
\v	5	vatengivawo vanoauraya, vanoti havana mhosva; nevatengesi vawo vanoti: Ngaarumbidzwe Jehovha, zvino ndafuma; nevafudzi vawo havaanzwiri ngoni.
\v	6	Nokutihandichazonzwirizve ngoni vagari vepanyika, anoreva Jehovha, asi tarira, ndinobudisa vanhu, umwe neumwe kupinda muruoko rwewekwake, nemuruoko rwamambo wake; zvino vachapwanya nyika, uye handinganunuri kubva muruoko rwavo.
\v	7	Zvinondichafudza makwai ekuuraiwa, hongu anosiririsa emakwai; uye ndichazvitorera tsvimbo mbiri; imwe ndichaiti Runako, uye imwe ndichaiti Zvisungo; zvino ndichafudza makwai.
\v	8	Zvinondichaparadza vafudzi vatatu mumwedzi umwe, uye mweya weupenyu wangu wakavarwadzirwa, nemweya wavo wakandisemawo.
\v	9	Zvinondakati: Handingakufudzii; anofa ngaafe, neanoparadzwa ngaaparadzwe, nevanosara ngavadye umwe neumwe nyama yeumwe.
\p
\v	10	Zvino ndakatora tsvimbo yangu, Runako, ndokuivhuna, kuti ndigure sungano yangu yandaiva ndaita nemarudzi ose.
\v	11	Zvino yakagurwa muzuva iro, zvino saizvozvo anosiririsa emakwai akandimirira akaziva kuti raiva shoko raJehovha.
\v	12	Zvino ndakati kwavari: Kana zvakanaka pameso enyu, ipai mubairo wangu, uye kana zvisizvo, regai. Naizvozvo vakayerera mubairo wangu makobiri makumi matatu esirivha.
\v	13	Zvino Jehovha wakati kwandiri: Zvikande kumuumbi; mutengo mukuru wandaiva ndaverengerwa navo! Zvino ndakatora makobiri makumi matatu esirivha ndokuakandira muimba yaJehovha kumuumbi.
\v	14	Ipapo ndikavhuna tsvimbo yangu yechipiri, Zvisungo, kuti ndiputse ukama pakati paJudha naIsraeri.
\p
\v	15	Zvino Jehovha wakati kwandiri: Zvitorerezve zvombo zvemufudzi benzi.
\v	16	Nokuti tarira, ndichamutsa mufudzi panyika; asingazofambiri akaparadzwa; asingazotsvaki madiki; uye asingazorapi akavhunika; uye asingazofudzi amire; asi achadya nyama yeakakora, nekubvamburanya mahwanda awo.
\v	17	Nhamo kumufudzi asingabatsiri, anosiya makwai! Munondo uchava pamusoro peruoko rwake nepamusoro peziso rake rekurudyi. Ruoko rwake rwuchaonyana chose, uye ziso rake rekurudyi richasvibirirwa chose.
\c	12
\s	Kusunungurwa kweJerusarema
\p
\v	1	Mutoroweshoko raJehovha pana Israeri, anoreva Jehovha, anotatamura matenga, nekuteya nheyo dzenyika, nekuumba mweya wemunhu mukati make:
\v	2	Tarirai,ndichaita Jerusarema mukombe wekudzedzeresesa kumarudzi ose akapoteredza; hongu, uchava pamusoro paJudhawo pakukombwa kurwisana neJerusarema.
\v	3	Zvinozvichaitika nezuva iro, ndichaita Jerusarema ibwe riri mutoro kumarudzi ose. Vose vanozviremedza naro vachaguranyiwa kwazvo; uye vanhu vose vepanyika vachaungana kurirwisa.
\v	4	Muzuvairo, anoreva Jehovha, ndicharova bhiza rimwe nerimwe nekuvhunduka, nemutasvi waro nekupenga; uye paimba yaJudha ndichazarura meso angu; uye ndicharova bhiza rimwe nerimwe revanhu neupofu.
\v	5	Ipapomadzishe aJudha achati mumoyo wavo: Vagari veJerusarema isimba rangu muna Jehovha wehondo Mwari wavo.
\p
\v	6	Muzuvairo ndichaita madzishe aJudha segango remoto pakati pehuni, uye sezhenje remoto pakati pezvisote. Zvino vachapedza marudzi ose akavapoteredza, kuruoko rwerudyi nekuruboshwe; uye Jerusarema richagarwazve panzvimbo yavo, muJerusarema.
\v	7	Jehovhaachaponesawo matende aJudha pakutanga, kuti mbiri yeimba yaDhavhidhi nembiri yevagari veJerusarema isazvikudza pana Judha.
\v	8	Muzuvairo Jehovha achadzitira vagari veJerusarema; neasina simba pakati pavo muzuva iro achava saDhavhidhi; uye imba yaDhavhidhi ichava saMwari, semutumwa waJehovha pamberi pavo.
\p
\v	9	Zvinozvichaitika muzuva iro, ndichatsvaka kuparadza marudzi ose anouya kurwisa Jerusarema.
\v	10	Zvino ndichadurura paimba yaDhavhidhi, nepavagari veJerusarema, mweya wenyasha newemikumbiro; zvino vachatarira kwandiri ini wavakabvovora; uye vachamuchemera seanochema  mwanakomana umwe oga; uye achashungurudzika seanoshungurudzikira dangwe  rake.
\v	11	Muzuva iro kuchava kukuru kurira muJerusarema, sekurira kwaHadhadhirimoni mumupata waMegidhoni.
\v	12	Zvino nyika icharira, mhuri imwe neimwe iri yoga: Mhuri yeimba yaDhavhidhi iri yoga, nevakadzi vavo vari voga; mhuri yeimba yaNatani iri yoga, nevakadzi vavo vari voga;
\v	13	mhuri yeimba yaRevhi iri yoga, nevakadzi vavo vari voga; mhuri yaShimei iri yoga, nevakadzi vavo vari voga;
\v	14	mhuri dzose dzakasara, mhuri imwe neimwe iri yoga, nevakadzi vavo vari voga.
\c	13
\s	Kunatswa kwezvivi zvevanhu vaJehovha
\p
\v	1	Muzuvairo kuchava nechitubu chakazarurirwa chivi netsvina kuimba yaDhavhidhi nekuvagari veJerusarema.
\v	2	Zvinozvichaitika muzuva iro, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, ndichagura mazita ezvifananidzo kubva munyika, uye hazvingazorangarirwizve; hongu ndichapfuudzawo vaporofita nemweya yakaipa kubva panyika.
\v	3	Zvinozvichaitika, kana munhu akaporofitazve, ipapo baba vake namai vake vakamubereka vachati kwaari: Haungararami, nokuti unotaura nhema muzita raJehovha. Uye baba vake namai vake vakamubereka vachamubvovora paachaporofita.
\v	4	Zvinozvichaitika, muzuva iro, vaporofita vachanyadziswa, umwe neumwe nechiratidzo chake, pakuporofita kwake; uye havangapfeki nguvo yemvere kunyengera.
\v	5	Asiachati: Handisi muporofita; ndiri murimi wevhu; nokuti munhu wakanditenga kubva paudiki hwangu.
\v	6	Zvino umwe achati kwaari: Mavanga awa chii ari pazvanza zvako? Iye achati: Ndiwo andakakuvadzwa mumba meshamwari dzangu.
\p
\v	7	Munondo,muka urwise mufudzi wangu, uye urwise munhu ari shamwari yangu, anoreva Jehovha wehondo; rova mufudzi, makwai agoparadzirwa; uye ndichatendeusira ruoko rwangu pamusoro pemadiki.
\v	8	Zvinozvichaitika panyika yose, anoreva Jehovha, zvikamu zviviri zviripo zvichagurwa zvipere, uye chikamu chetatu chichasiiwapo.
\v	9	Zvinondichapinza chikamu chetatu nemumoto, ndigovanatsa sekunatswa kwesirivha, nekuvaidza sekuidzwa kwegoridhe. Ivo vachadana zita rangu, neni ndichavapindura. Ndichati: Vanhu vangu; ivowo vachati: Jehovha ndiMwari wangu.
\c	14
\s	Jehovha achabata ushe hwose
\p
\v	1	Tarira,zuva raJehovha rinosvika, nezvakapambiwa zvako zvichagoverwa pakati pako.
\v	2	Zvinondichaunganidza marudzi ose kurwisa Jerusarema pahondo; uye guta richatorwa, nedzimba dzichapambiwa, nevakadzi vachabhinyiwa, uye hafu yeguta ichabudira kuutapwa, asi vakasara vevanhu havangagurwi kubva muguta.
\v	3	IpapoJehovha achabuda, ndokurwa nemarudzi awo, sepazuva rekurwa kwake pazuva rehondo.
\p
\v	4	Zvinomuzuva iro tsoka dzake dzichamira pagomo reMiorivhi, riri pamberi peJerusarema kumabvazuva; negomo reMiorivhi richapamuka pakati paro kumabvazuva nekumavirira, uve mupata mukuru kwazvo; uye hafu yegomo ichatsedukira kumaodzanyemba, uye hafu yaro kuchamhembe.
\v	5	Zvinomuchatizira  kumupata wemakomo, nokuti mupata wemakomo uchasvika paAzeri; hongu, muchatiza, sepamakatiza kubva pamberi pekudengenyeka kwenyika pamazuva aUziya mambo waJudha. Zvino Jehovha Mwari wangu achauya,  nevatsvene vose vanewe.
\v	6	Zvinozvichaitika nezuva iro, chiedza hachingacheni kana kusviba.
\v	7	Asirichava zuva rimwe, richazikanwa naJehovha; haangavi masikati, uye hahungavi usiku; asi zvichaitika, panguva yemadekwani pachava nechiedza.
\v	8	Zvinozvichaitika muzuva iro, mvura mhenyu zhinji ichabuda kubva muJerusarema; hafu yayo kuenda kugungwa rekumabvazuva, uye hafu yayo kuenda kugungwa rekumavirazuva; zvichavapo pazhizha nepachando.
\v	9	ZvinoJehovha achava mambo pamusoro penyika yose; muzuva iro kuchava naJehovha umwe chete, uye zita rake richava rimwe chete.
\v	10	Nyika yose ichashandurwa kuva sebani, kubva paGebha kusvika paRimoni kuchamhembe kweJerusarema; uye ichakwiridzwa nekugara panzvimbo yayo, kubva pasuwo raBhenjamini kusvika kunzvimbo yesuwo rekutanga kusvika kusuwo rekona, uye  kubva pashongwe yeHananeri kusvika kuzvisviniro zvewaini zvamambo.
\v	11	Zvino vachagaramo, uye hakuchazovizve nekuparara, uye Jerusarema richagara rakachengetedzeka.
\p
\v	12	Iri ndiro denda Jehovha raacharova naro marudzi ose akarwa neJerusarema: Achaodza nyama yavo vamire netsoka dzavo, nemeso avo achaorera mumakomba awo, nerurimi rwavo rwuchaorera mumuromo mavo.
\v	13	Zvino zvichaitika, muzuva iro, nyonga-nyonga huru yaJehovha ichava pakati pavo; uye vachabatana umwe neumwe ruoko rwewekwake, uye ruoko rwake rwuchamukira ruoko rwewekwake.
\v	14	NaJudha acharwawo paJerusarema; uye fuma yevechirudzi vose vakapoteredza ichaunganidzwa, goridhe, nesirivha, nenguvo, uwandu ukuru.
\v	15	Zvino richadaro denda rebhiza, rehesera, rekamera, nerembongoro, neremhuka dzose dzichava mumisasa iyi, sedenda iri.
\p
\v	16	Zvino zvichaitika, umwe neumwe wakasiiwa pamarudzi ose, aiva auya kurwisa Jerusarema, vachakwira gore negore kunoshumira Mambo, Jehovha wehondo, nekuchengetedza mutambo wematumba.
\v	17	Zvino zvichadai, kuti uyo asina kukwira kuJerusarema achibva pamhuri dzepanyika kunonamata Mambo, Jehovha wehondo, pamusoro pavo hapangavi nemvura.
\v	18	Zvino kana mhuri yeEgipita ikasakwira, uye ikasapinda, ipapo pavari hapangavi ne mvura, pachava nedenda, Jehovha raacharova naro marudzi asingakwiri kunochengetedza mutambo wematumba.
\v	19	Uyu ndiwo murango wechivi cheEgipita, nemurango wechivi chemarudzi ose asingakwiri kunochengetedza mutambo wematumba.
\p
\v	20	Muzuva iro pamabhera emabhiza pachava  nechinyorwa: UTSVENE KUNA JEHOVHA. Nehari muimba yaJehovha dzichafanana nedzimbiya pamberi pearitari.
\v	21	Hongu, hari imwe neimwe paJerusarema nepaJudha ichava tsvene kuna Jehovha wehondo; uye vose vanobaira vachauya ndokutora padziri, ndokubikira madziri; uye muzuva iro hakuchazovizve nemuKenani muimba yaJehovha wehondo.
\c	2
\s	Chiratidzo cherwonzi rwekuyera
\p
\v	1	Ndakasimudzameso angu zvekare, ndokuona, zvino tarira, munhu, ane rwonzi rwekuyera muruoko rwake.
\v	2	Zvinondakati: Unoenda kupi? Iye ndokuti kwandiri: Kunoyera Jerusarema, kuona kuti kufara kwaro chii, uye urefu hwaro chii.
\v	3	Zvinotarira, mutumwa waitaura neni wakabuda; umwe mutumwa ndokubuda kumuchingamidza.
\v	4	Zvinowakati kwaari: Mhanya, taura kujaya iri uchiti: Jerusarema richagarwa semaguta asina masvingo nekuda kweuwandu hwevanhu nezvipfuwo zviri mariri.
\v	5	Nokutiini, anoreva Jehovha, ndichava kwariri rusvingo rwemoto kukomberedza; uye ndichava kubwinya mukati maro.
\p
\v	6	Haiwa,haiwa! Zvino tizai mubve munyika yekumaodzanyemba, anoreva Jehovha, nokuti ndakakuparadzirai semhepo ina dzekumatenga, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	7	Haiwa!Ziyoni, pukunyuka, iwe ugere  nemukunda weBhabhironi.
\v	8	Nokutianoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Shure kwekubwinya wakandituma kumarudzi akakupambai; nokuti anokugunzvai anogunzva mboni yeziso rake.
\v	9	Nokutitarirai, ndichazunza ruoko rwangu pamusoro pavo; uye vachava chinhu chakapambiwa kuvaranda vavo; zvino muchaziva kuti Jehovha wehondo wakandituma.
\p
\v	10	Imba uye ufare, iwe mukunda weZiyoni; nokuti tarira, ndinouya, uye ndichagara mukati mako, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	11	Zvino marudzi mazhinji achabatanidzwa kuna Jehovha pazuva iro; uye achava vanhu vangu; uye ndichagara mukati mako; uye uchaziva kuti Jehovha wehondo wakandituma kwamuri.
\v	12	Zvino Jehovha achagara nhaka yaJudha, mugove wake panyika tsvene, uye achasarudzazve Jerusarema.
\v	13	Nyarara, nyama yose, pamberi paJehovha; nokuti wamutswa kubva paugaro hwake utsvene.
\c	3
\s	Chiratidzo chemuporofita pamusoro paJoshua mupristi mukuru
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakandiratidza Joshua mupristi mukuru, amire pamberi pemutumwa waJehovha, naSatani amire paruoko rwake rwerudyi, kupokana naye.
\v	2	ZvinoJehovha wakati kuna Satani: Jehovha ngaakutsiure, iwe Satani; zvirokwazvo Jehovha ngaakutsiure, iye wakasarudza Jerusarema. Ichi hachisi chitsiga chakabvutwa pamoto here?
\v	3	ZvinoJoshua waiva akapfeka nguvo dzine tsvina, uye amire pamberi pemutumwa.
\v	4	Zvinowakapindura ndokutaura kune vaiva vakamira pamberi pake achiti: Bvisai nguvo dzine tsvina kubva paari. Zvino akati kwaari: Ona, ndapfuudza uipi hwako kubva kwauri, uye ndichakufukidza nguvo refu dzemutambo.
\v	5	Zvinondakati: Ngavaise nguwani yakachena pamusoro wake. Ipapo vakaisa nguwani yakachena pamusoro wake, vakamupfekedza nguvo zhinji. Zvino mutumwa waJehovha waiva amire po.
\v	6	Zvinomutumwa waJehovha wakapupura kuna Joshua achiti:
\v	7	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Kana ukafamba munzira dzangu, uye kana ukachengeta murairo wangu, ipapo iwe uchatongawo imba yangu, uye uchachengetawo zvivanze zvangu; uye ndichakupa nzvimbo dzekufamba pakati peava vamire po.
\v	8	Inzwahako, Joshua mupristi mukuru, iwe nevafambidzani vako vagere pamberi pako, nokuti vanhu vechiratidzo; nokuti tarira, ndinouisa muranda wangu, Bukira.
\v	9	Nokutitarira, ibwe randakaisa pamberi paJoshua; pamusoro peibwe rimwe pachava nemaziso manomwe. Tarira, ndichafombora runyoro rwaro, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, uye ndichabvisa uipi hwenyika iyo muzuva rimwe.
\v	10	Muzuva iro, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, muchadanira umwe neumwe shamwari yake pasi pemuzambiringa nepasi pemuonde.
\c	4
\s	Chigadziko chemwenje nemiorivhi miviri
\p
\v	1	Zvinomutumwa waitaura neni wakauya zvekare, ndokundimutsa semunhu anomutswa kubva muhope dzake.
\v	2	Zvinowakati kwandiri: Unoonei? Ini ndokuti: Ndaona, zvino tarira, chigadziko chemwenje chegoridhe rose, nendiro pamusoro pemusoro wacho, nemwenje yacho minomwe pachiri; pombi nomwe nenomwe dziri pamwenje iri pamusoro pemusoro wacho.
\v	3	Nemitiyemiorivhi miviri parutivi rwacho, umwe kurudyi rwendiro, neumwe kuruboshwe rwayo.
\v	4	Zvinondakapindura ndokutaura kumutumwa waitaura neni ndichiti: Izvi zvii, ishe wangu?
\v	5	Zvinomutumwa waitaura neni wakapindura ndokuti kwandiri: Hauzivi kuti izvi zvii here? Ini ndokuti: Kwete, ishe wangu.
\v	6	Zvinowakapindura ndokutaura kwandiri achiti: Iri ishoko raJehovha kuna Zerubhabheri, richiti: Kwete nechisimba, uye kwete nesimba, asi neMweya wangu, anoreva Jehovha wehondo.
\v	7	Ndiweani, iwe gomo guru? Pamberi paZerubhabheri uchava bani. Iye achabudisa ibwe riri musoro  nekudanidzira kukuru: Nyasha, nyasha kwariri.
\p
\v	8	ShokoraJehovha rakasvikazve kwandiri richiti:
\v	9	MaokoaZerubhabheri akateya nheyo yeimba ino, uye maoko ake achaipedzisa. Zvino uchaziva kuti Jehovha wehondo wakandituma kwamuri.
\v	10	Nokuti ndiani akazvidza zuva rezvinhu zvidiki? Nokuti vachafara uye vachaona ibwe remhangura muruoko rwaZerubhabheri; awa manomwe ndiwo meso aJehovha, anoenda uko neuko panyika yose.
\p
\v	11	Zvino ndakapindura ndokuti kwaari: Miorivhi iyi miviri kurudyi rwechigadziko chemwenje nekuruboshwe rwacho chii?
\v	12	Zvino ndakapindura rwechipiri ndokuti kwaari: Matavi awa maviri emiorivhi anodira goridhe kubva maari nepombi mbiri dzegoridhe chii?
\v	13	Zvino wakataura kwandiri achiti: Hauzivi here kuti awa chii? Ini ndokuti: Kwete, ishe wangu.
\v	14	Zvino wakati: Ava ndivo vaviri vakazodzwa, vamire panaIshe wenyika yose.
\c	5
\s	Rugwaro rwakapetwa rwunobhururuka
\p
\v	1	Zvinondakadzoka, ndokusimudza meso angu, ndokuona, zvino tarira, rugwaro rwakapetwa rwuchibhururuka.
\v	2	Zvinowakati kwandiri: Unoonei? Ini ndokuti: Ndinoona rugwaro rwakapetwa rwunobhururuka; urefu hwarwo makubhiti makumi maviri, uye upamhi hwarwo makubhiti gumi.
\v	3	Zvinowakati kwandiri: Uku kutukwa kunobuda pachiso chepanyika yose. Nokuti umwe neumwe anoba achagurwa kurutivi runo zvichienderana nako; uye umwe neumwe anopika achagurwa kurutivi urwo zvichienderana nako.
\v	4	Ndichakubudisa, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, uye kuchapinda muimba yembavha nemuimba yeanopika nhema nezita rangu, kugogara mukati meimba yake, uye kuchaipedza pamwe nematanda ayo nemabwe ayo.
\s	Mukadzi wakabata efa
\p
\v	5	Zvinomutumwa waitaura neni wakabuda, ndokuti kwandiri: Simudza meso ako ikozvino, ugoona kuti chii ichi chinobuda.
\v	6	Zvinondakati: Chii? Iye ndokuti: Iyi iefa inobuda. Akatizve: Ichi chimiro chavo panyika yose.
\v	7	Zvinotarira, kwakasimudzwa chivharo chemutobvu; uye uyu waiva mukadzi waiva agere mukati meefa.
\v	8	Zvinowakati: Uyu ndiye zvakaipa. Ndokumuwisira mukati meefa, ndokuwisira uremu hwemutobvu mumuromo wayo.
\p
\v	9	Zvinondakasimudza meso angu ndokuona, zvino tarira, vakadzi vaviri vanobuda, nemhepo mumapapiro avo, uye vane mapapiro semapapiro ezimudo; zvino vakasimudzira efa pakati penyika nematenga.
\v	10	Zvino ndakati kumutumwa waitaura neni: Ava vanotakurirepi efa?
\v	11	Iye ndokuti kwandiri: Kunoivakira imba munyika yeShinari; uye ichamiswa ndokuiswapo pamusoro penheyo yake.
\c	6
\s	Ngoro ina
\p
\v	1	Zvinondakadzoka ndokusimudza meso angu, ndokuona, zvino tarira, ngoro ina dzinobuda kubva pakati pemakomo maviri; uye makomo aiva makomo endarira.
\v	2	Pangoroyekutanga paiva nemabhiza matsvuku, uye pangoro yechipiri mabhiza matema,
\v	3	nepangoroyechitatu mabhiza machena, nepangoro yechina mabhiza mapfumbu ane simba.
\v	4	Zvinondakapindura ndokuti kumutumwa waitaura neni: Izvi zvii, ishe wangu?
\v	5	Mutumwandokupindura ndokuti kwandiri: Iyi mweya mina yekumatenga inobuda kubva pakumira pamberi paIshe wenyika yose.
\v	6	Mabhizamatema arimo anobuda achienda kunyika yekumaodzanyemba; uye machena anobuda kuatevera; nemapfumbu anobuda achienda kunyika yekuchamhembe.
\v	7	Anesimba akabudawo ndokutsvaka kufamba kuenda uko neuko panyika. Iye ndokuti: Fambai muende uko neuko panyika. Zvino akaenda uko neuko panyika.
\v	8	Zvinowakandidana ndokutaura kwandiri achiti: Ona, awa anobuda kuenda kunyika yekumaodzanyemba akazorodza mweya wangu panyika yekumaodzanyemba.
\s	Kuzodzwa kwaJoshua mupristi mukuru
\p
\v	9	Zvinoshoko raJehovha rakasvika kwandiri richiti:
\v	10	Tora kune vakatapwa, kuna Heridhai, nekuna Tobhiya, nekuna Jedhaia, vakabva kuBhabhironi, uuye iwe nezuva iroro, zvino uende muimba yaJosiya mwanakomana waZefaniya.
\v	11	Uye utore sirivha negoridhe, ugoita korona zhinji, ugoisa pamusoro waJoshua, mwanakomana waJehozadhaki, mupristi mukuru.
\v	12	Zvino utaure kwaari uchiti: Anotaura seizvi Jehovha wehondo achiti: Tarira, munhu ane zita rinonzi Bukira, iye achabukira kubva panzvimbo yake, uye achavaka tembere yaJehovha.
\v	13	Iyewo achavaka tembere yaJehovha; iyewo achatakura umambo, uye achagara atonge pamusoro pechigaro chake cheumambo; uye achava mupristi pamusoro pechigaro chake cheumambo; uye zano rerugare richava pakati pavo vari vaviri.
\v	14	Zvino korona dzichava kuna Heremi, nekuna Tobhiya, nekuna Jedhaia, nekuna Heni, mwanakomana waZefaniya, chive chiyeudzo mutembere yaJehovha.
\v	15	Vari kure vachauya vachivaka mutembere yaJehovha; ipapo muchaziva kuti Jehovha wehondo wakanditumira kwamuri. Izvi zvichaitika kana muchiteerera zvekuteerera inzwi raJehovha Mwari wenyu.
\c	7
\s	Jehovha anotsvaka kururama
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika mugore rechina ramambo Dhariyasi; shoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Zekariya, nerechina remwedzi wepfumbamwe, muna Kisirevhi.
\v	2	Pavaivavatuma kuimba yaMwari Sharezeri, naRegemi-Mereki, nevarume vake, kunokumbira chiso chaJehovha,
\v	3	kutaurakuvapristi vaiva muimba yaJehovha wehondo nekuvaporofita, vachiti: Ndicheme misodzi here mumwedzi weshanu, ndizvitsaure, sezvandakaita makore awa akawanda kudai?
\p
\v	4	Zvinorakasvika shoko raJehovha wehondo kwandiri, richiti:
\v	5	Taurakuvanhu vose vepanyika, nekuvapristi, uchiti: Pamakatsanya nekurira mu mwedzi weshanu nemune wechinomwe, kunyange makore awo makumi manomwe, makatombotsanyira ini, iyeni here?
\v	6	Uyepamakadya, uye pamakanwa, hamusi imwi makadya uye imwi makanwa here?
\v	7	Haasiiwo mashoko Jehovha aakadanidzira neruoko rwevaporofita vekare here, Jerusarema paraigarwa uye rakatambarara, nemaguta aro akarikomberedza, richigarwa kuchamhembe nekumapani?
\p
\v	8	Zvinoshoko raJehovha rakasvika kuna Zekariya, richiti:
\v	9	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo, achiti: Tongai mutongo wechokwadi, uye muite unyoro nengoni, umwe neumwe kuhama yake.
\v	10	Zvino musamanikidza chirikadzi, nenherera, mutorwa, nemurombo; uye musafungira zvakaipa mumoyo menyu, umwe neumwe kuhama yake.
\v	11	Asi vakaramba kuteerera, vakapa fudzi rinoramba kuteerera, uye vakaremedza nzeve dzavo kuti varege kunzwa.
\v	12	Hongu, vakaita moyo yavo  seibwe rakaoma, kuti varege kunzwa murairo, nemashoko Jehovha wehondo aakatumira neMweya wake neruoko rwevaporofita vekare; naizvozvo kwakauya kutsamwa kukuru kubva kuna Jehovha wehondo.
\v	13	Naizvozvo zvakaitika paaidanidzira, asi havana kunzwa, saizvozvo vanodanidzira, asi handinganzwi, anoreva Jehovha wehondo.
\v	14	Asi ndakavaparadzira nechamupupuri kumarudzi ose avaiva vasingazivi. Saizvozvo nyika yakava dongo shure kwavo, zvekuti hakuna munhu wakapfuura uye hakuna wakadzoka. Nokuti vakaita nyika inofadza dongo.
\c	8
\s	Jerusarema richagarwazve
\p
\v	1	Zvinoshoko raJehovha wehondo rakasvika richiti:
\v	2	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Ndaiva negodo neZiyoni negodo guru, uye ndaiva negodo naro nekupfuta kukuru.
\v	3	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Ndadzoka paZiyoni, uye ndagara mukati meJerusarema; uye Jerusarema richanzi guta rechokwadi, uye gomo raJehovha wehondo, gomo dzvene.
\v	4	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Kuchagarazve vatana nechembere munzira dzemumusha dzeJerusarema; uye umwe neumwe ane mudonzvo wake muruoko rwake neuwandu hwemazuva.
\v	5	Zvinonzira dzemumusha dzeguta dzichazadzwa nevakomana nevasikana vachitamba munzira dzaro dzemumusha.
\v	6	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Kana zvichishamisa pameso evakasara vevanhu ava mumazuva awa, zvichashamisawo here pameso angu? anoreva Jehovha wehondo.
\v	7	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Tarirai, ndichaponesa vanhu vangu panyika yekumabvazuva, nepanyika yekumavirazuva.
\v	8	Uyendichavaunza, vagogara mukati meJerusarema; zvino vachava vanhu vangu, neni ndichava Mwari wavo, muchokwadi nemukururama.
\p
\v	9	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha wehondo: Maoko enyu ngaasimbe, imwi munonzwa mumazuva ano mashoko awa anobva mumuromo wevaporofita, vaivapo pazuva nheyo yeimba yaJehovha wehondo ichiiswa, kuti tembere ivakwe.
\v	10	Nokuti mazuva iwayo asati asvika, kwaiva kusina muhoro wemunhu, hongu kwaiva kusina muhoro wemhuka; uye kune aibuda, nekune waipinda, kwaiva kusina rugare nekuda kwemudzivisi. Zvino ndakatuma munhu wose umwe neumwe kurwisa wekwake.
\v	11	Asi ikozvino handichazovi kune vakasara vevanhu ava sepamazuva ekare, anoreva Jehovha wehondo.
\v	12	Nokuti mbeu ichabudirira, muzambiringa uchabereka chibereko chawo, uye ivhu richabereka zvibereko zvaro, uye matenga achapa dova rawo; zvino ndichagarisa vakasara vevanhu ava nhaka yezvinhu izvi zvose.
\v	13	Zvino zvichaitika, sezvamaiva chakatukwa pakati pevechirudzi, imwi imba yaJudha, neimba yaIsraeri, saizvozvo ndichakuponesai, uye muchava ropafadzo; musatya, maoko enyu ngaasimbe.
\v	14	Nokuti anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Sezvandakafunga kukuitirai zvakaipa, madzibaba enyu paakanditsamwisa, anoreva Jehovha wehondo, zvino handina kuzvidemba;
\v	15	saizvozvozve, ndakadzoka ndofunga pamazuva ano kuitira Jerusarema zvakanaka, uye kuitira imba yaJudha; musatya.
\p
\v	16	Izvi zvinhu zvamuchaita: Taurai chokwadi, umwe neumwe kune wekwake; tongai pamasuwo enyu chokwadi nemutongo werugare.
\v	17	Zvino ngakusava neumwe anofungira wekwake zvakaipa mumoyo menyu; uye musada mhiko yenhema; nokuti zvose zvinhu izvi ndinovenga, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	18	Zvino shoko raJehovha wehondo rakasvika kwandiri richiti:
\v	19	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Kutsanya kwe mwedzi wechina, nekutsanya kweweshanu, nekutsanya kwewechinomwe, nekutsanya kwewegumi, zvichava mufaro nekufarisisa kuimba yaJudha, nemabiko akanaka; naizvozvo idai chokwadi nerugare.
\p
\v	20	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Kuchasvikazve marudzi nevagari vemaguta mazhinji.
\v	21	Zvino vagari ve guta  rimwe vachaenda kune rimwe vachiti: Ngatiendei nekukurumidza kunonyengetera pamberi paJehovha, nekutsvaka Jehovha wehondo; neni ndichaenda.
\v	22	Hongu, kuchauya vanhu vazhinji nemarudzi ane simba kutsvaka Jehovha wehondo paJerusarema, nekunyengetera pamberi paJehovha.
\v	23	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha wehondo: Mumazuva iwawo, varume gumi vanobva kundimi dzose dzemarudzi vachabata, hongu, vachabata mupendero wenguvo yemunhu muJudha, vachiti: Tichaenda nemwi, nokuti tanzwa kuti Mwari anemwi.
\c	9
\s	Kuparadzwa kwevavengi vaIsraeri
\p
\v	1	Mutoroweshoko raJehovha panyika yeHadhiraki, neDhamasiko, zororo rake; nokuti ziso remunhu neremarudzi ose aIsraeri riri kuna Jehovha.
\v	2	NeHamatiworichaganhurana naro, Tire, neSidhoni, kunyange rakachenjera zvikuru.
\v	3	ZvinoTire rakazvivakira nhare, uye rakaunganidza sirivha seguruva, negoridhe rakaisvonaka sematope emunzira dzemumusha.
\v	4	Tarirai,Ishe acharidzinga, uye acharovera simba raro mugungwa; uye iro richadyiwa nemoto.
\v	5	Ashikeronirichaona, ndokutya; neGaza, rigoshungurudzika zvikuru; neEkironi; nokuti zvarakatarisira zvichanyadziswa; uye mambo achaparara kubva kuGaza, uye Ashikeroni haringagarwi.
\v	6	Zvinowakazvarwa neufeve achagara muAshidhodhi, uye ndichagura kuzvikudza kwevaFirisitia.
\v	7	Zvinondichabvisa ropa rake kubva mumuromo make, nezvinyangadzo zvake kubva pakati pemazino ake; asi anosara ndiye achava waMwari wedu, achava semuchinda kwaJudha, uye Ekironi semuJebhusi.
\v	8	Zvinondichadzika musasa paimba yangu nekuda kwehondo, nekuda kweanopfuura uye nekuda kweanodzoka, uye hakuna mumanikidzi achapfuura nemavarizve, nokuti ikozvino ndaona nemeso angu.
\s	Mambo weZiyoni
\p
\v	9	Farakwazvo, iwe mukunda weZiyoni; pururudza, iwe mukunda weJerusarema; tarira, mambo wako anouya kwauri; wakarurama, uye ane ruponeso, munyoro, uye wakatasva mbongoro, nemhuru, mwana wedzimbongoro hadzi.
\v	10	Zvino ndichagura ngoro ibve kwaEfuremu, nebhiza paJerusarema; neuta hwehondo huchagurwa; zvino achataura rugare kune vechirudzi, uye umambo hwake huchabva pagungwa kuenda pagungwa, uye huchabva parwizi kusvika pamigumo yenyika.
\v	11	Newe, neropa resungano yako ndabudisa vasungwa vako kubva mugomba risina mvura.
\p
\v	12	Dzokerai kunhare, imwi vasungwa vane tariro; iko nhasi ndinodanidzira kuti ndichadzosera kwamuri zvakapetwa kaviri.
\v	13	Pandichazvikungira Judha, ndikazadza uta  naEfuremu; uye ndichamutsa vanakomana vako, iwe Ziyoni, varwisane nevanakomana vako, iwe Girisi; uye ndichakuita uve semunondo wemhare.
\v	14	Zvino Jehovha achaonekwa pamusoro pavo; nemuseve wake uchabuda semheni; uye Ishe Jehovha acharidza hwamanda, uye achafamba nezvamupupuri zvekuchamhembe.
\v	15	Jehovha wehondo achavadzitira; uye vachadya, vachawisira pasi mabwe echipfuramabwe; uye vachanwa,  ndokuita ruzha sewaini; uye vachazara sendiro, semakona earitari.
\v	16	Zvino Jehovha Mwari wavo achavaponesa pazuva iro seboka revanhu vake; nokuti ivo mabwe ekorona, anosimudzwa pamusoro penyika yake.
\v	17	Nokuti kwakakura sei kururama kwake, uye rwakakura sei runako rwake; zviyo zvichamutsa majaya, uye waini itsva mhandara.
