\b	Isaya
\c	1
\s	Jehovha anotsiura Israeri pakutadza kwake
\p
\v	1	ChiratidzochaIsaya mwanakomana waAmozi, chaakaona maererano naJudha neJerusarema pamazuva aUziya, Jotamu, Ahazi, Hezekia, madzimambo aJudha.
\p
\v	2	Inzwaiimwi matenga, uye ipa nzeve iwe nyika, nokuti Jehovha wakataura  achiti: Ndakarera nekukurisa vana, asi ivo vakandimukira.
\v	3	Nzombeinoziva muridzi wayo, nembongoro chidyiro chavatenzi vayo; Israeri haazivi, vanhu vangu havanzwisisi.
\v	4	Haiwa,rudzi runotadza, vanhu vakaremedzwa neuipi, mbeu yevaiti vezvakaipa, vana vanoodza  zvinhu! Vakasiya Jehovha, vakashora Mutsvene waIsraeri, vadzokera shure.
\p
\v	5	Mucharoverweizvekare, munongoramba muchimukira  Jehovha? Musoro wose unorwara, uye moyo wose waziya.
\v	6	Kubvirapasi perutsoka kusvika mumusoro hapana patano pauri;  asi maronda nepakakuvara nemavanga matsva, haana kusvinwa, uye haana kusungwa, uye haana kunyoroveswa nemafuta.
\v	7	Nyikayenyu kuparara; maguta enyu apiswa nemoto; nyika yenyu vatorwa vanoipedza pamberi penyu; uye yava kuparara sekupidigurwa nevatorwa.
\v	8	Uyemukunda weZiyoni wasara sedumba mumunda wemizambiringa, sechirindo mumunda wemagaka, seguta rakakombwa nehondo.
\v	9	DaiJehovha wehondo asina kutisiira vasiiwa vashomanana, tingadai takava seSodhoma, takafanana neGomora.
\v	10	Inzwai shoko raJehovha, imwi vatongi veSodhoma; ipai nzeve kumurairo waMwari wedu, imwi vanhu veGomora.
\v	11	Kuwanda kwezvibairo zvenyu kwandiri chii? Anoreva Jehovha. Ndaguta zvipo zvinopiswa zvemakondohwe, nemafuta ezvakakodzwa; neropa renzombe diki, neremakwayana, kana renhongo, handifadzwi nazvo.
\v	12	Kana muchiuya kuonekwa pamberi pangu, ndiani wakakumbira izvi paruoko rwenyu, kuti mutsike vazhe dzangu?
\v	13	Musaunzazve chipo chisina maturo; zvinonhuhwira zvinyangadzo kwandiri; kugara kwemwedzi, nesabata, kukokera ungano, handikwanisi; chitadzo, kunyange ungano tsvene.
\v	14	Kugara kwemwedzi yenyu nemitambo yenyu yakatarwa mweya wangu uno zvivenga, izvo mutoro kwandiri; ndaneta nekutakura.
\v	15	Uye pakutandavadza kwenyu maoko enyu, ndinovanza meso angu kubva kwamuri; uye kana muchiwanza munyengetero, handinzwi; maoko enyu akazara neropa zhinji.
\p
\v	16	Shambai; muzvinatse; bvisai uipi hwezviito zvenyu kubva pamberi pemeso angu; mirai kuita zvakaipa;
\v	17	dzidzai kuita zvakanaka; tsvakai mutongo; nunurai akamanikidzwa; tongerai nherera; kumbirirai chirikadzi.
\p
\v	18	Uyai ikozvino, titaurirane, anoreva Jehovha; kunyange zvivi zvenyu zvikaita se mucheka mutsvuku, zvichava zvichena semagada echando; kunyange zviri zvishava sezvishava-shava, zvichava semakushe emakwai.
\v	19	Kana muchida, uye muchiteerera, muchadya zvakanaka zvenyika.
\v	20	Asi kana muchiramba, muchikakavara, muchaparadzwa nemunondo; nokuti muromo waJehovha wakataura.
\p
\v	21	Guta rakatendeka rava chifeve sei! Raiva rizere mutongo, kururama kuchigara mariri, asi ikozvino mhondi zhinji.
\v	22	Sirivha yako yava ngura; waini yako yakasanganiswa nemvura.
\v	23	Machinda ako vapanduki, uye ishamwari dzembavha; umwe neumwe anoda fufuro, anodzingana nemibairo; havatongeri nherera, uye nyaya yechirikadzi haisviki kwavari.
\p
\v	24	Naizvozvo, anoreva Ishe, Jehovha wehondo, ane simba waIsraeri: Haiwa, ndichazvinyaradza pavadzivisi vangu uye ndichazvitsivira kuvavengi vangu.
\v	25	Zvino ndichatendeusira ruoko rwangu pamusoro pako, ndigonatsa kwazvo ngura yako, ndichabvisa marara ako ose.
\v	26	Ndichadzosera vatongi vako sepakutanga, nemakurukota ako sepakuvamba; shure kwaizvozvo uchanzi guta rekururama, guta rakatendeka.
\v	27	Ziyoni richadzikunurwa nemutongo, nevanodzoka varo nekururama.
\v	28	Zvino kugurwa kwevadariki nekwevatadzi kuchava pamwe chete, uye vanosiya Jehovha vachapera.
\v	29	Nokuti vachanyara pamusoro pemiouki yamaifarira, uye muchasvoda pamusoro pemabindu amakasanangura.
\v	30	Nokuti muchava semuouki mashizha awo asvava, uye sebindu risina mvura.
\v	31	Zvino ane simba achava sefirakisi riri choto, nemuiti waro ave rubari rwemoto, uye zvichatsva zviri zviviri pamwe chete, uye hakuna anodzima.
\c	10
\p
\v	1	Nhamokune vanotema mitemo yezvakaipa, nekuvanyori vanonyora dambudziko;
\v	2	kutsausavarombo pamutongo, nekutora nechisimba mutongo wevarombo vevanhu vangu, kuti chirikadzi dzive vakapambiwa vavo, kuti vapambe nherera.
\v	3	Asimuchaitei pazuva retsivo, nepakuparara kuchabva kure? Muchatizira kuna ani kurubatsiro? Uye muchasiyepi rukudzo rwenyu?
\v	4	Kunzekwangu vachakotama pasi pevasungwa, uye vachawira pasi pevakaurawa. Pane zvose izvi, kutsamwa kwake hakungadzorwi, asi ruoko rwake rwakangotandavadzwa.
\s	Kurohwa kwevaAsiriya
\p
\v	5	NhamokumuAsiriya, mudonzvo wekutsamwa kwangu, uye hasha dzangu itsvimbo muruoko rwavo!
\v	6	Ndichamutumirakurudzi rusina Mwari, uye ndichamuraira kurwisa vanhu vehasha dzangu, kupamba zvakapambiwa nekutapa zvakatapiwa nekuita pekutsikira sedope renzira dzemumusha.
\v	7	Asiiye haafungi saizvozvo, nemoyo wake haurangariri zvakadaro; asi zviri mumoyo wake kuparadza, nekugura marudzi asiri mashoma.
\v	8	Nokutianoti: Machinda angu ose haasi madzimambo here?
\v	9	Karinoharina kuita seKarikemishi here? Hamati harina kuita seAripadhi here? Samariya harina kuita seDhamasiko here?
\v	10	Ruoko rwangu sezvarwakawana ushe uzhinji hwezvifananidzo, zvifananidzo zvahwo zvakavezwa zvaipfuura  zvepaJerusarema ne zveSamariya;
\v	11	sezvandakaitira Samariya nezvifananidzo zvayo, handingaitiri Jerusarema nezvifananidzo zvaro here?
\p
\v	12	Zvino zvakaitika, kana Ishe apedza basa rake rose pagomo reZiyoni nemuJerusarema, ndichatsiva chibereko chekuzvikudza kwemoyo wamambo weAsiriya, nekunaka kwemanyawi emeso ake.
\v	13	Nokuti wakati: Ndakazviita nesimba reruoko rwangu, uye neuchenjeri hwangu, nokuti ndine njere; uye ndakabvisa miganhu yemarudzi, uye ndakapamba fuma yavo, uye seane simba ndakawisira pasi vagari.
\v	14	Ruoko rwangu rwakawana fuma yemarudzi sedendere; uye se munhu anounganidza mazai akasiiwa, ini ndakaunganidza nyika yose; uye paiva pasina waipfakanyikisa bapiro, kana kushamisa muromo, kana kurira.
\v	15	Demo ringazvikudza kune anotema naro here? Saha ingazvitutumadza kune anoizungunusa here? Setsvimbo inozungunusa vanoisimudza. Sezvinonzi mudonzvo ungazvisimudza, sezvinonzi hausi danda.
\v	16	Naizvozvo Ishe, Jehovha wehondo achatuma kuonda pakati pevakakora vake; uye pasi perukudzo rwake achatungidza kupfuta sekupfuta kwemoto.
\v	17	Zvino chiedza chaIsraeri chichava moto, neMutsvene wake ave murazvo, unopisa nekupedza minzwa yake nerukato rwake nezuva rimwe.
\v	18	Zvino uchapedza rukudzo rwesango rake nemunda wake unobereka, kubva pamweya weupenyu kusvika panyama, zvigova sekupera kwesimba remutakuri wemureza.
\v	19	Nezvakasara zvemiti yesango rake zvichava zvishoma pauwandu, zvekuti mwana angazvinyora.
\p
\v	20	Zvino zvakaitika nezuva iro, vakasara vaIsraeri nevakapukunyuka veimba yaJakove havangazosendamirizve pamusoro pewakavarova, asi vachasendamira kuna Jehovha, Mutsvene waIsraeri, muchokwadi.
\v	21	Vakasara vachadzoka, vakasara vaJakove, kuna Mwari ane simba.
\v	22	Nokuti kunyange vanhu venyu, Israeri, vakaita sejecha regungwa, vakasara vavo vachadzoka; kuparadzwa kwakatemwa, kupfachukira  nekururama.
\v	23	Nokuti Ishe, Jehovha wehondo achaita kuparadza uye kwatemwa pakati penyika yose.
\v	24	Naizvozvo anoreva saizvozvo Ishe, Jehovha wehondo: Vanhu vangu vagari veZiyoni, musatya muAsiriya, paanokurovai netsvimbo, uye achakusimudzirai mudonzvo wake nenzira yevaEgipita.
\v	25	Nokuti kwasara chinguva chidiki, kutsamwa kuchapera, nehasha dzangu pakuparadzwa kwavo.
\v	26	Zvino Jehovha wehondo achamusimudzira tyava, sepakurohwa kwevaMidhiani padombo reOrebhi; uye shamhu yake ichava pamusoro pegungwa, achaisimudza nenzira yevaEgipita.
\v	27	Zvino zvichaitika pazuva iro, achabvisa mutoro wake kubva pafudzi rako, nejoko rake kubva pamutsipa wako; uye joko richaparadzwa nekuda kwekuzodzwa.
\p
\v	28	Auya paAiati, apfuura paMigironi, achengeta midziyo yake paMikimashi.
\v	29	Vapfuura pamupata; usiku vanorara paGebha; Rama rinodedera; Gibhiya raSauro ratiza.
\v	30	Danidzira inzwi rako kwazvo, mukunda weGarimi! Teerera, Raisha, Anatoti murombo!
\v	31	Madhimena anotiza; vagari veGhebhimi votsvaka utiziro.
\v	32	Kuchine zuva ranhasi achamira paNobhi; anozunungus ira ruoko rwake gomo remukunda weZiyoni, chikomo cheJerusarema.
\v	33	Tarirai, Ishe, Jehovha wehondo, achatema mashizha nesimba rinotyisa; nevakakwirira paurefu vachatemerwa pasi; nevanozvikudza vachaninipiswa.
\v	34	Zvino achatema matenhere esango nesimbi; neRebhanoni richawa neane simba.
\c	11
\s	Ushe hwakanaka hwaMesiasi
\p
\v	1	Zvinopahunde yaJese pachabuda davi, uye bukira rinomera pamidzi yake.
\v	2	ZvinoMweya waJehovha uchagara pamusoro pake, Mweya wenjere nekunzwisisa, Mweya wezano nesimba, Mweya weruzivo newekutya Jehovha.
\v	3	Hwemahwake huchava pakutya Jehovha; uye haangatongi nekuona kwemeso ake, uye haangatsiuri nekunzwa kwenzeve dzake.
\v	4	Asiachatongera varombo nekururama, achitsiura vanyoro venyika pauchokwadi. Zvino acharova nyika netsvimbo yemuromo wake, uye nekufema kwemiromo yake achauraya wakaipa.
\v	5	Zvinokururama kuchava bhanhire rechiuno chake, uye kutendeka kuchava bhanhire rezvidya zvake.
\v	6	Nebumhirichagara negwayana; neingwe icharara  pasi nembudzana, nemhuru nehando yeshumba nechakakora pamwe chete; uye mukomana mudiki achadzitungamirira.
\v	7	Mhounebere zvichafura; mhuru dzazvo dzicharara pamwe chete, uye shumba ichadya majanga senzombe.
\v	8	Zvinomwana anoyamwa achatambira pamwena wenyoka, uye wakarumurwa achapinza ruoko rwake mubako remvumbi.
\v	9	Hazvingaitichakaipa uye hazvingaparadzi pagomo rangu rose dzvene, nokuti nyika ichazara nekuziva Jehovha, sezvinofukidza mvura gungwa.
\p
\v	10	Zvino nezuva iro kuchava nemudzi waJese, uchamira kuva mureza wevanhu; marudzi achatsvaka kwauri, uye nzvimbo yawo yekuzorora ichava nembiri.
\v	11	Zvino zvichaitika, pazuva iro, Ishe achashandisazve ruoko rwake rwechipiri, kudzosa vakasara vevanhu vake, vachasiiwa kubva kuAsiriya nekubva kuEgipita nekubva kuPatirosi nekubva kuItiyopiya nekubva kuErami nekubva kuShinari nekubva kuHamati nekubva kuzviwi zvegungwa.
\v	12	Uye achasimudzira marudzi mureza, nekuunganidza vadzingwa vaIsraeri, nevakaparadzirwa vaJudha achaunganidza kubva kumigumo mina yenyika.
\v	13	Negodo raEfuremu richabva, nevavengi vaJudha vachagurwa; Efuremu haangagodori Judha, naJudha haangamanikidzi Efuremu.
\v	14	Asi vachabhururukira pamusoro pefudzi revaFirisitia kumavirazuva; vachapambara pamwe chete vana vemabvazuva; vachatambanudzira Edhomu naMoabhu ruoko rwavo; nevanakomana vaAmoni vachavateerera.
\v	15	Uye Jehovha achaparadza chose rurimi rwegungwa reEgipita, achavheyesa ruoko rwake pamusoro perwizi nesimba remweya wake, arwurove rwuve hova nomwe, ayambuse  vanhu neshangu.
\v	16	Zvino kuchava nenzira huru yevakasara vevanhu vake, vakasara kubva kuAsiriya, sezvazvaiva zvakaita kwaIsraeri nezuva rekukwira kwake kubva kunyika yeEgipita.
\c	12
\s	Rwiyo rwekuvonga
\p
\v	1	Nezuvairo achati: Ndinokuvongai Jehovha; kunyange maiva makanditsamwira, kutsamwa kwenyu kwakadzorwa, uye mandinyaradza.
\v	2	Tarirai,Mwari ruponeso rwangu; ndichavimba ndisingatyi; nokuti Jehovha, Jehovha isimba rangu nerwiyo; iye wava ruponeso rwangu.
\v	3	Naizvozvonemufaro muchachera mvura kubva muzvitubu zveruponeso.
\v	4	Zvinonezuva iro muchati: Vongai Jehovha, danai zita rake, zivisai zviito zvake pakati pemarudzi, mudanidzire kuti zita rake rikwirire.
\v	5	ImbiraiJehovha; nokuti wakaita zvinhu zvikuru kwazvo; izvi ngazvizikanwe panyika yose.
\v	6	Danidziraupururudze, mugarikadzi weZiyoni; nokuti Mutsvene waIsraeri mukuru pakati penyu.
\c	13
\s	Shoko raJehovha pamusoro peBhabhironi
\p
\v	1	MutoroweBhabhironi Isaya mwanakomana waAmozi waakaona.
\p
\v	2	Simudzaimureza pagomo riri bani; simudzirai inzwi kwavari; ninirai ruoko, kuti vapinde pamasuwo evanoremekedzwa.
\v	3	Inindakaraira vakaitwa vatsvene vangu, ndakadanawo mhare dzangu nekuda kwekutsamwa kwangu, avo vanofara paukuru hwangu.
\v	4	Kutinhirakwechaunga mumakomo, kufanana nevanhu vakawanda, inzwi reruzha rweumambo uzhinji hwemarudzi akaungana: Jehovha wehondo achiongorora uto rehondo.
\v	5	Vanobvakunyika iri kure, kumugumo wematenga, Jehovha pamwe nezvombo zvekutsamwa kwake, kuparadza nyika yose.
\p
\v	6	Ungudzai,nokuti zuva raJehovha rava pedo; richasvika sekuparara kunobva kuna Wemasimbaose.
\v	7	Naizvozvomaoko ose achashaya simba, nemoyo umwe neumwe wemunhu uchanyunguduka.
\v	8	Zvinovachavhunduka; marwadzo nematambudziko zvichavabata; vacharwadziwa semukadzi anorwadziwa nemimba; vachashamisika umwe kune umwe wake; zviso zvavo zviso zvemirazvo yemoto.
\v	9	Tarirai,zuva raJehovha rinouya, rine utsinye, nehasha, nekupisa kwekutsamwa, kuisa nyika ive dongo, nekuparadza vatadzi vayo kubva mairi.
\v	10	Nokuti nyeredzi dzematenga, nemapoka adzo, hadzingapenyesi chiedza chadzo; zuva richasvibiswa pakubuda kwaro; nemwedzi haungapenyesi chiedza chawo.
\v	11	Nokuti ndichatsiva uipi pamusoro penyika, nezvivi zvavo pamusoro pevatadzi; uye ndichagumisa manyawi evanozvikudza, nekuninipisa kuzvikudza kwevanotyisa.
\v	12	Ndichaita murume akoshe kupfuura goridhe rakaisvonaka, uye munhu kupfuura goridhe rakazara reOfiri.
\v	13	Naizvozvo ndichazungunusa matenga, nenyika ichadedera ibve panzvimbo yayo, pakutsamwa kwaJehovha wehondo, uye pazuva rekupisa kwekutsamwa kwake.
\v	14	Zvino zvichava semhara inovhimiwa, uye semakwai asina anoaunganidza; vachatendeukira umwe neumwe kuvanhu vekwake, vatizire umwe neumwe kunyika yake.
\v	15	Umwe neumwe achawanikwa achabaiwa; uye umwe neumwe achabatwa achawa nemunondo.
\v	16	Nevacheche vavo vachapwanyiwa pamberi pemeso avo; dzimba dzavo dzichapambiwa; uye vakadzi vavo vachararwa.
\v	17	Tarirai, ndichavamutsira vaMedhi, vasingakudzi sirivha, negoridhe havarifariri.
\v	18	Asi uta  hwavo huchapwanyira pasi majaya; uye havangavi netsitsi nechibereko chechizvaro; ziso ravo harina tsitsi nevana.
\p
\v	19	Zvino Bhabhironi, mbiri paumambo uzhinji, kunaka kwekuzvikudza kwevaKaradhiya, richava sekuparadzwa naMwari kweSodhoma neGomora.
\v	20	Haringatongogarwi kusvika rinhi narinhi, uye haringagarwi kuzera nezera; nemuArabhiya haangadziki tende rakemo, nevafudzi havangavatisipo  mapoka.
\v	21	Asi zvikara zvesango zvichararamo; uye dzimba dzavo dzichazara nezvinorira; nevakunda vemhou vachagaramo; nemweya yakaipa yakaita sembudzi ichatambapo.
\v	22	Zvino mapere achadavirana mumatongo avo, nemabukanana pamizinda ine mufaro. Zvino nguva yaro yava pedo kusvika, nemazuva aro haanganonoswi.
\c	14
\p
\v	1	NokutiJehovha achanzwira Jakove tsitsi, achasanangurazve Israeri, ndokuvaisa panyika yekwavo; nevatorwa vachasangana navo, uye vachanamatira kuimba yaJakove.
\v	2	Zvinovanhu vachavatora ndokuvaisa panzvimbo yavo; uye imba yaIsraeri ichavapfuwa panyika yaJehovha kuva varandarume nevarandakadzi; vagova vatapwa vevatapi vavo, uye vachatonga pamusoro pevamanikidzi vavo.
\v	3	Zvinozvichaitika pazuva Jehovha raachapa zororo kwauri kubva pakurwadziwa kwako, nepadambudziko rako, nepauranda hwakaoma hwawakashandiswa nahwo,
\v	4	uchasimudzadimikira iri pamusoro pamambo weBhabhironi, uchiti: Mumanikidzi wapera sei!  Guta regoridhe rapera sei!
\v	5	Jehovhawavhuna mudonzvo wevakaipa, tsvimbo yeushe yevatongi.
\v	6	Iyewakarova vanhu mukutsamwa  nekurova kusingaperi, iye waitonga marudzi nehasha, wadzingirirwa pasina anodzivisa.
\v	7	Nyikayose yazorora, yanyarara; vanodambuka kuimba.
\v	8	Nemisipiresiinofara nekuda kwako, misidhari yeRebhanoni,  ichiti: Chinguva chawarara pasi, hakuna mutemi anokwira kwatiri.
\v	9	Gehenapasi rakanyonganiswa newe, kuchingamidza kupinda kwako; rakakumutsira mweya yakafa, nhongo dzose dzenyika; rakasimudza madzimambo ose emarudzi kubva pazvigaro zvawo zveushe.
\v	10	Vose vachapindura vati kwauri: Newe hauchina simba sesu; wafanana nesu.
\v	11	Kuzvikudza kwako kwaderedzerwa mugehena,  nekurira kwembira dzako; honye dzichawadzwa pasi pako, nemakonye anokufukidza.
\v	12	Wawa zvakadini kubva kumatenga, iwe anopenya, mwanakomana wemambakwedza! Watemerwa  sei pasi, iwe wekuwisira marudzi pasi!
\v	13	Iwe ndokuti mumoyo mako: Ndichakwira kumatenga; ndichakwiridza chigaro changu cheushe pamusoro penyeredzi dzaMwari; zvino ndichagara pamusoro pegomo reungano, kumativi ekumaodzanyemba;
\v	14	ndichakwira kumusoro kwenzvimbo dzakakwirira dzemakore; ndichazvienzanisa neWekumusoro-soro.
\v	15	Zvakadaro uchaderedzerwa kugehena, kumativi egomba.
\v	16	Vanokuona vachakucherekedza, vachakutarira,  vachiti: Ndiye here munhu uyu aidederesa nyika, aizunungusa umambo uzhinji,
\v	17	wakaitisa nyika serenje, uye wakaparadza maguta avo, asina kuzarurira vasungwa vake vaende kumusha?
\v	18	Madzimambo ose emarudzi, ose zvawo, anorara nerukudzo, umwe neumwe mumba make.
\v	19	Asi iwe wakakandwa panze kubva muguva rako sedavi rinonyangadza, mucheka wevakaurawa, vakabaiwa nemunondo, vanoburukira kumabwe egomba, sechitunha chinotsikirwa pasi.
\v	20	Haungabatanidzwi navo pakuvigwa, nokuti wakaparadza nyika yako, wakauraya vanhu vako; mbeu yevaiti vezvakaipa haingatumidzwi kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	21	Gadzirirai kuurawa kwevana vavo nekuda kweuipi hwemadzibaba avo, kuti vasamuka vagare nhaka yenyika, kana kuzadza chiso chenyika nemaguta.
\p
\v	22	Nokuti ndichavamukira, anoreva Jehovha wehondo: Ndigogura kubvisa zita muBhabhironi, nevakasara, nemwanakomana nemuzukuru, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	23	Ndichariita nzvimbo yenungu uye madziva emvura; uye ndicharitsvaira nemutsvairo wekuparadza, anoreva Jehovha wehondo.

\v	2	VakwirakuBhayiti, nekuDhibhoni, nzvimbo dzakakwirira, kunochema; Moabhu achaungudzira Nebho neMedhebha; misoro yavo yose ichava nemhanza, ndebvu imwe neimwe yakavevurwa.
\v	3	Panziradzavo dzemumisha vachazvisunga zviuno nemasaga; pamusoro pedzimba dzavo nemunzira dzavo dzemumisha umwe neumwe achaungudza, vachiburuka vachichema misodzi.
\v	4	ZvinoHeshibhoni neEreare anodanidzira; inzwi ravo rinonzwikwa kusvika kuJahazi; naizvozvo vane zvombo vaMoabhu vanodanidzira; mweya wake unodedera mukati make.
\v	5	Moyowangu unochemera Moabhu; vatizi vake vanosenerera kuZoari, tsiru remakore matatu; nokuti mukwidza weRuhiti vakaukwira vachichema misodzi; nokuti munzira yeHoronaimi vanosimudza kuchema kwekuparadza.
\v	6	Nokutimvura zhinji yeNimirimi ichava kuparara kukuru; nokuti uswa hwasvava, bumhudza rapera, hapana  chinomera chitema.
\v	7	Naizvozvokuwanda kwavakaita nefuma yavo vachazvitakurira kurukova rwemikonachando.
\v	8	Nokutikuchema kwakapoteredza miganhu yaMoabhu, kuungudza kwake kusvika kuEgiraimi, hongu, kuungudza kwake kusvika paBheeri-Erimi.
\v	9	Nokutimvura zhinji yeDhimoni izere neropa; nokuti ndichatarira Dhimoni zvimwezve, shumba kune vakapukunyuka vaMoabhu, nekune vakasara venyika.

\v	4	Vakarashwavangu ngavagare sevatorwa pakati pako, Moabhu; iva uvando kwavari, kubva pachiso chemuparadzi; nokuti mumanikidzi waguma, muparadzi wapera, anotsikira pasi wapedzwa panyika.
\v	5	Zvinochigaro cheushe chichamiswa neunyoro; uye achagara pachiri neuchokwadi, mutende raDhavhidhi, achitonga nekutsvaka mutongo, uye kukurumidzira kururama.
\p
\v	6	Takanzwazvekuzvikudza kwaMoabhu, anodada zvikuru, manyawi ake, nekuzvikudza kwake, nehasha dzake;  asi kuropota kwake hakuna kudaro.
\v	7	NaizvozvoMoabhu achaungudzira Moabhu; vose vachaungudza; muchachemera mapundu emazambiringa eKiri-Hareseti, zvirokwazvo varohwa.
\v	8	Nokutiminda yeHeshibhoni yasvava,  nemuzambiringa weSibhima; madzishe emarudzi arovera pasi matavi awo akanakisisa, asenerera muJazeri, adzungaira  murenje; matavi awo azvitandavadza, ayambuka gungwa.
\v	9	Nekudakweizvozvi ndinochema misodzi nekuchema misodzi kwaJazeri  pamusoro pemuzambiringa weSibhima; ndichakudiridza nemisodzi yangu, iwe Heshibhoni neEreare; nokuti kupururudzira zvibereko zvako zvezhizha nekukohwa kwako kwawa.
\v	10	Kupembera nemufaro zvabviswa pamunda unobereka; uye muminda yemizambiringa havaimbi, havadanidziri; musvini haasvini waini muzvisviniro; ndagumisa mupururu.
\v	11	Naizvozvo ura hwangu pamusoro paMoabhu hwunorira sembira, nezvemukati zvangu pamusoro peKiri-Heresi.
\v	12	Zvino zvichaitika kana kwaonekwa kuti Moabhu waneta panzvimbo yakakwirira, ipapo achapinda paimba yake tsvene kunonyengetera, asi haangakundi.
\v	13	Iri ishoko Jehovha raakataura pamusoro paMoabhu kubva panguva iyo.
\v	14	Asi ikozvino Jehovha anotaura achiti: Mumakore matatu, semakore emushandi wakahaiwa, rukudzo rwaMoabhu rwuchazvidzwa, pamwe chete nechaunga chose chikuru; uye vakasara vachava vashoma, vadiki, vasina simba.

\s	Mutoro weEgipita
\p
\v	1	MutoroweEgipita. Tarirai, Jehovha wakatasva gore rinomhanya, uye achauya kuEgipita; uye zvifananidzo zveEgipita zvichazungunuswa pamberi pake, nemoyo weEgipita uchanyauka mukati mawo.
\v	2	Zvinondichamutsa vaEgipita kurwisa vaEgipita, kuti varwe, umwe neumwe achirwisa hama yake, uye umwe neumwe achirwisana nemuvakidzani wake, guta richirwisana neguta, ushe huchirwisana neushe.
\v	3	Zvinomweya weEgipita uchadururwa mukati mawo; uye ndichamedza zano rawo; uye vachabvunza zvifananidzo zvavo nemasvikiro nevavuki nen'anga.
\v	4	Zvinondichakumikidza vaEgipita muruoko rwatenzi wakaomarara, zvino mambo ane simba achatonga pamusoro pavo, anoreva Ishe Jehovha wehondo.
\v	5	Mvurazhinji yegungwa ichapwa, uye rwizi ruchaderera nekuoma.
\v	6	Zvinovacharamba nzizi; hova dzeEgipita dzichatapuka nekupwa; tsanga nenhokwe zvichasvava.
\v	7	Nhokwedzebani pamuromo wenzizi, nezvose zvakadzvarwa panzizi, zvichasvava, zvichapepereswa, zvikasazovapo.
\v	8	Zvinovabati vehove vachachema, uye vose vanokandira zviredzo munzizi vachaungudza, nevanotandira mumbure pachiso chemvura zhinji vachapera simba.
\v	9	Zvinovabati vefirakisi yakapfava nevaruki vezvichena vachanyadziswa.
\v	10	Zvino nheyo dzayo dzose dzichatswanyiwa; uye varume vanoshandira mubairo vachashungurudzika pamweya.
\p
\v	11	Zvirokwazvo machinda eZoani mapenzi; zano revakachenjera vevapi vemazano vaFarao rava upenzi. Munoreva sei kuna Farao  muchiti: Ndiri mwanakomana wevakachenjera, mwanakomana wemadzimambo ekare?
\v	12	Zvino varipi vakachenjera vako? Hongu, ngavakuudze, ngavazive Jehovha wehondo zvaakananga pamusoro peEgipita.
\v	13	Machinda eZoani ava mapenzi; machinda eNofi akanyengerwa; vachatsausa Egipita, mbiru yemarudzi ayo.
\v	14	Jehovha wakavhenganisa mukati mavo mweya wekutsauka; uye vakatsausa Egipita pabasa rayo rose, sechidhakwa chinodzedzereka mukurutsa kwacho.
\v	15	Zvino hapangavi nebasa kuEgipita, musoro kana muswe, davi kana nhokwe razvingaita.
\p
\v	16	Nezuva iro Egipita richava sevakadzi; uye richadedera nekutya nekuda kwekuzungunuka kweruoko rwaJehovha wehondo rwaakazungunusa pamusoro paro.
\v	17	Zvino nyika yaJudha ichava chityiso kuEgipita; umwe neumwe achataura nezvayo achavhunduka pachake nekuda kwezano raJehovha wehondo, raakatema kuvarwisa.
\p
\v	18	Nezuva iro maguta mashanu achavapo panyika yeEgipita, anotaura rurimi rweKenani, nekupika naJehovha wehondo; umwe achatumidzwa: Guta rekuparadzwa.
\v	19	Nezuva iro pachava nearitari pakati penyika yeEgipita kuna Jehovha, nembiru pamuganhu waro kuna Jehovha.
\v	20	Zvino zvichava chiratidzo nechapupu kuna Jehovha wehondo panyika yeEgipita; nokuti vachadanidzira kuna Jehovha nekuda kwevamanikidzi; uye achavatumira muponesi uye mukuru, agovanunura.
\v	21	Zvino Jehovha achazikanwa kuEgipita, nevaEgipita vachaziva Jehovha nezuva iro; vachaita chibairo nechipo, nekupikira Jehovha mhiko, nekuizadzisa.
\v	22	Zvino Jehovha acharova Egipita, achirova nekurapa; uye vachadzokera kuna Jehovha; iye achanyengetedzeka navo ndokuvaporesa.
\p
\v	23	Nezuva iro pachava nenzira huru inobva Egipita ichienda Asiriya; kuti vaAsiriya vauye kuEgipita, nevaEgipita kuAsiriya; uye vaEgipita vachanamata pamwe nevaAsiriya.
\v	24	Nezuva iro Israeri uchava wetatu pamwe neEgipita uye pamwe neAsiriya, ropafadzo mukati menyika.
\v	25	Jehovha wehondo waacharopafadza achiti: Rakaropafadzwa Egipita vanhu vangu, neAsiriya basa remaoko angu, naIsraeri nhaka yangu.
