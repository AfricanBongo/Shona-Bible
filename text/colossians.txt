\b	Tsamba yaPauro kuVaKorose
\c	1
\s	Kwaziso
\p
\v	1	Pauromuapositori waJesu Kristu nechido chaMwari, naTimotio hama,
\v	2	kuvatsvenenehama dzakatendeka muna Kristu dziri paKorose: Nyasha kwamuri nerugare rwunobva kuna Mwari Baba vedu naIshe Jesu Kristu.
\s	Kuvonga kwaPauro pamusoro pavo
\p
\v	3	TinovongaMwari naBaba vaIshe wedu Jesu Kristu, tichikunyengetererai nguva dzose,
\v	4	sezvotakanzwa zverutendo rwenyu muna Kristu Jesu, uye zverudo kuvatsvene vose,
\v	5	nekudakwetariro yakaisirwa imwi kumatenga, yamakagara manzwa pashoko rechokwadi cheevhangeri,
\v	6	yasvikakwamuri sepanyika yesewo; ichibereka chibereko, sezva inoitawo kwamuri, kubvira pazuva ramakanzwa nekuziva nyasha dzaMwari muchokwadi;
\v	7	sezvamakadzidzawokuna Epafrasi, muranda anodikanwa pamwe nesu, anova mushumiri wakatendeka waKristu nekuda kwenyu,
\v	8	wakatiudzawo zverudo rwenyu muMweya.
\s	Pauro anovanyengeterera
\p
\v	9	Nekudakweizvi isuwo, kubvira pazuva ratakanzwa, hatimiri kukunyengetererai, nekukumbira kuti muzadzwe neruzivo rwechido chake pauchenjeri hwose nekunzwisisa kwemweya,
\v	10	kuti mufambe zvinofanira Ishe pane zvose zvinofadza, muchibereka zvibereko pabasa rose rakanaka, muchikura paruzivo rwaMwari;
\v	11	muchisimbiswa nesimba rose, zvichienderana nesimba rekubwinya kwake, pamoyo murefu wose nekutsungirira pamwe nemufaro;
\v	12	muchivonga kuna Baba, vakatiita kuti tifanire kugoverana kwenhaka yevatsvene muchiedza,
\v	13	vakatinunura pasimba rerima, vakatishandurira muushe hweMwanakomana werudo rwavo,
\v	14	watine dzikunuro maari neropa rake,  iyo kangamwiro yezvivi.
\p
\v	15	Anova mufananidzo waMwari asingaonekwi, dangwe rechisikwa chega-chega.
\v	16	Nokuti maari zvinhu zvose zvakasikwa, zviri kumatenga nezviri panyika, zvinoonekwa nezvisingaonekwi, kana zviri zvigaro zveushe, kana umambo, kana ukuru, kana masimba; zvinhu zvose zvakasikwa naye,  zvikasikirwa iye.
\v	17	Ndiyewo anotangira zvinhu zvose, uye zvinhu zvose zvakabatanidzwa maari.
\v	18	Uye iye musoro wemuviri, wekereke; anova kutanga, dangwe kubva kune vakafa; kuti pazvose iye ave wekutanga;
\v	19	nokuti zvakafadza  Baba, kuti kuzara kose kugare maari,
\v	20	uye ayananisa naye zvinhu zvose kwaari, aita rugare neropa remuchinjikwa wake kubudikidza naiye, kunyange zviri panyika kunyange zviri kumatenga.
\p
\v	21	Nemwi maimbova vaeni nevavengi mupfungwa nemabasa akaipa, asi ikozvino wayanana  nemwi
\v	22	mumuviri wenyama yake kubudikidza nerufu, akukumikidzei muri vatsvene uye musina mhosva uye musingamhuriki pamberi pake;
\v	23	zvino kana muchirambira parutendo makateyiwa uye makasimbiswa, uye hamubviswi patariro yeevhangeri yamakanzwa, yakaparidzirwa chisikwa choga-choga chiri pasi pedenga; iyo yandakaitwa mushumiri wayo ini Pauro.
\s	Kutambudzika kwaPauro pamusoro pavo
\p
\v	24	Zvino ikozvino ndinofara pamatambudziko angu nekuda kwenyu, nekuzadzisa zvakasara pamatambudziko aKristu munyama yangu nekuda kwemuviri wake, unova kereke;
\v	25	ini yandakaitwa mushumiri wayo zvichienderana nekubatiswa kwaMwari chandakapiwa pamusoro penyu, kuzadzisa shoko raMwari,
\v	26	chakavanzika chaiva chakavanzwa kubvira kare uye kubva kumazera, asi ikozvino chinoratidzwa kuvatsvene vake,
\v	27	kune avo Mwari vaakada kuzivisa kuti fuma yekubwinya kwechakavanzika ichi pakati pevahedheni chii, chinova Kristu mamuri, tariro yekubwinya;
\v	28	watinoparidza isu, tichiyambira munhu umwe neumwe, nekudzidzisa munhu umwe neumwe munjere dzose, kuti tikumikidze munhu umwe neumwe akakwana muna Kristu Jesu;
\v	29	ndizvo zvandinoshingairirawo, ndichirwa zvinoenderana nekubata kwake kunobata mandiri nesimba.
\c	2
\p
\v	1	Nokutindinoda kuti muzive  kuti kurwa kukuru zvakadini kwandinako pamusoro penyu, nevari paRaodhikia, nevose vasina kuona chiso changu panyama,
\v	2	kutimoyo yavo inyaradzwe, vasonanidzwe murudo, nemufuma yose yekuvimbiswa kwakakwana kwekunzwisisa, paruzivo rwechakavanzika chaMwari naBaba, nechaKristu,
\v	3	maarifuma yose yeuchenjeri neyeruzivo yakavigwa.
\v	4	Ndinorevaizvi, kuti kusava nemunhu anokunyengerai nemashoko anonyengera.
\v	5	Nokutikunyange ndisipo panyama, zvakadaro ndinemwi pamweya, ndichifara nekuona kurongeka kwenyu, nekusimba kwerutendo rwenyu muna Kristu.
\s	Anovanyevera kuti varege kutsauswa
\p
\v	6	Naizvozvosezvamakagamuchira Kristu Jesu Ishe, fambai maari,
\v	7	makadzikamidzi nekuvakwa maari, nekusimbiswa murutendo, sezvamakadzidziswa, muchiwedzera makuri nekuvonga.
\v	8	Chenjerai,zvimwe umwe munhu angakupambai neuchenjeri, nekunyengera kusina maturo, setsika dzevanhu, sedzidziso dzekuvamba dzenyika, uye kwete sekuna Kristu.
\v	9	Nokutikuzara kose kweuMwari kunogara maari pamuviri.
\v	10	Uye makazadziswa maari, iye musoro weutungamiriri hwose nesimba;
\v	11	wamakadzingiswawo maari nedzingiso isina kuitwa nemaoko, pakubvisa muviri wezvivi zvenyama, pakudzingisa kwaKristu;
\v	12	makavigwa pamwe naye pabhabhatidzo, pamakamutswawo pamwe  naye nerutendo rwekuita kwaMwari, wakamumutsa kubva kuvakafa.
\v	13	Nemwi makafa mukudarika nekusadzingiswa kwenyama yenyu, wakakuitai vapenyu pamwe naye, akukanganwirai kudarika kose,
\v	14	akadzima rugwaro rwezviga rwaipikisana nesu, rwaipesana nesu, akarubvisa pakati, aruroverera pamuchinjikwa;
\v	15	akurura vatungamiriri nemasimba, akavabudisa pachena, akatungamira kuvakunda mauri.
\p
\v	16	Naizvozvo kusava nemunhu anokutongai pakudya kana pachinwiwa, kana panhau yemutambo kana yekugara kwemwedzi kana yemasabata;
\v	17	zvinova mumvuri wezvinhu zvichauya, asi muviri  ndewa Kristu.
\p
\v	18	Kusava nemunhu anokutorerai mubairo wenyu nemano, achifarira kuzvininipisa nekunamata vatumwa, achipindira pazvinhu zvaasina kuona, achizvikudza pasina nepfungwa dzake dzenyama,
\v	19	uye asingabatisisi Musoro, uyo maari muviri wose nezvisungo nemarunda unoriritirwa, nekurukirwa pamwe, uchikura nekukura kwaMwari.
\p
\v	20	Naizvozvo kana makafa pamwe naKristu kune dzidziso dzekuvamba dzenyika, munosungikanirei nemirairo semugere munyika,
\v	21	(usabata, usaravira, usagunzva,
\v	22	izvo zvose zvichaparara nekushandiswa) semirairo nedzidziso dzevanhu?
\v	23	Zvine chimiro cheuchenjeri pakuzvidira kunamata, nekuzvininipisa, nekutambudza muviri; hazvitongobatsiri kukunda kugutsa nyama.
\c	3
\s	Ivai vatsvene muchidanana
\p
\v	1	Naizvozvokana makamutswa pamwe naKristu, tsvakai zvekumusoro, uko kuna Kristu agere kuruoko rwerudyi rwaMwari.
\v	2	Rangariraizviri kumusoro, kwete zviri panyika.
\v	3	Nokutimakafa, uye upenyu hwenyu hwakavigwa pamwe naKristu muna Mwari.
\v	4	KanaKristu upenyu hwedu achionekwa, ipapo imwiwo muchaonekwa pamwe naye mukubwinya.
\p
\v	5	Naizvozvourayai mitezo yenyu iri panyika, upombwe, tsvina, kushumba kwakaipa, kuda zvakaipa, uye kuchiva, kunova kunamata zvifananidzo.
\v	6	Nekudakwezvinhu izvi, hasha dzaMwari dzinouya pavana vekusateerera;
\v	7	imwiwomaimbofamba mazviri, pamairarama mazviri.
\v	8	Asiikozvino imwiwo bvisai zvose izvi, kutsamwa, hasha, uipi, kunyomba, kutaura zvinonyadzisa kubva mumuromo wenyu;
\v	9	regaikureverana nhema, zvamakabvisa munhu wekare nemabasa ake,
\v	10	mukapfeka mutsva, anovandudzwa paruzivo semufananidzo wewakamusika;
\v	11	apo pasina muGiriki uye muJudha, kudzingiswa uye kusadzingiswa, mutorwa, muSikitia, musungwa, wakasununguka; asi Kristu  ndiye zvose, uye  ari mune zvose.
\v	12	Naizvozvo pfekai sevasanangurwa vaMwari, vatsvene nevanodikanwa, moyo yetsitsi, moyo munyoro, kuzvininipisa, unyoro, moyo murefu;
\v	13	muchiitirana moyo murefu, nekukanganwirana kana umwe ane mhosva neumwe; Kristu sezvaakakukanganwiraiwo, saizvozvowo imwi;
\v	14	zvino pamusoro pezvinhu zvose izvi  pfekai rudo, ndicho chisungo chekupedzeredza.
\v	15	Nerugare rwaMwari ngarwutonge mumoyo yenyu, ndirwo rwamakadanirwawo mumuviri mumwe; uye ivai vanovonga.
\v	16	Shoko raKristu ngarigare mamuri neufumi muuchenjeri hwose; muchidzidzisana nekurairana mumapisarema, nenziyo, nenziyo dzemweya, muimbire Ishe nenyasha mumoyo wenyu;
\v	17	uye zvose chero chipi chamunoita pashoko kana pachiito,  itai zvose muzita raIshe Jesu, muchivonga Mwari naBaba kubudikidza naye.
\s	VaKristu mumba mavo
\p
\v	18	Vakadzi, zviisei pasi pevarume venyu iyemwi, sezvo zvakafanira muna Ishe.
\v	19	Varume, idai vakadzi  venyu, uye regai kuvavavira.
\v	20	Vana, teererai vabereki pazvinhu zvose, nokuti izvi zvinofadza Ishe.
\v	21	Madzibaba, musatsamwisa vana venyu, kuti varege kuora moyo.
\p
\v	22	Varanda, teererai pazvinhu zvose vari vatenzi  venyu panyama; kwete nekushandira maziso sevafadzi vevanhu, asi nemoyo wakarurama, muchitya Mwari;
\v	23	uye zvose kunyange chipi chamunoita, itai nemoyo, sekuna Ishe, uye kwete kuvanhu;
\v	24	muchiziva kuti kuna Ishe muchagamuchira mubairo wenhaka; nokuti munoshandira Ishe Kristu.
\v	25	Asi anoita zvisakarurama achagamuchira zvisakarurama zvaakaita; uye hakuna kusarurwa kwavanhu.
\c	4
\p
\v	1	Madzishe,ipai varanda zvakarurama nezvakaringana, muchiziva kuti imwiwo muna Ishe kumatenga.
\s	Kutsungirira pakunamata
\p
\v	2	Tsungiriraipamunyengetero, murinde pakuri nekuvonga;
\v	3	mutinyengetererewopanguva imweyo, kuti Mwari atizarurire mukova weshoko, kutaura chakavanzika chaKristu, chandakasungirwawo;
\v	4	kutindichiratidze, sezvandinofanira kutaura.
\v	5	Fambaimuuchenjeri kune vari kunze, muchidzikinura nguva.
\v	6	Shokorenyu ngarigare munyasha, rakarungwa nemunyu, kuti muzive kuti munofanira kupindura sei umwe neumwe.
\s	Anotuma Tikiko naOnesimo
\p
\v	7	Zvosezvakanangana neni Tikiko achakuzivisai, hama inodikanwa nemushumiri wakatendeka nemushandi pamwe  neni muna Ishe;
\v	8	wandatumirakwamuri ichochi, kuti azive zviri maererano nemwi, nekunyaradza moyo yenyu;
\v	9	pamwenaOnesimo, hama yakatendeka inodikanwa, anova wekwenyu. Vachakuzivisai zvose zviri pano.
\s	Kuwonekana
\p
\v	10	Arisitako musungwa pamwe neni anokukwazisai, naMariko muzukuru waBhanabhasi, wamakagamuchira mirairo maererano naye; kana achisvika kwamuri, mumugamuchire,
\v	11	naJesu anonzi Justo, ve vekudzingiswa; ivava chete vabati pamwe  neni paushe hwaMwari, vaiva nyaradzo kwandiri.
\v	12	Epafrasi wekwenyu, muranda waKristu, anokukwazisai, achirwa nguva dzose muminyengetero nekuda kwenyu, kuti mumire makazara uye makakwana muchido chose chaMwari.
\v	13	Nokuti ndinomupupurira kuti ane shungu kwazvo nemwi, neveRaodhikia, neveHierapori.
\v	14	Anokukwazisai Ruka chiremba anodikanwa, naDhemasi.
\p
\v	15	Kwazisai hama dziri Raodhikia, naNimifa, nekereke iri mumba make.
\v	16	Zvino kana tsamba iyi yaverengwa pakati penyu, itai kuti nemukereke yevaRaodhikia iverengwe; kuti nemwi muverengewo inobva Raodhikia.
\v	17	Vudzai Akipo,  muti: Chenjerera ushumiri hwawakagamuchira muna Ishe, kuti uhupedzise.
\p
\v	18	Kwaziso neruoko rwangu rwaPauro. Rangarirai zvisungo zvangu. Nyasha  ngadzive nemwi. Ameni.
