\b	2 Samueri
\c	1
\s	Dhavhidhi anochema Sauro naJonatani
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kwekufa kwaSauro, Dhavhidhi paaiva adzoka pakurova vaAmareki, Dhavhidhi ndokugara mazuva maviri paZikiragi.
\v	2	Zvakaitikawonezuva retatu, kuti tarira, kwakasvika munhu kubva kumusasa, kubva kuna Sauro, ane nhumbi dzake dzakabvaruka, ane mavhu mumusoro wake. Zvino zvakaitika pakusvika kwake kuna Dhavhidhi, kuti wakawira pavhu, ndoku munamata.
\v	3	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakati kwaari: Unobvepi? Iye ndokuti kwaari: Ndapukunyuka kubva kumusasa waIsraeri.
\v	4	Dhavhidhindokuti kwaari: Yakadii nyaya? Ndiudze hako. Iye ndokuti: Vanhu vatiza pahondo, uyewo vazhinji vevanhu vawa uye vafa, uyewo Sauro nemwanakomana wake Jonatani vafa.
\v	5	Dhavhidhindokuti kujaya rakamuudza: Waziva sei kuti Sauro naJonatani mwanakomana wake vafa?
\v	6	Zvinojaya raiva ramuudza rakati: Ndakati ndichingosvikawo zvangu pagomo reGiribhoa, zvino tarira, Sauro wakasendama papfumo rake, zvino tarira, ngoro nevatasvi vemabhiza vaiva vakamunamatira.
\v	7	Paakatarisashure wakandiona ndokudana kwandiri. Zvino ndakati: Ndiri  pano.
\v	8	Zvinowakati kwandiri: Ndiwe ani? Zvino ndakati kwaari: Ndiri muAmareki.
\v	9	Zvinowakati kwandiri: Mira hako pamusoro pangu undiuraye, nokuti marwadzo makuru andibata, nokuti upenyu hwangu huchizere mandiri.
\v	10	Naizvozvo ndakamira pamusoro pake ndokumuuraya, nokuti ndaiva ndichiziva kuti haangararami shure kwekuwa kwake. Zvino ndakatora korona yaiva pamusoro wake, neruketani rwaiva paruoko rwake, ndokuuya nazvo pano kuna ishe wangu.
\v	11	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakabata nguvo dzake ndokudzibvarura; saizvozvo nevarume vose vaiva naye.
\v	12	Zvino vakaungudza nekuchema misodzi nekutsanya kusvika madekwani pamusoro paSauro nepamusoro paJonatani mwanakomana wake nepamusoro pevanhu vaJehovha nepamusoro peimba yaIsraeri nokuti vaiva vawa nemunondo.
\p
\v	13	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakati kujaya rakamuudza: Unobvepi? Iro ndokuti: Ndiri mwanakomana wemutorwa, muAmareki.
\v	14	Dhavhidhi ndokuti kwaari: Sei usina kutya kutandavadza ruoko rwako kuparadza muzodzwa waJehovha?
\v	15	Dhavhidhi ndokudana rimwe remajaya ndokuti: Swedera, umuwire. Iye ndokumurova kusvika afa.
\v	16	Dhavhidhi ndokuti kwaari: Ropa rako ngarive pamusoro pemusoro wako, nokuti muromo wako wakupupurira, uchiti: Ini ndakauraya muzodzwa waJehovha.
\p
\v	17	Dhavhidhi ndokuungudza mariro awa nekuda kwaSauro uye nekuda kwaJonatani mwanakomana wake.
\v	18	Ipapo wakati zvidzidziswe vana vaJudha 'Uta'. Tarira zvakanyorwa mubhuku raJashari.
\p
\v	19	Runako rwaIsraeri rwaurawa panzvimbo dzako dzakakwirira! Vane simba vakawa sei!
\v	20	Musazvitaura paGati, musazviparidza panzira dzeAshikeroni; zvimwe vakunda vevaFirisitia vangafara, zvimwe vangapembera vakunda vevasina kudzingiswa.
\v	21	 Imwi makomo eGiribhoa, kurege kuva nedova kana mvura pamusoro penyu, kana minda yezvipo. Nokuti nhovo yemhare yakasemeswapo, nhovo yaSauro, isina kuzodzwa nemafuta.
\v	22	Kubva paropa revakaurawa, pamafuta evane simba, uta hwaJonatani hahuna kudzokera. Uye munondo waSauro hauna kudzokera usina chinhu.
\v	23	Sauro naJonatani vaiva vadikanwa uye vakanaka paupenyu hwavo; nepakufa kwavo havana kuparadzaniswa; vaikurumidza kupfuura makondo, vaiva nesimba kupfuura shumba.
\v	24	 Imwi vakunda vaIsraeri, chemerai Sauro, wakakufukidzai nezvitsvuku nemufaro, wakaisa zvishongo zvegoridhe pazvipfeko zvenyu.
\v	25	Vakawa sei vane simba pakati pehondo! Jonatani wakaurawa panzvimbo dzako dzakakwirira.
\v	26	Ndinoshungurudzika newe, munin'ina wangu Jonatani; wanga uchifadza kwazvo kwandiri; rudo rwako kwandiri rwaishamisa rwuchipfuura rudo rwevakadzi.
\v	27	Vane simba vakawa sei! Zvakaparara nezvombo zvehondo!
\c	10
\s	Dhavhidhi anokunda vaAmoni nevaSiriya
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kweizvi kuti mambo wevana vaAmoni wakafa; Hanuni mwanakomana wake ndokutonga pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	2	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakati: Ndichamuitira ngoni Hanuni mwanakomana waNahashi, baba vake sezvavakandiitira ngoni. Dhavhidhi ndokutuma neruoko rwevaranda vake kunomunyaradza pamusoro pababa vake. Varanda vaDhavhidhi ndokusvika munyika yevana vaAmoni.
\v	3	Zvinomachinda evana vaAmoni akati kuna Hanuni ishe wavo: Dhavhidhi anokudza baba venyu pameso enyu here zvaatuma vanyaradzi kwamuri? Dhavhidhi haana kutuma varanda vake kwamuri kunzvera guta nekurishora nekuriputsa here?
\v	4	NaizvozvoHanuni wakabata varanda vaDhavhidhi, akaveura hafu yendebvu dzavo, ndokugura nguvo dzavo nepakati kusvika kumagaro avo, ndokuvaendesa.
\v	5	PavakaudzaDhavhidhi, iye wakatuma kunovachingamidza, nokuti varume vainyara kwazvo. Mambo ndokuti: Garai muJeriko kusvika ndebvu dzenyu dzabuka, mugodzoka.
\p
\v	6	VanavaAmoni pavakaona kuti vanhuhwa pamberi paDhavhidhi, vana vaAmoni vakatuma ndokuhaya vaSiriya veBheti-Rehobhu, uye vaSiriya veZobha, varume vaifamba nemakumbo zvuru makumi maviri, namambo weMaaka, varume churu, nevanhu veTobhi, zvuru gumi nezviviri.
\v	7	ZvinoDhavhidhi a zvinzwa wakatuma Joabhu nehondo yose yevane simba.
\v	8	Zvinovana vaAmoni vakabuda, ndokurongedza hondo pamukova wesuwo; uye vaSiriya veZobha neveRehobhu nevanhu veTobhi neMaaka vaiva vega pasango.
\v	9	Joabhupaakaona kuti hondo yemberi yaiva yakatarisa kwaari mberi neshure, wakasarudza pane vose vakasarudzwa kwaIsraeri, ndokurongedza kurwisa vaSiriya.
\v	10	Uye vakasara pavanhu wakavapa muruoko rwaAbhishai munin'ina wake kuti avarongedzere kurwisa vana vaAmoni.
\v	11	Zvino wakati: Kana vaSiriya vakava nesimba kundipfuura, ipapo iwe uchava rubatsiro rwangu; asi kana vana vaAmoni vakava nesimba kukupfuura, ipapo ndichauya kukununura.
\v	12	Tsunga, uye ngatizvisimbise nekuda kwevanhu vekwedu uye nekuda kwemaguta aMwari wedu; zvino Jehovha ngaaite zvakanaka pameso ake.
\v	13	Zvino Joabhu wakaswedera, nevanhu vaiva naye, kuhondo kurwisana nevaSiriya; ivo ndokutiza pamberi pake.
\v	14	Vana vaAmoni pavakaona kuti vaSiriya vatiza, naivo vakatiza pamberi paAbhishai ndokupinda muguta. Joabhu ndokudzoka kubva kuvana vaAmoni, ndokuuya kuJerusarema.
\p
\v	15	Zvino vaSiriya vaona kuti varohwa pamberi paIsraeri, ndokuungana pamwe chete.
\v	16	Zvino Hadhadhezeri wakatuma  nhume ndokubudisa vaSiriya vaiva mhiri kwerwizi, ivo ndokusvika paHeramu, naShobhaki mukuru wehondo yaHadhadhezeri mberi kwavo.
\v	17	Pazvakaudzwa Dhavhidhi, wakaunganidza Israeri wose, ndokuyambuka Joridhani, ndokusvika paHeramu. VaSiriya ndokuzvirongedzera kurwisa Dhavhidhi, ndokurwa naye.
\v	18	Zvino vaSiriya vakatiza pamberi paIsraeri; Dhavhidhi ndokuuraya kuvaSiriya ngoro mazana manomwe, nevatasvi vemabhiza zvuru makumi mana; uye Shobhaki mukuru wehondo yavo waka murova, ndokufirapo.
\v	19	Madzimambo ose aiva varanda vaHadhadhezeri, paakaona kuti varohwa pamberi paIsraeri, vakaita ruyanano naIsraeri, ndokuvashandira. Naizvozvo vaSiriya vakatya kununurazve vana vaAmoni.
\c	11
\s	Dhavhidhi naBhati-Shebha
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika pakudzokorora kwegore, panguva madzimambo yaaibuda  kunorwa, kuti Dhavhidhi wakatuma Joabhu nevaranda vake vanaye, naIsraeri wese; ndokuparadza vana vaAmoni, ndokukomba Rabha. Asi Dhavhidhi wakagara paJerusarema.
\p
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika panguva yemadekwani, kuti Dhavhidhi wakamuka kubva pamubhedha wake, ndokufamba-famba pamusoro pedenga reimba yamambo. Zvino ari padenga reimba wakaona mukadzi achizvishambidza; zvino mukadzi waiva akanaka kwazvo pakuonekwa.
\v	3	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakatuma  nhume ndokubvunza nezvemukadzi uyo. Zvikanzi: Haasi Bhati-Shebha mukunda waEriami, mukadzi waUriya muHiti here?
\v	4	Dhavhidhindokutuma nhume ndokumutora; iye ndokuuya kwaari; iye ndokurara naye (nokuti waiva azvinatsa pakusachena kwake); iye ndokudzokera kumba kwake.
\v	5	Mukadzindokubata mimba, ndokutuma ndokuudza Dhavhidhi ndokuti: Ndava nemimba.
\p
\v	6	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakatuma  shoko kuna Joabhu,  achiti: Tuma Uriya muHiti kwandiri. Joabhu ndokutuma Uriya kuna Dhavhidhi.
\v	7	Uriyawakati asvika kwaari, Dhavhidhi ndokubvunza mamiriro aJoabhu, nemamiriro evanhu, nemamiriro ehondo.
\v	8	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakati kuna Uriya: Burukira kumba kwako ugonoshambidza tsoka dzako. Uriya ndokubuda paimba yamambo, chipo chamambo ndokubuda kumutevera.
\v	9	AsiUriya wakarara pamukova weimba yamambo pamwe nevaranda vose vatenzi wake; haana kuburukira kumba kwake.
\v	10	Vakati vaudza Dhavhidhi vachiti: Uriya haana kuburukira kumba kwake; Dhavhidhi ndokuti kuna Uriya: Hauna kubva parwendo here? Waregerei kuburukira kumba kwako?
\v	11	Uriya ndokuti kuna Dhavhidhi: Areka naIsraeri naJudha vagere mumatende, nashe wangu Joabhu nevaranda vashe wangu vakavaka musasa kusango; zvino ini ndingapinda mumba mangu kudya nekunwa nekurara nemukadzi wangu here?  Neupenyu hwenyu, uye  neupenyu hwemweya weupenyu wenyu, handingaiti chinhu ichi.
\v	12	Dhavhidhi ndokuti kuna Uriya: Gara pano nanhasi, uye mangwana ndichakuendesa. Naizvozvo Uriya wakagara paJerusarema nezuva iro uye nezuva raitevera.
\v	13	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakamudana, iye ndokudya nekunwa pamberi pake, zvino akamudhakisa; zvino madekwani wakabuda akanorara pamubhedha wake pamwe nevaranda vashe wake, asi haana kuburukira kumba kwake.
\p
\v	14	Zvino zvakaitika mangwanani kuti Dhavhidhi wakanyora tsamba kuna Joabhu ndokutuma neruoko rwaUriya.
\v	15	Zvino wakanyora mutsamba achiti: Isai Uriya pamberi akatarisana nehondo painopisisa mugodzokera shure kubva kwaari kuti arohwe afe.
\v	16	Zvino zvakaitika Joabhu paaicherekedza guta, kuti wakamisa Uriya panzvimbo paaiziva kuti varume veumhare varipo.
\v	17	Zvino varume veguta vakabuda ndokurwa naJoabhu. Zvino kwakawa pavanhu pavaranda vaDhavhidhi; kukafawo Uriya muHiti.
\p
\v	18	Zvino Joabhu wakatuma  nhume ndokuudza Dhavhidhi mashoko ose ehondo.
\v	19	Zvino wakaraira nhume achiti: Paunenge wapedza kupira mambo mashoko ose ehondo,
\v	20	zvino zvikadai kuti hasha dzamambo dzikapfuta, uye iye akati kwauri: Makaswedererei kuguta kurwa? Hamuna kuziva here kuti vachafura vari parusvingo?
\v	21	Ndiani wakarova Abhimereki mwanakomana waJerubhesheti? Handiti mukadzi wakakanda huyo pamusoro pake ari parusvingo, kusvika afa paTebhezi? Makaswedererei kurusvingo? Zvino uchati: Muranda wenyu Uriya muHiti wafawo.
\p
\v	22	Zvino nhume yakaenda, ndokusvika, ndokuudza Dhavhidhi zvose Joabhu zvaakamutuma.
\v	23	Zvino nhume yakati kuna Dhavhidhi: Zvirokwazvo vanhu vatikurira, uye vatibudira kusango, asi tanga tiri pamusoro pavo kusvika pamukova wesuwo.
\v	24	Zvino vafuri vari parusvingo vakafura varanda venyu, uye  vamwe vevaranda vamambo vafa, uye muranda wenyu Uriya muHiti wafawo.
\v	25	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakati kunhume: Reva seizvi kuna Joabhu: Chinhu ichi ngachirege kuva chakaipa pameso ako, nokuti munondo unopedza wakafanana neuyu uye wakafanana neuyo; simbisa hondo yako kurwisa guta, uriputse; uye umusimbise.
\p
\v	26	Mukadzi waUriya paakanzwa kuti murume wake Uriya wafa, wakachema murume wake.
\v	27	Kuchema kwakati kwapera, Dhavhidhi wakatuma  nhume ndokumutora kuisa mumba make; iye ndokuva mukadzi wake, akamuberekera mwanakomana. Asi chinhu ichi Dhavhidhi chaakaita chaiva chakaipa pameso aJehovha.
\c	12
\s	Dhavhidhi anochema zvivi zvake
\p
\v	1	ZvinoJehovha wakatuma Natani kuna Dhavhidhi. Iye ndokusvika kwaari, ndokuti kwaari: Kwaiva nevarume vaviri muguta rimwe; umwe akafuma, asi umwe murombo.
\v	2	Mufumiwaiva nemakwai nemombe zhinji kwazvo.
\v	3	Asimurombo waiva asina chinhu zvachose kunze kwegwayana rimwe resheche raaiva atenga, ndokurirera; iro ndokukura kwaari uye pamwe nevana vake; rakadya pamusuva wake, nekunwa pamukombe wake, nekurara pachifuva chake, uye rakava semukunda kwaari.
\v	4	Mufambiwakati achisvika kumufumi, iye akarega kutora kumakwai ake kana pamombe dzake, kugadzirira mupfuuri waiva asvika kwaari; asi wakatora gwayana remurombo ndokurigadzirira munhu waiva asvika kwaari.
\v	5	Zvinokutsamwa kwaDhavhidhi kwakapfutira munhu uyu kwazvo, ndokuti kuna Natani:  NaJehovha mupenyu, munhu wakaita izvi mwanakomana werufu.
\v	6	Uyeacharipira gwayana nemana, nekuda kwekuti wakaita chinhu ichi, uye nekuda kwekuti haana kuva netsitsi.
\p
\v	7	ZvinoNatani wakati kuna Dhavhidhi: Ndiwe munhu uyo! Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha Mwari pamusoro paIsraeri: Ini ndakakuzodza kuva mambo pamusoro paIsraeri, uye ini ndakakununura paruoko rwaSauro.
\v	8	Zvinondakakupa imba yatenzi vako, nevakadzi vatenzi vako pachipfuva chako, uye ndakakupa imba yaIsraeri neyaJudha; uye dai zvaiva zvidiki ndaikuwedzera zvakadai nezvakadai.
\v	9	Wazvidzireishoko raJehovha nokuita zvakaipa pameso ake? Wauraya Uriya muHiti nemunondo, uye watora mukadzi wake kuva mukadzi wako, naiye wamuuraya nemunondo wevana vaAmoni.
\v	10	Zvino ikozvino munondo haungabvi paimba yako kusvika rinhi narinhi; nokuti wakandizvidza, ndokutora mukadzi waUriya muHiti kuti ave mukadzi wako.
\v	11	Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Tarira, ndichakumutsira zvakaipa kubva mumba mako, uye ndichatora vakadzi vako pameso ako ndigopa muvakidzani wako, uye acharara nevakadzi vako pameso ezuva iri.
\v	12	Nokuti iwe waka zviita pachivande, asi ini ndichaita chinhu ichi pamberi paIsraeri wose, uye pamberi pezuva.
\v	13	Zvino Dhavhidhi akati kuna Natani: Ndatadzira Jehovha. Natani ndokuti kuna Dhavhidhi: Jehovha wabvisawo chivi chako; haungafi.
\v	14	Zvakadaro, nokuti waita kuti vavengi vaJehovha vazvidze zvekuzvidza nechinhu ichi, mwanakomanawo wawaberekerwa achafa zvekufa.
\v	15	Zvino Natani wakaenda kumba kwake. Jehovha ndokurova mwana mukadzi waUriya waakaberekera Dhavhidhi, akarwara kwazvo.
\v	16	Naizvozvo Dhavhidhi wakakumbira mwana kuna Mwari; Dhavhidhi wakatsanya mutsanyo, ndokupinda, ndokurara pavhu usiku hwose.
\v	17	Zvino vakuru veimba yake vakasimuka ndokuenda kwaari kumumutsa pavhu; asi haana kuda, uye haana kudya zvekudya navo.
\v	18	Zvino zvakaitika pazuva rechinomwe kuti mwana wakafa. Zvino varanda vaDhavhidhi vakatya kumuudza kuti mwana wafa; nokuti vakati: Tarirai, mwana achiri mupenyu, takataura kwaari, asi haana kuteerera kuinzwi redu; zvino tichamuudza sei kuti mwana wafa; anga zvikuvadza.
\v	19	Dhavhidhi paakaona kuti varanda vake vanozevezerana, Dhavhidhi wakaziva kuti mwana wafa; naizvozvo Dhavhidhi wakati kuvaranda vake: Mwana wafa here? Ivo ndokuti: Wafa.
\v	20	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakasimuka kubva pavhu, ndokushamba, ndokuzora  mafuta, ndokuchinja nguvo yake, ndokupinda muimba yaJehovha, ndokushumira; ndokuenda kumba kwake, ndokukumbira; ivo ndokuisa zvekudya pamberi pake, iye ndokudya.
\v	21	Varanda vake ndokuti kwaari: Chii chinhu ichi chamaita? Nekuda kwemwana mupenyu makatsanya uye makachema, asi mwana afa masimuka mukadya zvekudya.
\v	22	Iye ndokuti: Mwana achiri mupenyu ndakatsanya ndokuchema, nokuti ndakati: Ndiani anoziva, Jehovha angandinzwira tsitsi, mwana ndokurarama.
\v	23	Asi ikozvino wafa, ndichatsanyirei? Ndinogona kumudzosazve here? Ndichaenda kwaari, asi iye haangadzoki kwandiri.
\p
\v	24	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakanyaradza Bhati-Shebha mukadzi wake, ndokupinda kwaari, ndokurara naye; iye ndokubereka mwanakomana, iye ndokutumidza zita rake Soromoni. Jehovha ndokumuda.
\v	25	Zvino wakatuma neruoko rwaNatani muporofita, ndokutumidza zita rake Jedhidhia, nekuda kwaJehovha.
\p
\v	26	Zvino Joabhu wakarwisana neRabha revana vaAmoni, ndokutapa guta ramambo.
\v	27	Joabhu ndokutuma nhume kuna Dhavhidhi, ndokuti: Ndarwisa Rabha, uye ndatapa guta remvura.
\v	28	Zvino ikozvino, unganidzai vakasara vevanhu, mudzike musasa pakatarisana neguta, mugoritapa; zvimwe ini ndingatapa guta, zita rangu rigodanwa pamusoro paro.
\v	29	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakaunganidza vanhu vose, ndokuenda kuRabha, ndokurirwisa, ndokuritapa.
\v	30	Zvino wakatora korona yamambo wavo kubva mumusoro wake; uye kurema kwayo kwaiva tarenda regoridhe neibwe rinokosha. Zvino yakava mumusoro waDhavhidhi. Wakabudisa zvakapambiwa zveguta zvizhinji kwazvo.
\v	31	Wakabudisawo vanhu vaivamo ndokuisa pasi pemasaha, nemidziyo yekuchekesa yematare, nemasanhu ematare, ndokuvapfuudza mumatope ezvitinha. Zvino wakaita saizvozvo nemaguta ose evana vaAmoni. Dhavhidhi nevanhu vose vakadzokera kuJerusarema.
\c	13
\s	Kutadza kwaAmunoni
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kweizvi kuti Abhusaromu mwanakomana waDhavhidhi waiva ane hanzvadzi yakanaka, zita rake raiva Tamari. Zvino Amunoni mwanakomana waDhavhidhi wakamuda.
\v	2	ZvinoAmunoni wakatambudzika kusvika arwara nekuda kwaTamari hanzvadzi yake; nokuti waiva ari mhandara, uye zvairema mumeso aAmunoni kumuitira chinhu.
\v	3	AsiAmunoni waiva ane shamwari, zita rayo raiva Jonadhabhu mwanakomana waShimea mukoma waDhavhidhi. Zvino Jonadhabhu waiva ari munhu waiva nemano kwazvo.
\v	4	Iyendokuti kwaari: Sei iwe, mwanakomana wamambo, uchionda zvakadai mangwanani nemangwanani? Haundiudzi here? Amunoni ndokuti kwaari: Ndinoda Tamari, hanzvadzi yemunin'ina wangu Abhusaromu.
\v	5	Jonadhabhundokuti kwaari: Rara pamubhedha wako, ugozviita murwere; zvino baba vako pavanosvika kuzokuona, uti kwavari: Regai henyu hanzvadzi yangu Tamari auye andipe zvekudya kuti ndidye, uye agadzire zvekudya pamberi pemeso angu, kuti ndione ndigodya paruoko rwake.
\p
\v	6	Amunonindokurara pasi, akazviita murwere. Zvino mambo wakati asvika kumuona, Amunoni wakati kuna mambo: Regai henyu Tamari, hanzvadzi yangu auye agoita makeke maviri pamberi pemeso angu, kuti ndidye paruoko rwake.
\v	7	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakatuma  shoko kumusha kuna Tamari achiti: Enda ikozvino kumba kwaAmunoni hanzvadzi yako ugomugadzirira zvekudya.
\v	8	NaizvozvoTamari wakaenda kumba kwehanzvadzi yake Amunoni, iye waiva arere pasi. Zvino wakatora mukanyiwa ndokuukanya, ndokuita makeke pamberi pemeso ake, ndokubika makeke.
\p
\v	9	Zvinowakatora gango ndokudurura pamberi pake; asi iye wakaramba kudya. Amunoni ndokuti: Budisa vanhu vose kwandiri. Zvino vakabuda, munhu wose kubva kwaari.

\v	31	NaBharizirai muGiriyadhi waiva aburuka kubva kuRogerimi ndokuyambuka Joridhani namambo, kumuperekedza mhiri kwaJoridhani.
\v	32	Zvino Bharizirai waiva mutana kwazvo ane makore makumi masere; uye iye waiva araramisa mambo paaigara paMahanayimu; nokuti waiva munhu mukuru kwazvo.
\v	33	Mambo ndokuti kuna Bharizirai: Iwe yambuka pamwe neni, uye ndichakuraramisa pamwe neni paJerusarema.
\v	34	Bharizirai ndokuti kuna mambo: Mangani mazuva emakore eupenyu hwangu kuti ndikwire pamwe namambo kuJerusarema?
\v	35	Nhasi ndiri wemakore makumi masere. Ndingaparadzanisa chakanaka nechakaipa here? Varanda venyu vangaravira zvandinodya here nezvandinonwa? Ndichiri kunzwa inzwi revarume vanoimba nerevakadzi vanoimba here? Muranda wenyu achanoitirei mutoro kuna ishe wangu mambo?
\v	36	Muranda wenyu achayambuka zvishoma Joridhani pamwe namambo; zvino mambo angandiripira nemubairo wakadai sei?
\v	37	Regai henyu muranda wenyu adzokere, kuti ndinofira muguta rangu, paguva rababa vangu neramai vangu. Asi tarirai, muranda wenyu Chimuhamu, ngaayambuke nashe wangu mambo, mugomuitira zvakanaka pameso enyu.
\v	38	Mambo ndokuti: Chimuhamu achayambuka neni, uye ini ndichamuitira zvakanaka pameso ako; uye zvose zvaunotsvaka kwandiri ndichakuitira izvo.
\v	39	Zvino vanhu vose vakayambuka Joridhani, mambo ayambukawo; mambo wakatsvoda Bharizirai ndokumuropafadza, iye ndokudzokera kunzvimbo yake.
\v	40	Naizvozvo mambo wakapfuurira kuGirigari, Chimuhamu ndokupfuurira naye, nevanhu vose vaJudha vakapfuudzira mberi mambo, uyewo hafu yerudzi rwaIsraeri.
\p
\v	41	Zvino tarira varume vose vaIsraeri vakauya kuna mambo, ndokuti kuna mambo: Hama dzedu varume vaJudha vakakubai nemhaka yei, vakayambusa mambo neimba yake Joridhani, nevarume vose vaDhavhidhi pamwe naye?
\v	42	Zvino varume vose vaJudha vakapindura varume vaIsraeri  ndokuti: Nokuti mambo ihama yedu yepedo; uye matsamwirei nenyaya iyi? Takatongodya zvamambo here? Kana wakatipa chipo here?
\v	43	Varume vaIsraeri ndokupindura varume vaJudha ndokuti: Tine zvikamu gumi kuna mambo, uyezve tine zvizhinji kuna Dhavhidhi kupfuura imwi; zvino makatizvidzirei kuti inzwi redu risava rekutanga pakudzosa mambo wedu? Asi shoko revarume vaJudha raiva rakaoma kupfuura shoko revarume vaIsraeri.
\c	2
\s	Dhavhidhi anoitwa mambo waJudha, naIshibhosheti mambo waIsraeri
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika shure kweizvi, kuti Dhavhidhi wakabvunza Jehovha achiti: Ndingakwira kune rimwe remaguta aJudha here? Jehovha ndokuti kwaari: Kwira. Dhavhidhi ndokuti: Ndikwire kupi? Iye ndokuti: KuHebhuroni.
\v	2	NaizvozvoDhavhidhi wakakwirako, nevakadzi vake vaviriwo, Ahinowamu mukadzi muJezireeri, naAbhigairi mukadzi waNabhari muKarimeri.
\v	3	Dhavhidhindokukwidzawo varume vake vaiva naye, umwe neumwe neveimba yake, ndokugara pamaguta eHebhuroni.
\v	4	Zvinovarume vaJudha vakauya ndokuzodzapo Dhavhidhi kuva mambo pamusoro peimba yaJudha. Zvino vakaudza Dhavhidhi vachiti: Varume veJabheshi-Giriyadhi ndivo vakaviga Sauro.
\p
\v	5	Dhavhidhindokutuma nhume kuvarume veJabheshi-Giriyadhi, ndokuti kwavari: Muropafadzwe naJehovha, zvamakaitira ishe wenyu Sauro tsitsi idzi, mukamuviga.
\v	6	Zvinoikozvino Jehovha ngaakuitirei tsitsi nechokwadi. Ini neni ndichakuitirai zvakanaka izvi, nokuti makaita chinhu ichi.
\v	7	Zvinoikozvino maoko edu ngaasimbe, uye muve veumhare; nokuti Sauro tenzi wenyu wafa, uyewo veimba yaJudha vandizodza kuva mambo pamusoro pavo.
\p
\v	8	AsiAbhineri mwanakomana waNeri, mukuru wehondo waiva waSauro, wakatora Ishibhosheti mwanakomana waSauro ndokumuyambusira kuMahanayimu;
\v	9	ndokumuitamambo pamusoro peGiriyadhi nepamusoro pevaAshuri nepamusoro peJezireeri nepamusoro paEfuremu nepamusoro paBhenjamini nepamusoro paIsraeri wose.
\v	10	Ishibhosheti mwanakomana waSauro waiva mwanakomana wemakore makumi mana paakava mambo pamusoro paIsraeri, ndokutonga makore maviri. Asi veimba yaJudha vakatevera Dhavhidhi.
\v	11	Zvino uwandu hwemazuva Dhavhidhi hwaaiva mambo muHebhuroni pamusoro peimba yaJudha hwaiva makore manomwe nemwedzi mitanhatu.
\p
\v	12	Zvino Abhineri mwanakomana waNeri wakabuda, nevaranda vaIshibhosheti mwanakomana waSauro, kubva Mahanayimu kuenda kuGibhiyoni.
\v	13	NaJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya nevaranda vaDhavhidhi vakabuda, ndokusangana pamwe navo padziva reGibhiyoni. Zvino vakagara, ava kuno kwedziva, neavo uko kwedziva.
\v	14	Abhineri ndokuti kuna Joabhu: Majaya ngaasimuke hawo uye atambe pamberi pedu. Joabhu ndokuti: Ngaasimuke.
\v	15	Zvino kwakasimuka ndokupfuura pauwandu gumi nevaviri kwaBhenjamini nekwaIshibhosheti mwanakomana waSauro, uye gumi nevaviri vevaranda vaDhavhidhi.
\v	16	Zvino vakabata umwe neumwe musoro weumwe uye munondo wake  ndokubaya parutivi rweumwe; naizvozvo vakawira pasi pamwe. Naizvozvo nzvimbo iyo yakatumidzwa Herikati-Haruzimi, iri paGibhiyoni.
\v	17	Zvino kwakava nehondo inorwadza zvikuru zuva iro. Abhineri nevarume vaIsraeri ndokurohwa pamberi pevaranda vaDhavhidhi.
\p
\v	18	Zvino kwaiva nevanakomana vatatu vaZeruya ipapo: Joabhu naAbhishai naAsaheri; uye Asaheri waiva mhepo seimwe yechengu iri musango.
\v	19	Zvino Asaheri wakadzingirira shure Abhineri; zvino pakufamba iye haana kutsaukira kurudyi kana kuruboshwe pakuteverera Abhineri.
\v	20	Zvino Abhineri wakacheuka ndokuti: Ndiwe Asaheri here? Iye ndokuti: Ndini.
\v	21	Zvino Abhineri wakati kwaari: Tsaukira iwe kurudyi rwako kana kuruboshwe rwako, uzvibatire rimwe remajaya, ugozvitorera zvombo zvaro. Asi Asaheri haana kuda kutsauka pakumuteverera.
\v	22	Abhineri ndokutizve zvekare kuna Asaheri: Tsauka iwe pakunditeverera; ndichakurovererei muvhu? Uye ndichasimudzira sei chiso changu kuna Joabhu mukoma wako?
\v	23	Asi wakaramba kutsauka; naizvozvo Abhineri wakamurova neshure kwepfumo mudumbu, kusvika pfumo rabuda neseri kwake; zvino akawira pasi ipapo, ndokufira panzvimbo iyo. Zvino zvakaitika  kuti vose vakasvika panzvimbo paiva pawira Asaheri akafa, vakamira.
\v	24	Asi Joabhu naAbhishai vakadzingirira shure Abhineri, zuva ndokuvira ivo vachisvika kuchikomo cheAma, chiri pamberi peGiya panzira yerenje reGibhiyoni.
\p
\v	25	Zvino vana vaBhenjamini vakaungana shure kwaAbhineri ndokuva boka rimwe, ndokumira panhongonya yechikomo.
\v	26	Zvino Abhineri wakadanidzira kuna Joabhu ndokuti: Munondo uchadya rinhi narinhi here? Hauzivi here kuti kuchava kuvava pakupedzisira? Kusvika rinhi usingavaudzi vanhu kuti vadzoke pakuteverera hama dzavo?
\v	27	Joabhu ndokuti:  NaMwari mupenyu, kunze kwekuti dai wataura, zvirokwazvo zvino kubva mangwanani vanhu vangadai vakwira umwe neumwe achibva pakuteverera hama yake.
\v	28	Naizvozvo Joabhu wakaridza hwamanda, vanhu vose ndokumira, ndokurega kuzodzingirirazve shure Israeri, ndokurega kurwazve zvekare.
\v	29	Zvino Abhineri nevarume vake vakafamba vachigura nemumapani usiku uhwo hwose, ndokuyambuka Joridhani, ndokugura neBhitironi yose, ndokusvika paMahanayimu.
\v	30	Zvino Joabhu wakadzoka pakuteverera Abhineri; paaiva aunganidza vanhu vose, kwakashayikwa pavaranda vaDhavhidhi varume gumi nevapfumbamwe naAsaheri.
\v	31	Asi varanda vaDhavhidhi vaiva vakarova varume mazana matatu nemakumi matanhatu vevaBhenjamini nevevarume vaAbhineri vakafa.
\v	32	Zvino vakasimudza Asaheri, ndokumuviga muguva rababa vake raiva paBheterehemu. Joabhu nevarume vake ndokufamba usiku hwose, ndokuedza vava paHebhuroni.
\c	20
\s	Shebha anomukira Dhavhidhi
\p
\v	1	Zvinozvakaitika ipapo kuti kuvepo munhu waBheriari, zita rake raiva Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri, muBhenjamini. Zvino wakaridza hwamanda ndokuti: Hatina mugove kuna Dhavhidhi, uye hatina nhaka kumwanakomana waJese; munhu umwe neumwe kutende rake, Israeri.
\v	2	Naizvozvomunhu wose wekwaIsraeri wakakwira kubva shure kwaDhavhidhi kutevera Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri; asi vanhu vekwaJudha vakanamatira kuna mambo wavo, kubva paJoridhani kusvika paJerusarema.
\p
\v	3	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakasvika kumba kwake paJerusarema; mambo ndokutora vakadzi gumi, varongo vaaiva asiya kuti varinde imba, ndokuvaisa muimba yakachengetwa, ndokuvariritira, asi haana kupinda kwavari. Naizvozvo vaiva vakapfigirwa kusvika pazuva rekufa kwavo, vachirarama pauchirikadzi.
\v	4	Zvinomambo wakati kuna Amasa: Ndidanire varume vaJudha  mumazuva matatu; newe uvepo pano.
\v	5	Amasandokuenda kunodanira pamwe Judha, asi wakanonoka kudarika nguva yakatarwa yaaiva amutarira iye.
\v	6	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakati kuna Abhishai: Ikozvino Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri achatiitira zvakaipa kupfuura Abhusaromu. Iwe tora varanda vashe wako, umudzingirire shure, zvimwe angazviwanira maguta akashoshewa, ndokuzvinunura  kubva pameso edu.
\v	7	Zvinokwakabuda shure kwake varume vaJoabhu, nevaKereti, nevaPereti, nevarume vose veumhare, ndokubuda muJerusarema kudzingirira shure Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri.
\v	8	Vakativava pabwe guru raiva paGibhiyoni, Amasa wakapinda pamberi pavo. Zvino Joabhu waiva akazvisunga chiuno nenguvo yaaiva akapfeka, uye pamusoro payo paiva nebhanhire  rine munondo wakasungirwa pachiuno chake uri mumuhara mawo; zvino ari kuenderera mberi,  munondo wakawa.
\v	9	Joabhundokuti kuna Amasa: Wakasimba here, munin'ina wangu? Joabhu ndokubata Amasa nechirebvu neruoko rwerudyi kuti amutsvode.
\v	10	Asi Amasa haana kucherekedza munondo waiva muruoko rwaJoabhu; naizvozvo wakamurova nawo mudumbu, ndokutumburira ura hwake pavhu, akasamudzokorora, ndokufa. Zvino Joabhu nemunin'ina wake Abhishai ndokudzingirira shure Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri.
\v	11	Zvino murume wemajaya aJoabhu waiva amirepo naye, ndokuti: Anoda Joabhu, neari waDhavhidhi, tevera Joabhu.
\v	12	Asi Amasa wakaumburuka muropa pakati penzira huru. Murume uyu paakaona kuti vanhu vose vakamira, wakabvisa Amasa panzira huru  ndokumuisa musango, ndokukanda nguvo pamusoro pake, paakaona kuti wose anosvika kwaari wakamira.
\v	13	Paakabviswa kubva panzira huru, vanhu vose vakapfuura vakatevera Joabhu, kudzingirira shure Shebha mwanakomana waBhikiri.
\p
\v	14	Zvino wakapfuura nemumarudzi ose aIsraeri kusvika paAbheri nekuBheti-Maaka, nevaBheri vese; ndokuungana naivo ndokumutevera.
\v	15	Zvino vakasvika ndokumukomba paAbheri reBheti-Maaka, vakatutira churu chevhu pakatarisana neguta, ndokumira panhare; vanhu vose vaiva naJoabhu vakakanganisa rusvingo kuti varuputsire pasi.
\p
\v	16	Zvino kwakadanidzira mukadzi wakachenjera ari muguta,  akati: Inzwai, inzwai, itii henyu kuna Joabhu: Swedera pano, kuti nditaure newe.
\v	17	Zvino wakaswedera kwaari. Mukadzi ndokuti: Ndiwe Joabhu here? Iye ndokuti: Ndini. Iye ndokuti kwaari: Inzwai mashoko emurandakadzi wenyu. Iye ndokuti: Ndinonzwa.
\v	18	Zvino wakataura achiti: Kare vaiva vakajaira kutaura vachiti: Ngavabvunzisise muAbheri. Vakapedza saizvozvo.
\v	19	Ndiri weverugare, wevakavimbika kwaIsraeri; munotsvaka kuparadza guta uye amai kwaIsraeri; unomedzerei nhaka yaJehovha?
\v	20	Joabhu ndokupindura ndokuti: Ngazvive kure, ngazvive kure neni kuti ndimedze kana kuparadza.
\v	21	Nyaya haina kudaro; asi munhu wegomo raEfuremu, ndiShebha mwanakomana waBhikiri zita rake, wakasimudzira mambo ruoko rwake, kurwisa Dhavhidhi; mupei iye ega, zvino ndichabva paguta. Mukadzi ndokuti kuna Joabhu: Tarirai, musoro wake uchakandirwa nepamusoro perusvingo.
\v	22	Zvino mukadzi wakaenda muuchenjeri hwake kuvanhu vose. Ivo ndokugura musoro waShebha mwanakomana waBhikiri, ndokuukandira kuna Joabhu. Iye ndokuridza hwamanda, zvino vakapararira kubva muguta, umwe neumwe kuenda kutende rake. Joabhu ndokudzokera kuJerusarema kuna mambo.
\p
\v	23	Zvino Joabhu waiva pamusoro pehondo yose yaIsraeri; naBhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha waiva pamusoro peKereti nevaPereti;
\v	24	naAdhoramu waiva pamusoro pechibharo; naJehoshafati mwanakomana waAhirudhi waiva munyori wenhoroondo;
\v	25	uye Sheva waiva munyori; naZadhoki naAbhiatari vaiva vapristi;
\v	26	uye naIra muJairi waiva mupristi waDhavhidhi.
\c	21
\s	Nzara pakati paIsraeri nekuguma kwayo
\p
\v	1	Zvinokwakava nenzara pamazuva aDhavhidhi makore matatu, gore negore achiteverana; Dhavhidhi ndokutsvaka chiso chaJehovha. Jehovha ndokuti: Imhaka yaSauro nemhaka yeimba yeropa, nokuti akauraya VaGibhiyoni.
\v	2	Mambondokudana VaGibhiyoni, ndokuti kwavari: (Zvino VaGibhiyoni vaiva vasiri vevana vaIsraeri, asi vevasiiwa vevaAmori, uye vana vaIsraeri vaiva vapika kwavari; asi Sauro wakatsvaka kuvauraya neshungu dzake pavana vaIsraeri naJudha).
\v	3	NaizvozvoDhavhidhi wakati kuvaGibhiyoni: Ndichakuitirai chii? Uye ndichaita chiyananiso nei, kuti muropafadze nhaka yaJehovha?
\v	4	VaGibhiyonindokuti kwaari: Hatichina sirivha kana goridhe pana Sauro nepaimba yake; uye hazvisi kwatiri kuuraya munhu kwaIsraeri. Iye ndokuti: Chamunoreva ndichakuitirai icho.
\v	5	Ivondokuti kuna mambo: Munhu wakatipedza uye wakatirongera kuti tiparadzwe, tirege kugara pamuganhu upi neupi waIsraeri,
\v	6	varumevanomwe vevanakomana vake ngavapiwe kwatiri. Zvino tichavaremberedzera Jehovha paGibhiya raSauro, musarudzwa waJehovha. Mambo ndokuti: Ini ndicha kupa i.
\v	7	Asimambo wakarega Mefibhosheti mwanakomana waJonatani, mwanakomana waSauro, nekuda kwemhiko yaJehovha yaiva pakati pavo, pakati paDhavhidhi naJonatani mwanakomana waSauro.
\v	8	Asimambo wakatora vanakomana vaviri vaRizipa, mukunda waAya, vaaiva aberekera Sauro, Arimoni naMefibhosheti, nevanakomana vashanu vaMikari mukunda waSauro, vaakaberekera Adhirieri mwanakomana waBharizirai muMehorati;
\v	9	ndokuvapamuruoko rwevaGibhiyoni; ivo ndokuvaremberedza mugomo pamberi paJehovha; vanomwe ndokuwa pamwe chete. Uye vakaurawa pamazuva ekukohwa, ekutanga, pakutanga kwekukohwa bhari.
\p
\v	10	Zvino Rizipa mukunda waAya wakatora saga, ndokuzviwarira iro pamusoro pedombo, kubva pakutanga kwekukohwa kusvika mvura yadonhera pamusoro pavo kubva kumatenga; haana kutendera shiri dzekumatenga kuti dzimhare pamusoro pavo masikati, kana zvikara zvesango usiku.
\v	11	Zvino zvakaudzwa Dhavhidhi izvo Rizipa mukunda waAya murongo waSauro zvaakaita.
\p
\v	12	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakaenda akatora mapfupa aSauro nemapfupa aJonatani mwanakomana wake kuvagari veJabheshi-Giriyadhi, vaiva vaaba kubva panzira yeBheti-Shani, vaFirisitia pavaiva vavaremberedza, pazuva revaFirisitia vachirova Sauro paGiribhoa.
\v	13	Zvino wakakwidza kubvapo mapfupa aSauro nemapfupa aJonatani mwanakomana wake, uye vakaunganidza mapfupa evakaremberedzwa.
\v	14	Zvino mapfupa aSauro neaJonatani mwanakomana wake vakaaviga munyika yaBhenjamini paZera, paguva raKishi baba vake. Vakaita zvose mambo zvaakaraira. Zvino shure kweizvozvo Mwari wakanyengetedzeka nekuda kwenyika.
\s	Kurwa kwevaIsraeri nevaFirisitia
\p
\v	15	Zvino vaFirisitia vakavazve nehondo naIsraeri. Dhavhidhi ndokuburuka nevaranda vake vanaye, zvino vakarwa nevaFirisitia; Dhavhidhi ndokuneta.
\v	16	Zvino Ishibhi-Bhenobhi, waiva wevanakomana vemurume ane simba, kurema kwepfumo rake kwaiva  mashekeri mazana matatu endarira pakurema, iye waiva akasungira muchiuno  munondo mutsva, akati acharova Dhavhidhi.
\v	17	Asi Abhishai mwanakomana waZeruya wakamubatsira, ndokurova muFirisitia, ndokumuuraya. Zvino varume vaDhavhidhi vakapika kwaari, vachiti: Hamuchabudizve nesu kuhondo, zvimwe mungadzima mwenje waIsraeri.
\v	18	Zvino zvakaitika shure kweizvi kuti kwakavazve nehondo nevaFirisitia paGobhi. Ipapo Sibhekai muHushati wakarova Safi, waiva wevanakomana vemurume ane simba.
\v	19	Zvino kwakavazve nehondo paGobhi nevaFirisitia; Erihanani mwanakomana waJaari-Oregimi weBheterehemu paakarova  mukoma waGoriati muGiti; uye rwiriko rwepfumo rake rwaiva rwakaita sedanda remuruki.
\v	20	Zvino kwakavazve nehondo paGati, paiva nemunhu murefu waiva neminwe mitanhatu pamaoko ake, nezvigunwe zvitanhatu patsoka dzake, makumi maviri nezvina pauwandu; naiyewo waiva akaberekerwa murume ane simba.
\v	21	Paakazvidza Israeri, Jonatani mwanakomana waShimea munin'ina waDhavhidhi wakamuuraya.
\v	22	Ava vana vaiva vaberekerwa murume ane simba paGati; zvino vakawa neruoko rwaDhavhidhi uye neruoko rwevaranda vake.
\c	22
\s	Rwiyo rwaDhavhidhi
\p
\v	1	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakataura kuna Jehovha mashoko erwiyo urwu nezuva Jehovha raakamusunungura paruoko rwevavengi vake vose nemuruoko rwaSauro.
\v	2	Ndokuti:Jehovha idombo rangu, nenhare yangu, nemununuri wangu kwandiri,
\v	3	Mwariwedombo rangu, ndichatizira kwaari; chidzitiro changu, nerunyanga rweruponeso rwangu, shongwe yangu yakakwirira, neutiziro hwangu. Muponesi wangu, makandiponesa pakumanikidzwa.
\v	4	Ndichadanakuna Jehovha, wakafanira kurumbidzwa; naizvozvo ndichaponeswa kubva kuvavengi vangu.
\p
\v	5	Nokutimafungu erufu akandikomba, mafashame ezvakaipa akandivhundusa.
\v	6	MaboteeSheori akandikomberedza, misungo yerufu yaiva mberi kwangu.
\v	7	Pakutambudzikakwangu ndakadana kuna Jehovha, uye ndakadana kuna Mwari wangu; iye ndokunzwa inzwi rangu ari mutembere yake, uye kuchema kwangu kwakava munzeve dzake.
\v	8	Ipaponyika yakadengenyeka ndokudedera; nheyo dzekumatenga dzakadengenyeka, ndokuzununguka, nokuti iye waiva atsamwa.
\v	9	Kwakakwirautsi kubva mumhino dzake, uye moto wakaparadza kubva mumuromo make; mazimbe akapfutidzwa nawo.
\v	10	Wakakotamisawo matenga, ndokuburuka; nerima gobvu raiva pasi petsoka dzake.
\v	11	Zvino wakatasva kerubhi ndokubhururuka; akaonekwawo ari pamapapiro emhepo.
\v	12	Zvino wakamisa rima kuva dumba mativi ake ose, muunganidzwa wemvura, makore makobvu emuchadenga.
\v	13	Mukupenya pamberi pake kwakapfuta mazimbe emoto.
\v	14	Jehovha wakatinhira ari kumatenga, uye Wekumusoro-soro wakabudisa inzwi rake.
\v	15	Zvino wakatuma miseve, ndokuvaparadza; mheni, ndokuvavhiringidza.
\v	16	Zvino migero yegungwa yakaonekwa, nheyo dzenyika dzakaratidzwa pachena, pakutsiura kwaJehovha, pakudhuuka kwemweya wemhino dzake.

\c	9
\s	Dhavhidhi anochengeta Mefibhosheti mwanakomana waSauro
\p
\v	1	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakati: Kuchine umwe wakasara weimba yaSauro kuti ndimuitire tsitsi nekuda kwaJonatani here?
\v	2	Zvinoimba yaSauro yaiva nemuranda wezita raiva Zibha. Pavaiva vamudanira kuna Dhavhidhi, mambo ndokuti kwaari: Ndiwe Zibha here? Iye ndokuti: Muranda wenyu  ndiye.
\v	3	Zvinomambo wakati: Hakuchina munhu here weimba yaSauro, wandingaitira tsitsi dzaMwari? Zibha ndokuti kuna mambo: Kuchine mwanakomana waJonatani akaremara tsoka dziri mbiri.
\v	4	Mambondokuti kwaari: Aripi? Zibha ndokuti kuna mambo: Tarirai, ari mumba maMakiri mwanakomana waAmieri kuRhodhebhari.
\p
\v	5	Zvinomambo Dhavhidhi wakatuma  nhume akandomutora kubva mumba maMakiri mwanakomana waAmieri kuRhodhebhari.
\v	6	Mefibhoshetimwanakomana waJonatani mwanakomana waSauro paakasvika kuna Dhavhidhi wakawa nechiso chake, ndoku mukotamira. Dhavhidhi ndokuti: Mefibhosheti! Iye ndokuti: Tarirai, muranda wenyu.
\p
\v	7	Dhavhidhindokuti kwaari: Usatya, nokuti zvirokwazvo ndichakuitira tsitsi nekuda kwaJonatani baba vako, uye ndichakudzosera nyika yose yaSauro baba vako; uye iwe uchadya chingwa patafura yangu nguva dzose.
\v	8	Zvinoiye wakakotama ndokuti: Muranda wenyu chii kuti mutarise imbwa yakafa yakaita seni.
\p
\v	9	Zvinomambo wakadana Zibha muranda waSauro ndokuti kwaari: Zvose zvaiva zvaSauro nezveimba yake yose ndakazvipa mwanakomana watenzi wako.
\v	10	Zvino iwe uchamurimira ivhu, iwe nevanakomana vako nevaranda vako, uye muchaunza  mbesa kuti mwanakomana watenzi wako ave nezvekudya zvaangadya; asi Mefibhosheti mwanakomana watenzi wako achagara achidya zvekudya patafura yangu. Zvino Zibha waiva nevanakomana gumi nevashanu nevaranda makumi maviri.
\v	11	Zvino Zibha wakati kuna mambo: Zvichienderana nezvose ishe wangu, mambo, zvaaraira muranda wake, muranda wenyu achaita saizvozvo. Kana ari Mefibhosheti,  mambo anoti: Achadya patafura yangu seumwe wevanakomana vamambo.
\v	12	Zvino Mefibhosheti waiva nemwanakomana mudiki, zita rake raiva Mika. Uye vose vaiva vagere mumba maZibha, vakava varanda vaMefibhosheti.
\v	13	Zvino Mefibhosheti wakagara paJerusarema, nokuti waidya patafura yamambo nguva dzose; waiva akaremara tsoka dzake dziri mbiri.
