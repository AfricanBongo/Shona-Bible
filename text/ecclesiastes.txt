\b	Muparidzi
\c	1
\s	Zvose hazvina maturo
\p
\v	1	MashokoeMuparidzi, mwanakomana waDhavhidhi, mambo paJerusarema.
\p
\v	2	Kushayamaturo kwezvisina maturo, anoreva Muparidzi; kushaya maturo kwezvisina maturo; zvose hazvina maturo!
\v	3	Munhuachawanei pakushingaira kwake kose kwaanoshingaira pasi pezuva?
\v	4	Zerarinoenda, uye zera rinouya; asi nyika imire kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	5	Zuvarinobudawo, uye zuva rinovira; uye richitakwairira kunzvimbo kwarinobuda.
\v	6	Mhepoinoenda kuchamhembe, ichitendeukira kumaodzanyemba; ichitendeuka tendeuka ichienda; uye mhepo inodzokera pakutenderera kwayo.
\v	7	Nzizidzose dzinoenda mugungwa, zvakadaro gungwa harizari; kunzvimbo kunoenda nzizi ndiko kwadzinodzokerazve.
\v	8	Zvinhuzvose zvinonetesa; hakuna munhu anogona kuzvitaura; ziso hariguti nekuona, uye nzeve haizari nekunzwa.
\v	9	Chakambovapondicho chichazovapo; uye chakamboitwa ndicho chichazoitwa; uye hapana chinhu chitsva pasi pezuva.
\v	10	Chinhu chiriko here chinganzi: Tarirai, ichi chitsva? Chaiva chichingovapo kare-kare kutitangira.
\v	11	Hapana chirangaridzo chezvinhu zvekutanga; uye nezvinhu zvinouya zvichavako hakungaviwo nechirangaridzo chazvo nevachazovapo shure.
\p
\v	12	Ini Muparidzi ndaiva ndiri mambo pamusoro paIsraeri paJerusarema.
\v	13	Zvino ndakapa moyo wangu kutsvaka nekunzvera neuchenjeri maererano nezvose zvinoitwa pasi pematenga. Kutambudzika uku kunorwadza Mwari wakapa kuvanakomana vevanhu kuti vabatikane nako.
\v	14	Ndakaona mabasa ose anoitwa pasi pezuva; uye tarirai, zvose hazvina maturo uye kudzingana kwemhepo.
\v	15	Chakakombama hachigoni kutasamurwa; uye chinoshaikwa hachigoni kuverengwa.
\v	16	Ini ndakataura nemoyo wangu, ndichiti: Tarira ini ndakakura ndikawedzera uchenjeri pamusoro peumwe neumwe wakanditangira paJerusarema, uye moyo wangu wakaona zvikuru uchenjeri neruzivo.
\v	17	Zvino ndakapa moyo wangu pakuziva uchenjeri, nekuziva upengo neupenzi. Ndakaziva kuti kunyange izvi kutambudzika kwemhepo.
\v	18	Nokuti mukuwanda kweuchenjeri mune kuwanda kwekusuwa; uye anowedzera ruzivo anowedzera shungu.
\c	10
\s	Uchenjeri neupenzi
\p
\v	1	Nhunzidzakafa dzinodururisa kunhuhwa chizoro chemuvhenganisi wezvinonhuhwira; upenzi ushoma  hunodaro kune wakakosha nekuda kweuchenjeri nekuda kwerukudzo.
\v	2	Moyowewakachenjera uri kuruoko rwake rwerudyi, asi moyo webenzi uri kuruboshwe rwake.
\v	3	Zvirokwazvowobenzi parinofamba panzira, pfungwa dzaro dzinosarira, uye rinoti kune umwe neumwe ibenzi.
\p
\v	4	Kanamweya wemutongi uchikukwirira, usasiya nzvimbo yako, nokuti kudzikama kunonyaradza zvitadzo zvikuru.
\v	5	Panechakaipa chandakaona pasi pezuva, sechitadzo chinobuda pachiso chemutongi:
\v	6	Utununuhunoiswa panzvimbo dzakakwirira zvikuru, uye vakafuma vanogara panzvimbo yakaderera.
\v	7	Ndakaonavaranda vakatasva mabhiza, nemachinda achifamba sevaranda panyika.
\p
\v	8	Anocheragomba achawira mariri; uye anokoromora rusvingo, nyoka ichamuruma.
\v	9	Anobvisamabwe achakuvadzwa nawo; anobanzura huni achava panjodzi nadzo.
\v	10	Kana simbi yagomara, uye iye akasarodza mucheto, naizvozvo anofanira kuwedzera simba; uye uchenjeri hunobatsira pakururamisa.
\p
\v	11	Kana nyoka ikaruma isina kubviswa uturu, nyanzvi yerurimi haina rubatsiro.
\v	12	Mashoko emuromo wewakachenjera ane nyasha, asi miromo yebenzi inorimedza.
\v	13	Kutanga kwemashoko emuromo waro upenzi, uye mugumo wemuromo waro upengo hwakashata.
\v	14	Zvino benzi rinowanza mashoko; munhu haazivi zvichazovapo; nezvichavapo shure kwake ndiyani achamuudza?
\v	15	Kubata kwebenzi kunomunetesa, nokuti haazivi kuenda kuguta.
\p
\v	16	Nhamo kwauri iwe nyika, kana mambo wako ari mwana, nemachinda ako achidya mangwanani.
\v	17	Wakaropafadzwa iwe nyika, kana mambo wako ari mwanakomana wevanokudzwa, nemachinda ako achidya panguva yakafanira, kuitira simba uye kwete kudhakwa.
\p
\v	18	Neusimbe ukuru nhungo dzinotiva; uye nekusabata kwemaoko imba inodonha.
\p
\v	19	Mabiko anoitirwa kuseka, uye waini inofadza vapenyu; asi mari imhinduro yazvo zvose.
\p
\v	20	Usatuka mambo, kunyange mumufungo wako; uye mumakamuri emukati eimba yako yekurara usatuka mufumi; nokuti shiri yematenga inoendesa inzwi, uye ane mapapiro anopira shoko.
\c	11
\s	Itai zvakanaka panguva dzose
\p
\v	1	Kandirachingwa chako pamusoro pechiso chemvura zhinji, nokuti uchachiwana shure kwemazuva mazhinji.
\v	2	Ipamugove kuvanomwe, kunyangewo kuvasere, nokuti hauzivi kuti chii chakaipa chiri panyika.
\v	3	Kanamakore azara mvura, ano idururira panyika; uye kana muti ukawira kuchamhembe, uye kana kumaodzanyemba, panzvimbo muti paunowira ndipo pauchava.
\v	4	Anocherekedzamhepo haangadzvari; neanotarira makore haangakohwi.
\p
\v	5	Sezvausingazivikuti nzira yemweya yakadini kana  makuriro emapfupa mudumbu remukadzi ane mimba, saizvozvowo hauzivi basa raMwari, anoita zvose.
\v	6	Dzvarambeu dzako mangwanani, uye madekwani usabvisa ruoko rwako; nokuti hauzivi kuti chichabudirira ndechipi, ichi kana icho, kana zvimwe zviviri zvose zvichava zvakanaka.
\p
\v	7	Zvirokwazvo,chiedza chakanaka, uye zvakanakira meso kuona zuva.
\v	8	Zvirokwazvo,kana munhu akararama makore mazhinji, ngaafarire ose; asi ngaarangarire mazuva erima, nokuti achava mazhinji; zvose zvinouya hazvina maturo.
\s	Munhu ngaafarire zvose, asi ngaarangarire Musiki wake paudiki hwake
\p
\v	9	Fara,jaya, paudiki hwako, uye moyo wako ngaukufadze pamazuva eujaya hwako; uye ufambe nenzira dzemoyo wako, nepakuona kwemeso ako; asi ziva kuti pane izvozvi zvose, Mwari anokuisa pamutongo.
\v	10	Naizvozvo bvisa kusuruvara pamoyo wako, ubvise chakaipa panyama yako, nokuti udiki neujaya hazvina maturo.
\c	12
\p
\v	1	RangarirawoMusiki wako pamazuva eujaya hwako, mazuva akaipa asati asvika, kana makore asvika, pauchazoti: Handina mufaro maari.
\v	2	Zuvanechiedza nemwedzi nenyeredzi zvisati zvasvibiriswa, nemakore achidzoka pashure pemvura.
\v	3	Pazuvavachengeti veimba pavachadedera, uye varume vesimba vachikotama; uye vakuyi vamira, nokuti vava vashoma; neavo vanotarira nepamahwindo vasvibirirwa;
\v	4	nemikovayazarirwa kunzira yekumusha, neruzha rwekukuya rwaderera, uye achimuka neinzwi reshiri, nevakunda vose verwiyo vakotamiswa.
\v	5	Zvinovachazotya icho chakakwirira, uye zvinotyisa panzira, uye muti wemuarimondi uchatumbuka, nemhashu ichazvikwekweredza, uye chishuwo chichapera; nokuti munhu anoenda kumusha wake wekusingaperi, nevachemi vachafamba-famba panzira yemumusha.
\v	6	Kusatikwabviswa rwonzi rwesirivha, nembiya yegoridhe  isati yaputswa, uye chirongo  chisati chaputswa pachitubu, uye ndirayi isati yadambuka patsime,
\v	7	uyeguruva rinodzokera kuvhu sezvaraiva rakaita; uye mweya unodzokera kuna Mwari wakaupa.
\p
\v	8	Kushayamaturo kwezvisina maturo, anoreva muparidzi, zvose hazvina maturo.
\s	Itya Mwari uchengete mirairo yake
\p
\v	9	Pamusoropazvowo nokuti muparidzi waiva akachenjera, waiva achiri kudzidzisa vanhu ruzivo, hongu wakadzamisa pfungwa, akatsvakisisa, akamisa zvakanaka zvirevo zvizhinji.
\v	10	Muparidzi wakatsvaka kuwana mashoko anofadza; uye zvakanyorwa zvakarurama, mashoko echokwadi.
\v	11	Mashoko evakachenjera akaita sezvibayiso uye sembambo dzakaroverwa dzikadzika, madzishe edziungano, akapiwa nemufudzi umwe chete.
\v	12	Pamusoro peizvizve, mwanakomana wangu, unyeverwe; kuita mabhuku mazhinji hakuna magumo, uye kudzidza kuzhinji kunonetesa nyama.
\p
\v	13	Magumo eshoko rose ranzwikwa ndi rori: Itya Mwari, ugochengeta mirairo yake; nokuti izvi  ifaniro yemunhu wose.
\v	14	Nokuti Mwari achauisa basa rimwe nerimwe pamutongo, nechose chakavanzika, kana chakanaka, uye kana chakaipa.
\c	2
\s	Mufaro wenyika nefuma hazvifadzi
\p
\v	1	Ndakatiini mumoyo mangu: Uya hako, ndichakuedza nemufaro; uye tarira kuzvakanaka! Asi tarira, izviwo zvaiva zvisina maturo.
\v	2	Maereranonekuseka ndakati: Upenzi! Uye maererano nemufaro: Unoitei?
\v	3	Ndakatsvakamumoyo wangu kufadza nyama yangu newaini (asi ndakatungamirira moyo wangu muuchenjeri), nekubatirira paupenzi, kusvika ndaona kuti chii ichi chakanakira vanakomana vevanhu, chavangaita pasi pematenga,  neuwandu hwemazuva eupenyu hwavo.
\p
\v	4	Ndakazviitiramabasa makuru; ndakazvivakira dzimba; ndakazvidzvarira minda yemizambiringa;
\v	5	ndakazviitiramabindu nemabindu emiti, ndikadzvara maari miti yemichero yose;
\v	6	ndakazviitiramadziva emvura, kudiridza kubva maari masango anomera miti;
\v	7	ndakatengavaranda nevarandakadzi; ndakava nevakaberekerwa mumba  mangu; uye ndakava nefuma zhinji yemombe nemakwai, pamusoro pevose vakanditangira paJerusarema;
\v	8	ndakazviunganidzirawosirivha negoridhe, nefuma yakasanangurwa yemadzimambo neyematunhu; ndakazviwanira vaimbi vechirume nevaimbi vechikadzi, nezvinofadza zvevanakomana vevanhu, vasikana vemarudzi ose.
\v	9	Saizvozvondakava mukuru uye ndakawanda kupfuura ani nani waivapo pamberi pangu paJerusarema; uye huchenjeri hwangu hwakamira neni.
\v	10	Zvose meso angu zvaakakumbira handina kuanyima izvo; handina kunyima moyo wangu mufaro upi neupi; nokuti moyo wangu wakafara nekuda kwekushingaira kwangu kose; uye uyu wakava mugove wangu kubva pakushingaira kwangu kose.
\v	11	Zvino ini ndakatendeukira kumabasa angu ose maoko angu aakaita, uye kubasa randakashingairira kuita; zvino tarira, zvose hazvina maturo uye kutambudzika kwemhepo; uye hapana chinobatsira pasi pezuva.
\p
\v	12	Zvino ini ndakatendeuka kuona uchenjeri, neupengo ukuru, neupenzi. Nokuti anga itei munhu anotevera mambo? Izvo zvakatoitwa.
\v	13	Zvino ini ndakaona kuti kune zvinobatsira pauchenjeri kupfuura upenzi, sezvinobatsira zvechiedza kupfuura rima.
\v	14	Wakachenjera, meso ake ari mumusoro make, uye benzi rinofamba murima; neniwo ndakaziva kuti chiitiko chimwe chakaitika kwavari vose.
\v	15	Zvino ini ndakati mumoyo mangu: Sezvakaitika kubenzi, zvakaitikawo kwandiri ini; zvino sei ini ndakachenjera kupfuura? Zvino ndakataura mumoyo mangu kuti izviwo hazvina maturo.
\v	16	Nokuti hapana kurangarirwa kune wakachenjera kunopfuura kubenzi pakusingaperi; nokuti izvi zviripo ikozvino, mazuva anouya zvinokanganikwa zvose; zvino sei wakachenjera achifa pamwe nebenzi?
\v	17	Zvino ndakavenga upenyu, nokuti anosuwisa kwandiri mabasa akaitwa pasi pezuva, nokuti zvose hazvina maturo uye kutambudzika kwemoyo.
\v	18	Zvino ini ndakavenga kubata kwangu kose kwandakabata pasi pezuva, nokuti ndinozvisiira munhu anonditevera.
\v	19	Zvino ndiyani angaziva kana iye achava wakachenjera kana benzi? Zvakadaro anotonga pamusoro pekubata kwangu kose kwandakabata, uye kuti ndakaita neuchenjeri pasi pezuva! Izviwo hazvina maturo.
\p
\v	20	Zvino ini ndakatendeuka kuodza moyo wangu nekubata kose kwandakabata pasi pezuva.
\v	21	Nokuti pane munhu kubata kwake kuri muuchenjeri nemuruzivo nemuumhizha; zvakadaro achakupa munhu asina kubata pakuri uve mugove wake. Izviwo hazvina maturo uye chitadzo chikuru.
\v	22	Nokuti munhu anei pakubata kwake kose, nekushungurudzika kwemoyo wake kwaanobata pasi pezuva?
\v	23	Nokuti mazuva ake ose inhamo huru, nekutambudzika kwake marwadzo; kunyange neusiku moyo wake hauzorori. Kunyange ichi hachina maturo.
\p
\v	24	Hakune chinhu chakanaka kumunhu kuti adye anwe aratidze mweya wake zvakanaka pakubata kwake? Izvi ini ndakaonawo kuti zvinobva muruoko rwaMwari.
\v	25	Nokuti ndiyani angadya, uye ndiyani anganakirwa kupfuura ini?
\v	26	Nokuti anopa munhu wakanaka pamberi pechiso chake uchenjeri neruzivo nemufaro; asi mutadzi anomupa basa rekuunganidza nekututira, kuti ape wakanaka pamberi paMwari. Izviwo hazvina maturo, uye kutambudzika kwemoyo.
\c	3
\s	Chimwe nechimwe chine nguva yacho
\p
\v	1	Chinhuchimwe nechimwe chine nguva yakatarwa, uye kune nguva yenyaya imwe neimwe pasi pematenga.
\v	2	Nguvayekuberekwa, nenguva yekufa; nguva yekudzvara, nenguva yekudzura chakadzvarwa;
\v	3	nguvayekuuraya, nenguva yekuporesa; nguva yekuputsa, nenguva yekuvaka;
\v	4	nguvayekuchema misodzi, nenguva yekuseka; nguva yekurira, nenguva yekupembera;
\v	5	nguvayekurasa mabwe, nenguva yekuunganidza mabwe; nguva yekumbundikira, nenguva yekuva kure nekumbundikira;
\v	6	nguvayekuwana, nenguva yekurasikirwa; nguva yekuchengeta, nenguva yekurasa;
\v	7	nguvayekubvarura, nenguva yekusona; nguva yekunyarara, nenguva yekutaura;
\v	8	nguvayekuda, nenguva yekuvenga; nguva yehondo, nenguva yerugare.
\p
\v	9	Anowaneianoshanda pane zvaanoshingairira?
\v	10	Ndakaona basa Mwari raakapa vanakomana vevanhu, kuti vabatikane naro.
\p
\v	11	Wakaita chimwe nechimwe chakanaka panguva yacho; akaisawo zvisingaperi mumoyo mavo, kuti kusava nemunhu anowana basa Mwari raakaita kubva pakutanga kusvika pakupedzisira.
\v	12	Ndinoziva kuti hakuna chakanaka pakati pavo kunze kwekufara nekuita zvakanaka paupenyu hwake.
\v	13	Uyewo kuti munhu umwe neumwe adye nekunwa, anakirwe nezvakanaka zvekubata kwake kose; kunova chipo chaMwari.
\v	14	Ndinoziva kuti chipi nechipi Mwari chaanoita chichavapo nekusingaperi; hakuna chingawedzerwa kwachiri uye hakuna chingatapudzwa kwachiri; uye Mwari waka zviita, kuti vatye pamberi pake.
\v	15	Zvaiva zviripo zviripo nhasi, nezvichavapo zvakagara zviripo; uye Mwari anoda zvakadzingwa.
\p
\v	16	Uyewo ndakaona pasi pezuva nzvimbo yemutongo, uipi hwaiva huripo; nenzvimbo yekururama, uipi hwaiva huripo.
\v	17	Ini ndakati mumoyo mangu: Mwari achatonga wakarurama newakaipa, nokuti nguva iripo ipapo yechinangwa chipi nechipi nepamusoro pebasa ripi neripi.
\p
\v	18	Ini ndakati mumoyo mangu: Pachimiro chevanakomana vevanhu, kuti Mwari avaratidze pachena kuti vaone kuti ivo pachavo imhuka.
\v	19	Nokuti zvinowira vanakomana vevanhu zvinowira mhukawo; ichi chinhu chimwe chinovawira; sekufa kunoita chimwe, chimwe chinofa saizvozvo; hongu zvine kufema kumwe; munhu haana paanopfuura mhuka; nokuti zvose hazvina maturo.
\v	20	Zvose zvinoenda kunzvimbo imwe; zvose zvinobva kuguruva, uye zvose zvinodzokera kuguruva.
\v	21	Ndiyani anoziva mweya wevanakomana vevanhu unokwira kumusoro, nemweya wemhuka unoburuka kuburukira kuvhu?
\v	22	Naizvozvo ndakaona kuti hakuna chiri nani kupfuura kuti munhu afare pamabasa ake, nokuti ndiwo mugove wake; nokuti ndiyani achamuunza kuti aone zvichavako shure kwake?
\c	4
\s	Zvinotambudza vanhu
\p
\v	1	Zvinoini ndakadzoka ndokuona kumanikidza kose kukuru kunoitwa pasi pezuva; zvino tarira misodzi yevanomanikidzwa, uye havana munyaradzi; uye paruoko rwevamanikidzi vavo pane simba; asi havana munyaradzi.
\v	2	Naizvozvondakarumbidza vakafa, vakatofa, kupfuura vapenyu vachiri vapenyu.
\v	3	Hongu,asati avapo uri nani pavaviri vavo, pakuti haana kuona basa rakaipa rakaitwa pasi pezuva.
\p
\v	4	Zvinoini ndakaona kubata kose, nemibairo yose yebasa, kuti igodo remunhu kubva kune wekwake. Izviwo hazvina maturo uye kutambudzika kwemoyo.
\v	5	Benzirinofungatira maoko aro, richidya nyama yaro.
\v	6	Ruokorwuzere murunyararo rwunopfuura maoko maviri azere mukubata nekutambudzika kwemoyo.
\p
\v	7	Zvinoini ndakadzoka, ndikaona kushaya maturo pasi pezuva.
\v	8	Kuneumwe uye hakuna wepiri, kunyange haana mwanakomana kana munin'ina, asi kubata kwake kose hakuna mugumo. Uye ziso rake harigutswi nefuma, uye  haati: Ndinobatira ani, ndichishaisa mweya weupenyu wangu zvakanaka? Izviwo hazvina maturo, hongu, kutambudzika kunoshungurudza.
\v	9	Vavirivari nani pane umwe, pakuti vane mubairo wakanaka mukubata kwavo.
\v	10	Nokuti kana vakawa, umwe achasimudza shamwari yake; asi ane nhamo uyo ari oga kana akawa; nokuti hapana wepiri kumumutsa.
\v	11	Uye kana vaviri vachirara pamwe, vane rudziira; asi umwe angava nerudziira sei?
\v	12	Zvino kana umwe akamukunda ari oga, vaviri vachamira kumurwisa; uye rwonzi rwemutatu harungakurumidzi kudamburwa.
\p
\v	13	Jaya murombo rakachenjera riri nani pana mambo mutana benzi, asingachazivi kutsiurwa.
\v	14	Nokuti anobuda mutirongo kuti azova mambo; kunyangewo paushe hwake akaberekwa ari murombo.
\v	15	Ndakaona vapenyu vose vanofamba pasi pezuva, pamwe nemukomana wechipiri achamira pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	16	Hakuna mugumo kuvanhu vose, kune vose vakavatangira; kunyange vanozotevera havangamufariri. Zvirokwazvo, izviwo hazvina maturo nekutambudzika kwemoyo.
\c	5
\s	Nzira dzinouisa mufaro
\p
\v	1	Ngwarirarutsoka rwako kana uchienda kuimba yaMwari; uye swederera kuteerera pane kupa chibairo chemapenzi; nokuti haazivi kuti anoita chakaipa.
\v	2	Usakurumidzisanemuromo wako, uye moyo wako ngaurege kukurumidza kubudisa shoko pamberi paMwari; nokuti Mwari ari kumatenga, iwewo uri panyika; naizvozvo mashoko ako ngaave mashoma.
\v	3	Nokutichiroto chinouya neuwandu hwebasa, uye inzwi rebenzi neuwandu hwemashoko.
\v	4	Paunopikamhiko kuna Mwari, usanonoka kuizadzisa, nokuti hakuna mufaro kumapenzi; zadzisa icho chawakapika.
\v	5	Zvirinani kuti urege kupika, pakuti upike usingazadzisi.
\v	6	Usatenderamuromo wako kutadzisa nyama yako; uye usareva pamberi pemutumwa kuti imhosho; Mwari achatsamwirei inzwi rako, akaparadza basa remaoko ako?
\v	7	Nokutipauwandu hwezviroto nemashoko mazhinji pane zvisina maturo zvizhinji; asi iwe utye Mwari.
\p
\v	8	Kanauchiona kumanikidzwa kwemurombo, nekupambwa kwekururama nemutongo mudunhu, usashamiswa nechinhu, nokuti ari pamusoro pekumusoro anocherekedza, uye kune vakuru pamusoro pavo.
\p
\v	9	Mubairowenyika ndewe vose; mambo anoshandirwa nemunda.
\v	10	Anoda mari haagutsikani  nemari, uye ani nani anoda zvakawanda, haanga gutsikani nezvakawanda. Izviwo hazvina maturo.
\v	11	Mukuwanda kwezvinhu vadyi vazvo vanowanda; uye zvinobatsirei kuvamwene vazvo kunze kwekuona nemeso avo?
\v	12	Hope dzeanoshanda dzinotapira, angava adya zvishoma kana zvakawanda; asi kuguta kwemufumi hakumutenderi kurara.
\v	13	Pane chakaipa chinorwadza chandakaona pasi pezuva: Fuma inochengeterwa vamwene vayo, ive kutambudzika kwavo.
\v	14	Zvino fuma iyo inoparara nebasa rakaipa; nepaanobereka mwana, hamuna chinhu muruoko rwake.
\v	15	Sekubuda kwake mudumbu ramai vake, achadzoka asina chinhu, achienda sekuuya kwake; uye haangatori chinhu pakubata kwake chaangaenda nacho muruoko rwake.
\v	16	Neichiwo chakaipa chinorwadza: Sezvaakangouya, saizvozvo anoenda; zvino chii chinowanikwa kune anotamburira mhepo?
\v	17	Mazuva ake osewo anodyira murima, nekushushikana kukuru, neutenda hwake, nekutsamwa.
\p
\v	18	Tarira, zvandakaona ini, zvakafanira zvakanaka kudya nekunwa nekuona zvakanaka pakubata kwake kose kwaakabata pasi pezuva, uwandu hwemazuva eupenyu hwake Mwari hwaakapa kwaari, nokuti ndiwo mugove wake.
\v	19	Uye munhu wose Mwari waakapa fuma neufumi, ndokumupa simba rekudya pazviri, nekugamuchira mugove wake, nekufara pabasa rake, ichi ndicho chipo chaMwari.
\v	20	Nokuti haangarangariri zvikuru mazuva eupenyu hwake, nokuti Mwari anopindura pamufaro wemoyo wake.
\c	6
\s	Mufaro unobva pafuma yenyika hauna kukwana
\p
\v	1	Panechakaipa chandakaona pasi pezuva, uye chikuru pavanhu:
\v	2	MunhuMwari waanopa kwaari fuma, neufumi, nerukudzo, kuti asashaiwa pamweya weupenyu wake pazvose zvaanoshuva, asi Mwari asingamupi simba kuti adye pairi, asi mutorwa anoidya. Ichi hachina maturo, uye ichi chirwere chakaipa.
\p
\v	3	Kanamunhu akabereka zana, ndokurarama makore mazhinji, kuti mazuva emakore ake ave mazhinji, asi mweya weupenyu wake usingagutswi nezvakanaka, uyezve akasavigwa, ndinoti gavamwedzi riri nani pana iye.
\v	4	Nokutianopinda mukushaya maturo, uye anoenda murima, uye zita rake rinofukidzwa murima.
\v	5	Kunyangezuva haana ku riona uye haana kuziva, uyu ane zororo guru kukunda uyo.
\v	6	Hongu,kunyange akararama makore zvuru zvakapetwa kaviri, zvakadaro asina kuona chakanaka, vose havaendi here kunzvimbo imwe?
\p
\v	7	Kubatakose kwemunhu ndekwemuromo wake; zvakadarowo chishuwo hachizadzwi.
\v	8	Nokutiwakachenjera anei kupfuura benzi? Anei murombo anoziva kufamba pamberi pevapenyu?
\v	9	Kuonakwemeso kuri nani pane kufamba kwechishuwo. Izviwo hazvina maturo uye kutambudzika kwemoyo.
\p
\v	10	Chinhu chakavapo chakatotumidzwa zita racho; uye zvinozikanwa kuti munhu; uye haagoni kupikisana neakasimba kumupfuura.
\v	11	Nokuti zvinhu zvizhinji zvinowanza kushaya maturo; zvinobatsirei kumunhu?
\v	12	Nokuti ndiyani anoziva zvakanakira munhu muupenyu, uwandu hwemazuva eupenyu hwake husina maturo hwaakaita semumvuri? Nokuti ndiyani angaudza munhu izvo zvichavako shure kwake pasi pezuva?
\c	7
\s	Uchenjeri hwakanaka kupfuura zvose
\p
\v	1	Zita rakanaka riri nani pane chizoro chakanaka, nezuva rekufa pane zuva rekuzvarwa kwake.
\p
\v	2	Zvirinani kuenda kuimba yekuchema, pane kuenda kuimba yemabiko; nokuti ndiwo mugumo wemunhu wose; uye mupenyu achazviisa mumoyo make.
\v	3	Kushungurudzikakuri nani pane kuseka, nokuti nekusuwa kwechiso moyo unoitwa nani.
\v	4	Moyowevakachenjera uri muimba yekuchema, asi moyo wemapenzi uri muimba yemufaro.
\v	5	Zvirinani kunzwa kutsiura kwewakachenjera, pakuti munhu anzwe rwumbo rwemapenzi.
\v	6	Nokutisekubarika kweminzwa pasi pehari, ndizvo zvakaita kuseka kwebenzi. Uye izviwo hazvina maturo.
\v	7	Zvirokwazvomanikidzo inopenza wakachenjera; uye chipo chinoparadza moyo.
\v	8	Mugumowechinhu uri nani pane kutanga kwacho; ane moyo murefu ari nani pane ane kuzvikudza kwemweya.
\v	9	Usakurumidzapamweya wako kutsamwa, nokuti kutsamwa kunogara pachipfuva chemapenzi.
\v	10	Usati: Zvinoitwa sei kuti mazuva ekutanga aiva nani pane awa? Nokuti naizvozvi haubvunzi neuchenjeri.
\p
\v	11	Uchenjeri hwakanaka pamwe nenhaka; uye ruyamuro kune vanoona zuva.
\v	12	Nokuti uchenjeri chitaviridzo, mari chitaviridzo, asi kuva nani kweruzivo  kuti hunopa upenyu kuvene vahwo.
\v	13	Cherekedza basa raMwari, nokuti ndiyani anogona kutasanura chaakaminamisa?
\p
\v	14	Fara muzuva rekubudirira, asi rangarira muzuva rechakaipa; nairori Mwari wakariita rikava seroro, kusvika pachinangwa chekuti munhu asawana chinhu shure kwake.
\v	15	Zvose ndakazviona pamazuva ekushaya maturo kwangu: Aripo wakarurama anoparara mukururama kwake, uye aripo muiti wezvakaipa anorebesa  upenyu mukuipa kwake.
\v	16	Usava wakarurama kupfuurikidza, uye usazviita wakachenjera kupfuurikidza; unozviparadzirei?
\v	17	Usava wakaipa kupfuurikidza, uye usava benzi; unofirei pasati pari panguva yako?
\v	18	Zvakanaka kuti ubatisise chinhu ichi, uyewo kuti usabvisa ruoko rwako pane ichocho; nokuti anotya Mwari achapukunyuka pazviri zvose.
\v	19	Uchenjeri hunosimbisa wakachenjera kupfuura vatongi gumi vari muguta.
\v	20	Nokuti hakuna munhu wakarurama panyika anoita zvakanaka akasatadza.
\v	21	Uye usaisa moyo wako pamashoko ose avanotaura, kuti usanzwa kuti muranda wako anokutuka.
\v	22	Nokuti panguva zhinjiwo moyo wako unoziva kuti newe unotuka vamwe.
\p
\v	23	Zvose izvi ndakazvicherekedza neuchenjeri, ndikati: Ndichachenjera; asi zvaiva kure neni.
\v	24	Zviri kure, nezvakadzika zvikuru, ndiyani achazviwana?
\v	25	Ini ndakatendeuka, nemoyo wangu, kuziva nekunzvera nekutsvaka uchenjeri, nechinangwa, nekuziva kutadza kweupenzi, uye utununu hweupengo ukuru.
\v	26	Zvino ndakawana zvinovava kupfuura rufu: Mukadzi wemoyo wake uri mimbure nemisungo, maoko ake zvisungo. Wakanaka pamberi paMwari achapukunyuka kubva paari, asi mutadzi anobatwa naye.
\v	27	Tarira, ndakawana izvi, wakadaro muparidzi, chimwe kune chimwe kuwana chikonzero;
\v	28	mweya weupenyu wangu uchiri kuchitsvaka, asi handichiwani. Murume umwe pakati pechuru ndakamuwana, asi mukadzi pakati pavo vose handina kumuwana.
\v	29	Tarira, ndakawana ichi chega, kuti Mwari wakaita munhu akarurama, asi ivo vakatsvaka mano mazhinji.
\c	8
\s	Madzimambo anofanira kuteererwa
\p
\v	1	Ndiyaniwakaita sewakachenjera? Uye ndiyani anoziva dudziro yechinhu? Uchenjeri hwemunhu hunopenyesa chiso chake, uye kuoma kwechiso chake hunoshandurwa.
\v	2	Ndinoti: Chengeta murairo wamambo, uye nekuda kwemhiko yaMwari.
\v	3	Usakurumidzakubva pamberi pake; usamira panyaya yakaipa; nokuti anoita zvose zvaanoda.
\v	4	Apopane shoko ramambo, pane simba; uye ndiyani angati kwaari: Munoitei?
\v	5	Anochengetamurairo haazivi chinhu chakaipa; uye moyo wewakachenjera unoziva nguva nemutongo.
\p
\v	6	Nokutipanyaya imwe neimwe pane nguva nemutongo; nokuti kushata kwemunhu kukuru pamusoro pake.
\v	7	Nokutihaazivi kuti zvichavei; nokuti ndiyani angamuudza kuti zvichava zvarinhi?
\v	8	Hapanamunhu ane simba pamusoro pemweya kunyima mweya; hapanawo  ane simba pazuva rerufu; uye hapana kununurwa pahondo iyo; uye chakaipa hachinganunuri vene vacho.
\v	9	Zvoseizvi ndaka zviona, kuti ndipe moyo wangu kubasa rimwe nerimwe rakaitwa pasi pezuva, nguva munhu paakatonga munhu chive chinhu chakaipa kwaari.
\s	Mubairo wevakarurama nevasakarurama
\p
\v	10	Zvino saizvozvo ndakaona vakaipa vachivigwa, vaiva vakapinda vakaenda kubva panzvimbo tsvene. Zvino vakakanganikwa muguta mavakaita zvakadaro. Izviwo hazvina maturo.
\v	11	Nokuti mutongo pabasa rakaipa haungaitwi nekukurumidza, nekuda kweizvi moyo wevanakomana vevanhu wakazara mukati mavo kuita zvakaipa.
\v	12	Kunyange mutadzi akaita chakaipa kazana,  mazuva ake akarebeswa, zvakadaro zvirokwazvo ndinoziva kuti zvichavanakira vanotya Mwari, vanotya pamberi pake.
\v	13	Asi chakanaka hachingavi kune wakaipa, uye haangarebesi mazuva semumvuri, nokuti haatyi pamberi paMwari.
\v	14	Pane chisina maturo chinoitwa panyika, kuti varipo vakarurama vanowirwa zvinoenderana nebasa revakaipa, uye varipo vakaipa vanowirwa zvinoenderana nebasa revakarurama. Ndakati: Ichiwo hachina maturo.
\v	15	Naizvozvo ini ndakashambadza mufaro; nokuti munhu haana chakanaka pasi pezuva kunze kwekudya, nekunwa, nekufara; uye izvo zvigare naye pakubata kwake kwemazuva eupenyu hwake Mwari aakamupa pasi pezuva.
\p
\v	16	Pandakapa moyo wangu kuziva uchenjeri nekuona basa rakaitwa panyika, nokutiwo pane asingaoni hope nemeso ake masikati neusiku,
\v	17	ipapo ndakaona basa rose raMwari, kuti munhu haagoni kuwana basa rakaitwa pasi pezuva, nokuti kunyange munhu achishingaira kutsvaka, haawani, uye kunyange wakachenjera achitaura kuziva, haagoni kuwana.
\c	9
\s	Nzira dzaMwari hadzizikanwi
\p
\v	1	Nokutizvose izvi ndakazviisa mumoyo wangu, kududzira zvose izvi, kuti vakarurama nevakachenjera nemabasa avo zviri muruoko rwaMwari. Hakuna munhu anoziva rudo pamwe neruvengo, zvose zviri pamberi pavo.
\v	2	Zvosezvakafanana kune vose, chiitiko chimwe kune wakarurama nekune wakaipa, kune wakanaka nekune wakachena nekune asina kuchena, nekune anobaira nekune asingabairi. Sewakanaka, wakadaro mutadzi, anopika seanotya mhiko.
\v	3	Ichichakaipa pakati pezvose zvinoitwa pasi pezuva, kuti chiitiko chimwe chive kune vose, uyewo moyo wevanakomana vevanhu uzere nechakaipa neupengo ukuru pamoyo wavo muupenyu hwavo; uye shure kwazvo  vanoenda kuvakafa.
\p
\v	4	Nokutikune vanobatanidzwa kuvapenyu vose kune chivimbo, nokuti imbwa mhenyu iri nani pane shumba yakafa.
\v	5	Nokutivapenyu vanoziva kuti vachafa, asi vakafa havazivi chinhu, uye havachinazve mubairo; nokuti kurangarirwa kwavo kwakanganikwa.
\v	6	Rudorwavowo neruvengo rwavo negodo ravo zvaiva zvaparara, uye havachavi nemugove nekusingaperi pane zvose zvinoitwa pasi pezuva.
\p
\v	7	Enda,udye zvekudya zvako nemufaro, unwe waini yako nemoyo wakafarisa; nokuti Mwari atofadzwa nemabasa ako.
\v	8	Nguvadzose nguvo dzako ngadzive dzakachena, uye mafuta ngaarege kushaikwa mumusoro wako.
\v	9	Nakirwaneupenyu nemukadzi wawakada, mazuva ose eupenyu hwekushaya maturo kwako, aakakupa pasi pezuva, mazuva ose ekushaya maturo kwako, nokuti ndiwo mugove wako muupenyu, iwo wekubata kwako kwawakabata pasi pezuva.
\v	10	Chipi nechipi ruoko rwako charwunowana kuita, ita nesimba rako, nokuti hakuna basa, kana zano, kana ruzivo, kana uchenjeri muguva maunoenda.
\s	Uchenjeri hunopfuura zvose
\p
\v	11	Ndakadzoka, ndikaona pasi pezuva, kuti mujaho hausi wevanomhanyisisa, nehondo haisi yevane simba, nechingwawo hachisi chevakachenjera, uyewo fuma haisi yevane ruzivo, nenyashawo hadzisi dzenyanzvi, nokuti nguva nemukana zvakaitika kwavari vose.
\v	12	Nokuti munhu haaziviwo nguva yake; sehove dzinobatwa nemumbure wakaipa, uye seshiri dzinobatwa nemusungo, seizvi vanakomana vevanhu vanoteerwa musungo panguva yakaipa, kana ichivawira pakarepo.
\p
\v	13	Ndakaonawo ichi pasi pezuva, uchenjeri, uye ukuru kwandiri:
\v	14	Kwaiva neguta diki, nevanhu vashoma mukati maro; asi mambo mukuru wakauya mariri, ndokurikomba, ndokurivakira masvingo makuru ekurwisa.
\v	15	Zvino makawanikwa mukati maro munhu murombo wakachenjera, zvino akanunura guta neuchenjeri hwake; asi munhu haana kurangarira munhu uyu murombo.
\v	16	Ini ndokuti: Uchenjeri huri nani pane simba, asi uchenjeri hwemurombo hunozvidzwa, uye mashoko ake haateererwi.
\v	17	Mashoko evakachenjera anonzwika pakudzikama kupfuura kudanidzira kwemutungamiriri pakati pemapenzi.
\v	18	Uchenjeri hunopfuura zvombo zvehondo; uye mutadzi umwe anoparadza zvakanaka zvizhinji.
