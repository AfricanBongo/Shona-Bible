\b	Mabasa avaapositori
\c	1
\s	Kukwira kwaJesu kudenga
\p
\v	1	Rondedzeroyekutanga ndakaiita, haiwa Teofiro, pamusoro pezvose Jesu zvaakatanga kuita uyewo kudzidzisa,
\v	2	kusvikazuva raakakwidzwa, amboraira neMweya Mutsvene kuvaapositori vaaiva asarudza;
\v	3	vaakazviratidzawokwavari ari mupenyu shure kwekutambudzika kwake neuchapupu uzhinji hunopwisa, achionekwa navo kwemazuva makumi mana, achitaura zvinhu zveushe hwaMwari.
\v	4	Zvinoaungana navo, akavaraira kuti varege kubva muJerusarema asi vamirire chivimbiso chaBaba, chamakanzwa neni;
\v	5	nokutiJohwani zvirokwazvo wakabhabhatidza nemvura, asi imwi muchabhabhatidzwa neMweya Mutsvene pasina mazuva mazhinji kubva ikozvino.
\s	Kukwira kwaJesu
\p
\v	6	Naizvozvoivo vakati vaungana vakamubvunza vachiti: Ishe, muchadzosera ushe kuna Israeri nenguva ino here?
\v	7	Zvinoakati kwavari: Hazvisi zvenyu kuziva nguva kana misi Baba yavakatara nesimba ravo pachavo;
\v	8	asimuchagamuchira simba, Mweya Mutsvene asvika pamusoro penyu; uye muchava zvapupu zvangu zvose paJerusarema nemuJudhiya mose nemuSamariya, kusvikirawo kumugumo wenyika.
\p
\v	9	Zvinowakati areva zvinhu izvi, akakwidzwa vakatarira, negore rikamubvisa pameso avo.
\v	10	Zvino vachakatarisisa kudenga, achikwira, zvino tarira, varume vaviri vakamira navo nenguvo chena;
\v	11	ivowo vakati: Varume veGarirea, makamirirei makatarisisa kudenga? Iyeyu Jesu, anobviswa kwamuri achikwidzwa kudenga, achauya saizvozvo sezvamunomuona achienda kudenga.
\s	Vadzidzi vanogarira Mweya
\p
\v	12	Zvino vakadzokera kuJerusarema vachibva pagomo rinonzi reMiorivhi riri pedo neJerusarema, pane  chinhambwe cherwendo rwesabata.
\v	13	Vakati vapinda, vakakwira mukamuri yepamusoro apo paigara vose Petro naJakobho naJohwani naAndiriya, Firipi naTomasi, Bhatironomiyo naMatewu, Jakobho waArifio naSimoni muZiroti, naJudhasi waJakobho.
\v	14	Ava vose vakatsungirira nemoyo umwe mumunyengetero nemukumbiro, pamwe nevakadzi, naMaria mai vaJesu, uye nevanin'ina vake.
\s	Vanosanangura Matiasi panzvimbo yaJudhasi
\p
\v	15	Zvino mumazuva iwayo Petro wakasimuka pakati pevadzidzi akati (kwaiva nechaunga nevanhu pamwe vanenge zana nemakumi maviri):
\v	16	Varume hama, zvakafanira kuti rugwaro urwu rwuzadziswe, Mweya Mutsvene zvawakagara wataura nemuromo waDhavhidhi pamusoro paJudhasi, waiva ari mutungamiriri wevaya vakabata Jesu,
\v	17	nokuti waiva achiverengwa pamwe nesu, akagamuchira mugove weushumiri uhu.
\v	18	Zvino uyu wakawana munda nemubairo wezvakaipa, akawa nemusoro, akaparuka nepakati, ura hwake hwose hukabvajukira kunze.
\v	19	Zvikanzwikwa kuvagari vose paJerusarema, zvekuti munda uyo watumidzwa nerurimi rwavo pachavo  kuti Akeridhama, ndiko kuti: Munda weropa.
\v	20	Nokuti zvakanyorwa mubhuku reMapisarema  zvichinzi: Musha wake ngauve dongo, uye kurege kuva neanogara mauri; uye: Utariri hwake, umwe ngaatore.
\v	21	Naizvozvo zvakafanira pavarume ava vaifamba nesu munguva yose, apo Ishe Jesu aipinda nekubuda pakati pedu,
\v	22	kutanga parubhabhatidzo rwaJohwani, kusvika pazuva raakwidzwa kubva kwatiri, umwe wavo ngaave chapupu pamwe nesu chekumuka kwake.
\v	23	Zvino vakamisa vaviri: Josefa wainzi Bharisabhasi, wakatumidzwawo Jusito, naMatiasi.
\v	24	Vakanyengetera vachiti: Imwi Ishe, imwi muzivi wemoyo yevose, taridzai umwe pavaviri ava wamasarudza,
\v	25	kugamuchira mugove paushumiri nevuapositori, Judhasi hwaakatsauka pahuri, kuti aende panzvimbo yake pachake.
\v	26	Zvino vakavapa mijenya yavo, mujenya ukawira pana Matiasi; akaverengwa pamwe nevaapositori vanegumi neumwe.
\c	10
\s	Chiratidzo chaKoneriyo
\p
\v	1	Kwaivakune umwewo murume muKesariya wainzi Koneriyo, mukuru wezana wehondo yainzi yeItaria,
\v	2	wakazvipirauye anotya Mwari pamwe neimba yake yose, waiita mabasa erudo mazhinji kuvanhu, nekunyengetera kuna Mwari nguva yose;
\v	3	akaonamuchiratidzo pachena, neawa rinenge repfumbamwe rezuva, mutumwa waMwari achipinda kwaari, achiti kwaari: Koneriyo!
\v	4	Zvinowakati amutarisisa akatya, akati: Chii, Ishe? Zvino akati kwaari: Minyengetero yako nemabasa ako erudo zvakwira kuva chirangaridzo pamberi paMwari.
\v	5	Zvinoikozvino tuma varume kuJopa, udane Simoni anonzi Petro;
\v	6	anogaraneumwe Simoni musuki wematehwe, imba yake iri parutivi rwegungwa; iye achakuudza zvaunofanira kuita.
\v	7	Zvinomutumwa waiva ataura naKoneriyo wakati abva, wakadana vaviri vevaranda vake vemumba nemurwi wakatendeka pane vaimumirira nguva dzose,
\v	8	zvinowakati arondedzera zvinhu zvose kwavari, akavatuma kuJopa.
\p
\v	9	Zvinochifume ivo vachiri kufamba voswedera muguta, Petro wakakwira pamusoro pedenga reimba kunonyengetera, neawa rinenge rechitanhatu.
\v	10	Akava nenzara kwazvo, akashuva kudya; asi ivo vakati vachagadzirira, chiyeverwa chikamuwira;
\v	11	ndokuona denga rakazaruka, neumwe mudziyo uchiburukira kwaari, semucheka mukuru, wakasungwa makona mana uchiburusirwa pasi.
\v	12	Maiva nemhuka dzose dzemakumbo mana dzenyika nemhuka dzesango nezvinokambaira neshiri dzedenga.
\v	13	Zvino inzwi rikauya kwaari  richiti: Simuka, Petro, baya udye.
\v	14	Asi Petro wakati: Aiwa kwete, Ishe; nokuti handina kutongodya chero chinhu chisakachena kana chine tsvina.
\v	15	Zvino inzwi  rikauyazve kwaari rwechipiri  richiti: Mwari chaanatsa, iwe usati chakasviba.
\v	16	Zvino izvi zvakaitwa kusvika katatu; mudziyo ndokukwidzwazve kudenga.
\v	17	Zvino Petro wakati achakashamiswa maari kuti chiratidzo chaaiva aona chairevei, zvino tarira, varume vaiva vatumwa kubva kuna Koneriyo, vabvunza imba yaSimoni, vakamira pasuwo,
\v	18	vakadanidzira vachibvunza kana Simoni, anonzi Petro achigarapo.
\v	19	Zvino Petro achafunga nezvechiratidzo, Mweya wakati kwaari: Tarira, varume vatatu vanokutsvaka.
\v	20	Naizvozvo simuka uburuke, uende navo usiri asina chokwadi nechinhu; nokuti ndini ndavatuma.
\v	21	Petro ndokuburukira kuvarume vaiva vatumwa kwaari kubva kuna Koneriyo, akati: Tarirai, ndini wamunotsvaka; chikonzeroi chamauyira?
\v	22	Zvino vakati: Koneriyo mukuru wezana, murume wakarurama neanotya Mwari, anopupurirwa nerudzi rwose rwevaJudha, wakayambirwa naMwari nemutumwa mutsvene kuti akudanirei kumba kwake, uye kuti anzwe mashoko kwamuri.
\v	23	Ipapo akavadanira mukati, aka vagarisa po. Zvino chifume Petro wakabuda akaenda navo, nedzimwe dzehama dzaibva paJopa dzikamuperekedza.
\p
\v	24	Zvino chifume vapinda Kesariya. NaKoneriyo waiva akavamirira, uye akadanira pamwe chete hama dzake neshamwari dzepedo.
\v	25	Zvino zvakaitika kuti Petro apinda, Koneriyo akamuchingamidza, akawira patsoka  dzake, aka munamata.
\v	26	Asi Petro wakamusimudza, achiti: Simuka; ini pachangu ndiri munhuwo.
\v	27	Zvino wakati achataura naye akapinda, akawana vazhinji vakaungana,
\v	28	ndokuti kwavari: Imwi munoziva kuti hazvisi pamutemo kumurume ari muJudha kufambidzana kana kuuya ku munhu werumwe rudzi; asi Mwari wandiratidza kuti ndirege kuti munhu haana kunaka kana kuchena;
\v	29	naizvozvowo ndauya ndisingapikisi zvandangodanwa. Naizvozvo ndinobvunza kuti mandidanira chinangwai?
\v	30	Koneriyo ndokuti: Mazuva mana apfuura kusvika paawa rino ndaiva ndichitsanya, neawa repfumbamwe ndaiva ndichinyengetera mumba mangu; zvino tarira, murume wakamira pamberi pangu ane nguvo dzinopenya,
\v	31	ndokuti: Koneriyo, munyengetero wako wanzwikwa, nemabasa ako erudo arangarirwa pamberi paMwari.
\v	32	Tumira naizvozvo kuJopa, uye udane Simoni anonzi Petro; iye anogara mumba maSimoni musuki wematehwe parutivi rwegungwa; iye kana auya achataura newe.
\v	33	Naizvozvo pakarepo ndatuma kwamuri; zvino imwi maita zvakanaka kuti mauya. Naizvozvo ikozvino tose tiripo pamberi paMwari, kuti tinzwe zvinhu zvose zvamakarairwa naIshe.
\p
\v	34	Zvino Petro wakashamisa muromo, akati: Zvirokwazvo ndinonzwisisa kuti Mwari haasi musaruri wevanhu;
\v	35	asi parudzi rupi nerupi uyo anomutya achiita kururama anogamuchirika kwaari.
\v	36	Shoko raakatuma kuvana vaIsraeri achiparidza rugare naJesu Kristu (iye ndiIshe wevese).
\v	37	Imwi munoziva shoko rakaitika paJudhiya rose, kutanga paGarirea, shure kwerubhabhatidzo Johwani rwaakaparidza;
\v	38	 raJesu weNazareta, kuti Mwari wakamuzodza sei neMweya Mutsvene nesimba, waipfuura, achiita zvakanaka uye achiporesa vose vakamanikidzwa nadhiabhorosi, nokuti Mwari waiva naye.
\v	39	Nesu tiri zvapupu zvezvinhu zvose zvaakaita, zvose munyika yevaJudha nemuJerusarema; iye wavakauraya, nekuremberedza pamuti.
\v	40	Iye Mwari waakamutsa nezuva retatu, akamupa kuti aonekwe,
\v	41	kwete kuvanhu vose, asi kuzvapupu zvakagara zvasarudzwa naMwari, kwatiri taidya nekunwa naye, mushure mekunge amuka kubva kuvakafa.
\v	42	Zvino wakatiraira kuti tiparidze kuvanhu, nekupupura kwazvo kuti ndiye wakagadzwa naMwari  kuva mutongi wevapenyu nevakafa.
\v	43	Vaporofita vose vanopupura nezvake, kuti nezita rake ani nani anotenda maari achagamuchira kanganwiro yezvivi.
\p
\v	44	Petro wakati achataura mashoko awa, Mweya Mutsvene wakaburukira pamusoro pavo vose vakanzwa shoko.
\v	45	Zvino vekudzingiswa vaitenda vakashamisika, vose vaiva vauya naPetro, nokuti nepamusoro pevahedheni chipo cheMweya Mutsvene chaiva chadururwa;
\v	46	nokuti vakavanzwa vachitaura nendimi, vachikudza Mwari. Ipapo Petro akapindura  akati:
\v	47	Kune munhu anogona kurambidza mvura here, kuti ava vasabhabhatidzwa, vakagamuchira Mweya Mutsvene sesuwo?
\v	48	Zvino akaraira kuti vabhabhatidzwe muzita raIshe. Ipapo vakamukumbira kuti agare mamwe mazuva.
\c	11
\s	Petro anotsanangura nezvaKoneriyo paJerusarema
\p
\v	1	Zvinovaapositori nehama dzaiva paJudhiya vakanzwa kuti vahedheni vagamuchirawo shoko raMwari.
\v	2	ZvinoPetro wakati akwira kuJerusarema, vekudzingiswa vakapokana naye,
\v	3	vachiti:Wakapinda muvarume vasina kudzingiswa, ukadya navo.
\v	4	AsiPetro atanga wakavarondedzera zvichitevedzana achiti:
\v	5	Inindaiva ndiri muguta reJopa ndichinyengetera, zvino muchiyeverwa ndakaona chiratidzo, umwe mudziyo wakaburuka, semucheka mukuru unoburuswa kubva kudenga nemakona mana, ndokusvika kwandiri;
\v	6	wandakatindatarisisa ndacherekedza, zvino ndakaona mhuka dzine makumbo mana dzenyika nemhuka dzesango nezvinokambaira neshiri dzedenga.
\v	7	Zvinondakanzwa inzwi richiti kwandiri: Simuka, Petro, baya udye.
\v	8	Asindakati: Aiwa kwete Ishe; nokuti hakuna chisakachena kana chine tsvina chakatongopinda mumuromo mangu.
\v	9	Asiinzwi rakandipindura rwechipiri richibva kudenga  richiti: Mwari chaachenesa, iwe usati chakasviba.
\v	10	Uye izvi zvakaitika kusvika katatu, zvose ndokukweverwazve kumusoro kudenga.
\v	11	Zvino tarira, pakarepo varume vatatu vamire pamberi pemba yandaiva ndiri, vatumwa kwandiri kubva kuKesariya.
\v	12	Mweya ndokuti kwandiri ndiende navo, ndisiri asina chokwadi nechinhu; nehama idzi nhanhatuwo dzakaenda neni, zvino takapinda mumba memurume  uyu;
\v	13	iye akatipira kuti wakaona sei mutumwa amire mumba make, achiti kwaari: Tumira varume kuJopa, ugodana Simoni, anonzi Petro,
\v	14	achakuudza mashoko, auchaponeswa nawo iwe nemhuri yako yose.
\v	15	Zvino ndakati ndichatanga kutaura, Mweya Mutsvene wakaburukira pamusoro pavo, sezva zvakaitawo pamusoro pedu pakutanga.
\v	16	Zvino ndakarangarira shoko raIshe, kuti wakataura sei  achiti: Johwani zvirokwazvo wakabhabhatidza nemvura, asi imwi muchabhabhatidzwa neMweya Mutsvene.
\v	17	Naizvozvo kana Mwari avapa chipo chakafanana ne chaakapa kwatiriwo, takatenda kuna Ishe Jesu Kristu, ini ndaiva ani waigona kudzivisa Mwari?
\v	18	Zvino vakati vanzwa zvinhu izvozvi vakanyarara, vakarumbidza Mwari, vachiti: Naizvozvo Mwari wakapawo kuvahedheni kutendeukira kuupenyu.
\s	Bhanabhasi naSauro vanoparidza paAndiyokiya
\p
\v	19	Zvino avo vakapararira nekushushwa kwakaitika pamusoro paSitefano vakagura kusvika paFenikia neSaipuresi neAndiyokiya, vasingatauri shoko kuna ani asi kuvaJudha chete.
\v	20	Asi kwaiva nevamwe vavo, varume vaSaipuresi nevaSairini vakati vasvika kuAndiyokiya, vakataura kuvaHerenisiti, vachiparidza mashoko akanaka  aIshe Jesu.
\v	21	Neruoko rwaIshe rwaiva navo; uye uwandu ukuru hwakatenda hukatendeukira kuna Ishe.
\v	22	Zvino shoko pamusoro pavo rikanzwikwa munzeve dzekereke yaiva paJerusarema; zvino vakatuma Bhanabhasi kuti agure kunosvika Andiyokiya;
\v	23	iye asvika ndokuona nyasha dzaMwari akafara, akavakurudzira vose kuti varambire panaIshe nekuzvipira kwemoyo;
\v	24	nokuti waiva murume wakanaka, uye azere neMweya Mutsvene uye nekutenda; chaunga chikuru ndokuwedzerwa kuna Ishe.
\v	25	Bhanabhasi ndokubuda akaenda kuTaso kunotsvaka Sauro.
\v	26	Zvino wakati amuwana akamuuisa kuAndiyokiya. Zvino zvikaitika kuti gore rose vakaungana mukereke vakadzidzisa chaunga chikuru; uye vadzidzi vakatanga kunzi vaKristu paAndiyokiya.
\s	VaKristu vanotumira zvipo kuhama dzepaJudhiya
\p
\v	27	Zvino nemazuva iwayo, vaporofita vakaburukira kuAndiyokiya vachibva kuJerusarema.
\v	28	Zvino kwakasimuka umwe wavo wainzi Agabhusi, ndokuratidza neMweya kuti nzara huru ichavapo panyika yose; yakaitikawo panguva yaKiraudhio Kesari.
\v	29	Uye vadzidzi vakazvipira kuti chero ani paaigona napo, umwe neumwe wavo atume zvinobatsira kuhama dzigere muJudhiya;
\v	30	zvavakaitawo, vakatumira kuvakuru neruoko rwaBhanabhasi naSauro.
\c	12
\s	Petro anosungwa naHerodhi ndokubudiswa nemutumwa waIshe
\p
\v	1	Nenguvaiyoyo Herodhi mambo wakaisa maoko kuti atambudze vamwe kubva mukereke.
\v	2	NdokuurayaJakobho mukoma waJohwani nemunondo.
\v	3	Zvinowakati achiona kuti zvinofadza vaJudha, wakapfuurira mberi akabata Petrowo; zvino aiva mazuva ezvingwa zvisina mbiriso;
\v	4	akatiamubatawo akaisa mutirongo, aka mukumikidza kumapoka mana evanhu vana emauto kuti vamurinde, achida kumuisa kuvanhu mushure mepasika.
\v	5	NaizvozvoPetro wakachengetwa mutirongo; asi munyengetero wakaenderera mberi uchiitwa nekereke kuna Mwari uchiitirwa iye.
\v	6	ZvinoHerodhi wakati oda kumubudisa, usiku ihwohwo Petro waiva arere pakati pemauto maviri, akasungwa nemaketani maviri; nevarindi pamberi pemusuwo vakarinda tirongo.
\v	7	Zvinotarira, mutumwa waIshe wakamirapo, chiedza ndokupenya muchitokisi; zvino akarova rutivi rwaPetro, akamumutsa achiti: Simuka nekukurumidza. Zvino maketani ake akawa pamaoko  ake.
\v	8	Mutumwandokuti kwaari: Zvisunge chiuno, uye sunga manyatera ako. Ndokuita saizvozvo. Zvino akati kwaari: Monera nguvo yako, unditevere.
\v	9	Zvinowakabuda akamutevera; uye asingazivi kuti ichokwadi chinoitwa nemutumwa, asi waifunga kuti anoona chiratidzo.
\v	10	Zvino vakati vapfuura chirindo chekutanga nechepiri, vakasvika pasuwo reutare raipinda muguta; iro rakavazarukira rega; zvino vakabuda, vakapfuura nzira imwe yemumusha, pakarepo mutumwa ndokubva kwaari.
\v	11	Zvino Petro, wakati apengenuka, akati: Zvino ndinoziva zvirokwazvo kuti Ishe watuma mutumwa wake, uye wandisunungura paruoko rwaHerodhi netarisiro yose yevanhu vevaJudha.
\v	12	Zvino wakati arangarira akaenda kumba kwaMaria mai vaJohwani wainzi Mariko, vazhinji pavaiva vakaungana vachinyengetera.
\v	13	Zvino Petro wakati agogodza pagonhi resuwo, murandakadzi wakauya kuzoteerera, wainzi Rodha.
\v	14	Zvino wakati achiziva inzwi raPetro, haana kuzarura suwo nekuda kwemufaro, asi wakamhanyira mukati akavaudza kuti Petro amire pamberi pesuwo.
\v	15	Asi vakati kwaari: Unopenga. Asi wakaramba achisimbisa kuti zvaiva zvakadaro. Ivo ndokuti: Mutumwa wake.
\v	16	Asi Petro wakaramba achigogodza; zvino vakati vazarura vakamuona, vakashamisika.
\v	17	Zvino wakavaninira neruoko kuti vanyarare, akavarondedzera kuti Ishe wakamubudisa sei mutirongo, akati: Pirai kuna Jakobho nekuhama zvinhu izvi. Zvino akabuda akaenda kune imwe nzvimbo.
\p
\v	18	Zvino kwakati kwaedza, bongozozo risati riri diki rikavapo pakati pemauto, kuti chii chakawira Petro.
\v	19	Zvino Herodhi wakati amutsvaka akasa muwana, wakabvunzisisa varindi, akaraira kuti vabviswe. Zvino akabva Judhiya akaburukira Kesariya akagara ko.
\s	Kufa kwaHerodhi
\p
\v	20	Zvino Herodhi wakatsamwira zvikuru veTire nevaSidhoni; asi vakauya kwaari nemoyo umwe, vakashamwaridzana naBhurasto mutariri weimba yekurara yamambo, vakakumbira rugare, nokuti nyika yavo yaipiwa kudya ne nyika yamambo.
\v	21	Zvino nezuva rakatarwa Herodhi wakapfeka nguvo dzeumambo, akagara pachigaro chekutonga, akaita nhaurwa kwavari.
\v	22	Vanhu ndokudanidzira  vachiti: Inzwi raMwari, uye kwete remunhu.
\v	23	Zvino pakarepo mutumwa waIshe wakamurova, nokuti haana kupa Mwari rukudzo; ndokudyiwa nehonye, akapera.
\p
\v	24	Asi shoko raMwari rakakura rikawanda.
\v	25	Zvino Bhanabhasi naSauro vakadzoka vachibva kuJerusarema, vapedzisa basa, vakaenda naJohwaniwo wainzi Mariko.
\c	13
\s	Rwendo rwaSauro rwekutanga. Kutumwa kwaSauro naBhanabhasi
\p
\v	1	Zvinopaivapo muAndiyokiya mukereke ipapo vamwe vaporofita nevadzidzisi, Bhanabhasi naSimioniwo wainzi Nigeri, naRukio muKurini, naManaeni waiva arerwa pamwe naHerodhi umwe wevatungamiriri vana, naSauro.
\v	2	Vakativachishumira kuna Ishe nekutsanya, Mweya Mutsvene akati: Nditsaurirei Bhanabhasi pamwe naSauro pabasa randakavadanira.
\v	3	Ipapovatsanya nekunyengetera nekuisa maoko pamusoro pavo, vakavaendesa.
\s	Vanodziviswa naErimasi paPafosi
\p
\v	4	Naizvozvozvirokwazvo ivo vakati vatumwa naMweya Mutsvene, vakaburukira Serukia, vakabvapowo vakaenda kuSaipuresi nechikepe.
\v	5	Zvinovasvika paSarami, vakaparidza shoko raMwari mumasinagoge evaJudha; uye vaiva vanaJohwaniwo mubatsiri.
\v	6	Zvinovakati vagura nemuchiwi kusvika paPafosi, vakawana imwe n'anga, muporofita wenhema, muJudha, wainzi Bhari-Jesu.
\v	7	Waingeane mutungamiriri Sejiyo Pauro, murume wakachenjera. Iye wakadanira kwaari Bhanabhasi naSauro akatsvaka kunzwa shoko raMwari.

\s	Firipi nemuranda waKandase
\p
\v	26	Zvino mutumwa waIshe wakataura kuna Firipi, achiti: Simuka, upfuurire kuchamhembe kunzira inoburuka kubva kuJerusarema ichienda kuGaza; iyo irenje.
\v	27	Zvino wakasimuka akaenda; zvino tarira murume weEtiopia, mutenwa, gurukota guru raKandase mambokadzi wevaEtiopia, waiona nezvefuma yake yose, uye waiva auya kuJerusarema kuzonamata,
\v	28	waiva achidzokera uye agere mungoro yake, achiverenga muporofita Isaya.
\v	29	Mweya ndokuti kuna Firipi: Swedera unamatire pangoro iyi.
\v	30	Firipi ndokumhanyirako, akamunzwa achiverenga muporofita Isaya, ndokuti: Zvino unonzwisisa zvaunoverenga here?
\v	31	Iye ndokuti: Zvino ndingagona sei, kunze kwekuti umwe munhu andirairira? Zvino akakumbira Firipi kuti akwire agare naye.
\v	32	Zvino ndima yerugwaro yaaiverenga yaiva iyi: Wakatungamirirwa segwai  rinoenda kunobaiwa; uye segwayana rinyerere pamberi pemuvevuri waro, saizvozvo haana kushamisa muromo wake.
\v	33	Mukuninipiswa kwake mutongo wake wakabviswa, zvino ndiani acharondedzera zera rake? Nokuti upenyu hwake hunobviswa panyika.
\v	34	Mutenwa ndokupindura Firipi akati: Ndinokukumbirisa, muporofita anoreva izvi maererano nani? Nezvake amene kana nezveumwewo?
\v	35	Firipi ndokuzarura muromo wake; akatanga parugwaro irworwo, ndokumuparidzira Jesu.
\v	36	Zvino pavaifamba munzira, vakasvika pane imwe mvura; mutenwa ndokuti: Heyi mvura; chii chinondidzivirira kubhabhatidzwa?
\v	37	Firipi ndokuti: Kana uchitenda nemoyo wose, zvinotenderwa. Akapindura ndokuti: Ndinotenda kuti Jesu Kristu Mwanakomana waMwari.
\v	38	Zvino akaraira ngoro kuti imire; ndokuburukira mumvura vari vaviri, Firipi nemutenwa; ndokumubhabhatidza.
\v	39	Zvino vakati vakwira kubva mumvura, Mweya waIshe wakabvuta Firipi; mutenwa ndokusazomuonazve; nokuti wakafamba nenzira yake achifara.
\v	40	Asi Firipi wakawanikwa paAzota; uye achipfuura wakaparidza evhangeri kumaguta ose, kusvika asvika paKesariya.
\c	9
\s	Kutendeuka kwaSauro
\p
\v	1	ZvinoSauro, achiri kufema kutyisa nekuuraya akamirisana nevadzidzi vaIshe, wakaenda kumupristi mukuru,
\v	2	akakumbirakwaari magwaro ekuenda Dhamasiko kumasinagoge, kuti kana awana chero venzira iyo, vose varume nevakadzi, avauise vakasungwa kuJerusarema.
\v	3	Zvinopakufamba, wakaswedera kuDhamasiko; zvino kamwe-kamwe kwakapenya kwakamupoteredza chiedza kubva kudenga;
\v	4	zvinowakawira pasi, akanzwa inzwi richiti kwaari: Sauro, Sauro, unondishushirei?
\v	5	Zvinoakati: Ndimwi ani, Ishe? Ishe ndokuti: Ndini Jesu waunoshusha iwe; zvinokuremera kukavana nezvibayo.
\v	6	Achidederawonekushamisika wakati: Ishe, chii chamunoda kuti ndiite? Ishe ndoku ti kwaari: Simuka upinde muguta, zvino uchaudzwa zvaunofanira kuita.
\v	7	Zvinovarume vaifamba naye vakamira vasina remuromo, vachinzwa inzwi, asi vasingaoni munhu.
\v	8	ZvinoSauro akasimuka kubva pasi; asi meso ake azarurwa, haana kuona munhu. Zvino vakamubata ruoko, vakamuuisa kuDhamasiko.
\v	9	Akavanemazuva matatu asingaoni, uye asingadyi kana kunwa.
\p
\v	10	Zvino paiva pane umwe mudzidzi paDhamasiko wainzi Ananiasi; Ishe ndokuti kwaari muchiratidzo: Ananiasi! Zvino akati: Tarirai ndiri  pano Ishe.
\v	11	Ishe ndoku ti kwaari: Simuka uende munzira yemuguta inonzi Yakarurama, ugobvunza mumba maJudhasi  munhu anonzi Sauro, weTaso; nokuti tarira anonyengetera,
\v	12	uye waona muchiratidzo murume anonzi Ananiasi achipinda nekuisa ruoko paari, kuti aonezve.
\v	13	Asi Ananiasi wakapindura  akati: Ishe, ndakanzwa nevazhinji zvemurume uyu, zvakakura sei zvakaipa zvaakaitira vatsvene venyu paJerusarema;
\v	14	uye pano ane simba kubva kuvapristi vakuru, kusunga vose vanodana zita renyu.
\v	15	Asi Ishe wakati kwaari: Enda, nokuti iye mudziyo wakasarudzwa kwandiri, kutakurira zita rangu pamberi pevahedheni nemadzimambo nevana vaIsraeri;
\v	16	nokuti ini ndichamuratidza kuti anofanira kutambura zvakadini nekuda kwezita rangu.
\v	17	Zvino Ananiasi wakaenda ndokupinda mumba, ndokuisa maoko pamusoro pake akati: Sauro hama, Ishe wandituma, Jesu wakaonekwa kwauri munzira yawakauya nayo, kuti uonezve uye uzadzwe neMweya Mutsvene.
\v	18	Zvino pakarepo kwakawa mumeso ake zvinenge mafunurwa, akaonazve pakarepo, uye asimuka wakabhabhatidzwa.
\v	19	Zvino wakati agamuchira kudya akawana simba. Sauro wakavawo nevadzidzi vaiva paDhamasiko mamwe mazuva.
\p
\v	20	Zvino pakarepo wakaparidza Kristu mumasinagoge, kuti iye Mwanakomana waMwari.
\v	21	Asi vose vaka zvinzwa vakashamisika ndokuti: Ko uyu haasi iye waiparadza avo vaidana zita iri muJerusarema here, uye akauyira izvozvo pano, kuti avatinhire vakasungwa kuvapristi vakuru?
\v	22	Asi Sauro wakaramba achisimbiswa, akavhiringidza vaJudha vaigara paDhamasiko, achibudisa pachena kuti uyu ndiye Kristu.
\s	Sauro anodzingwa paDhamasiko neJerusarema
\p
\v	23	Zvino mazuva mazhinji akati azadziswa, vaJudha vakarangana pamwe chete kuti vamuuraye;
\v	24	asi zano ravo rakazikanwa kuna Sauro. Vakarindawo masuwo masikati neusiku, kuti vamuuraye;
\v	25	asi vadzidzi vakamutora usiku, vaka muburusa neparusvingo vachi muburusa  ari mudengu.
\p
\v	26	Zvino Sauro wakati asvika kuJerusarema, akaidza kuzvibatanidza kuvadzidzi; asi vose vaimutya, vasingatendi kuti mudzidzi.
\v	27	Asi Bhanabhasi wakamutora aka muuisa kuvaapositori, ndokurondedzera kwavari kuti wakaona Ishe sei munzira, uye kuti waiva ataura kwaari, uye kuti wakataura akashinga sei paDhamasiko muzita raJesu.
\v	28	Zvino wakava navo achipinda nekubuda paJerusarema,
\v	29	ndokutaura akashinga muzita raIshe Jesu, akataurawo achipikisa vaHerenisiti; asi vakaidza kumuuraya.
\v	30	Asi hama dzakati dza zviziva dzikamuburusira Kesariya, ndokumutumira kuTaso.
\p
\v	31	Naizvozvo kereke paJudhiya yose neGarirea neSamariya dzikava nerugare, dzikavakwa, dzikawedzerwa, dzichifamba mukutya Ishe nemunyaradzo yeMweya Mutsvene.
\s	Kuporeswa kwaEneasi
\p
\v	32	Zvino zvakaitika Petro achigura  nzvimbo dzose, akaburukirawo kuvatsvene vaiva vagere paRidha.
\v	33	Akawanapo umwe munhu wainzi Eneasi, waiva arere panhovo makore masere, akafa mitezo.
\v	34	Petro ndokuti kwaari: Eneasi, Jesu Kristu anokuporesa; simuka, uzviwaridzire. Zvino akasimuka pakarepo.
\v	35	Zvino vose vaiva vagere Ridha neSaroni vakamuona, vakatendeukira kuna Ishe.
\s	Kumutswa kwaTabhita parufu
\p
\v	36	Zvino paivapo paJopa umwe mudzidzikadzi wainzi Tabhita, ndiko kuti Dhokasi kana zvichitsanangurwa; uyo waiva azere nemabasa akanaka nemabasa erudo aaiita.
\v	37	Zvino zvakaitika mumazuva iwayo kuti iye akarwara ndokufa; vakati vamushambidza, vakamuradzika mukamuri yekumusoro.
\v	38	Zvino Ridha zvaraiva riri pedo neJopa, vadzidzi vaiva vanzwa kuti Petro waiva aripo, vakatumira kwaari varume vaviri, vachikumbira zvikuru kuti asanonoka kuuya kwavari.
\v	39	Zvino Petro wakasimuka akaenda navo; akati asvika vakamuisa kuimba yekumusoro, uye chirikadzi dzose dzikamirapo naye dzichichema dzichiratidza nguvo refu nezvipfeko Dhokasi zvaaiva aita achiri navo.
\v	40	Asi Petro wakavabudisira panze vose akafugama akanyengetera; ndokutendeukira kumutumbi, akati: Tabhita, muka! Akazarura meso ake; zvino wakati achiona Petro, akagara.
\v	41	Zvino wakamupa ruoko, akamusimudza; ndokuti adana vatsvene nechirikadzi, akamuisa pamberi  pavo achirarama.
\v	42	Zvino zvikazikanwa paJopa yose; vazhinji ndokutenda kuna Ishe.
\v	43	Zvino zvikaitika kuti wakagara mazuva mazhinji paJopa neumwe Simoni musuki wematehwe.
