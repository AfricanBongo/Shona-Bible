\b	Evhangeri yakanyorwa naMariko
\c	1
\s	Johwani Mubhabhatidzi anoparidza kuuya kwaJesu
\p
\v	1	Kutangakweevhangeri yaJesu Kristu, Mwanakomana waMwari;
\v	2	sezvazvakanyorwamuvaporofita  zvichinzi: Tarira, ini ndinotuma mutumwa wangu pamberi pechiso chako, achagadzira nzira yako pamberi pako.
\v	3	Inzwireanodanidzira murenje,  richiti: Gadzirai mugwagwa waIshe; ruramisai nzira dzake.
\v	4	KwakauyaJohwani achibhabhatidza murenje, achiparidza rubhabhatidzo rwekutendeukira kukanganwiro yezvivi;
\v	5	zvinokwakabudira kwaari nyika yose yeJudhiya, naivo veJerusarema, ndokubhabhatidzwa vose naye murwizi rweJoridhani vachireurura zvivi zvavo.
\v	6	UyeJohwani waiva akapfeka makushe ekamera, nebhanhire rechikumba rakapoteredza chiuno chake, waidya mhashu neuchi hwedondo.
\p
\v	7	Akaparidza,achiti: Kunouya ane simba kupfuura ini shure kwangu, wandisina kufanira kukotama kuti ndisunungure tambo yeshangu dzake.
\v	8	Inizvirokwazvo ndakubhabhatidzai nemvura; asi iye achakubhabhatidzai neMweya Mutsvene.
\s	Kubhabhatidzwa kwaJesu
\p
\v	9	Zvinozvakaitika nemazuva ayo  kuti Jesu wakabva Nazareta yeGarirea, akabhabhatidzwa naJohwani muna Joridhani.
\v	10	Pakarepo achibuda mumvura, wakaona matenga akazarurwa, neMweya senjiva achiburukira pamusoro pake.
\v	11	Zvino kwakaitika inzwi richibva kumatenga  richiti: Iwe uri Mwanakomana wangu anodikanwa, wandinofara maari kwazvo.
\s	Kuedzwa kwaJesu
\p
\v	12	Zvino pakarepo Mweya wakamutinhira kurenje.
\v	13	Akavako murenje mazuva makumi mana achiidzwa naSatani, uye waiva ane mhuka dzesango, vatumwa vachimushandira.
\s	Jesu anodana vadzidzi vekutanga
\p
\v	14	Zvino shure kwekukumikidzwa kwaJohwani, Jesu wakasvika muGarirea, achiparidza evhangeri yeushe hwaMwari,
\v	15	achiti: Nguva yazadziswa, uye ushe hwaMwari hwaswedera; tendeukai, mutende evhangeri.
\p
\v	16	Wakati achifamba-famba parutivi rwegungwa reGarirea, akaona Simoni naAndiriya munin'ina wake vachikandira mumbure mugungwa; nokuti vaiva vari varedzi.
\v	17	Jesu akati kwavari: Nditeverei, ndichakuitai muve varedzi vevanhu.
\v	18	Pakarepo vakasiya mimbure yavo, ndokumutevera.
\v	19	Wakati apfuura zvishoma ipapo, akaona Jakobho waZebhedhi naJohwani munin'ina wake, naivo vari muchikepe vachigadzira mimbure.
\v	20	Pakarepo akavadana; ndokusiya baba vavo Zebhedhi muchikepe nevarikichi, vakamutevera.
\s	Jesu anoporesa munhu waiva nedhimoni paKapenaume
\p
\v	21	Zvino vakapinda muKapenaume; zvino pakarepo nesabata wakapinda musinagoge, akadzidzisa.
\v	22	Vakashamisika nedzidziso yake; nokuti wakavadzidzisa seane simba, uye kwete sevanyori.
\p
\v	23	Zvino kwaiva musinagoge ravo nemunhu waiva ane mweya wetsvina, akadanidzira,
\v	24	achiti: Regai, tinei nemwi, Jesu weNazareta? Mauya kuzotiparadza here? Ndinokuzivai kuti ndimwi ani: Mutsvene waMwari.
\v	25	Jesu ndokumutsiura achiti: Nyarara, ubude maari!
\v	26	Zvino mweya wetsvina wakati amugwinhisa, ndokudanidzira nenzwi guru, akabuda kwaari.
\v	27	Vakashamisika vose, kusvika vabvunzana, vachiti: Chii ichi? Idzidziso itsva yei iyi? Nokuti nesimba anoraira kunyange mweya yetsvina, uye inomuteerera.
\v	28	Zvino pakarepo shoko rake rikabudira panyika yose yakapoteredza Garirea.
\s	Jesu anoporesa mai vemukadzi waPetro nevamwe
\p
\v	29	Zvino pakarepo vakati vachibuda musinagoge, vakapinda mumba maSimoni naAndiriya vana Jakobho naJohwani.
\v	30	Asi mai vemukadzi waSimoni vaiva varere vane fivhiri, pakarepo vakamuudza nezvavo;
\v	31	akaswedera, akavabata ruoko, akavamutsa; fivhiri ikavarega pakarepo, ivo vakavashandira.
\p
\v	32	Zvino ava madekwani, zuva rovira, vakauisa kwaari vose vaiva vachirwara nevaiva vane madhimoni.
\v	33	Zvino guta rose raiva rakaungana pamukova.
\v	34	Akaporesa vazhinji vaiva vachirwara nezvirwere zvakasiyana-siyana, nekubudisa madhimoni mazhinji; uye haana kutendera madhimoni kutaura, nokuti aimuziva.
\p
\v	35	Zvino mangwanani kuchine rima guru, wakasimuka, akabuda, akaenda kunzvimbo murenje, akanonyengeterapo.
\v	36	Simoni nevaiva vanaye vakamutsvakisisa.
\v	37	Zvino vakati vamuwana vakati kwaari: Vose vanokutsvakai.
\v	38	Ndokubva ati kwavari: Ngatiende kumisha iri pedo, kuti ndinoparidzawo ikoko, nokuti ndizvo zvandakabudira.
\v	39	Akabva aparidza mumasinagoge avo paGarirea rose, achibudisa madhimoni.
\s	Jesu anoporesa munhu ane maperembudzi
\p
\v	40	Zvino kwakauya kwaari ane maperembudzi, achimukumbirisa, ndokumufugamira achiti kwaari: Kana muchida munogona kundinatsa.
\v	41	Jesu akanzwa tsitsi, akatandavadza ruoko, akamubata akati kwaari: Ndinoda, natswa;
\v	42	achangotaura, maperembudzi akabva kwaari pakarepo, akanatswa.
\v	43	Zvino akamuraira zvikuru, akamurega achienda pakarepo,
\v	44	akati kwaari: Ona kuti urege kuudza munhu chinhu; asi enda, uzviratidze kumupristi, ubaire pamusoro pekunatswa kwako Mozisi zvaakaraira, chive chapupu kwavari.
\v	45	Asi iye wakabuda akatanga kuparidza zvikuru nekuvavaza kunze shoko  iro, zvekuti waiva asingachagoni kupinda muguta pachena, asi waiva ari kunze kunzvimbo dzerenje. Vakauya kwaari vachibva kumativi ose.
\c	10
\s	Kurambana kwemurume nemukadzi
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakati asimukapo, akauya kumiganhu yeJudhiya, achigura nemhiri kwaJoridhani; zvaunga ndokuunganirazve kwaari; zvino sezvaaiva ajaira, akavadzidzisa zvekare.
\p
\v	2	VaFarisivakauya kwaari, vakamubvunza  vachiti: Zvinotenderwa here kuti murume arambe mukadzi? vachimuidza.
\v	3	Akapindura,akati kwavari: Mozisi wakakurairai chii?
\v	4	Zvinovakati: Mozisi wakatendera kunyora rugwaro rwerambano, neku muramba.
\v	5	Jesuakapindura akati kwavari: Nekuda kweukukutu hwemoyo wenyu wakakunyorerai murairo uyu.
\v	6	Asikubva pakutanga kwezvisikwa Mwari wakavaita murume nemukadzi.
\v	7	Nekudakweizvozvo munhu achasiya baba namai vake, anamatire mukadzi wake;
\v	8	zvinoava vaviri vachava nyama imwe. Naizvozvo havasichiri vaviri, asi nyama imwe.
\v	9	NaizvozvoMwari zvaakabatanidza, ngapasava nemunhu anoparadzanisa.
\p
\v	10	Mumba vadzidzi vake vakapamha kumubvunza pamusoro pazvo.
\v	11	Zvino akati kwavari: Ani nani anoramba mukadzi wake, akawana umwe, anoita upombwe kwaari.
\v	12	Uye kana mukadzi achiramba murume wake, achiwanikwa neumwe, anoita upombwe.
\s	Jesu anoropafadza vanana
\p
\v	13	Zvino vaiuya nevana vadiki kwaari, kuti avabate; asi vadzidzi vakatsiura avo vaiuya na vo.
\v	14	Zvino Jesu wakati achi zviona, akatsamwa zvikuru, akati kwavari: Tenderai vana vadiki kuuya kwandiri, uye musavadzivisa, nokuti ushe hwaMwari ndohwevakadai.
\v	15	Zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Ani nani asingagamuchiri ushe hwaMwari semucheche, haangatongopindi mahuri.
\v	16	Uye akavagumbatira, achiisa maoko pamusoro pavo, akavaropafadza.
\s	Jaya mufumi
\p
\v	17	Zvino wakati achibudira kunzira, umwe wakamumhanyira, akamufugamira akamubvunza,  achiti: Mudzidzisi wakanaka, ndingaitei kuti ndigare nhaka yeupenyu husingaperi?
\v	18	Jesu akati kwaari: Unondiidzirei wakanaka? Hakuna wakanaka, kunze kweumwe,  iye Mwari.
\v	19	Unoiziva mirairo  inoti: Usaita upombwe, usauraya, usaba, usapupura nhema, usabiridzira, kudza baba namai vako.
\v	20	Zvino akapindura akati kwaari: Mudzidzisi, izvi zvose ndakazvichengeta kubva paudiki hwangu.
\v	21	Jesu ndokumutarira akamuda, akati kwaari: Chinhu chimwe chaunoshaiwa; enda, utengese zvose zvaunazvo, upe varombo, zvino uchava nefuma kudenga; ugouya utakure muchinjikwa, unditevere.
\v	22	Asi asuwa neshoko iro, wakaenda neshungu, nokuti waiva ane nhumbi zhinji.
\p
\v	23	Ipapo Jesu akaringa-ringa, akati kuvadzidzi vake: Vacharemerwa sei vane fuma kupinda muushe hwaMwari!
\v	24	Vadzidzi vakashamisika nemashoko ake. Asi Jesu wakapindurazve, akati kwavari: Vana, zvinorema sei kune vanovimba nefuma kupinda muushe hwaMwari.
\v	25	Zvingarerukira kamera kudarika nemuburi retsono, pane kuti mufumi apinde muushe hwaMwari.
\v	26	Ipapo vakashamisika zvisingaverengeki, vachitaurirana  vachiti: Ndiani zvino angagoponeswa?
\v	27	Zvino Jesu wakati achivatarira, akati: Kuvanhu hazvibviri, asi kwete kuna Mwari; nokuti kuna Mwari zvinhu zvose zvinobvira.
\p
\v	28	Petro akatanga kuti kwaari: Tarirai, isu takasiya zvose tikakuteverai.
\v	29	Jesu akapindura akati: Zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Hakuna munhu wakasiya imba, kana vanin'ina nemadzikoma, kana hanzvadzi, kana baba, kana mai, kana mukadzi, kana vana, kana minda, nekuda kwangu nekweevhangeri,
\v	30	asingazogamuchiri kane zana ikozvino nenguva ino, dzimba, nevanin'ina nemadzikoma, nehanzvadzi, nanamai, nevana, neminda, pamwe nekushushwa, neupenyu husingaperi munguva inouya.
\v	31	Asi vazhinji vekutanga vachava vekupedzisira, nevekupedzisira vekutanga.
\s	Jesu anovaudza zverufu rwake nekumuka kwake
\p
\v	32	Zvino vachiri munzira vachikwira kuJerusarema; Jesu akavatungamirira; vakashamisika; zvino pavaitevera, vaitya. Akatorazve vanegumi nevaviri, akatanga kuvaudza zvinhu zvaiva zvichizoitika kwaari,
\v	33	achiti: Tarirai, tinokwira kuJerusarema, uye Mwanakomana wemunhu achakumikidzwa kuvapristi vakuru nekuvanyori, uye vachamutongera rufu, nekumukumikidza kuvahedheni,
\v	34	vachamusveveredza, vachamurova netyava, nekumupfira, nekumuuraya; zvino nezuva retatu achamukazve.
\s	Vana vaZebhedhi vanokumbira kuva vakuru
\p
\v	35	Zvino Jakobho naJohwani, vanakomana vaZebhedhi, vakauya kwaari vachiti: Mudzidzisi, tinoda kuti mutiitire chero chatichakumbira.
\v	36	Akati kwavari: Chii chamunoda kuti ndikuitirei?
\v	37	Vakati kwaari: Tipei kuti tigare, umwe kuruoko rwenyu rwerudyi, uye umwe kuruboshwe rwenyu mukubwinya kwenyu.
\v	38	Asi Jesu wakati kwavari: Hamuzivi chamunokumbira. Mungagona kunwa mukombe wandinonwa ini here, nekubhabhatidzwa nerubhabhatidzo rwandinobhabhatidzwa narwo ini here?
\v	39	Vakati kwaari: Tingagona. Jesu ndokuti kwavari: Muchanwa zvirokwazvo mukombe wandinonwa ini, nekubhabhatidzwa norubhabhatidzo rwandinobhabhatidzwa narwo ini;
\v	40	asi kugara kuruoko rwangu rwerudyi nekuruboshwe rwangu, hazvisi zvangu kupa, asi zvevakazvigadzirirwa.
\p
\v	41	Vanegumi vakati vachi zvinzwa, vakatanga kutsamwira zvikuru Jakobho naJohwani.
\v	42	Asi Jesu akavadanira kwaari akati kwavari: Munoziva kuti avo vanonzi machinda evahedheni anovadzvinyirira, nevakuru vavo vanoshandisa simba pamusoro pavo.
\v	43	Zvino hazvingadaro pakati penyu; asi ani nani anoda kuva mukuru pakati penyu, achava mushandiri wenyu;
\v	44	uye ani nani wenyu anoda kuva wekutanga, achava mushandi wevose.
\v	45	Nokuti kunyange Mwanakomana wemunhu haana kuuya kuzoshumirwa, asi kushumira, nekupa upenyu hwake  ruve rudzikunuro rwevazhinji.
\s	Jesu anosvinudza Bhatimeo paJeriko
\p
\v	46	Vakasvika kuJeriko. Zvino wakati achibuda Jeriko nevadzidzi vake nechaunga chikuru, Bhatimeo bofu, mwanakomana waTimeo, aiva agere padivi penzira achipemha.
\v	47	Wakati anzwa kuti ndiJesu weNazareta, akatanga kudanidzira, nekuti: Jesu Mwanakomana waDhavhidhi, ndinzwirei tsitsi!
\v	48	Vazhinji vakamutsiura kuti anyarare; asi iye wakanyanya kudanidzira kwazvo,  achiti: Mwanakomana waDhavhidhi, ndinzwirei tsitsi!
\v	49	Jesu ndokumira, akati adanwe; vakadana bofu vachiti kwariri: Tsunga moyo; simuka, anokudana.
\v	50	Ipapo rikarasa nguvo yaro rikasimuka, rikauya kuna Jesu.
\v	51	Jesu akapindura, akati kwariri: Unoda kuti ndikuitirei? Bofu rikati kwaari: Rabhoni, kuti ndionezve.
\v	52	Jesu akati kwariri: Enda; rutendo rwako rwakuponesa. Pakarepo rikaonazve, rikatevera Jesu munzira.
\c	11
\s	Jesu anogamuchirwa paJerusarema
\p
\v	1	Vakativasvika pedo neJerusarema, paBhetifage neBhetaniya, pagomo reMiorivhi, akatuma vaviri vevadzidzi vake,
\v	2	akatikwavari: Fambai muende kumusha wakatarisana nemwi; pakarepo kana muchipinda mauri, muchawana dhongwana rakasungirwa, risina munhu wakamborigara; murisunugure, mugouya  naro.
\v	3	Kanamunhu akati kwamuri: Munoitirei izvi? Muti: Ishe anorida. Pakarepo acharitumira pano.
\v	4	Vakaenda,vakanowana dhongwana rakasungirwa pamukova kunze, panzira yemumusha; vakarisunungura.
\v	5	Vamwevevaiva vamirepo vakati kwavari: Munoitei muchisunungura dhongwana?
\v	6	VakavaudzaJesu sezvaakaraira; vakavarega vachienda.
\v	7	Zvinovakauya nedhongwana kuna Jesu, vakakandira nguvo dzavo pamusoro paro, akagara pamusoro paro.
\v	8	Nevazhinjivakawarira nguvo dzavo munzira, nevamwe vakatema masanzu kubva pamiti vakawarira munzira.
\v	9	Zvinoavo vaiva vachitungamira nevaiva vachitevera vakadanidzira, vachiti: Hosana! Wakaropafadzwa anouya muzita raIshe;
\v	10	hwakaropafadzwa ushe hwababa vedu Dhavhidhi, hunouya muzita raIshe! Hosana kumusoro-soro!
\p
\v	11	Jesu ndokupinda muJerusarema, nemutembere; akati aringa-ringa zvinhu zvose, yava nguva yemadekwani, akabuda akaenda kuBhetaniya nevanegumi nevaviri.
\s	Jesu anotuka muonde waiva usingabereki
\p
\v	12	Uye mangwana vachibuda kubva Bhetaniya, akanzwa nzara.
\v	13	Wakati achiona muonde uri kure, une mashizha, akaenda ko achiti zvimwe angawana chinhu pauri; asi asvika pauri haana kuwana chinhu kunze kwemashizha; nokuti yaiva isiri nguva yemaonde.
\v	14	Zvino Jesu akapindura, akati kwauri: Kusava nemunhu anozodyazve zvibereko pauri kusvika kusingaperi. Vadzidzi vake vaka zvinzwa.
\s	Jesu anonatsa tembere
\p
\v	15	Vakasvika kuJerusarema; Jesu akapinda mutembere, akatanga kudzingira panze avo vaitengesa nevaitenga mutembere; akapidigura matafura evaitsinhanisa mari, nezvigaro zvaivo vaitengesa njiva.
\v	16	Zvino akasatendera kuti munhu atakure nhumbi achipfuura nemutembere.
\p
\v	17	Akadzidzisa, achiti kwavari: Hakuna kunyorwa here kuchinzi: Imba yangu ichanzi imba yekunyengetera nemarudzi ose? Asi imwi makaiita bako remakororo.
\p
\v	18	Zvino vanyori nevapristi vakuru vaka zvinzwa, vakatsvaka kuti vangamuparadza sei; nokuti vaiva vachimutya, nokuti chaunga chose chakashamisika nedzidziso yake.
\p
\v	19	Zvino ava madekwani, wakabuda kunze kweguta.
\p
\v	20	Zvino mangwanani-ngwanani vakati vachipfuura, vakaona muonde waoma kubva pamidzi.
\v	21	Petro akarangarira akati kwaari: Rabhi, tarirai muonde wamakatuka waoma.
\v	22	Jesu akapindura akati kwavari: Ivai nerutendo kuna Mwari.
\v	23	Nokuti zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Ani nani anoti kugomo iri: Simudzwa, ukandwe mugungwa, uye asiri asina chokwadi mumoyo make, asi achitenda kuti zvinhu zvaanoreva zvichaitika, zvichaitika kwaari chero zvaanoreva.
\v	24	Naizvozvo ndinoti kwamuri: Zvinhu zvose chero zvamunokumbira muchinyengetera, tendai kuti muchagamuchira, uye zvichava zvenyu.
\v	25	Kana mumire muchinyengetera, kanganwirai kana mune chakanangana neumwe; kuti Baba venyu vari kumatenga vakukanganwireiwo kudarika kwenyu.
\v	26	Asi kana imwi musingakanganwiri, Baba venyu vari kumatenga havakanganwiriwo kudarika kwenyu.
\s	Simba raJesu rinobvepi
\p
\v	27	Vakasvikazve kuJerusarema; zvino wakati achifamba mutembere, vapristi vakuru nevanyori nevakuru vakauya kwaari,
\v	28	zvino vakati kwaari: Unoita zvinhu izvozvi nesimba ripi? Kana ndiani wakakupa simba iri, kuti unoita zvinhu izvi?
\v	29	Jesu akapindura akati kwavari: Iniwo ndichakubvunzai mubvunzo umwe, mundipindure, neni ndichakuudzai kuti ndinoita zvinhu izvozvi nesimba ripi.
\v	30	Rubhabhatidzo rwaJohwani rwakabva kudenga kana kuvanhu? Ndipindurei.
\v	31	Vakaonesana, vachiti: Kana tikati: Kudenga; achati: Makagoregerei kumutenda?
\v	32	Asi kana tikati: Kuvanhu; vaiva vachitya vanhu; nokuti vose vaiva vachiverenga Johwani kuti muporofita zvirokwazvo.
\v	33	Vakapindura vachiti kuna Jesu: Hatizivi. Jesu akapindura akati kwavari: Ini handingakuudziyiwo kuti ndinoita zvinhu izvi nesimba ripi.
\c	12
\s	Mufananidzo wevarimi vakaipa
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakatanga kutaura kwavari nemifananidzo,  achiti: Munhu wakasima munda wemizambiringa, akaisa ruzhowa rwakaukomberedza, akachera pekusvinira waini, akavaka shongwe, akauhaisa kuvarimi, ndokuenda parwendo.
\v	2	Nenguvayakafanira, wakatumira muranda kuvarimi, kuti agamuchire kubva kuvarimi zvechibereko chemunda wemizambiringa.
\v	3	Vakamubatavakarova, vaka muendesa asina chinhu.
\v	4	Akatumazvekwavari umwe muranda; uyu vakamuposhera mabwe, vaka mukuvadza mumusoro, vaka muendesa  anyadziswa.
\v	5	Zvinowakatumazve umwe; uyu vakamuurayawo; nevamwe vazhinji, vachirova vamwe, nekuuraya vamwe.
\v	6	Naizvozvowaiva achine mwanakomana umwe mudikanwi wake, akamutumirawo kwavari pakupedzisira, achiti: Vachakudza mwanakomana wangu.
\v	7	Asivarimi ivavo vakataurirana, vachiti: Uyu ndiye mugari wenhaka; uyai ngatimuurayei, nhaka ichava yedu.
\v	8	Vakamutoravakauraya, vakaposhera kunze kwemunda wemizambiringa.
\v	9	Naizvozvomwene wemunda wemizambiringa achaitei? Achauya akaparadza varimi, akapa munda wemizambiringa kune vamwe.
\v	10	Ko hamuna kuverenga here rugwaro urwu  rwunoti: Ibwe vavaki ravakarasa, ndiro rava musoro wekona;
\v	11	izvi zvakaitwa naIshe, uye zvinoshamisa pameso edu?
\v	12	Zvino vakatsvaka kumubata, asi vakatya chaunga; nokuti vaiziva kuti waiva ataura mufananidzo uyu akanangana navo. Vakamusiya vakaenda.
\s	Kuripira Kesari mutero
\p
\v	13	Vakati vatuma kwaari vamwe vevaFarisi nevevaHerodhi, kuti vamubate pakutaura.
\v	14	Zvino vakati vauya, vakati kwaari: Mudzidzisi, tinoziva kuti ndimwi wechokwadi, uye hamuna hanya nemunhu; nokuti hamutariri chimiro chemunhu, asi munodzidzisa nzira yaMwari muchokwadi. Zviri pamutemo kupa Kesari mutero here, kana kwete?
\v	15	Tipe here, kana tirege kupa? Asi iye wakaziva unyepedzeri hwavo, akati kwavari: Munondiidzirei? Ndivigirei dhenario, kuti ndione.

\v	36	Ndokuvaraira, kuti varege kuudza munhu; asi paainyanya kuvaraira, vakanyanya zvikuru kuparidza  izvozvo.
\v	37	Vakashamisika zvikuru kwazvo, vachiti: Wakaita zvose zvakanaka; wakaita matsi kuti dzinzwe, nezvimumumu kuti zvitaure.
\c	8
\s	Vanezvuru zvina vanogutswa nekudya naJesu
\p
\v	1	Nemazuvaiwayo zvakwaiva kune chaunga chikuru kwazvo, vasina chavangadya, Jesu akadanira vadzidzi vake kwaari, akati kwavari:
\v	2	Ndinetsitsi nechaunga; nokuti chadogara neni mazuva matatu uye vasina zvavangadya.
\v	3	Kanandikavarega vaende kumba kwavo vasina kudya, vachaziya panzira; nokuti vamwe vavo vanobva kure.
\v	4	Vadzidzivake vakamupindura,  vakati: Zvingabvepi zvingwa munhu zvaangagutsa nazvo ava murenje muno?
\v	5	Akavabvunza, akati: Mune zvingwa zvingani? Vakati: Zvinomwe.
\v	6	Zvinoakaraira chaunga kuti chigare pasi pavhu; akatora zvingwa zvinomwe, akavonga, aka zvimedura, ndokupa vadzidzi vake, kuti vaise pamberi  pavo; vakazviisa pamberi pechaunga.
\v	7	Uyevaiva vane hove diki shoma; zvino aka dziropafadza, akati vadziisewo pamberi  pavo.
\v	8	Vakadya,vakaguta; ndokununga zvimedu zvakasara, matengu manomwe.
\v	9	Avovaiva vadya vainge vaiva zvuru zvina; akavarega vachienda.
\p
\v	10	Pakarepo akapinda muchikepe nevadzidzi vake, akauya kumativi eDharimanuta.
\s	Jesu anoramba kuratidza chiratidzo
\p
\v	11	Zvino vaFarisi vakabuda, vakatanga kupikisana naye, vachitsvaka kwaari chiratidzo chinobva kudenga, vachimuidza.
\v	12	Akagomera zvikuru pamweya wake akati: Zera iri rinotsvakirei chiratidzo? Zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Hakuna chiratidzo chichapiwa kuzera iri.
\v	13	Ndokubva avasiya, akapindazve muchikepe, akabva akayambukira kumhiri.
\s	Jesu anodudzira shoko rembiriso yevaFarisi nevaSadhusi
\p
\v	14	Zvino vadzidzi vaiva vakanganwa kutora zvingwa; uye vaiva vasina navo  chinhu muchikepe kunze kwechingwa chimwe chete.
\v	15	Zvino akavaraira, achiti: Ngwarirai, muchenjere mbiriso yevaFarisi nembiriso yaHerodhi.
\v	16	Vakarangana pakati pavo vachiti: Nokuti hatina zvingwa.
\v	17	Zvino Jesu wakati achi zviziva akati kwavari: Munorangana sei kuti hamuna zvingwa? Hamusati maziva kana kunzwisisa here? Muchine moyo mukukutu here?
\v	18	Mune meso hamuoni here? Uye mune nzeve hamunzwi here? Uye hamurangariri here?
\v	19	Pandakamedurira vanezvuru zvishanu zvingwa zvishanu, matengu mangani azere nezvimedu amakanonga? Vakati kwaari: Gumi nemaviri.
\v	20	Uye pazvinomwe pavanezvuru zvina, matengu mangani azere nezvimedu amakanonga? Ivo ndokuti: Manomwe.
\v	21	Ndokubva ati kwavari: Sei musinganzwisisi?
\s	Jesu anosvinudza bofu paBhetisaidha
\p
\v	22	Zvino wakasvika kuBhetisaidha; vakauisa bofu kwaari, vakamukumbirisa kuti aribate.
\v	23	Akabata ruoko rwebofu, akaribudisira kunze kwemusha; akati apfira meso aro, akaisa maoko pamusoro paro, akaribvunza kana richiona chinhu.
\v	24	Ndokutarira kumusoro rikati: Ndinoona vanhu vakaita semiti vachifamba.
\v	25	Mushure maizvozvo akaisazve maoko pamusoro pameso aro akaritaridza kumusoro; uye rikaporeswa, rikaona vose zvakajeka.
\v	26	Akariendesa kumba kwaro, achiti: Usapinda mumusha uye usaudza ani nani mumusha.
\s	Petro anopupura kuti Jesu ndiye Kristu
\p
\v	27	Zvino Jesu akabuda nevadzidzi vake vakaenda kumisha yeKesariya yeFiripi. Vari munzira akabvunza vadzidzi vake achiti kwavari: Vanhu vanoti ndini ani?
\v	28	Vakapindura  vachiti: Johwani Mubhabhatidzi; asi vamwe: Eria; vamwezve: Umwe wevaporofita.
\v	29	Iye akati kwavari: Ko imwi munoti ndini ani? Petro akapindura akati kwaari: Imwi muri Kristu.
\v	30	Akavaraira zvikuru kuti varege kuudza munhu pamusoro pake.
\p
\v	31	Zvino akatanga kuvadzidzisa kuti Mwanakomana wemunhu anofanira kutambudzika zvinhu zvizhinji nekurambwa nevakuru nevapristi vakuru nevanyori, agourawa, uye mushure memazuva matatu amukezve.
\v	32	Akataura shoko iro pachena. Zvino Petro akamutorera parutivi, akatanga kumutsiura.
\v	33	Asi iye atendeuka, ndokutarira vadzidzi vake, akatsiura Petro achiti: Ibva shure kwangu, Satani; nokuti haurangariri zvinhu zvaMwari, asi zvinhu zvevanhu.
\s	Kutevera Jesu
\p
\v	34	Zvino wakati adanira kwaari chaunga pamwe nevadzidzi vake, akati kwavari: Ani nani anoda kuuya shure kwangu, ngaazvirambe, atakure muchinjikwa wake, anditevere.
\v	35	Nokuti ani nani anoda kuponesa upenyu hwake, acharasikirwa nahwo; asi ani nani acharasikirwa neupenyu hwake nekuda kwangu nekweevhangeri, uyu achahuponesa.
\v	36	Nokuti zvinobatsirei munhu, kana akawana nyika yose, akarasikirwa nemweya weupenyu wake?
\v	37	Kana kuti munhu achapei kuti chive muripo wemweya weupenyu wake?
\v	38	Nokuti ani nani anonyara pamusoro pangu nepamashoko angu pazera iri reupombwe nekutadza, Mwanakomana wemhunhu achanyarawo pamusoro pake, paanouya mukubwinya kwaBaba vake pamwe nevatumwa vatsvene.
\c	9
\s	Kubwinya kwaJesu pagomo
\p
\v	1	Zvinowakati kwavari: Zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Vamwe varipo kune vamire pano, vasingazoraviri rufu, kusvika vaona ushe hwaMwari huchiuya nesimba.
\p
\v	2	Zvinoshure kwemazuva matanhatu, Jesu akatora Petro naJakobho naJohwani, akavatungamidza mugomo refu, pachavo vari voga; akashandurwa chimiro pamberi pavo.
\v	3	Zvipfekozvake zvikapenya, zvikachenesesa zvikuru semagada echando; hakuna musuki panyika angagona kuzvichenesa zvikadaro.
\v	4	Zvinokwakaonekwa kwavari Eria naMozisi, uye vaitaurirana naJesu.
\v	5	Petroakapindura akati kuna Jesu: Rabhi, zvakanaka kuti tiri pano; uye ngativake matende matatu, rimwe renyu, uye rimwe raMozisi, uye rimwe raEria.
\v	6	Nokutiwaiva asingazivi chekutaura; nokuti vaitya zvikuru.
\v	7	Zvinokwakava negore rakavadzikatira, inzwi rikabuda mugore, richiti: Uyu Mwanakomana wangu anodikanwa, inzwai iye.
\v	8	Kamwe-kamwe,vakati varinga-ringa, havana kuzochiona munhu, asi Jesu chete anavo.
\p
\v	9	Zvinovakati vachiburuka mugomo, akavaraira kuti varege kuudza munhu zvavaiva vaona, kusvika Mwanakomana wemunhu amuka kubva kuvakafa.
\v	10	Vakachengeta shoko iro mukati mavo, vachibvunzana kuti kumuka kwevakafa chii.
\p
\v	11	Vakamubvunza, vachiti: Vanyori vanoreverei kuti Eria anofanira kutanga kuuya?
\v	12	Akapindura, akati kwavari: Eria zvirokwazvo anotanga kuuya, avandudze zvinhu zvose; kwakagonyorwa sei zveMwanakomana wemunhu, kuti achatambudzika zvinhu zvizhinji nekuzvidzwa?
\v	13	Asi ndinoreva kwamuri kuti Eriawo wakasvika, vakamuitira zvose zvavaida, sezvazvakanyorwa pamusoro pake.
\s	Jesu anoporesa mukomana ane zvipusha
\p
\v	14	Zvino wakati asvika kuvadzidzi, akaona chaunga chikuru chakavakomba, nevanyori vachipikisana navo.
\v	15	Pakarepo chaunga chose chakati chichimuona chikashamisika zvikuru, ndoku mumhanyira, chikamukwazisa.
\v	16	Akabvunza vanyori  akati: Munopikisanei navo?
\v	17	Umwe wechaunga akapindura akati: Mudzidzisi, ndauisa kwamuri mwanakomana wangu, ane mweya wechimumumu;
\v	18	pangava papi paunomubata, unomubvambura; opupuma furo, nekugeda-geda meno ake, uye anooma; zvino ndakataura kuvadzidzi venyu kuti vaubudise, uye vakasagona.
\v	19	Akamupindura akati: Haiwa zera risina rutendo, ndichava nemwi kusvika rinhi? Ndichakuitirai moyo murefu kusvika rinhi? Muuyisei kwandiri.
\v	20	Vakamuuisa kwaari; wakati achimuona, pakarepo mweya ukamubvambura; akawira pasi, akaumburuka achipupuma furo.
\v	21	Ndokubva abvunza baba vake  akati: Yava nguva yakadini kubvira izvozvi zvichiitika kwaari? Akati: Kubva paupwere.
\v	22	Kazhinji wakamuwisira nemumoto nemumvura, kuti umuparadze; asi kana muchigona kuita chinhu, mutinzwire tsitsi, mutibatsire.
\v	23	Jesu ndokuti kwaari: Kana uchigona kutenda, zvinhu zvose zvinogoneka kune anotenda.
\v	24	Uye pakarepo baba vemwana vakadanidzira nemisodzi vakati: Ndinotenda, Ishe, batsirai pakusatenda kwangu.
\v	25	Jesu wakati achiona kuti chaunga chinouya chichimhanyirana, akatsiura mweya wetsina, akati kwauri:  Iwe, mweya wechimumumu nematsi, ini ndinokuraira: Buda maari, uye urege kupindazve maari.
\v	26	Zvino  mweya ukadanidzira, ukamubvundisa zvikuru ukabuda; akaita sewakafa, nekudaro vazhinji vakati: Wafa.
\v	27	Asi Jesu wakabata ruoko rwake akamusimudza; akasimuka.
\p
\v	28	Zvino wakati apinda mumba, vadzidzi vake vakamubvunza vari voga  vachiti: Sei isu taiva tisingagoni kuubudisa?
\v	29	Ndokubva ati kwavari: Zera iri harigoni kubuda nechinhu, kunze nekunyengetera nekutsanya.
\s	Jesu anotaura zverufu rwake nekumuka kwake
\p
\v	30	Vakabva ipapo, vakagura neGarirea, uye aiva asingadi kuti chero ani azvizive.
\v	31	Nokuti aidzidzisa vadzidzi vake, achiti kwavari: Mwanakomana wemunhu achakumikidzwa mumaoko evanhu, uye vachamuuraya; kana aurawa achamuka nezuva retatu.
\v	32	Asi havana kunzwisisa shoko iro, vachitya kumubvunza.
\s	Mukuru paushe hwekudenga, nezvigumbuso
\p
\v	33	Zvino wakasvika muKapenaume; akati ava mumba, akavabvunza,  achiti: Manga muchikakavadzana chii munzira?
\v	34	Asi vakanyarara, nokuti munzira vaiva vakakavadzana  kuti mukurusa ndiani.
\v	35	Zvino akagara pasi, akadana vanegumi nevaviri, akati kwavari: Kana munhu achida kuva wekutanga, achava wekupedzisira kune vose nemushandi wevose.
\v	36	Ndokutora mucheche, akamumisa pakati pavo; akati amugumbatira, akati kwavari:
\v	37	Ani nani anogamuchira umwe wevacheche vakadai muzita rangu, anogamuchira ini; uye ani nani anogamuchira ini, haagamuchiri ini, asi uyo wakandituma.
\p
\v	38	Zvino Johwani akamupindura, achiti: Mudzidzisi, takaona umwe asingatiteveri achibudisa madhimoni muzita renyu; tikamudzivisa, nokuti haana kutitevera.
\v	39	Asi Jesu wakati: Musamudzivisa, nokuti hakuna munhu angaita basa resimba muzita rangu, anogona kukurumidza kutaura zvakaipa pamusoro pangu.
\v	40	Nokuti asingapikisani nesu, ndewedu.
\p
\v	41	Nokuti ani nani anokupai mukombe wemvura kuti munwe muzita rangu, nokuti muri vaKristu, zvirokwazvo ndinoti kwamuri: Haangamborasikirwi nemubairo wake.
\p
\v	42	Uye ani nani anogumbusa umwe wevadiki ava vanotenda kwandiri, zviri nani kwaari kuti guyo guru risungirirwe kupoteredza pamutsipa wake, akandirwe mugungwa.
\v	43	Uye kana ruoko rwako ruchikugumbusa, rugure; zviri nani kwauri kupinda muupenyu uri chirema, pane kuti une maoko maviri uende mugehena, mumoto usingadzimiki,
\v	44	apo pasingafi honye yavo, nemoto usingadzimi.
\v	45	Uye kana rutsoka rwako ruchikugumbusa, rugure; zviri nani kwauri kupinda muupenyu uchikamhina, pane kuti une tsoka mbiri ukandirwe mugehena, mumoto usingadzimiki,
\v	46	apo pasingafi honye yavo, nemoto usingadzimi.
\v	47	Zvino kana ziso rako richikugumbusa, ridzure; zviri nani kwauri kupinda muushe hwaMwari une ziso rimwe, pakuti une maziso maviri, ukandirwe mugehena remoto,
\v	48	apo pasingafi honye yavo, nemoto usingadzimi.
\p
\v	49	Nokuti wose acharungwa nemoto uye chibairo chose chicharungwa nemunyu.
\v	50	Munyu wakanaka; asi kana munyu warasa kuvava, mungaurunga nei? Ivai nemunyu mamuri, mugarisane nerugare.
