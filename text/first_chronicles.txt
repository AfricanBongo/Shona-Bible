\b	1 Makoronike
\c	1
\s	Zvizvarwa zvaAdhamu
\p
\v	1	Adhamu,Seti, Enoshi,
\v	2	Kenani,Maharareri, Jaredhi,
\v	3	Enoki,Metusera, Rameki,
\v	4	Nowa,Shemu, Hamu, naJafeti.
\p
\v	5	VanakomanavaJafeti: Gomeri, naMagogi, naMadhai, naJavhani, naTubhari, naMesheki, naTirasi.
\v	6	NevanakomanavaGomeri: Ashikenazi, naRifati, naTogarima.
\v	7	NevanakomanavaJavhani: Erisha, naTarishishi, Kitimi, naRodhanimi.
\p
\v	8	VanakomanavaHamu: Kushi, naMiziraimu, Puti, naKenani.
\v	9	NevanakomanavaKushi: Shebha, naHavhira, naSabhita, naRaama, naSabhiteka. Nevanakomana vaRaama: Shebha, naDhedhani.
\v	10	Kushi ndokubereka Nimurodhi; iye wakatanga kuva ane simba panyika.
\v	11	Mizirami ndokubereka vaRudhimi, nevaAnami, nevaRehabhi, nevaNafutuhi,
\v	12	nevaPatirusimi, nevaKasiruhimi (kwakabudako vaFirisitia), nevaKafitorimi.
\v	13	Kenani ndokubereka Sidhoni dangwe rake, naHeti,
\v	14	nemuJebhusi, nemuAmori, nemuGirigashi,
\v	15	nemuHivhi, nemuAriki, nemuSini,
\v	16	nemuArivhadhi, nemuZemari, nemuHamati.
\p
\v	17	Vanakomana vaShemu: Eramu, naAshuri, naAripakishadhi, naRudhi, naAramu, naUzi, naHuri, naGeteri, naMesheki.
\v	18	Aripakishadhi ndokubereka Shera, Shera ndokubereka Ebheri.
\v	19	Zvino Ebheri wakaberekerwa vanakomana vaviri. Zita reumwe raiva Peregi, nokuti mumazuva ake nyika yakakamurwa; uye zita remunin'ina wake raiva Jokitani.
\v	20	Jokitani ndokubereka Arimodhadhi, naSherefi, naHazarimavheti, naJera,
\v	21	naHadhoramu, naUzari, naDhikira,
\v	22	naEbhari, naAbhimaeri, naShebha,
\v	23	naOfiri, naHavhira, naJobhabhu. Ava vose vaiva vanakomana vaJokitani.
\s	Chizvarwa chaShemu
\p
\v	24	Shemu, Aripakishadhi, Shera,
\v	25	Ebheri, Peregi, Reu,
\v	26	Serugi, Nahori, Tera,
\v	27	Abhuramu, ndiye Abhurahama.
\v	28	Vanakomana vaAbhurahama: Isaka, naIshimaeri.
\p
\v	29	Ndizvo zvizvarwa zvavo: Dangwe raIshimaeri Nebhaioti, naKedhari, naAdhidheeri, naMibhisami,
\v	30	Mishima, naDhuma, Masa, Hadhadhi, naTema,
\v	31	Jeturi, Nafishi, naKedhema. Ava vanakomana vaIshimaeri.
\p
\v	32	Uye vanakomana vaKetura, murongo waAbhurahama: Iye wakabereka Zimirani, naJokishani, naMedhani, naMidhiani, naIshibhaki, naShua. Uye vanakomana vaJokishani: Shebha, naDhedhani.
\v	33	Nevanakomana vaMidhiani: Efa, naEferi, naHanoki, naAbhidha, naEridhaha. Ava vose vaiva vanakomana vaKetura.
\v	34	Abhurahama ndokubereka Isaka. Vanakomana vaIsaka: Esau, naIsraeri.
\p
\v	35	Vanakomana vaEsau: Erifazi, Reueri, naJeushi, naJaramu, naKora.
\v	36	Vanakomana vaErifazi: Temani, naOmari, Zefi, naGatami, Kenazi, naTimina, naAmareki.
\v	37	Vanakomana vaReueri: Nahati, Zera, Shama, naMiza.
\v	38	Nevanakomana vaSeiri: Rotani, naShobhari, naZibheoni, naAna, naDhishoni, naEzeri, naDhishani.
\v	39	Nevanakomana vaRotani: Hori, naHomami; nehanzvadzi yaRotani yaiva Timina.
\v	40	Vanakomana vaShobhari: Ariani, naManahati, naEbhari, Shefi, naOnamu. Nevanakomana vaZibheoni: Aya, naAna.
\v	41	Vanakomana vaAna: Dhishoni. Nevanakomana vaDhishoni: Hamurani, naEshibhani, naItirani, naKerani.
\v	42	Vanakomana vaEzeri: Bhirihani, naZavani, Jakani. Vanakomana vaDhishani: Uzi, naArani.
\p
\v	43	Zvino awa madzimambo akatonga munyika yaEdhomu, pasati patonga mambo pamusoro pevanakomana vaIsraeri: Bhera mwanakomana waBheori; uye zita reguta rake raiva Dhinihabha.
\p
\v	44	Zvino Bhera wakafa; Jobhabhu mwanakomana waZera weBhozira ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\p
\v	45	Jobhabhu ndokufa; Hushamu wenyika yevaTemani ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\p
\v	46	Hushamu ndokufa; Hadhadhi mwanakomana waBhedhadhi, wakarova Midhiani pamunda waMoabhu, ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake; nezita reguta rake raiva Avhiti.
\v	47	Hadhadhi ndokufa; Samira weMasireka ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	48	Shamira ndokufa; Shauri weRehobhoti parwizi ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	49	Shauri ndokufa; Bhaari-Hanani mwanakomana waAkibhori ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	50	Bhaari-Hanani ndokufa; Hadhadhi ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake; uye zita reguta rake raiva Pai; uye zita remukadzi wake raiva Mehetabheri mukunda waMatiredhu mukunda waMezahabhi.
\v	51	Hadhadhi ndokufa. Zvino madzishe eEdhomu aiva: Ishe Timina, ishe Aria, ishe Jeteti,
\v	52	ishe Ohoribhama, ishe Era, ishe Pinoni,
\v	53	ishe Kenazi, ishe Temani, ishe Mibhizari,
\v	54	ishe Magidhieri, ishe Iramu. Awa madzishe eEdhomu.
\c	10
\s	Kufa kwaSauro nevanakomana vake vatatu
\p
\v	1	ZvinovaFirisitia vakarwisa Israeri; varume vaIsraeri ndokutiza kubva pamberi pevaFirisitia, ndokuwa vaurawa pagomo reGiribhoa.
\v	2	ZvinovaFirisitia vakanamatira shure kwaSauro uye shure kwevanakomana vake; vaFirisitia ndokuuraya Jonatani, naAbhinadhabhu, naMariki-Shua, vanakomana vaSauro.
\v	3	Zvinohondo yakava yakarema kuna Sauro uye vapfuri veuta vakamuwana; zvino akakuvadzwa nevapfuri.
\v	4	ZvinoSauro wakati kumutakuri wezvombo zvake: Vhomora munondo wako ugondibaya nawo, zvimwe ava vasina kudzingiswa vangasvika vakandidadira. Asi mutakuri wezvombo zvake haana kuda, nokuti wakatya zvikuru. Naizvozvo Sauro wakatora munondo, ndokuwira pamusoro pawo.
\v	5	Mutakuriwezvombo zvake paakaona kuti Sauro wafa, naiye wakawira pamusoro pemunondo, ndokufa.
\v	6	Saurondokufa nevanakomana vake vatatu; neimba yake yose vakafa pamwe chete.
\v	7	Varumevose vaIsraeri vaiva pamupata pavakaona kuti vatiza, uye kuti Sauro nevanakomana vake vafa, vakasiya maguta avo ndokutiza; vaFirisitia vakasvika ndokugara maari.
\p
\v	8	Zvinozvakaitika chifume kuti vaFirisitia vakasvika kutakanura vakaurawa, ndokuwana Sauro nevanakomana vake vakawa pagomo reGiribhoa.
\v	9	Vakativamutakanura, vakatora musoro wake nezvombo zvake, ndokutumira kunyika yevaFirisitia kumativi ose, kunodanidzira shoko kuzvifananidzo zvavo nekuvanhu.
\v	10	Zvino vakaisa zvombo zvake muimba yavamwari vavo, nedehenya rake vakarinamatidza mumba maDhagoni.
\p
\v	11	Jabheshi-Giriyadhi rose rakati ranzwa zvose vaFirisitia zvavakaita kuna Sauro,
\v	12	vakasimuka varume vose veumhare ndokutakura chitunha chaSauro nezvitunha zvevanakomana vake, ndokuzviuisa kuJabheshi, ndokuviga mapfupa avo pasi pemuoki muJabheshi, ndokutsanya mazuva manomwe.
\p
\v	13	Naizvozvo Sauro wakafa nekuda kwechitadzo chake chaakadarikira Jehovha, kupikisa shoko raJehovha raasina kuchengeta; uye nekubvunza kusvikiro, kutsvaka  kwariri,
\v	14	uye haana kubvunza kuna Jehovha; naizvozvo wakamuuraya, ndokushandurira ushe kuna Dhavhidhi mwanakomana waJese.
\c	11
\s	Dhavhidhi anogadzwa mambo paHebhuroni, anokunda vaJebhusi
\p
\v	1	ZvinoIsraeri wose wakaungana kuna Dhavhidhi paHebhuroni, achiti: Tarirai, isu tiri pfupa renyu nenyama yenyu.
\v	2	Nazuronamarimwezuro, kunyange Sauro aiva mambo, imwi ndimwi maibudisa nekupinza Israeri; uye Jehovha Mwari wenyu wakati kwamuri: Iwe uchafudza vanhu vangu Israeri, uye iwe uchava mutongi pamusoro pevanhu vangu Israeri.
\v	3	Zvinokwakasvika vakuru vose vaIsraeri kuna mambo paHebhuroni; Dhavhidhi ndokuita sungano navo paHebhuroni pamberi paJehovha; ivo ndokuzodza Dhavhidhi kuva mambo pamusoro paIsraeri, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha neruoko rwaSamueri.
\p
\v	4	ZvinoDhavhidhi naIsraeri wose vakaenda kuJerusarema, rinova Jebhusi; nokuti ipapo kwaiva nevaJebhusi, vagari venyika iyo.
\v	5	Zvinovagari veJebhusi vakati kuna Dhavhidhi: Haungapindi pano. Asi Dhavhidhi wakatapa nhare yeZiyoni; ndiro guta raDhavhidhi.
\v	6	Dhavhidhindokuti: Ani nani anotanga kurova vaJebhusi achava musoro nemukuru. Zvino Joabhu mwanakomana waZeruya wakatanga ndokukwira, ndokuva musoro.
\v	7	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakagara munhare; naizvozvo vakaritumidza  kuti guta raDhavhidhi.
\v	8	Zvinowakavaka guta kupoteredza, kubva paMiro kusvika aripoteredza; Joabhu ndokuvandudza zvakasara zveguta.
\v	9	Dhavhidhindokuenda, achienderera achikura, nokuti Jehovha wehondo waiva naye.
\s	Machinda aDhavhidhi
\p
\v	10	Zvino ava misoro yevane simba Dhavhidhi vaaiva navo, vaizvisimbisa pamwe naye paumambo hwake, naIsraeri wose, kumuita mambo, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha maererano naIsraeri.
\v	11	Ndihwo uwandu hwevane simba Dhavhidhi vaaiva navo: Jashobheyami mwanakomana wemuHakimoni, musoro wevane makumi matatu; iye wakamutsira pfumo rake vane mazana matatu, akauraya nenguva imwe.
\v	12	Zvino shure kwake kwakava naEriazari, mwanakomana waDhodho muAhohi, waiva wevatatu vaiva nesimba.
\v	13	Iye waiva naDhavhidhi paPasi-Dhamini; uye ipapo vaFirisitia vaiva vaunganira kurwa, uye ndima yemunda yaiva izere nebhari; vanhu ndokutiza pamberi pevaFirisitia.
\v	14	Ivo ndokuzvimisa pakati pendima, ndokuisunungura, ndokuuraya vaFirisitia; Jehovha ndokuponesa nekusunungura kukuru.
\p
\v	15	Zvino vatatu vevakuru makumi matatu vakaburukira kudombo kuna Dhavhidhi kubako reAdhuramu; zvino hondo yevaFirisitia yaiva yadzika matende pamupata wevaRefaimu.
\v	16	Zvino panguva iyo Dhavhidhi waiva munhare, uye boka revarwi vevaFirisitia raiva paBheterehemu panguva iyo.
\v	17	Dhavhidhi wakapanga, ndokuti: Dai umwe andipa ndinwe mvura kubva mutsime reBheterehemu riri pasuwo!
\v	18	Zvino vatatu ava vakapaza nemukati memusasa wevaFirisitia, ndokuchera mvura kubva mutsime reBheterehemu, riri pasuwo, vakatora, ndokuuisa kuna Dhavhidhi. Asi Dhavhidhi haana kuda kuinwa, asi wakaidururira kuna Jehovha,
\v	19	ndokuti: Ngazvive kure neni, naMwari wangu, kuti ndiite chinhu ichi! Ndinganwa ropa revarume ava vakaisa upenyu hwavo panjodzi, nokuti neupenyu hwavo vauya nayo; naizvozvo haana kuda kuinwa. Zvinhu izvi vakazviita vane simba vatatu ava.
\p
\v	20	NaAbhishai munin'ina waJoabhu, iye waiva musoro wevatatu avo, nekuda kwekumutsira kwake pfumo rake kumazana matatu, waka vauraya; ndokuva nezita pakati pevatatu.
\v	21	Pavatatu waikudzwa nevaviri, ndokuva mutungamiriri wavo; asi haana kunanavira kuvatatu  vekutanga.
\v	22	Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha, mwanakomana wemhare yeKabhizeeri, waiva mukuru pazviito. Iye wakarova vekwaMoabhu vaviri vakaita seshumba, akaburukawo ndokuuraya shumba mukati megomba nezuva remagada echando.
\v	23	Akaurayawo muEgipita, murume murefu,  wemakubhiti mashanu; uye muruoko rwemuEgipita uyu maiva nepfumo rakaita sedanda remuruki; asi iye wakaburukira kwaari netsvimbo, ndokubvuta pfumo kubva muruoko rwemuEgipita, ndokumuuraya nepfumo rake  pachake.
\v	24	Izvi Bhenaya mwanakomana waJehoyadha wakaita, ndokuva nezita pakati pevatatu vane simba.
\v	25	Tarirai, wakakudzwa pakati pemakumi matatu, asi haana kunanavira kuvatatu  vekutanga. Dhavhidhi ndokumuisa pamusoro pevachengetedzi vake.
\p
\v	26	Nemhare dzehondo: Asaheri munin'ina waJoabhu, Erihanani mwanakomana waDhodho weBheterehemu,
\v	27	Shamoti muHarori, Herezi muPeroni,
\v	28	Ira mwanakomana waIkeshi muTekowa, Abhi-Ezeri muAnatoti,
\v	29	Sibhekai muHushati, Irai muAhohi,
\v	30	Maharai muNetofati, Heredhi mwanakomana waBhahana muNetofati,
\v	31	Itai mwanakomana waRibhayi weGibhiya revana vaBhenjamini, Bhenaya muPiratoni,
\v	32	Hurai wehova dzeGaashi, Abhieri muAribhati,
\v	33	Azimavheti muBhaharumi, Eriabha muShaaribhoni,
\v	34	vanakomana vaHashemu muGizoni, Jonatani mwanakomana waShage muHarari,
\v	35	Ahiamu mwanakomana waSakari muHarari, Erifari mwanakomana waUri,
\v	36	Heferi muMekerati, Ahiya muPeroni,
\v	37	naHeziro muKarimeri, Naarai mwanakomana waEzibhayi,
\v	38	Joweri munin'ina waNatani, Mibhari mwanakomana waHagiri,
\v	39	Zereki muAmoni, Naharai muBheroti, mutakuri wezvombo zvaJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya,
\v	40	Ira muItiri, Garebhu muItiri,
\v	41	Uriya muHiti, Zabhadhi mwanakomana waAherayi,
\v	42	Adhina mwanakomana waShiza muRubheni, musoro wevaRubheni, uye vane makumi matatu vanaye,
\v	43	Hanani mwanakomana waMahaka, naJoshafati muMitini,
\v	44	Uziya muAshiterati, Shama naJeyieri vanakomana vaHotamu muAroeri,
\v	45	Jedhiyaeri mwanakomana waShimiri, naJoha munin'ina wake muTizi,
\v	46	Erieri muMahavhi, naJeribhayi naJoshavhiya vanakomana vaErinahamu, naItima muMoabhu,
\v	47	naErieri, naObhedhi, naJahasieri muMezobha.
\c	12
\s	Mazita evamwe vaitevera Dhavhidhi
\p
\v	1	Zvinoava ndivo vakauya kuna Dhavhidhi kuZikiragi, achakavharirwa nekuda kwaSauro mwanakomana waKishi. Uye vaiva pakati pevarume vane simba, vabatsiri vehondo,
\v	2	vakashongazvombo neuta, vaishandisa rudyi neruboshwe pamabwe uye pamiseve kubva muuta, pahama dzaSauro vekwaBhenjamini.
\v	3	Musorowaiva Ahiyezeri, naJoashi, vanakomana vaShemaha muGibhiya; naJezieri, naPereti, vanakomana vaAzimavheti; naBheraka, naJehu muAnatoti;
\v	4	naIshimayamuGibhiyoni, murume ane simba pakati pemakumi matatu, uye ari pamusoro pemakumi matatu; naJeremiya, naJahaziyeri, naJohwanani, naJozabhadhi muGedherati;
\v	5	Eruzai,naJerimoti, naBheyariya, naShemariya, naShefatiya muHarufi;
\v	6	Erikana,naIshiya, naAzareri, naJowezeri, naJashobheyami, vaKora;
\v	7	naJoera,naZebhadhia, vanakomana vaJerohamu weGedhori.
\v	8	ZvinokuvaGadhi vakazvitsaura kuna Dhavhidhi kunhare kurenje, varume vane simba veumhare, varume vehondo vekurwa, vabata nhovo nepfumo; nezviso zvavo zvaiva zviso zveshumba, uye vakaita semharapara pamakomo pakumhanya.
\v	9	Ezeriwaiva musoro, Obhadhiya wepiri, Eriabhu wetatu,
\v	10	Misimana wechina, Jeremiya weshanu,
\v	11	Atai wetanhatu, Erieri wechinomwe,
\v	12	Johwanani werusere, Erizabhadhi wepfumbamwe,
\v	13	Jeremiya wegumi, Makibhanai wegumi neumwe.
\v	14	Ava vaiva vevana vaGadhi, misoro yehondo, umwe wevadiki waiva pamusoro pezana, uye mukuru pamusoro pechuru.
\v	15	Ava ndivo vakayambuka Joridhani pamwedzi wekutanga, parwakafashamira kunhivi dzarwo dzose; vakatizisa vose vaiva pamipata, chekumabvazuva nechekumavirira.
\v	16	Zvino kwakauya vakabva kuvana vaBhenjamini naJudha kuenda kunhare kuna Dhavhidhi.
\v	17	Zvino Dhavhidhi wakabuda kuvachingamidza, ndokupindura ndokuti kwavari: Kana muchiuya kwandiri nerugare kuzondibatsira, moyo wangu uchabatanidzwa nemwi; asi kana kuri kunditengesa kuvavengi vangu, zvandisina chakaipa mumaoko angu, Mwari wemadzibaba edu ngaaone, atsiure.
\v	18	Zvino mweya wakafukidza Amasayi musoro wevakuru,  ndokuti:  Tiri venyu, Dhavhidhi, uye  tiri pamwe nemwi, mwanakomana waJese! Rugare, rugare ngaruve nemwi, uye rugare kuvabatsiri venyu; nokuti Mwari wenyu anokubatsirai. Dhavhidhi ndokuvagamuchira, ndokuvaisa kuvakuru veboka.
\v	19	VekwaManasewo vakawira kuna Dhavhidhi pakusvika kwake nevaFirisitia kunorwisa Sauro pahondo, asi havana kuvabatsira; nokuti madzishe evaFirisitia akamudzinga nezano vachiti: Nemisoro yedu achawa kuna tenzi wake Sauro.
\v	20	Pakuenda kwake kuZikiragi, kwakawira kwaari vaibva kwaManase: Adhina, naJozabhadhi, naJedhiayeri, naMikayeri, naJozabhadhi, naErihu, naZiretai, misoro yezvuru vaiva vekwaManase.
\v	21	Zvino ava vakabatsira pamwe naDhavhidhi kurwisa hondo yakarongwa; nokuti vose vaiva varume vane simba veumhare, uye vaiva vakuru pahondo.
\v	22	Nokuti nenguva zuva nezuva vaiuya kuna Dhavhidhi kuzomubatsira, kusvika yava hondo huru, sehondo yaMwari.
\p
\v	23	Zvino uhu uwandu hwemisoro yaiva yakashongera hondo, vakauya kuna Dhavhidhi paHebhuroni, kutendeusira ushe hwaSauro kwaari, zvichienderana nemuromo waJehovha.
\v	24	Vana vaJudha, vakatakura nhovo nepfumo, vaiva zvuru zvitanhatu nemazana masere, vakashonga zvombo zvehondo.
\v	25	Kuvana vaSimioni varume vane simba veumhare vehondo, zvuru zvinomwe nezana.
\v	26	Kuvana vaRevhi, zvuru zvina nemazana matanhatu.
\v	27	NaJehoyadha waiva mutungamiriri wevaAroni; uye pamwe naye kwaiva nezvuru zvitatu nemazana manomwe.
\v	28	NaZadhoki, jaya raiva nesimba reumhare, neveimba yababa vake, vakuru makumi maviri nevaviri.
\v	29	Nekuvana vaBhenjamini, hama dzaSauro, zvuru zvitatu; nokuti kusvika panguva iyo vazhinji vavo vakachengetedza kutendeka kuimba yaSauro.
\v	30	Nekuvana vaEfuremu zvuru makumi maviri nemazana masere, varume vane simba veumhare, varume vane mazita muimba yemadzibaba avo.
\v	31	Nepahafu yerudzi rwaManase, zvuru gumi nezvisere, vaiva vakataurwa nemazita, kuti vauye kuzoita Dhavhidhi mambo.
\v	32	Nepavana vaIsasikari, vaiziva kunzwisisa zvenguva, kuziva Israeri zvaanofanira kuita, misoro yavo yaiva mazana maviri; uye hama dzavo dzose dzaiva pakurairwa navo.
\v	33	KwaZebhuruni, vaibudira kuhondo, vachirongedzera hondo nezvombo zvose zvehondo, zvuru makumi mashanu; uye kuchengetedza hondo yakarongwa pasina moyo miviri.
\v	34	NekwaNafutari misoro churu, uye zvuru makumi matatu nezvinomwe pamwe navo vane nhovo nepfumo.
\v	35	NekwaDhani vairongedza hondo, zvuru makumi maviri nezvisere nemazana matanhatu.
\v	36	NekwaAsheri, vaibudira kuhondo, kurongedza hondo, zvuru makumi mana.
\v	37	Uye kubva mhiri kwaJoridhani, kuvaRubheni nevaGadhi nehafu yerudzi rwaManase, vane zvombo zvose zvehondo kunorwa, zvuru zana nemakumi maviri.

\s	Dhavhidhi anogadzira zvekuvaka tembere nazvo
\p
\v	2	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakaraira kuunganidza vatorwa vaiva panyika yaIsraeri; ndokugadza vavezi vemabwe, kuti vaveze mabwe akagadzirwa kuvaka imba yaMwari.
\v	3	Dhavhidhiakagadzirawo matare mazhinji ezvipikiri zvemagonhi emikova neekubatanidza; uye ndarira zhinji isina  kuyerwa uremu.
\v	4	Nematandaemisidhari asina kuverengwa; nokuti vaSidhoni nevaTire vakauya nematanda emusidhari mazhinji kuna Dhavhidhi.
\v	5	Dhavhidhindokuti: Soromoni mwanakomana wangu mudiki asina simba, uye imba ichavakirwa Jehovha inofanira kuva huru kwazvo pazita neparumbidzo panyika dzose; ndichaigadzirira hangu. Dhavhidhi ndokugadzira zvizhinji asati afa.
\p
\v	6	Zvinowakadana Soromoni mwanakomana wake, ndokumuraira kuvakira Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri imba.
\v	7	Dhavhidhindokuti kuna Soromoni: Mwanakomana wangu: Kana ndiri ini, zvaiva mumoyo mangu kuvakira zita raJehovha Mwari wangu imba.
\v	8	Asishoko raJehovha rakasvika kwandiri richiti: Wakateura ropa zhinji, uye wakaita dzihondo dzakakura; haungavakiri zita rangu imba, nokuti wakateura ropa zhinji panyika pamberi pangu.
\v	9	Tarira,uchaberekerwa mwanakomana; iye achava munhu wezororo; uye ndichamupa zororo kubva pavavengi vake vose vakapoteredza; nokuti zita rake richava Soromoni, uye ndichapa rugare nerunyararo kuna Israeri mumazuva ake.
\v	10	Iye achavakira zita rangu imba; uye iye achava mwanakomana wangu, neni  ndichava baba vake. Zvino ndichasimbisa chigaro cheushe cheumambo hwake pamusoro paIsraeri kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	11	Ikozvino, mwanakomana wangu, Jehovha ngaave newe; uye ubudirire ugovaka imba yaJehovha Mwari wako, sekutaura kwake pamusoro pako.
\v	12	Koga Jehovha ngaakupe njere nekunzwisisa, agokuraira pamusoro paIsraeri; kuti uchengete murairo waJehovha Mwari wako.
\v	13	Ipapo uchabudirira, kana uchichenjerera kuzadzisa zvimiso nemitongo Jehovha zvaakaraira Mozisi nazvo maererano naIsraeri. Simba, uye uve neushingi, usatya, kana kuodzwa moyo.
\v	14	Zvino tarira, pakutambudzika kwangu ndagadzirira imba yaJehovha matarenda zvuru zana zvegoridhe, nematarenda churu chezvuru esirivha, nendarira nematare zvisina  kuyerwa uremu, nokuti zvizhinji; uye matanda nemabwe ndagadzira; zvino uchawedzera kwazviri.
\v	15	Uye vaiti vebasa vazhinji unavo, vavezi vemabwe, nenyanzwi dzemabwe nematanda, nemurume umwe neumwe wakachenjera pabasa ripi neripi.
\v	16	Pagoridhe, pasirivha, nepandarira, nepamatare hapana  kuyerwa uwandu. Simuka ushande, uye Jehovha ave newe.
\p
\v	17	Dhavhidhi wakarairawo misoro yose yaIsraeri kubatsira Soromoni mwanakomana wake,  akati:
\v	18	Jehovha Mwari wenyu haasi nemwi here? Uye wakakupai zororo kudivi rimwe nerimwe. Nokuti wakapa vagari venyika muruoko rwangu, uye nyika yakaiswa pasi pamberi paJehovha nepamberi pevanhu vake.
\v	19	Ikozvino misai moyo yenyu nemweya yeupenyu yenyu kutsvaka Jehovha Mwari wenyu. Simukai zvino muvake nzvimbo tsvene yaJehovha Mwari, kupinza areka yesungano yaJehovha nenhumbi tsvene dzaMwari muimba yakavakirwa zita raJehovha.
\c	23
\s	VaRevhi vanopiwa mabasa avo
\p
\v	1	ZvinoDhavhidhi waiva akwegura, agutsikana pamazuva; wakaita Soromoni mwanakomana wake mambo pamusoro paIsraeri.
\p
\v	2	Zvinowakaunganidza machinda ose aIsraeri pamwe nevapristi nevaRevhi.
\v	3	ZvinovaRevhi vakaverengwa kubva pamakore makumi matatu zvichikwirawo; zvino uwandu hwavo, nemisoro yavo, nevarume, vaiva zvuru makumi matatu nezvisere.
\v	4	Paneava paiva nezvuru makumi maviri nezvina kufambisa basa reimba yaJehovha; uye vatariri nevatongi zvuru zvitanhatu;
\v	5	nevachengetivemasuwo zvuru zvina; nevarumbidzi vaJehovha zvuru zvina nezvinhu zvandakaitira kurumbidza,  wakadaro Dhavhidhi.
\v	6	ZvinoDhavhidhi wakavakamura kuita mapoka zvichienderana nevanakomana vaRevhi: Gerishoni, Kohati, naMerari.
\p
\v	7	KuvaGerishoni:Rahadhani naShimei.
\v	8	VanakomanavaRahadhani: Jehieri musoro, naZetamu, naJoweri, vatatu.
\v	9	VanakomanavaShimei: Sheromiti, naHazieri, naHarani, vatatu. Ava vaiva misoro yemadzibaba aRahadhani.
\v	10	Nevanakomana vaShimei: Jahati, Zina, naJeushi, naBheriya. Ava vaiva vanakomana vaShimei, vana.
\v	11	Zvino Jahati waiva musoro, naZiza ari wepiri; asi Jeushi naBheriya vaiva vasina vanakomana vakawanda; naizvozvo vakava paimba yemadzibaba nekuverengwa kumwe.
\p
\v	12	Vanakomana vaKohati: Amiramu, Izihari, Hebhuroni, naUziyeri, vana.
\v	13	Vanakomana vaAmiramu: Aroni, naMozisi. Uye Aroni wakatsaurwa kuti atsvenese zvinhu zvitsvene-tsvene, iye nevanakomana vake kusvika rinhi narinhi, kupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira pamberi paJehovha, kumushumira, nekuropafadza muzita rake rinhi narinhi.
\v	14	Zvino Mozisi munhu waMwari, vanakomana vake vakadanwa nerudzi rwaRevhi.
\v	15	Vanakomana vaMozisi: Gerishomu naEriezeri.
\v	16	Vanakomana vaGerishomu: Shebhueri musoro.
\v	17	Nevanakomana vaEriezeri vaiva Rehabhiya musoro. Zvino Eriezeri waiva asina vamwe vanakomana; vanakomana vaRehabhiya ndokuwanda zvikuru.
\v	18	Vanakomana vaIzihari: Sheromiti musoro.
\p
\v	19	Vanakomana vaHebhuroni: Jeriya musoro, Amariya wepiri, Jahaziyeri wetatu, Jekameyamu wechina.
\v	20	Vanakomana vaUziyeri: Mika musoro, naIshishiya wepiri.
\p
\v	21	Vanakomana vaMerari: Mahiri naMushi. Vanakomana vaMahiri: Eriazari naKishi.
\v	22	Zvino Eriazari wakafa, uye waiva asina vanakomana, asi vanasikana. Hama dzavo, vanakomana vaKishi, ndokuvatora.
\v	23	Vanakomana vaMushi: Mahiri, naEdheri, naJeremoti, vatatu.
\p
\v	24	Ava vanakomana vaRevhi zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba avo, misoro yemadzibaba, sekuverengwa kwavo, neuwandu hwemazita nemisoro yavo, vaiita basa reshumiro reimba yaJehovha, kubva pamakore makumi maviri zvichikwirawo.
\v	25	Nokuti Dhavhidhi wakati: Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri wakapa vanhu vake zororo; uye achagara muJerusarema kusvika rinhi narinhi.
\v	26	Uyewo kuvaRevhi, hakuna achatakura tabhenekari kana midziyo yose yayo yeshumiro yayo.
\v	27	Nokuti nemashoko ekupedzisira aDhavhidhi vanakomana vaRevhi vakaverengwa, kubva pamakore makumi maviri zvichikwirawo.
\v	28	Nokuti nzvimbo yavo yaiva muruoko rwevanakomana vaAroni pashumiro yeimba yaJehovha, pamusoro pezvivanze, nepamusoro pemakamuri, nepamusoro pekuchenurwa kwezvinhu zvitsvene zvose, uye basa reshumiro yeimba yaMwari;
\v	29	nerechingwa chekuratidza, nereupfu hwakatsetseka hwechipo chechidyiwa, nerezvingwa zvitete zvisina mbiriso, nere zvakabikwa mupani, nerezvakakangiwa  nemafuta, nerezviyero zvose nemakuriro,
\v	30	nekumira mangwanani nemangwanani kuvonga nekurumbidza Jehovha, uye saizvozvo madekwani;
\v	31	nekupa zvose zvezvipo zvinopiswa kuna Jehovha zvemasabata, zvekugara kwemwedzi, nezvepamitambo yakatarwa, neuwandu, zvichienderana nechimiso pamusoro pazvo rinhi narinhi pamberi paJehovha;
\v	32	nekuchengeta chirindo chetende rekusangana, nechirindo cheimba tsvene, nechirindo chevanakomana vaAroni, hama dzavo, pashumiro yeimba yaJehovha.
\c	24
\s	Dhavhidhi anokamura vapristi
\p
\v	1	Zvinovanakomana vaAroni vaiva nemapoka avo. Vanakomana vaAroni: Nadhabhi, naAbhihu, Eriazari, naItamari.
\v	2	AsiNadhabhi naAbhihu vakafa kutangira baba vavo, uye vaiva vasina vana. Naizvozvo Eriazari naItamari vakashanda paupristi.
\v	3	Dhavhidhindokuvakamura, naZadhoki pavanakomana vaEriazari, naAhimereki pavanakomana vaItamari, zvichienderana nezvinzvimbo zvavo pashumiro yavo.
\v	4	Zvinokwakawanikwa kuvanakomana vaEriazari misoro yevane simba vazhinji kupfuura kuvanakomana vaItamari; ndokuvakamura: Pavanakomana vaEriazari, misoro yeimba yemadzibaba yaiva gumi nemitanhatu, uye vasere pavanakomana vaItamari neimba yemadzibaba avo.
\v	5	Zvinovakakamurwa nemijenya, ava neavo; nokuti machinda eimba tsvene nemachinda aMwari aibva pavanakomana vaEriazari nepavanakomana vaItamari.
\v	6	ZvinoShemaya mwanakomana waNetaneri munyori, anobva kuvaRevhi, wakavanyora pamberi pamambo, nemachinda, naZadhoki mupristi, naAhimereki mwanakomana waAbhiyatari, nemisoro yemadzibaba evapristi neevaRevhi; imba yababa imwe yatorerwa Eriazari, uye imwe yatorerwa Itamari.
\v	7	Zvinomujenya wekutanga wakabudira kuna Jehoiyaribhi; wepiri kuna Jedhaya;
\v	8	wetatukuna Harimi; wechina kuna Seorimi;
\v	9	weshanukuna Marikiya; wetanhatu kuna Miyamini;
\v	10	wechinomwe kuna Hakozi; wesere kuna Abhija;
\v	11	wepfumbamwe kuna Jeshuwa; wegumi kuna Shekaniya;
\v	12	wegumi neumwe kuna Eriashibhi; wegumi nemiviri kuna Jakimi;
\v	13	wegumi nemitatu kuna Hupa; wegumi nemina kuna Jeshebhiyabhi;
\v	14	wegumi nemishanu kuna Bhiriga; wegumi nemitanhatu kuna Imeri;
\v	15	wegumi neminomwe kuna Heziri; wegumi nemisere kuna Hapizezi;
\v	16	wegumi nemipfumbamwe kuna Petahiya; wemakumi maviri kuna Jehezekeri;
\v	17	wemakumi maviri neumwe kuna Jakini; wemakumi maviri nemiviri kuna Gamuri;
\v	18	wemakumi maviri nemitatu kuna Dheraya; wemakumi maviri nemina kuna Mahaziya.
\v	19	Zvinzvimbo zveavo pashumiro yavo zvaiva zvekupinda muimba yaJehovha zvichienderana nekugadzwa kwavo neruoko rwaAroni baba vavo, seizvo Jehovha Mwari waIsraeri zvaakaraira.
\s	Vakuru vevanakomana vaRevhi
\p
\v	20	Zvino pavanakomana vaRevhi vakasara, pavanakomana vaAmiramu: Shubhaeri; pavanakomana vaShubhaeri: Jehidheya.
\v	21	PaRehabhiya: Pavanakomana vaRehabhiya, musoro Ishishiya.
\v	22	PavaIzihari: Sheromoti; pavanakomana vaSheromoti: Jahati.
\v	23	Nevanakomana  vaHebhuroni: Jeria  wekutanga, Amariya wepiri, Jahaziyeri wetatu, Jekameyamu wechina.
\v	24	Vanakomana vaUziyeri: Mika; pavanakomana vaMika: Shamiri.
\v	25	Munin'ina waMika: Ishishiya; pavanakomana vaIshishiya: Zekariya.
\v	26	Vanakomana vaMerari: Mahiri naMushi; vanakomana vaJahaziya: Bheno.
\v	27	Vanakomana vaMerari: KwaJahaziya: Bheno, naShohamu, naZakuri, naIbhiri.
\v	28	Pana Mahiri: Eriazari; waiva asina vanakomana.
\v	29	Pana Kishi: Vanakomana vaKishi: Jerahimeeri.
\v	30	Nevanakomana vaMushi: Mahiri, naEdheri, naJerimoti. Ava vanakomana vaRevhi zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba avo.
\v	31	Neavawo vakakanda mijenya sehama dzavo, vanakomana vaAroni pamberi paDhavhidhi mambo, naZadhoki, naAhimereki, nemisoro yemadzibaba yevapristi neyevaRevhi, misoro yemadzibaba seyemunin'ina wake.
\c	25
\s	Mapoka evaimbi
\p
\v	1	ZvinoDhavhidhi nevakuru vehondo vakatsaurira shumiro kuvanakomana vaAsafi nekuna Hemani nekuna Jedhutuni, vanoporofita nembira, nemitengeranwa, uye nemakandira; neuwandu hwevarume vebasa hwaienderana neshumiro yavo.
\v	2	KuvanakomanavaAsafi: Zakuri, naJosefa, naNetaniya, naAsarera, vanakomana vaAsafi, pasi peruoko rwaAsafi, waiporofita nemurairo wamambo.
\v	3	KunaJedhutuni: Vanakomana vaJedhutuni: Gedhariya, naZeri, naJeshaya, Hashabhiya, naMatitia, vatanhatu pasi pemaoko ababa vavo Jedhutuni waiporofita nembira, kuvonga nekurumbidza Jehovha.
\v	4	KwaHemani:Vanakomana vaHemani: Bhukiya, Mataniya, Uziyeri, Shebhueri, naJerimoti, Hananiya, Hanani, Eriyata, Gidhariti, naRomamiti-Ezeri, Joshibhekasha, Maroti, Hotiri, Mahazioti.
\v	5	Avavose vanakomana vaHemani, muoni wamambo pamashoko aMwari kusimudza runyanga. Mwari ndokupa kuna Hemani vanakomana gumi nevana, nevakunda vatatu.
\v	6	Avavose vaiva mumaoko ababa vavo pakuimba muimba yaJehovha, nemakandira, mitengeranwa, nembira, pashumiro yeimba yaMwari pasi pemaoko amambo, Asafi, naJedhutuni, naHemani.
\v	7	Zvinouwandu hwavo pamwe nehama dzavo vaiva vakadzidziswa zvenziyo dzaJehovha, vose vaiva neruzivo, hwaiva mazana maviri nemakumi masere nevasere.
\p
\v	8	Zvinovakakanda mijenya pakudondana kwebasa, sezvakaita mudiki sezvakaita mukuru, mudzidzisi pamwe nemudzidzi.
\v	9	Zvinomujenya wekutanga wakabudira Asafi kuna Josefa; wepiri kuna Gedhariya; iye nevanin'ina vake nevanakomana vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	10	wetatu kuna Zakuri, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	11	wechina kuna Iziri, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	12	weshanu kuna Netaniya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	13	wetanhatu kuna Bhukiya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	14	wechinomwe kuna Jesarera, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	15	wesere kuna Jeshaya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	16	wepfumbamwe kuna Mataniya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	17	wegumi kuna Shimei, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	18	wegumi neumwe kuna Azareeri, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	19	wegumi nevaviri kuna Hashabhiya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	20	wegumi nevatatu kuna Shubhayeri, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	21	wegumi nena kuna Matitia, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	22	wegumi neshanu kuna Jeremoti, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	23	wegumi netanhatu kuna Hananiya, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	24	wegumi nenomwe kuna Joshibhekasha, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	25	wegumi nesere kuna Hanani, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	26	wegumi nepfumbamwe kuna Maroti, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;
\v	27	wemakumi maviri kuna Eriyata, vanakomana vake, nevanin'ina vake, gumi nevaviri;

\c	26
\s	Vatariri vetembere
\p
\v	1	Maereranonemapoka evachengeti vemasuwo. KuvaKora: Mesheremiya mwanakomana waKore, wevanakomana vaAsafi.
\v	2	NevanakomanavaMesheremiya: Zekariya dangwe, Jedhiyaeri wepiri, Zebhedhia wetatu, Jatiniyeri wechina,
\v	3	Eramuweshanu, Jehohanani wetanhatu, Erioenai wechinomwe.
\v	4	NaObhedhi-Edhomuwaiva nevanakomana: Shemaya dangwe, Jehozabhadhi wepiri, Jowa wetatu, naSakari wechina, naNetaneri weshanu,
\v	5	Amieriwetanhatu, Isasikari wechinomwe, Peuretai wesere; nokuti Mwari waiva amuropafadza.
\v	6	NekunaShemaya mwanakomana wake kwakaberekwa vanakomana vaitonga imba yababa vavo; nokuti vaiva varume vesimba veumhare.
\v	7	VanakomanavaShemaya: Otini, naRefayeri, naObhedhi, Erizabhadhi, vanin'ina vake, vanakomana veumhare; Erihu naShemakiya.
\v	8	Avavose vaiva vevanakomana vaObhedhi-Edhomu; ivo nevanakomana vavo, nevanin'ina vavo, varume vakakwana nesimba vebasa; makumi matanhatu nevaviri vaObhedhi-Edhomu.
\v	9	UyeMesheremiya waiva nevanakomana nevanin'ina, vanakomana veumhare, gumi nevasere.
\v	10	NaHosa, wevanakomana vaMerari, waiva nevanakomana: Shimiri musoro (kunyange waiva asiri dangwe, baba vake vakamuita musoro),
\v	11	Hirikiya wepiri, Tebhariya wetatu, Zekariya wechina; vose vanakomana nevanin'ina vaHosa, gumi nevatatu.
\v	12	Pakati peava paiva nemapoka evachengeti vemasuwo, pakati pemisoro yevarume vane ngava sehama dzavo, kushumira muimba yaJehovha.
\p
\v	13	Zvino vakakanda mijenya, nemudikiwo semukuru, zvichienderana neimba yemadzibaba avo kuitira suwo nesuwo.
\v	14	Zvino mujenya nechekumabvazuva wakawira kuna Sheremiya; zvino vakakanda mujenya  pamusoro paZekariya mwanakomana wake, gurukota rakachenjera; mujenya wake ndokubudira kumaodzanyemba.
\v	15	Kuna Obhedhi-Edhomu nechekuchamhembe, nekuvanakomana vake, imba yematura.
\v	16	Kuna Shupimi nekuna Hosa nechekumavirira, pamwe nesuwo reShareketi riri pamugwagwa mukuru unokwira, chirindo nechirindo.
\v	17	Nechekumabvazuva kwaiva nevaRevhi vatanhatu, nechekuchamhembe zuva rimwe nerimwe vana, nechekumaodzanyemba zuva rimwe nerimwe vana, nepamatura vaviri-vaviri.
\v	18	PaParibhari nechekumavirazuva, vana pamugwagwa mukuru, vaviri paParibhari.
\v	19	Awa mapoka evachengeti vemikova vevanakomana vevaKora nevevanakomana vaMerari.
\s	Vachengeti vefuma
\p
\v	20	Zvino kuvaRevhi Ahija waiva pamusoro pezvinokosha zveimba yaMwari uye pamusoro pezvinokosha zvezvinhu zvitsvene.
\v	21	Vanakomana vaRadhani: Vanakomana vemuGerishoni, kuRadhani misoro yemadzibaba kwaRadhani muGerishoni: Jehieri.
\v	22	Vanakomana vaJehiyeri: Zetami naJoweri munin'ina wake vaiva pamusoro pezvinokosha zveimba yaJehovha.
\v	23	KuvaAmiramu, kuvaIzihari, kuvaHebhuroni, kuvaUziyeri:
\v	24	NaShebhueri mwanakomana waGerishomu, mwanakomana waMozisi, waiva mukuru pamusoro pezvinokosha.
\v	25	Nevanin'ina vake vaEriezeri: Rehabhia mwanakomana wake, uye Jeshaya mwanakomana wake, uye Joramu mwanakomana wake, uye Zikiri mwanakomana wake, uye Sheromiti mwanakomana wake.
\v	26	Sheromiti uyu nevanin'ina vake vaiva pamusoro pezvinokosha zvose zvezvinhu zvitsvene, izvo Dhavhidhi mambo zvaakatsvenesa, nemisoro yemadzibaba, nevakuru vezvuru nevemazana, nevakuru vehondo.
\v	27	Kubva pahondo nepane zvakapambiwa vakazviita zvitsvene kugadzira imba yaJehovha.
\v	28	Nezvose Samueri muoni zvaakatsvenesa, naSauro mwanakomana waKishi, naAbhineri mwanakomana waNeri, naJoabhu mwanakomana waZeruya, zvose zvaakatsvenesa, zvaiva pasi peruoko rwaSheromoti nevanin'ina vake.
\p
