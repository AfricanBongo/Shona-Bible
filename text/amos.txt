\b	Amosi
\c	1
\s	Kutongwa kwemarudzi akavakidzana neIsraeri
\p
\v	1	MashokoaAmosi, waiva pakati pevafudzi veTekowa, aakaona pamusoro paIsraeri pamazuva aUziya mambo waJudha, nepamazuva aJerobhoamu mwanakomana waJoashi mambo waIsraeri, makore maviri akatangira kudengenyeka kwenyika.
\v	2	Zvinowakati: Jehovha uchaomba ari paZiyoni, nekunzwisa inzwi rake ari paJerusarema; uye mafuro evafudzi achachema, uye musoro weKarimeri uchasvava.
\v	3	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zveDhamasiko, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakapura Giriyadhi nemipuro yematare.
\v	4	Asindichatuma moto muimba yaHazaeri, uchapedza mizinda yaBheni-Hadhadhi.
\v	5	Ndichavhunawochihuri cheDhamasiko, nekugura mugari kubva pabani reAvheni, newakabata tsvimbo yeushe kubva paBheti-Edheni; nevanhu veSiriya vachatapirwa kuKiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	6	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zveGaza, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakatapa zvekutapa kwakaperera, kuti va vape kuna Edhomu.
\v	7	Asindichatuma moto kurusvingo rweGaza, uchapedza mizinda yaro.
\v	8	Zvinondichagura mugari paAshidhodhi, newakabata tsvimbo yeushe paAshikeroni, uye ndichatendeusira ruoko rwangu kurwisa Ekironi; nevakasara vevaFirisitia vachaparara, anoreva Ishe Jehovha.
\p
\v	9	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zveTire, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingadzori; nokuti va vatapira zvekutapa kwakaperera kuna Edhomu, uye vakasarangarira sungano yeukama.
\v	10	Asi ndichatuma moto parusvingo rweTire, uchapedza mizinda yaro.
\p
\v	11	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zveEdhomu, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti wakadzingirira munin'ina wake nemunondo, wakarasa tsitsi dzake, uye kutsamwa kwake kwakabvambura zvichingoenda, uye wakachengeta hasha dzake nekusingaperi.
\v	12	Asi ndichatuma moto paTemani, uchapedza mizinda yeBhozira.
\p
\v	13	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zvevana vaAmoni, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakatumbura vakadzi vaiva nemimba veGiriyadhi, kuti vakurise muganhu wavo.
\v	14	Asi ndichabatidza moto parusvingo rweRabha, uchapedza mizinda yaro, nekudanidzira pazuva rekurwa, nedutu remhepo pazuva rechamupupuri.
\v	15	Uye mambo wavo achaenda muutapwa, iye pamwe chete nemachinda ake, anoreva Jehovha.
\c	2
\p
\v	1	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zvaMoabhu, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakapisa mapfupa amambo weEdhomu kuita raimu.
\v	2	Asindichatuma moto paMoabhu, uchapedza mizinda yeKerioti. Zvino Moabhu achafa nebope, nekudanidzira, nekurira kwehwamanda.
\v	3	Zvinondichagura mutongi kubva pakati pavo, uye ndichauraya machinda ose avo pamwe navo, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	4	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zvaJudha, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakaramba mutemo waJehovha, uye havana kuchengeta mirairo yake, uye nhema dzavo dzakavarasisa, madzibaba avo dzaakatevera.
\v	5	Zvinondichatuma moto paJudha, uchapedza mizinda yeJerusarema.
\p
\v	6	Anorevaseizvi Jehovha: Nekuda kwezvitadzo zvitatu zvaIsraeri, uye nekuda kwezvina, handingakudzori; nokuti vakatengesa wakarurama nesirivha, neanoshaiwa nemanyatera maviri;
\v	7	ivovanotakwairira guruva renyika pamusoro pemusoro wevarombo, uye vanotsausa nzira yevanyoro; uye munhu nababa vake vachienda kumusikana, kuti musvibise zita rangu dzvene.
\v	8	Zvinovanorara pasi panguvo dzechibatiso kumacheto kwearitari iyo neiyo, nekunwa waini yevakaraswa  muimba yevamwari vavo.
\p
\v	9	Zvakadaroini ndakaparadza vaAmori pamberi pavo, kureba kwavo kwaiva kureba kwemisidhari, uye vaiva vakasimba semioki, zvakadaro ndakaparadza chibereko chavo chaiva kumusoro, nemidzi yavo yaiva pasi.
\v	10	Zvino ini ndakakukwidzai kubva munyika yeEgipita, ndokukutungamirirai murenje makore makumi mana, kuti mugare nhaka yenyika yevaAmori.
\v	11	Ndakamutsawo vaporofita pavanakomana venyu, nevaNaziri pamajaya enyu. Hazvina kudaro here, imwi vana vaIsraeri? Anoreva Jehovha.
\v	12	Asi makanwisa vaNaziri waini, nekuraira vaporofita muchiti: Regai kuporofita.
\v	13	Tarirai, ndichakutsikirirai pasi, sengoro izere nemwanda ichitsikira.
\v	14	Naizvozvo nhare ichaparara kune anomhanya, uye ane simba haangasimbisi simba rake, nemhare hainganunuri upenyu hwayo.
\v	15	Neakabata uta haangamiri; neanomhanyisa netsoka dzake haanga zvinunuri; neakatasva bhiza haanganunuri upenyu hwake.
\v	16	Zvino ane moyo wakasimba pakati pemhare achatiza akashama pazuva iro, anoreva Jehovha.
\c	3
\s	Basa remuporofita
\p
\v	1	Inzwaishoko iri Jehovha raakataura akanangana nemwi, imwi vana vaIsraeri, akanangana nemhuri yose yandakakwidza kubva munyika yeEgipita, achiti:
\v	2	Ndimwimoga vandakaziva pamarudzi ose epanyika; naizvozvo ndichakushanyirai nekuda kwezvakaipa zvenyu zvose.
\v	3	Vavirivangafamba pamwe here kunze kwekuti vasangana?
\v	4	Shumbaingaomba musango isina chijimbiwa here? Shumba diki ingapa inzwi rayo mubako rayo isina kubata here?
\v	5	Shiriingawira mumusungo panyika pasina rugombe rwayo here? Musungo ungaurukira kumusoro kubva panyika usina kutongobata here?
\v	6	Hwamandaingaridzwa muguta, vanhu vakasadedera here? Kungava nechakaipa muguta here, Jehovha asina kuzviita?
\v	7	Zvirokwazvo,Ishe Jehovha haangaiti chinhu, asina kuzivisa zvakavanzika zvake kuvaranda vake vaporofita.
\v	8	Shumbayaomba, ndiani angarega kutya? Ishe Jehovha wataura, ndiani angarega kuporofita?
\p
\v	9	Paridzaipamizinda muAshidhodhi nepamizinda munyika yeEgipita; zvino muti: Unganai pamusoro pemakomo eSamariya, mugoona mabope makuru mukati maro, nekumanikidza kuzhinji mukati maro.
\v	10	Nokuti havazivi kuita zvakarurama, anoreva Jehovha, ivo vanounganidza kumanikidza nekupamba mumizinda yavo.
\p
\v	11	Naizvozvo anoreva seizvi Ishe Jehovha: Muvengi, anokomberedza nyika, uye achaburusa simba rako kubva kwauri, uye mizinda yako ichapambiwa.
\v	12	Anoreva seizvi Jehovha: Mufudzi sezvaanonunura kubva mumuromo meshumba makumbo maviri, kana chipandi chenzeve, saizvozvo vana vaIsraeri vachanunurwa, ivo vagere muSamariya pamucheto wemubhedha, nepachidimbu cheuvato.
\p
\v	13	Inzwa, mugopupura muimba yaJakove, anoreva Ishe Jehovha, Mwari wehondo,
\v	14	kuti pazuva randichashanyira zvitadzo zvaIsraeri pamusoro pake, ndichashanyirawo aritari dzeBheteri; nenyanga dzearitari dzichagurwa ndokuwira pasi.
\v	15	Zvino ndicharova imba yemuchando pamwe neimba yemuchirimo, uye dzimba dzenyanga dzenzou dzichaparara, uye dzimba huru dzichapera, anoreva Jehovha.
\c	4
\s	VaIsraeri vanonyeverwa naJehovha
\p
\v	1	Inzwaishoko iri, imwi mhou dzeBhashani, muri mugomo reSamariya, munomanikidza varombo, munopwanya vanoshaiwa, munoti kumadzishe enyu: Unzai tinwe.
\v	2	IsheJehovha wakapika neutsvene hwake, kuti: Tarirai, mazuva achauya pamusoro penyu, avachakusimudzai nedzingwegwe, uye vanokuteverai venyu nezviredzo zvehove.
\v	3	Zvinomuchabuda nepakakoromoka, umwe neumwe pamberi pake; zvino mucha varasira Harimoni, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	4	UyaikuBheteri, mugotadza; paGirigari muwedzere chitadzo; uye muunze chibairo chenyu mangwanani, zvegumi zvenyu makore matatu emazuva.
\v	5	Zvinopisai chibairo chekuvonga nezvakaviriswa, zvino muparidze muzivise chibairo chekuzvidira; nokuti zvakadai munozvida, imwi vana vaIsraeri, anoreva Ishe Jehovha.
\p
\v	6	Neniwondakakupai kuchena kwemazino mumaguta enyu ose, nekushaiwa zvechingwa panzvimbo dzenyu dzose; zvakadaro hamuna kudzokera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	7	Uyezveini ndakanyima mvura kwauri, kuchine mwedzi mitatu kusvika pakukohwa; zvino ndakanaisa pamusoro peguta rimwe, ndikasanaisa pamusoro perimwe guta; rumwe rupandi rwakanaiwa, uye rupandi rwusina kunaiwa rwakatsva.
\v	8	Zvinokwakadzungaira maguta maviri  kana matatu kunonwa mvura muguta rimwe chete, asi haana kugungwa; zvakadaro hamuna kudzokera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	9	Ndakakurovainenyunje uye nekuvhuvha; kuwanda kwemabindu enyu neminda yenyu yemizambiringa nemiti yenyu yemionde nemiti yenyu yemiorivhi, gonye rakazvipedza; zvakadaro hamuna kudzokera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	10	Ndakatuma pakati penyu hosha zvichitevera nzira yeEgipita; majaya enyu ndakaauraya nemunondo, uye ndakatapa mabhiza enyu; uye ndakakwidza munhuhwi wemisasa yenyu kumhino dzenyu; zvakadaro hamuna kudzokera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	11	Ndakaparadza  vamwe venyu, Mwari sezvaakaparadza Sodhoma neGomora. Zvino maiva makaita sechitsiga chakabvutwa pamupfuto; zvakadaro hamuna kudzokera kwandiri, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	12	Naizvozvo ndichaita seizvi kwauri, iwe Israeri; uye nokuti ndichaita izvi kwauri, gadzirira kusangana naMwari wako, iwe Israeri.
\p
\v	13	Nokuti tarirai, uyo wakaumba makomo, nekusika mhepo, nekuzivisa kumunhu kuti mufungo wake chii, iye anosvibirisa mangwanani, nekutsika-tsika panzvimbo dzakakwirira dzepanyika, Jehovha, Mwari wehondo ndiro zita rake.
\c	5
\p
\v	1	Inzwaishoko iri randinokwidza pamusoro penyu, mariro, imba yaIsraeri.
\v	2	MhandarayaIsraeri yawa, haachamukizve; wasiiwa pamusoro penyika yake; hakuna angamumutsa.
\v	3	Nokutianoreva seizvi Ishe Jehovha: Guta rakabuda riri churu richasara rava zana, uye rakabuda riri zana richasara rava gumi, kuimba yaIsraeri.
\p
\v	4	Nokutianoreva seizvi Jehovha kuimba yaIsraeri: Nditsvakei mugorarama.
\v	5	Asimusatsvaka Bheteri, uye musapinda muGirigari, uye musapfuurira  kuBheeri-Shebha; nokuti Girigari richatapwa zvirokwazvo, uye Bheteri richava pasina.
\p
\v	6	TsvakaiJehovha mugorarama, zvimwe apazire imba yaJosefa semoto, ndokupedza, ndokushaya ano udzima paBheteri.
\v	7	Imwimunoshandura mutongo kuva gavakava, nekukanda kururama pasi.
\v	8	Iyeanoita Chirimirira neOrionhi, nekushandura mumvuri werufu kuva mangwanani, nekusvibisa zuva  neusiku, anodana mvura zhinji yegungwa, nekuidurura pamusoro pechiso chenyika, Jehovha ndiro zita rake.
\v	9	Anopenyesakuparara pamusoro pevane simba, kuti kuparara kuuye pamusoro penhare.
\p
\v	10	Vanovenga anotsiura pasuwo, uye vanosema anotaura zvakarurama.
\v	11	Naizvozvo zvamunotsika-tsika murombo, nekutora mutoro wezviyo kwaari, makavaka dzimba nemabwe akavezwa, asi hamungagari madziri, makasima minda yemizambiringa inofadza, asi hamunganwi waini yayo.
\v	12	Nokuti ndinoziva kuti zvitadzo zvenyu zvizhinji, nezvivi zvenyu zvine simba; vanovenga wakarurama, vanogamuchira fufuro, nekutsaura varombo pasuwo.
\v	13	Naizvozvo wakachenjera achanyarara panguva iyo, nokuti inguva yakaipa.
\p
\v	14	Tsvakai zvakanaka, uye kwete zvakaipa, kuti mugorarama, kuti saizvozvo Jehovha Mwari wehondo ave nemwi, sezvamakataura.
\v	15	Vengai zvakaipa, mugoda zvakanaka, mumise mutongo pasuwo; zvimwe Jehovha Mwari wehondo achanzwira nyasha vakasara vaJosefa.
\p
\v	16	Naizvozvo anoreva seizvi Jehovha, Mwari wehondo, Ishe: Kuchava nekuungudza panzira dzose huru, uye panzira dzose dzemumusha vachati: Maiwe! Maiwe! Uye vachadana varimi kuzorira, nevanoziva mariro kuzoungudza.
\v	17	Uye kuungudza kuchavapo paminda yose yemizambiringa, nokuti ndichapfuura nepakati penyu, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	18	Nhamo kunemwi vanoshuva zuva raJehovha! Zuva raJehovha richava rei kwamuri? Richava rima, uye kwete chiedza.
\v	19	Zvakaita semunhu anotiza pamberi peshumba, bheya ndokusangana naye; kana anopinda mumba, ruoko rwake ndokuzembera pamadziro, nyoka ndokumuruma.
\v	20	Zuva raJehovha haringavi rima here, ndokusava chiedza? Nerima guru, kupenya kukasava mariri?
\p
\v	21	Ndinovenga, ndinozvidza mitambo yenyu, uye handinganhuhwi paungano dzenyu dzakadzama.
\v	22	Nokuti kunyange mukandipa zvipo zvinopiswa, nezvipo zvenyu zvezvidyiwa, handingagamuchiri, uye handingarangariri chipo chekuyananisa chezvipfuwo zvenyu zvakakora.
\v	23	Bvisai kwandiri ruzha rwenziyo dzenyu, uye ngandirege kunzwa kurira kwembira dzenyu.
\v	24	Asi mutongo ngauyerere semvura, uye kururama serukova rwunongodira.
\v	25	Makapa here kwandiri zvibairo nechipo murenje makore makumi mana, imwi imba yaIsraeri?
\v	26	Asi makatakura tabhenakeri yaMoroki wenyu neKiyuni zvifananidzo zvenyu, nyeredzi yamwari wenyu wamakazviitira.
\v	27	Naizvozvo ndichakutapirai mhiri kweDhamasiko, anoreva Jehovha, ane zita rinonzi Mwari wehondo.
\c	6
\s	Kuparadzwa kwaIsraeri
\p
\v	1	Nhamokune vakagarika paZiyoni, uye vakatambarara pagomo reSamariya, vakasarudzwa vekutanga pamarudzi, kunoenda kwavari veimba yaIsraeri!
\v	2	PfuuriraikuKarine mugoona, uye kubva ipapo endai kuHamati guru, mugoburukira kuGati revaFirisitia. Vari nani here pane umambo uzhinji uhu? Kana muganhu wavo wakakura kupfuura muganhu wenyu here?
\v	3	Imwimunoisa kure zuva rakaipa, nekuswededza chigaro chemhirizhonga.
\v	4	Varerepamusoro pemibhedha yenyanga dzenzou, vanozvizamura pamusoro penhovo dzavo, nekudya makwayana kubva paboka, nemhuru kubva mukati mechirugu.
\v	5	Vanoimbazvinoenderana nembira, munozvigadzirira midziyo yekuridzisa saDhavhidhi.
\v	6	Vanonwawaini mundiro hofu zhinji, nekuzvizodza nezvizoro zvakaisvonaka, asi havarwadziwi nekuparara kwaJosefa.
\v	7	Naizvozvoikozvino vachatapwa pakati pevekutanga vanoenda muutapwa, uye mabiko evanozvizamura achapera.
\p
\v	8	IsheJehovha wakapika naiye amene, anoreva Jehovha, Mwari wehondo: Ndinosema kuzvikudza kwaJakove, nekuvenga mizinda yake; naizvozvo ndichapa guta nekuzara kwaro ku vavengi.
\p
\v	9	Zvinozvichaitika, kana kukasara varume gumi muimba imwe, kuti vachafa.
\v	10	Uye sekuru vemunhu vachamukwidza, neanomupisa, kubudisa mapfupa mumba, uye achati kune ari pamativi eimba: Kune achinewe here? Zvino iye achati: Kwete. Ipapo iye achati: Nyarara, nokuti ha tingarevi zita raJehovha.
\p
\v	11	Nokuti tarirai, Jehovha anoraira, uye acharova imba huru kuita mafufu, neimba diki kuita mitswe.
\v	12	Mabhiza angamhanya paruware here?  Pangarimwa nemombe here? Nokuti makashandura mutongo kuita nduru, nechibereko chekururama kuita gavakava.
\v	13	Imwi munofarira chinhu chisina maturo; munoti: Hatina kuzvitorera nyanga nesimba redu here?
\v	14	Nokuti tarirai, ndichakumutsirai rudzi, imwi imba yaIsraeri, anoreva Jehovha, Mwari wehondo; rwuchakutambudzai kubva pakupinda paHamati kusvika kurukova rwerenje.
\c	7
\s	Muporofita anoratidzwa mhashu nemoto nerwonzi rwekuyera narwo
\p
\v	1	IsheJehovha wakandiratidza seizvi. Zvino tarira, wakaumba mhashu zhinji pakutanga kukwira kwemabukira ekupedzisira; zvino tarirai, mabukira ekupedzisira shure kwekukohwa kwamambo.
\v	2	Zvinozvakaitika, pavaiva vapedza kudya uswa hwenyika, ndikati: Ishe Jehovha, dai makanganwira, Jakove angamira sei? Nokuti mudiki.
\v	3	Jehovhawakazvidemba pane izvi. Hazvichaitika, anoreva Jehovha.
\p
\v	4	IsheJehovha wakandiratidza seizvi. Zvino tarira, Ishe Jehovha wakadana kurwa nemoto; zvino wakapedza udziku ukuru, ndokudya bandi renyika.
\v	5	Ipapondakati: Ishe Jehovha, dai mamira; Jakove angamira sei? Nokuti mudiki.
\v	6	Jehovhawakazvidemba pamusoro peizvi. Izviwo hazvingaitika, anoreva Ishe Jehovha.
\p
\v	7	Wakandiratidzaseizvi; zvino tarira, Ishe waiva akamira pamadziro  akaitwa serwonzi rwekuyera narwo, ane rwonzi rwekuyera muruoko rwake.
\v	8	Jehovhandokuti kwandiri: Amosi, unoonei? Ini ndokuti: Rwonzi rwekuyera narwo. Ishe ndokuti: Tarirai, ndichaisa rwonzi rwekuyera pakati pevanhu vangu Israeri; handichavadarikizve zvekare.
\v	9	Asinzvimbo dzakakwirira dzaIsaka dzichava matongo, uye nzvimbo tsvene dzaIsraeri dzichaparadzwa; zvino ndichamukira imba yaJerobhoamu nemunondo.
\s	Amaziya anoramba shoko raAmosi paBheteri
\p
\v	10	Zvino Amaziya mupristi weBheteri wakatuma kuna Jerobhoamu mambo waIsraeri achiti: Amosi wakakurangana pakati peimba yaIsraeri; nyika haigoni kutakura mashoko ake ose.
\v	11	Nokuti Amosi wakareva seizvi: Jerobhoamu achafa nemunondo, uye Israeri vachatapwa zvekutapwa kubva munyika yake.
\v	12	Amaziya akatiwo kuna Amosi: Iwe muoni, enda utizire kunyika yaJudha, uye udye chingwa ipapo, ugoporofitapo.
\v	13	Asi usachiporofitazve paBheteri; nokuti inzvimbo tsvene yamambo, uye iimba yeumambo.
\p
\v	14	Zvino Amosi wakapindura ndokuti kuna Amaziya: Ndaiva ndisiri muporofita, uye ndaiva ndisiri mwanakomana wemuporofita; asi ndaiva mufudzi nemurimi wemionde.
\v	15	Zvino Jehovha wakanditora pandaitevera makwai, Jehovha ndokuti kwandiri: Enda, unoporofita kuvanhu vangu Israeri.
\v	16	Naizvozvo inzwa shoko raJehovha: Iwe unoti: Rega kuporofita uchipesana naIsraeri, uye rega kudonhedza shoko uchipesana neimba yaIsaka.
\v	17	Naizvozvo anoreva seizvi Jehovha: Mukadzi wako achava chifeve muguta; uye vanakomana vako nevanasikana vako vachawa nemunondo; uye nyika yako ichagovewa nerwonzi rwekuyera narwo; zvino iwe uchafira munyika yakasvibiswa; naIsraeri achatapwa zvekutapwa abude munyika yake.
\c	8
\s	Amosi anoratidzwa tswanda ine zvibereko
\p
\v	1	IsheJehovha wakandiratidza seizvi; zvino tarirai, tswanda yechibereko chezhizha.
\v	2	Zvinoakati: Amosi, unoonei? Ini ndokuti: Tswanda yechibereko chezhizha. Zvino Jehovha wakati kwandiri: Kuguma kwasvika pamusoro pevanhu vangu Israeri; handichatongovapfuurizve.
\v	3	Asinziyo dzetembere dzichava kuungudza pazuva iro, anoreva Ishe Jehovha; kuchava nezvitunha zvizhinji; panzvimbo imwe neimwe vachazvikandira kunze chinyararire.
\p
\v	4	Inzwaiizvi, imwi munomedza vanoshaiwa, nekuparadza varombo venyika;
\v	5	muchiti:Kugara kwemwedzi kuchapfuura rinhi kuti titengese zviyo? Uye sabata kuti tizarure gorosi?  Tichaita efa rive diki, neshekeri rive guru, nekuita nhema  pazviyero nekunyengera.
\v	6	Kutititenge varombo nesirivha, neanoshaiwa nemanyatera maviri, nekutengesa makoto ezviyo.
\v	7	Jehovhawakapika neukuru hwaJakove  achiti: Zvirokwazvo handingakanganwi mabasa avo ose rinhi narinhi.
\v	8	Nyikahaingadederi nekuda kweizvi here, neumwe neumwe anogaramo akachema? Zvino ichakwira yose serwizi, uye icharasirwa kunze ndokunyudzwa, serwizi rweEgipita.
\v	9	Zvinozvichaitika pazuva iro, anoreva Ishe Jehovha, kuti ndichadokesa zuva pamasikati makuru, uye ndichasvibisa nyika pamasikati machena.
\v	10	Zvino ndichashandura mabiko enyu kuva kurira, nenziyo dzenyu dzose kuva mariro; uye ndichaunza saga pazviuno zvose, neubaravara pamusoro umwe neumwe; uye ndichazviita sekuchemwa  kwemwanakomana umwe oga, nemugumo wazvo sezuva rinovava.

\v	12	Zvino vachadzungaira kubva kugungwa kusvika kugungwa, uye kubva kumaodzanyemba kuenda kumabvazuva; vachamhanya uko neuko kutsvaka shoko raJehovha, asi havangawani.

\c	9
\s	Kutonga kwaJehovha
\p
\v	1	NdakaonaIshe amire pamusoro pearitari; iye ndokuti: Rova chivivo chemukova kuti magwatidziro azungunuke, uzvigure pamusoro wezvose; uye ndichauraya vekupedzisira vavo nemunondo; hakungatizi anotiza wavo, uye hakungapukunyuki anopukunyuka wavo.
\v	2	Kunyangevakachera kuSheori, ruoko rwangu ruchanovatorapo; uye kunyange vakakwira kudenga, ndichavaburusapo.
\v	3	Kunyangevakanovandawo panhongonya yeKarimeri, ndichatsvaka ndivatorepo; uye kunyange vakavanda vachibva pamberi pemeso angu pasi pegungwa, ndicharaira nyoka kubvapo, ichavaruma.
\v	4	Kunyangevachiendawo muutapwa pamberi pevavengi vavo, ndicharaira munondo kubva ipapo kuti uvauraye; uye ndichamisa ziso rangu pamusoro pavo kuita zvakaipa, kwete kuita zvakanaka.
\p
\v	5	ZvinondiIshe Jehovha wehondo anobata nyika, kuti inyauke, uye vose vageremo vacharira; uye ichakukumara yose serwizi, uye ichanyura serwizi rweEgipita.
\v	6	Iyeanovaka nhurikwa dzake kumatenga, uye wakateya hondo yake yakarongwa panyika; anodana mvura zhinji yegungwa, nekuidiridza pachiso chenyika; Jehovha ndiro zita rake.
\p
\v	7	Hamunakuita kwandiri sevana vevaItiyopiya here, imwi vana vaIsraeri? anoreva Jehovha. Handina kukwidza Israeri here kubva panyika yeEgipita, nevaFirisitia paKafitori, nevaSiriya paKiri?
\v	8	Tarirai,meso aIshe Jehovha ari pamusoro peushe hunotadza, uye ndichahuparadza hubve pachiso chenyika; zvakadaro handingaparadzi zvachose imba yaJakove, anoreva Jehovha.
\v	9	Nokutitarirai, ndicharaira, uye ndichapepeta imba yaIsraeri pakati pemarudzi ose, sezvinopepetwa nemusero; zvakadaro hapana tsanga dikisa ichawira pavhu.
\v	10	Vatadzi vose vevanhu vangu vachafa nemunondo, vanoti: Zvakaipa hazvingasenereri kana kusangana nesu.
\v	11	Nezuva iro ndichamutsa dumba raDhavhidhi rakawa, nekudzivira maburi aro, nekumutsa matongo aro, nekurivaka sepamazuva ekare;
\v	12	kuti vagare nhaka yezvakasara zveEdhomu, nemarudzi ose akadanwa nezita rangu, anoreva Jehovha anoita izvi.
\p
\v	13	Tarirai, mazuva anouya, anoreva Jehovha, anorima paachaswedera kune anokohwa, neanotsika zvibereko zvemizambiringa kune anodzvara mbeu; uye makomo achadonha waini inotapira, uye zvikomo zvose zvichanyauka.
\v	14	Zvino ndichashandura utapwa hwevanhu vangu Israeri; uye vachavaka maguta akaparara ndokugara, uye vachadzvara mizambiringa ndokunwa waini yayo, nekuita mabindu ndokudya zvibereko zvawo.
\v	15	Zvino ndichavadzvara panyika yavo, kuti vasachidzurwazve kubva panyika yandakavapa, anoreva Jehovha Mwari wako.
