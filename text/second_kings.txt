\b	2 Madzimambo
\c	1
\s	Kurwara nekufa kwaAhaziya mambo waIsraeri
\p
\v	1	ZvinoMoabhu wakamukira Israeri shure kwekufa kwaAhabhi.
\v	2	ZvinoAhaziya wakawa nepakarukwa pahwindo rekamuri yake yekumusoro, yaiva paSamariya, ndokurwara. Zvino wakatuma nhume ndokuti kwadziri: Endai mubvunze Bhaari-Zebhubhi, mwari weEkironi, kana ndichapora pakurwara uku.
\v	3	Asimutumwa waJehovha wakati kuna Eria muTishibhi: Simuka ukwire kunosangana nenhume dzamambo weSamariya, ugoti kwadziri: Imhaka yekuti hakuna Mwari here kwaIsraeri kuti muende munobvunza Bhaari-Zebhubhi mwari weEkironi?
\v	4	Naizvozvozvino, ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Mubhedha wawakakwira pauri haungaburuki pauri, nokuti uchafa zvekufa. Eria ndokuenda.
\p
\v	5	Zvinonhume dzakadzokera kwaari, iye ndokuti kwadziri: Madzokera chii?
\v	6	Idzondokuti kwaari: Kwakwira umwe murume kusangana nesu ndokuti kwatiri: Endai mudzokere kuna mambo wakakutumai, mugoti kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha. Imhaka yekuti hakuna Mwari kwaIsraeri here, kuti utume kunobvunza Bhaari-Zebhubhi mwari weEkironi? Naizvozvo mubhedha wawakakwira pauri haungaburuki pauri, nokuti uchafa zvekufa.
\v	7	Iyendokuti kwadziri: Murume wakaita sei wakakwira kusangana nemwi ndokukuudzai mashoko awa?
\v	8	Zvinodzakati kwaari: Wanga ari murume ane mvere akasunga nguvo yedehwe pachiuno chake. Iye ndokuti: NdiEria muTishibhi.
\p
\v	9	Zvinowakatuma kwaari mukuru wevane makumi mashanu pamwe nevane makumi mashanu vake. Zvino wakakwira kwaari, zvino tarira, agere panhongonya yegomo. Iye ndokuti kwaari: Munhu waMwari, mambo anoti: Burukai.
\v	10	Zvino Eria wakapindura ndokuti kumukuru wevane makumi mashanu: Kana zvirokwazvo ndiri munhu waMwari, ngakuburuke moto kubva kumatenga, ugokupedza iwe nevane makumi mashanu vako. Zvino moto wakaburuka kubva kumatenga ndokumupedza pamwe nevane makumi mashanu vake.
\p
\v	11	Zvino wakatumazve kwaari umwe mukuru wevane makumi mashanu nevane makumi mashanu vake. Iye ndokupindura ndokuti kwaari: Munhu waMwari, ndizvo zvinoreva mambo: Burukai nekukurumidza.
\v	12	Eria ndokupindura ndokuti kwavari: Kana ndiri munhu waMwari, ngakuburuke moto kubva kumatenga, ukupedze iwe nevane makumi mashanu vako. Zvino moto waMwari wakaburuka kubva kumatenga, ndokumupedza iye nevane makumi mashanu vake.
\p
\v	13	Zvino wakatumazve mukuru wevane makumi mashanu vetatu pamwe nevane makumi mashanu vake. Zvino mukuru wevane makumi mashanu wechitatu wakakwira ndokufugama nemabvi ake pamberi paEria, ndokumukumbira, ndokuti kwaari: Munhu waMwari, upenyu hwangu neupenyu hwevaranda venyu ava vane makumi mashanu ngahuve hahwo hwakakosha pameso enyu.
\v	14	Tarirai, waburuka moto kubva kumatenga, ndokupedza vakuru vaviri vekutanga vevane makumi mashanu nevane makumi mashanu vake; zvino ikozvino, mweya wangu ngauve wakakosha pameso enyu.
\v	15	Zvino mutumwa waJehovha wakati kuna Eria: Buruka pamwe naye, usamutya. Zvino wakasimuka ndokuburuka pamwe naye kuna mambo.
\v	16	Zvino wakati kwaari: Ndizvo zvinoreva Jehovha: Nemhaka yekuti watuma nhume kunobvunza Bhaari-Zebhubhi mwari weEkironi, imhaka yekuti hakuna Mwari here kwaIsraeri kuti  ubvunze shoko rake? Naizvozvo mubhedha wawakakwira pauri haungaburuki pauri, nokuti uchafa zvekufa.
\p
\v	17	Zvino wakafa zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha Eria raakataura. Jehoramu ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake, negore rechipiri raJehoramu mwanakomana waJehoshafati mambo waJudha; nokuti iye waiva asina mwanakomana.
\p
\v	18	Zvino dzimwe nyaya dzaAhaziya dzaakaita, hadzina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri?
\c	10
\s	Rudzi rwaAhabhi nevaporofita vaBhaari vanoparadzwa
\p
\v	1	ZvinoAhabhi waiva ane vanakomana makumi manomwe paSamariya. Jehu ndokunyora tsamba, ndokudzitumira kuSamariya kumisoro yeJezireeri, vakuru, nekuvareri  vevana vaAhabhi, achiti:
\v	2	Zvinoikozvino pakusvika kwetsamba iyi kwamuri, vanakomana vatenzi wenyu zvavari kwamuri, uye ngoro nemabhiza zviri kwamuri, neguta rakashoshewa, nezvombo,
\v	3	zvionereiwakanakisisa uye wakafanira kuvanakomana vatenzi wenyu, muise pachigaro cheushe chababa vake, uye murwire imba yatenzi wenyu.
\v	4	Asivakatya zvikurusa, ndokuti: Tarirai, madzimambo maviri haana kumira pamberi pake, zvino isu tichamira sei?
\v	5	Zvinoumwe waitarira imba, neumwe waitarira guta, nevakuru, nevareri  vevana vakatuma kuna Jehu, vachiti: Tiri varanda venyu, uye zvose zvamuchareva kwatiri tichaita. Hatingaisi munhu ave mambo. Itai zvakanaka pameso enyu.
\p
\v	6	Zvinowakavanyorera tsamba rwechipiri achiti: Kana muri vangu uye muchiteerera kuinzwi rangu, torai misoro yevarume ava, vanakomana vamambo wenyu, mugouya kwandiri kuJezireeri mangwana nenguva inenge ino. Zvino vanakomana vamambo, vanhu makumi manomwe, vaiva kuvakuru veguta vaivarera.
\v	7	Zvinozvakaitika pakusvika kwetsamba kwavari, kuti vakatora vanakomana vamambo, ndokuuraya vanhu makumi manomwe, ndokuisa misoro yavo mumatengu, ndokutumira kwaari kuJezireeri.
\p
\v	8	Zvinokwakauya nhume ndokumuudza ichiti: Vaunza misoro yevanakomana vamambo. Iye ndokuti: Iitei mirwi miviri pamukova wesuwo kusvika mangwanani.
\v	9	Zvinozvakaitika mangwanani kuti wakabuda ndokumira ndokuti kuvanhu vese: Imwi makarurama, tarirai ini ndakarongera tenzi wangu, ndokumuuraya; asi ndiani wauraya ava vese?
\v	10	Zivai ikozvino kuti hakungawiri chinhu pasi pashoko raJehovha, Jehovha raakataura pamusoro peimba yaAhabhi; nokuti Jehovha wakaita zvaakataura neruoko rwemuranda wake Eria.
\v	11	Naizvozvo Jehu wakauraya vose vaiva vasara veimba yaAhabhi muJezireeri, nevakuru vake vose, nevazikanwi vake, nevapristi vake, kusvika asina kumusiira musiiwa.
\p
\v	12	Zvino wakasimuka ndokubva ndokuenda kuSamariya; ari paimba yekuveurira  makwai yevafudzi panzira,
\v	13	Jehu wakasangana nevanin'ina vaAhaziya mambo waJudha, ndokuti: Ndimwi vana ani? Ivo ndokuti: Tiri vanin'ina vaAhaziya, uye tinoburuka kunokwazisa vana vamambo nevana vavahosi.
\v	14	Iye ndokuti: Vabatei vari vapenyu. Zvino vakavabata vari vapenyu, ndokuvauraira pagomba reBheti-Ekedhi, varume makumi mana nevaviri; uye haana kusiya umwe wavo.
\p
\v	15	Wakati abvapo, wakawana Jehonadhabhi mwanakomana waRekabhu,  achizomuchingamidza; ndokumukwazisa, ndokuti kwaari: Moyo wako wakarurama here, sezvo moyo wangu une moyo wako? Jehonadhabhi ndokuti: Wakadaro. Kana zvakadaro,  ndipe ruoko rwako. Zvino wakapa ruoko rwake ndokumukwidza mungoro kwaari.
\v	16	Zvino wakati: Hendei neni, mugovona kushingairira kwangu Jehovha. Zvino vakamutasvisa mungoro yake.
\v	17	Pakusvika kwake kuSamariya, wakarova vose vaiva vasara kuna Ahabhi muSamariya kusvika amupedza, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha raakataura kuna Eria.
\p
\v	18	Zvino Jehu akaunganidza vanhu vose, ndokuti kwavari: Ahabhi wakashumira Bhaari zvishoma; Jehu achamushumira zvikuru.
\v	19	Naizvozvo ikozvino ndidanirei vaporofita vose vaBhaari, varanda vake vose, nevapristi vake vose, kusava neanoshaikwa; nokuti ndine chibairo chikuru kuna Bhaari; ani nani anoshaikwa haangararami. Asi Jehu wakaita nekunyengera, nechinangwa chekuti aparadze varanda vaBhaari.
\v	20	Jehu ndokuti: Tsaurirai Bhaari mutambo wakadzama. Zvino vakaudanidzira.
\v	21	Zvino Jehu wakatuma  nhume kuna Israeri wose, varanda vose vaBhaari ndokuuya; hakuna kutongosara umwe asina kuuya. Zvino vakapinda mumba maBhaari kusvika yazara imba yaBhaari kubva kumuromo kusvika kumuromo.
\v	22	Zvino wakati kuumwe waitarira imba yenguvo: Budisira varanda vose vaBhaari nguvo. Iye akavabudisira nguvo.
\v	23	Zvino Jehu wakapinda ana Jehonadhabhi mwanakomana waRekabhi muimba yaBhaari, ndokuti kuvaranda vaBhaari: Nzverai muone kuti hakuna kwamuri vevaranda vaJehovha here pano, asi varanda vaBhaari chete.
\v	24	Zvino vapinda kuita zvibairo nezvipo zvinopiswa, Jehu waiva azvigadzira varume makumi masere panze ndokuti: Munhu kuvarume vandaisa mumaoko enyu achipukunyuka, upenyu hwake huchava pachinzvimbo cheupenyu hwake.
\v	25	Zvino zvakaitika paakangopedza kuita chipo chinopiswa, kuti Jehu wakati kuvarindi nekuvatungamiriri: Pindai, muvauraye; kusabuda munhu. Zvino vakavarova nekwakapinza kwemunondo; varindi nevatungamiriri ndokuvapotsera, ndokuenda kuguta reimba yaBhaari.
\v	26	Zvino vakabudisa mbiru dziri zvifananidzo zveimba yaBhaari, ndokudzipisa.
\v	27	Vakaputsawo mbiru yaBhaari iri chifananidzo, ndokuputsa imba yaBhaari, ndokuiita imba yezvimbuzi kusvika nhasi.
\v	28	Saizvozvo Jehu wakaparadza Bhaari kubva muIsraeri.
\p
\v	29	Asi zvivi zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze, Jehu haana kubva pakuzvitevera, mhuru dzegoridhe dzaiva paBheteri nedzaiva paDhani.
\v	30	Zvino Jehovha wakati kuna Jehu: Zvawakaita zvakanaka pakuita zvakarurama pameso angu, waita kuimba yaAhabhi zvinoenderana nezvose zvanga zviri mumoyo mangu, vana vako verudzi rwechina vachagara pachigaro cheushe hwaIsraeri.
\v	31	Asi Jehu haana kuchenjerera kufamba mumurairo waJehovha Mwari waIsraeri nemoyo wake wese; haana kubva pazvivi zvaJerobhoamu zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze.
\p
\v	32	Pamazuva iwawo Jehovha wakatanga kutapudza Israeri; Hazaeri ndokuvarova pamiganhu yose yekwaIsraeri:
\v	33	Kubva kuna Joridhani nechekumabvazuva, nyika yose yeGiriyadhi, vekwaGadhi, nevekwaRubheni, nevekwaManase, kubva paAroeri riri pamupata weArinoni, neGiriyadhi neBhashani.
\p
\v	34	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaJehu, nezvose zvaakaita, nesimba rake rose, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri?
\v	35	Jehu ndokurara nemadzibaba ake, ivo ndokumuviga paSamariya; Jehoahazi mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	36	Zvino mazuva aJehu aakatonga pamusoro paIsraeri paSamariya aiva makore makumi maviri nemasere.
\c	11
\s	Atariya mai vaAhaziya anobata ushe hwaJudha
\p
\v	1	Atariyamai vaAhaziya paakaona kuti mwanakomana wake wafa, wakasimuka ndokuparadza mbeu yose yeumambo.
\v	2	AsiJehoshebha mukunda wamambo Joramu, hanzvadzi yaAhaziya, wakatora Joashi mwanakomana waAhaziya, ndokumuba kubva pakati pevanakomana vamambo vaiurawa, iye nemureri wake mukamuri yemibhedha. Zvino vakamuviga pachiso chaAtariya kuti asaurawa.
\v	3	Zvinowaiva naye muimba yaJehovha akazvivanza makore matanhatu; Atariya ndokutonga pamusoro penyika.
\p
\v	4	Zvinonegore rechinomwe Jehoyadha wakatuma  nhume ndokutora misoro yemazana yevaKari neyevarindi, ndokuiunza kwaari muimba yaJehovha, ndokuita sungano navo, ndokuvapikisa muimba yaJehovha, ndokuvaratidza mwanakomana wamambo.
\v	5	Zvinowakavaraira achiti: Ichi chinhu chamuchaita: Chetatu chenyu chinopinda nesabata chichava varindi vechirindo cheimba yamambo;
\v	6	uyechetatu chichava pasuwo reSuri; uye chetatu pasuwo shure kwevarindi; saizvozvo muchachengeta chirindo cheimba, isapaziwa.
\v	7	Zvinomapoka maviri enyu mose munobuda nesabata, naivo vachachengetedza chirindo cheimba yaJehovha maererano namambo.
\v	8	Zvinomuchakomba mambo kupoteredza, murume umwe neumwe ane zvombo zvake muruoko rwake; uye anopinda pamitsara achaurawa. Muve namambo pakubuda kwake nepakupinda kwake.
\v	9	Zvinomisoro yemazana yakaita zvinoenderana nezvose mupristi Jehoyadha zvaakaraira; ndokutora umwe neumwe vanhu vake vaipinda nesabata, pamwe nevaibuda nesabata, ndokusvika kuna Jehoyadha mupristi.
\v	10	Zvino mupristi wakapa misoro yemazana mapfumo nenhovo zvaiva zvamambo Dhavhidhi zvaiva mumba maJehovha.
\v	11	Zvino varindi vakamira, umwe neumwe ane zvombo zvake muruoko rwake, kubva kudivi rerudyi reimba kusvika kudivi reruboshwe reimba, paaritari nepaimba kuna mambo mativi ose.
\v	12	Zvino wakabudisa mwanakomana wamambo, ndokuisa korona pamusoro pake, nechipupuriro, zvino vakamuisa akava mambo, vakamuzodza; ndokurova maoko, ndokuti: Mambo ngaararame!
\p
\v	13	Atariya paakanzwa mhere-mhere yevarindi  nevanhu, wakaenda kuvanhu muimba yaJehovha.
\v	14	Zvino wakaona, tarira zvino, mambo amire pambiru setsika, nevatungamiriri nevaridzi vehwamanda vari kuna mambo; uye vanhu vose venyika vakafara vachiridza dzihwamanda. Zvino Atariya akabvarura nguvo dzake, ndokudanidzira achiti: Kurongera, kurongera!
\v	15	Zvino Jehoyadha mupristi wakaraira misoro yemazana, vabati vehondo, ndokuti kwavari: Mubudisirei kunze kwemitsara; neanomutevera mumuuraye nemunondo. Nokuti mupristi wakati: Ngaasaurairwa muimba yaJehovha.
\v	16	Zvino vakamubata nemaoko, iye ndokuenda nenzira mabhiza yaakapinda nayo muimba yamambo, ndokuurayirwapo.
\p
\v	17	Zvino Jehoyadha wakaita sungano pakati paJehovha namambo nevanhu, kuti vave vanhu vaJehovha; uye pakati pamambo nevanhu.
\v	18	Zvino vanhu vose venyika vakapinda muimba yaBhaari, ndokuiputsira pasi; aritari dzake nemifananidzo yake vakazviparadza kwazvo, uye Matani mupristi waBhaari vakamuuraya pamberi pedziaritari. Mupristi ndokuisa vatariri pamusoro peimba yaJehovha.
\v	19	Zvino wakatora misoro yemazana, nevaKari, nevarindi, nevanhu vose venyika, ndokuburusa mambo kubva paimba yaJehovha, ndokuuya nenzira yesuwo revarindi kuimba yamambo. Zvino akagara pachigaro cheushe chemadzimambo.
\v	20	Zvino vanhu vose venyika vakafara, guta ndokuva murunyararo, vauraya Atariya nemunondo  paimba yamambo.
\v	21	Jehoashi waiva ane makore manomwe paakava mambo.
\c	12
\s	Jehoashi anogadzira tembere
\p
\v	1	Mugorerechinomwe raJehu Jehoashi wakava mambo, ndokutonga makore makumi mana paJerusarema; uye zita ramai vake raiva Zibhia weBheeri-Shebha.
\v	2	Jehoashindokuita zvakarurama pameso aJehovha mazuva ake ose mupristi Jehoyadha aakamudzidzisa nawo.
\v	3	Asinzvimbo dzakakwirira hadzina kusuduruswa; vanhu vaingopa zvibairo vachipisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira panzvimbo dzakakwirira.
\p
\v	4	ZvinoJehoashi wakati kuvapristi: Mari yose yezvinhu zvakaitwa zvitsvene inouiswa muimba yaJehovha, mari yeumwe neumwe anopfuura  kune vakaverengwa, mari yeumwe neumwe yaanoyera, mari yose, zvinobva pamoyo yeupi neupi munhu kuiuisa kuimba yaJehovha,
\v	5	vapristingavaitore kwavari, umwe neumwe kumuzikanwi wake; zvino ivo vanogadzirisa maburi eimba pose panowanikwa buri.
\v	6	Zvinozvakaitika mugore remakumi maviri nematatu ramambo Jehoashi, vapristi vaiva va sati vagadzirisa maburi eimba.
\v	7	Zvinomambo Jehoashi wakadana Jehoyadha mupristi nevapristi, ndokuti kwavari: Munoregerei kugadzira maburi eimba? Naizvozvo musatora mari kuvazikanwi venyu, asi muipe kumaburi eimba.
\v	8	Zvinovapristi vakatenda kusatora mari kuvanhu, kana kugadzira maburi eimba.
\v	9	AsiJehoyadha mupristi wakatora bhokisi rimwe, ndokuboora buri pachifunhiro charo, ndokurigadzika parutivi rwearitari, kurudyi kana munhu achipinda paimba yaJehovha. Zvino vapristi vanorinda sasa vakaisa mariri mari yose yakauiswa kuimba yaJehovha.
\v	10	Zvino zvakaitika pakuona kwavo kuti mari yawanda mubhokisi, kuti kwakakwira munyori wamambo nemupristi mukuru ndokuuya ndoku isunga ndokuverenga mari yakawanikwa muimba yaJehovha.
\v	11	Zvino vakapa mari yakayerwa mumaoko evaiti vebasa, vaongorori veimba yaJehovha; ndokuibudisira kuvavezi vematanda nekuvavaki vaishanda muimba yaJehovha,
\v	12	nekuvarongi vemabwe nekuvavezi vemabwe, nekutenga matanda nemabwe akavezwa, kugadzira maburi eimba yaJehovha, nekune zvose zvakabudisirwa imba kuigadzirisa.
\v	13	Asi hakuna kuitirwa imba yaJehovha ndiro dzesirivha, mbato dzemwenje, madhishi ekusasira, hwamanda, midziyo ipi neipi yegoridhe, nemidziyo yesirivha, kubva pamari yakauiswa kuimba yaJehovha.
\v	14	Asi vakaipa kuvaiti vebasa, ivo ndokugadzirisa nayo imba yaJehovha.
\v	15	Uye havana kuzoverengera vanhu muruoko rwavo mavakapa mari kupa kuvaiti vebasa; nokuti vaiita nekuvimbika.
\v	16	Mari yechipo chekudarika nemari yechipo chechivi haina kuuiswa muimba yaJehovha; yaiva yevapristi.
\p
\v	17	Panguva iyoyo Hazaeri mambo weSiriya wakakwira ndokurwa neGati, ndokuritapa; Hazaeri ndokuringira chiso chake kukwira kuJerusarema.
\v	18	Zvino Jehoashi mambo waJudha wakatora zvinhu zvose zvakaitwa zvitsvene izvo Jehoshafati naJehoramu naAhaziya, madzibaba ake, madzimambo aJudha zvavaiva vaita zvitsvene, nezvinhu zvake zvakaitwa zvitsvene, negoridhe rose rakawanikwa pafuma yeimba yaJehovha neimba yamambo, ndokutumira kuna Hazaeri mambo weSiriya; iye ndokukwira kubva Jerusarema.
\p
\v	19	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaJoashi, nezvose zvaakaita, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aJudha?
\v	20	Zvino varanda vake vakasimuka, ndokuita rangano, ndokurova Joashi muimba yaMiro, inoburukira kuSira.
\v	21	Nokuti Jozabhadhi mwanakomana waShimeati, naJehozabhadhi mwanakomana waShomeri, varanda vake, vakamurova, iye ndokufa; zvino vakamuviga pamwe nemadzibaba ake muguta raDhavhidhi. Amaziya mwanakomana wake akava mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\c	13
\s	Jehoahazi naJoashi madzimambo aIsraeri
\p
\v	1	Mugoreremakumi maviri nematatu raJoashi mwanakomana waAhaziya mambo waJudha, Jehoahazi mwanakomana waJehu wakava mambo pamusoro paIsraeri muSamariya, makore gumi nemanomwe.
\v	2	Zvinowakaita zvakaipa pameso paJehovha, ndokutevera zvivi zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze; haana kutsauka kwazviri.
\p
\v	3	Zvinokutsamwa kwaJehovha kwakapfutira Israeri, ndokuvapa muruoko rwaHazaeri mambo weSiriya, nemuruoko rwaBheni-Hadhadhi mwanakomana waHazaeri, mazuva ose.
\v	4	ZvinoJehoahazi wakanyengeterera chiso chaJehovha; Jehovha ndokumunzwa; nokuti waiva aona kudzvinyirirwa kwaIsraeri nokuti mambo weSiriya waivadzvinyirira.
\v	5	ZvinoJehovha wakapa kuna Israeri muponesi, kuti vabude pasi peruoko rwevaSiriya. Vana vaIsraeri ndokugara mumatende avo sazuro  namarimwezuro.
\v	6	Kunyangezvakadaro havana kutsauka kubva pazvivi zveimba yaJerobhoamu zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze; vakafamba mazviri; nedandawo rekunamata naro raingova paSamariya.
\v	7	Nokutihaana kusiira Jehoahazi  vamwe vevanhu, kunze kwevatasvi vemabhiza makumi mashanu, nengoro gumi, nevanofamba netsoka zvuru gumi; nokuti mambo weSiriya waiva avaparadza, ndokuvaita seguruva nekupura.
\p

\v	11	Zvino wakaita zvakaipa pameso aJehovha; haana kutsauka pazvivi zvose zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze; akafamba mazviri.

\v	13	Zvino Joashi wakarara nemadzibaba ake. Jerobhoamu ndokugara pachigaro chake cheushe. Joashi ndokuvigwa muSamariya pamwe nemadzimambo aIsraeri.

\v	15	Erisha ndokuti kwaari: Tora uta nemiseve. Iye ndokuzvitorera uta nemiseve.

\v	18	Zvino wakati: Tora miseve. Iye ndokutora. Iye ndokuti kuna mambo waIsraeri: Rova pasi! Iye ndokurova katatu, ndokumira.
\v	19	Zvino munhu waMwari wakatsamwiswa naye, ndokuti: Dai wakarova kashanu kana katanhatu; ipapo waizorova Siriya kusvika waipedza; zvino ikozvino ucharova Siriya katatu.

\v	20	Zvino Erisha wakafa, ivo ndokumuviga. Zvino hondo dzakarongwa dzaMoabhu dzakapinda munyika pakupinda kwegore.
\v	21	Zvino zvakaitika pavaiviga munhu, zvino tarira, vakaona hondo yakarongwa, ndokukanda munhu muguva raErisha; zvino munhu paakanogunzva mapfupa aErisha, wakararama, ndokusimuka nemakumbo ake.
\p
\v	22	Zvino Hazaeri mambo weSiriya wakatambudza Israeri mazuva ose aJehoahazi.
\v	23	Asi Jehovha wakavaitira nyasha, ndokuvanzwira tsitsi, ndokutendeukira kwavari, nekuda kwesungano yake naAbhurahama, Isaka, naJakove, uye haana kuda kuvaparadza, uye haana kuvarasa pachiso chake kusvika ikozvino.
\v	24	Zvino Hazaeri mambo weSiriya wakafa; Bheni-Hadhadhi mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\v	25	Zvino Jehoashi mwanakomana waJehoahazi akatorerazve kubva muruoko rwaBheni-Hadhadhi mwanakomana waHazaeri maguta aaiva atora muruoko rwaJehoahazi baba vake nehondo. Joashi ndokumurova katatu, ndokudzosera maguta aIsraeri.

\v	3	Zvinowakaita zvakarurama pameso aJehovha, asi hazvina kufanana nababa vake Dhavhidhi; wakaita sezvose baba vake Joashi zvavakaita.
\v	4	Kunyangezvakadaro nzvimbo dzakakwirira hadzina kubviswa; vanhu vaingobaira nekupisa zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira panzvimbo dzakakwirira.
\p
\v	5	Zvinozvakaitika ushe hwasimbiswa muruoko rwake kuti wakarova varanda vake vaiva varova mambo baba vake.
\v	6	Asivana vevaurayi haana kuuraya, zvichienderana nezvakanyorwa mubhuku remurairo waMozisi, Jehovha maakaraira achiti: Madzibaba haangaurawi nekuda kwevana, uye vana havangaurawi nekuda kwemadzibaba; asi munhu umwe neumwe achaurairwa chivi chake.
\v	7	Iyewakarova vaEdhomu zvuru gumi pamupata wemunyu, ndokutapa Sera nehondo, ndokutumidza zita rayo Joketeeri kusvika zuva ranhasi.
\p
\v	8	IpapoAmaziya wakatuma nhume kuna Jehoashi mwanakomana waJehoahazi mwanakomana waJehu, mambo waIsraeri, achiti: Uya titarisane chiso nechiso.
\v	9	ZvinoJehoashi mambo waIsraeri wakatuma kuna Amaziya mambo waJudha, achiti: Munzwa uyu waiva paRebhanoni wakatuma kumusidhari waiva paRebhanoni, uchiti: Ipa mukunda wako kumwanakomana wangu kuva mukadzi. Zvino kwakapfuura chikara chesango chiri paRebhanoni, ndokutsikira munzwa pasi.
\v	10	Warova zvirokwazvo Edhomu, uye moyo wako wakusimudzira. Kudzika, ugare mumba mako; nokuti unozvimutsirirei njodzi, kuti uwe iwe naJudha pamwe newe?
\v	11	Asi Amaziya haana kunzwa. Naizvozvo Jehoashi mambo waIsraeri wakakwira, iye naAmaziya mambo waJudha vakatarisana chiso nechiso paBheti-Shemeshi riri raJudha.
\v	12	Zvino Judha wakarohwa pamberi paIsraeri, ndokutiza umwe neumwe kutende rake.
\v	13	Jehoashi mambo waIsraeri ndokubata Amaziya mambo waJudha, mwanakomana waJehoashi, mwanakomana waAhaziya, paBheti-Shemeshi, ndokusvika paJerusarema, ndokuputsa rusvingo rweJerusarema pasuwo raEfuremu kusvika pasuwo rapakona, makubhiti mazana mana.
\v	14	Zvino wakatora goridhe rose, nesirivha, nemidziyo yose yakawanikwa muimba yaJehovha, nepafuma yeimba yamambo, nevaiva chibatiso, ndokudzokera kuSamariya.
\p
\v	15	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaJehoashi dzaakaita, nesimba rake, nokuti wakarwa sei naAmaziya mambo waJudha, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri?
\v	16	Zvino Jehoashi wakarara nemadzibaba ake, ndokuvigwa paSamariya kumadzimambo aIsraeri; Jerobhoamu mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\p
\v	17	Zvino Amaziya mwanakomana waJoashi mambo waJudha wakararama makore gumi nemashanu shure kwekufa kwaJehoashi mwanakomana waJehoahazi mambo waIsraeri.
\v	18	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaAmaziya hadzina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aJudha?
\v	19	Zvino vakamurongera rangano paJerusarema, iye ndokutizira kuRakishi; asi vakatuma  vanhu kumutevera kuRakishi, ndokumuurairapo.
\v	20	Zvino vakamutakura nemabhiza, uye wakavigwa muJerusarema kumadzibaba ake muguta raDhavhidhi.
\p
\v	21	Zvino vanhu vose vaJudha vakatora Azariya, waiva nemakore gumi nematanhatu, ndokumuita mambo pachinzvimbo chababa vake Amaziya.
\v	22	Iye wakavaka Erati, ndokuridzosera kuna Judha, shure kwekurara kwamambo pamwe nemadzibaba ake.
\p
\v	23	Mugore regumi nemashanu raAmaziya mwanakomana waJoashi mambo waJudha, Jerobhoamu mwanakomana waJoashi mambo waIsraeri wakava mambo paSamariya, makore makumi mana nerimwe.
\v	24	Zvino wakaita zvakaipa pameso aJehovha; haana kutsauka kubva pazvivi zvose zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, zvaakatadzisa Israeri.
\v	25	Iye wakadzosera muganhu waIsraeri kubva pakupinda paHamati kusvika pagungwa remapani, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha Mwari waIsraeri, raakataura neruoko rwemuranda wake Jona mwanakomana waAmitai, muporofita waibva kuGati-Heferi.
\v	26	Nokuti Jehovha wakaona kutambudzika kwaIsraeri, kwarwadza zvikuru; nokuti kwaiva kusina anovharirwa, uye kusina wakasununguka, uye kusina mubatsiri kwaIsraeri.
\v	27	Zvino Jehovha haana kureva kuti achadzima zita raIsraeri pasi pematenga, asi wakavaponesa neruoko rwaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waJoashi.
\p
\v	28	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaJerobhoamu, nezvose zvaakaita, nesimba rake, kuti wakarwa sei, uye kuti wakadzosa sei Dhamasiko neHamati kuna Israeri, aiva aJudha, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri?
\v	29	Zvino Jerobhoamu wakarara nemadzibaba ake, nemadzimambo aIsraeri; Zekariya mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.

\v	18	Zvino wakaita zvakaipa pameso aJehovha; haana kutsauka mazuva ake ose pazvivi zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati zvaakaita kuti Israeri atadze.
\v	19	Puri mambo weAsiriya wakauya kurwisa nyika; Menahemi ndokupa kuna Puri matarenda churu esirivha, kuti ruoko rwake rwuve naye kusimbisa umambo muruoko rwake.
\v	20	Menahemi ndokuripisa Israeri mari, vanhu vose vesimba paufumi, kupa mambo weAsiriya, pamunhu umwe neumwe mashekeri makumi mashanu esirivha. Mambo weAsiriya ndokudzokera, akasagarapo panyika.

\v	21	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaMenahemi, nezvose zvaakaita, hazvina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri?

\v	24	Zvino wakaita zvakaipa pameso aJehovha; haana kutsauka pazvivi zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, wakatadzisa Israeri.
\v	25	Asi Peka mwanakomana waRemariya mukuru wake wakamurongera ndokumurova paSamariya, munhare yeimba yamambo, pamwe naArigobhi naAriye, nevanhu makumi mashanu vevanakomana vevaGiriyadhi vaaiva navo; ndokumuuraya, ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.
\p
\v	26	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaPekahia, nezvose zvaakaita, tarirai zvakanyorwa mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aIsraeri.
\p
\v	27	Mugore remakumi mashanu nemaviri raAzariya mambo waJudha, Peka mwanakomana waRemariya wakava mambo pamusoro paIsraeri paSamariya, makore makumi maviri.
\v	28	Zvino wakaita chakaipa pameso paJehovha; haana kutsauka pazvivi zvaJerobhoamu mwanakomana waNebhati, wakatadzisa Israeri.
\v	29	Mumazuva aPeka mambo waIsraeri kwakauya Tigirati-Pireseri mambo weAsiriya, ndokutora Ijoni, neAbheri-Bheti-Maaka, neJanowa, neKadheshi, neHazori, neGiriyadhi, neGarirea, nyika yose yaNafutari; ndokuvatapa kuenda kuAsiriya.
\v	30	Zvino Hoshea mwanakomana waEra wakarongera rangano Peka mwanakomana waRemariya, ndokumurova, ndokumuuraya, ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake mugore remakumi maviri raJotamu mwanakomana waUziya.

\v	5	IpapoRezini mambo weSiriya, naPeka mwanakomana waRemariya mambo waIsraeri, vakakwira kuJerusarema kuhondo; vakakomba Ahazi, asi havana kugona ku mukunda.
\v	6	Panguvaiyo Rezini mambo weSiriya wakadzosera Erati kuna Siriya, ndokubvisa vaJudha paErati; VaSiriya ndokuuya paErati, ndokugarapo kusvika nhasi.
\v	7	ZvinoAhazi wakatuma nhume kuna Tigirati-Pireseri mambo weAsiriya, achiti: Ndiri muranda wenyu uye mwanakomana wenyu; kwirai mundiponese kubva muruoko rwamambo weSiriya, nekubva muruoko rwamambo waIsraeri, vanondimukira.
\v	8	ZvinoAhazi wakatora sirivha negoridhe zvakawanikwa muimba yaJehovha uye mufuma yeimba yamambo, ndokutumira kuna mambo weAsiriya,  chive chipo.
\v	9	Zvinomambo weAsiriya wakamuteerera, nokuti mambo weAsiriya wakakwira kunorwisa Dhamasiko, ndokuritapa, ndokutapira  vanhu  varo kuKiri, ndokuuraya Rezini.

\v	11	Zvino Uriya mupristi wakavaka aritari zvichienderana nezvose mambo Ahazi zvaakatuma kubva kuDhamasiko, mupristi Uriya wakaiita saizvozvo kusvika mambo Ahazi asvika kubva kuDhamasiko.
\v	12	Mambo wakati adzoka Dhamasiko, mambo wakaona aritari; mambo ndokuswedera paaritari, ndokubaira pamusoro payo.
\v	13	Zvino wakapisa chipo chake chinopiswa nechipo chake chechidyiwa, ndokudira chipo chake chechinwiwa, ndokusasa ropa rechipo chekuyananisa chaaiva nacho pamusoro pearitari.
\v	14	Asi wakaunzawo aritari yendarira yaiva pamberi paJehovha kubva pamberi peimba, kubva pakati pearitari neimba yaJehovha, ndokuiisa parutivi rwekumaodzanyemba rwearitari.
\v	15	Zvino mambo Ahazi wakaraira Uriya mupristi, achiti: Pamusoro pearitari huru pisa chipo chinopiswa chemangwanani, nechipo chechidyiwa chemadekwani, nechipo chinopiswa chamambo, nechipo chake chechidyiwa, nechipo chinopiswa chevanhu vose venyika, nechipo chavo chechidyiwa, nezvipo zvavo zvechinwiwa; usase pamusoro payo ropa rose rechipo chinopiswa, neropa rose rechibairo; asi aritari yendarira ichava yangu yekubvunzira.
\v	16	Mupristi Uriya ndokuita zvichienderana nezvose mambo Ahazi zvaakaraira.

\v	17	Mambo Ahazi ndokugura mipendero yezvigadziko, ndokubvisa dhishi rekugezera pamusoro pazvo, nekuburusa gungwa pamusoro penzombe dzendarira dzaiva pasi paro, ndokuriisa pakarongerwa nemabwe.
\v	18	Nepakafukidzwa pesabata pavaiva vakavaka muimba nesuwo ramambo kunze wakazvibvisa kubva muimba yaJehovha, nekuda kwamambo weAsiriya.

\v	19	Zvino dzimwe dzenyaya dzaAhazi dzaakaita, hadzina kunyorwa here mubhuku reMakoronike emadzimambo aJudha?
\v	20	Zvino Ahazi wakarara nemadzibaba ake, ndokuvigwa kumadzibaba ake muguta raDhavhidhi. Hezekia mwanakomana wake ndokuva mambo pachinzvimbo chake.

\v	4	Iyewakabvisa nzvimbo dzakakwirira, ndokuputsa shongwe dziri zvifananidzo, ndokutema matanda ekunamata nawo; ndokupwanya nyoka yendarira Mozisi yaaiva aita; nokuti kusvika pamazuva awa vana vaIsraeri vaiipisira zvipfungaidzo zvinonhuhwira; akaitumidza Nehushitani.

\v	28	Zvino Rabhi-Shake wakamira, ndokudanidzira nenzwi guru nechiJudha, ndokutaura achiti: Inzwai shoko ramambo mukuru, mambo weAsiriya.

\v	26	Mambo waMoabhu wakati aona kuti hondo yakamusimbira zvikuru, wakatora pamwe naye varume mazana manomwe vaivhomora munondo, kuti apazire kuna mambo waEdhomu; asi vakakonewa.

\s	Mafuta echirikadzi
\p
\v	1	Zvinokwakachema umwe mukadzi wevakadzi vevanakomana vevaporofita kuna Erisha achiti: Muranda wenyu murume wangu wafa. Zvino imwi munoziva kuti muranda wenyu waitya Jehovha; zvino kwasvika mukweretesi kuzvitorera vana vangu vaviri vave nhapwa  dzake.
\v	2	Erishandokuti kwaari: Ndingakuitirei? Ndiudze. Unei mumba? Iye ndokuti: Murandakadzi wenyu haana chinhu mumba, kunze kwechinu chemafuta.
\v	3	Iyendokuti: Enda uzvikumbirire midziyo kubva kunze kuvavakidzani vako vose, midziyo isina chinhu; usaita kuti ive mishoma.

\s	Mwana wemukadzi weShunemi anoraramiswa naErisha
\p
\v	8	Zvinokwakati ne rimwe zuva Erisha wakapfuurira kuShunemi. Zvino ipapo paiva nemukadzi wakakurumbira, iye wakamumanikidza kudya chingwa. Zvino zvakaitika pega-pega paaipfuura, kuti wakatsaukirako kuti adye chingwa.
\v	9	Zvinowakati kumurume wake: Tarirai ikozvino, ndinoziva kuti uyu munhu mutsvene waMwari, anogara achipfuura kwatiri.
\v	10	Ngatiitei hedu kamuri diki rekumusoro parusvingo, uye ngatimuisiremo mubhedha netafura nechituro nechigadziko chemwenje. Zvino zvichadai, paanouya kwatiri, kuti achatsaukiramo.
\p
\v	11	Zvino zvakaitika ne rimwe zuva, kuti wakasvikapo, ndokutsaukira kukamuri yepamusoro, ndokuraramo.
\v	12	Zvino wakati kuna Gehazi muranda wake: Dana mukadzi muShunemi uyu. Zvino wakati amudana, iye ndokumira pamberi pake.
\v	13	Zvino wakati kwaari: Iti hako kwaari: Tarirai, makatichengeta nekuchengeta uku kwese; muchaitirwei? Pane zvamungataurirwa kuna mambo kana kumukuru wehondo here? Iye ndokuti: Ndinogara pakati pevanhu vangu.
\v	14	Zvino wakati: Zvino chii chaachaitirwa? Gehazi ndokuti: Zvirokwazvo haana mwanakomana, nemurume wake wakwegura.
\v	15	Iye ndokuti: Mudane. Zvino amudana, wakamira pamukova.
\v	16	Zvino wakati: Panguva ino yakatarwa, zvichienderana nenguva yeupenyu, uchava wakagumbata mwanakomana. Asi wakati: Kwete ishe wangu, munhu waMwari, musarevera muranda wenyu nhema.
\v	17	Zvino mukadzi wakabata mimba, ndokupona mwanakomana nenguva iyo yakatarwa zvichienderana nenguva yeupenyu Erisha yaakareva kwaari.
\p
\v	18	Zvino mwana wakati akura, kwakati ne rimwe zuva, wakabuda kuenda kuna baba vake kuvakohwi.
\v	19	Zvino wakati kuna baba vake: Musoro wangu! Musoro wangu! Ivo ndokuti kumuranda: Mutakure umuise kuna mai vake.
\v	20	Zvino wakamutakura, ndokuuya naye kuna mai vake. Iye ndokugara pamabvi avo kusvika kwava masikati makuru, ndokufa.
\v	21	Zvino vakakwira ndokumuradzika pamubhedha wemunhu waMwari, ndokumupfigira ndokubuda.
\v	22	Zvino wakadana murume wake ndokuti: Nditumire henyu umwe wevaranda neimwe yembongoro, kuti ndimhanyire kumunhu waMwari, ndigodzoka.
\v	23	Iye ndokuti: Unoenderei kwaari nhasi? Hakusi kugara kwemwedzi uye haisi sabata. Iye ndokuti: Zvakanaka.
\v	24	Zvino wakasungira chigaro pambongoro, ndokuti kumuranda wake: Chaira uende, usandinonosa pakutasva, kunze kwekuti ndakuudza.
\v	25	Zvino wakaenda, ndokusvika kumunhu waMwari kugomo reKarmeri. Zvino zvakaitika munhu waMwari paakamuona ari chinhambwe, kuti wakati kuna Gehazi muranda wake: Tarira mukadzi uya muShunemi.
\v	26	Mhanya hako ikozvino umuchingamidze, uti kwaari: Kwakanaka here kwauri? Kwakanaka here kumurume wako? Kwakanaka here kumwana? Iye ndokuti: Kwakanaka!
\v	27	Paakasvika kumunhu waMwari pagomo wakabata tsoka dzake. Asi Gehazi wakaswedera kumusundira kure; munhu waMwari ndokuti: Murege, nokuti mweya weupenyu wake une shungu maari. Asi Jehovha wakandivanzira  izvozvo, haana kundiudza.
\v	28	Zvino wakati: Ndaiva ndakumbira mwanakomana here kuna ishe wangu? Handina here  kuti: Musandinyengedzera?
\v	29	Zvino  Erisha wakati kuna Gehazi: Zvisunge chiuno, ugotora tsvimbo yangu muruoko rwako uende. Kana ukasangana nemunhu usamumhorosa, uye kana munhu akakumhorosa usamupindura. Zvino uise tsvimbo yangu pachiso chemwana.
\v	30	Zvino mai vemwana vakati:  NaJehovha mupenyu, uye  nemweya weupenyu wenyu mupenyu, handingakusiii. Zvino wakasimuka, ndokumutevera.
\p
\v	31	Zvino Gehazi wakapfuurira mberi kwavo, ndokuisa tsvimbo pachiso chemwana, asi hapana kuvapo inzwi, uye kwaiva kusina kunzwa. Naizvozvo wakadzoka kusangana naye, ndokumuudza achiti: Mwana haana kumuka.
\v	32	Zvino Erisha wakapinda mumba, tarira, mwana waiva afa, akaradzikwa pamubhedha wake.
\v	33	Zvino wakapinda ndokupfiga mukova shure kwavo vari vaviri, ndokunyengetera kuna Jehovha.
\v	34	Zvino wakakwira ndokurara pamusoro pemwana, ndokuisa muromo wake pamuromo wake, uye meso ake pameso ake, nemaoko ake pamaoko ake; ndokutsivama pamusoro pake, nyama yemwana ndokudziya.
\v	35	Zvino wakadzoka ndokufamba mumba kamwe pano uye kamwe apo, ndokukwira ndokutsivama pamusoro pake; mwana ndokuhotsira kusvika kanomwe, mwana ndokuzarura meso ake.
\v	36	Zvino wakadana Gehazi, ndokuti: Dana mukadzi muShunemi uyu. Iye ndokumudana. Wakati apinda kwaari, iye ndokuti: Takura mwanakomana wako.
\v	37	Zvino wakapinda, ndokuwira patsoka dzake, ndokukotamira pavhu, ndokusimudza mwana wake ndokubuda.
\s	Erisha anorapa muriwo waiva uchivava
\p
\v	38	Zvino Erisha wakadzoka kuGirigari; uye paiva nenzara panyika; uye vana vevaporofita vaiva vagere pamberi pake; iye ndokuti kumuranda wake: Isa hari huru, ubikire vanakomana vevaporofita zvekudya zvakabikwa.
\v	39	Zvino umwe wakabuda ndokuenda kusango kunounganidza miriwo, ndokuwana muzambiringa wemusango, ndokuunganidzapo magaka, nguvo yake ndokuzara  nawo, ndokusvika ndokuachekerera muhari yezvekudya zvakabikwa, nokuti vaiva vasingaazivi.
\v	40	Zvino vakadururira vanhu kuti vadye. Zvino zvakaitika kuti vachiri kudya zvekudya zvakabikwa ivo ndokudanidzira ndokuti: Rufu muhari, munhu waMwari! Zvino vaiva vasingagoni kudya.
\v	41	Asi wakati: Zvino uyai neupfu. Iye ndokudira muhari, ndokuti: Dururirai vanhu kuti vadye. Kwaiva kusisina chakaipa muhari.

\s	Naamani anoporeswa maperembudzi ake

\v	1	ZvinoNaamani mukuru wehondo yamambo weSiriya waiva munhu mukuru pamberi patenzi wake, uye waikudzwa; nokuti naye Jehovha waiva apa Siriya kununurwa; uye waiva munhu wesimba neumhare,  asi waiva wemaperembudzi.
\v	2	ZvinoVaSiriya vaiva vabuda nehondo dzakarongwa ndokutapa musikana mudiki panyika yekwaIsraeri, wai mira pamberi pemukadzi waNaamani.
\v	3	Zvinowakati kuna tenzikadzi wake: Dai ishe wangu aiva pamberi pemuporofita ari paSamariya; ipapo angadai akamuporesa kubva pamaperembudzi ake.
\v	4	Zvino Naamani wakapinda ndokuudza ishe wake achiti: Musikana anobva kunyika yaIsraeri wakataura chekuti nechekuti.
\v	5	MamboweSiriya ndokuti: Enda, uende, uye ndichatuma tsamba kuna mambo waIsraeri. Zvino wakaenda ndokutora muruoko rwake matarenda gumi esirivha, nengoda zvuru zvitanhatu dzegoridhe, nenguvo dzekuchinja gumi.
\v	6	Zvinowakauya netsamba kuna mambo waIsraeri ichiti: Ikozvino zvino, panosvika tsamba iyi kwamuri, tarira, ndatuma Naamani muranda wangu kwamuri, kuti mumuporese kubva pamaperembudzi ake.
\p
\v	7	Zvinozvakaitika mambo waIsraeri paakaverenga tsamba kuti wakabvarura nguvo dzake ndokuti: Ndiri Mwari here kuti ndiuraye kana kuti ndiraramise, uyu zvaanotuma kwandiri kuti ndiporese munhu kubva pamaperembudzi ake? Nokuti zvirokwazvo fungai henyu muone kuti anotsvaka mukana wekurwisana neni.
\p
\v	8	Zvinozvakaitika Erisha munhu waMwari anzwa kuti mambo waIsraeri wabvarura nguvo dzake, kuti wakatuma  shoko kuna mambo achiti: Mabvarurirei nguvo dzenyu? Ngaauye ikozvino kwandiri; zvino achaziva kuti kune muporofita kwaIsraeri.
\v	9	NaizvozvoNaamani wakauya nemabhiza ake uye nengoro yake ndokumira pamukova weimba yaErisha.
\v	10	Erisha ndokutuma nhume kwaari, achiti: Enda unoshamba kanomwe muna Joridhani, uye nyama yako ichadzokera kwauri, uve wakachena.
\v	11	Asi Naamani wakatsamwa ndokuenda ndokuti: Tarirai, ndanga ndichizviti: Zvirokwazvo achabuda, amire adane zita raJehovha Mwari wake, nekufambisa-fambisa ruoko rwake pamusoro penzvimbo, agoporesa wemaperembudzi.
\v	12	Abhana neFaripari nzizi dzeDhamasiko hadzisi nani pamvura yose yaIsraeri here? Handingashambi madziri, ndikava wakachena here? Zvino wakatendeuka, ndokuenda nehasha.
\v	13	Zvino varanda vake vakaswedera, ndokutaura kwaari ndokuti: Baba vangu,  dai muporofita ataura nemwi chinhu chikuru, hamuzaiita here? Ndoda zvaati kwamuri: Shamba uve wakachena?
\v	14	Zvino wakaburuka, ndoku zvinyika muna Joridhani kanomwe zvichienderana neshoko remunhu waMwari; nyama yake ndokudzoka ndokufanana nenyama yemwana mudiki, akava wakachena.
\p
\v	15	Zvino wakadzokera kumunhu waMwari, iye nechaunga chake chose, ndokusvika ndokumira pamberi pake ndokuti: Tarirai henyu, ndava kuziva kuti hakuna Mwari panyika yose, kunze kwekwaIsraeri; zvino ikozvino gamuchirai henyu chipo kubva kumuranda wenyu.
\v	16	Asi iye wakati:  NaJehovha mupenyu wandimire pamberi pake, handingagamuchiri chinhu. Zvino wakamugombedzera kuti agamuchire, asi wakaramba.
\v	17	Zvino Naamani wakati: Kana zvisakadaro, ngakupiwe henyu muranda wenyu mutoro wemanyurusi maviri wevhu. Nokuti muranda wenyu haachazopi chipo chinopiswa nechibairo kuna vamwe vamwari, asi kuna Jehovha.
\v	18	Pachinhu ichi Jehovha ngaaregerere muranda wake, kana tenzi wangu achipinda mumba maRimoni kunamatapo, ndokusendamira paruoko rwangu, uye ndikafugama mumba maRimoni; kana ndikafugama mumba maRimoni Jehovha ngaaregererewo muranda wake pachinhu ichi.
\v	19	Zvino wakati kwaari: Enda nerugare. Zvino wakabva kwaari chinhambwe chidiki.
\p
\v	20	Asi Gehazi muranda waErisha munhu waMwari wakati: Tarirai, tenzi wangu warega Naamani muSiriya uyu pakusagamuchira paruoko rwake zvaakauya nazvo;  naJehovha mupenyu, zvirokwazvo ndichamhanya ndimutevere, nditore chimwe kwaari.
\v	21	Naizvozvo Gehazi wakadzingirirana naNaamani. Naamani paaka muona achimhanya kumutevera, wakaburuka pangoro kumuchingamidza, ndokuti: Kwakanaka here?
\v	22	Iye ndokuti: Kwakanaka. Tenzi wangu wandituma achiti: Tarirai, ikozvinowo kwasvika kwandiri majaya maviri achibva kugomo raEfuremu evanakomana vevaporofita; ape hako tarenda resirivha nenguvo mbiri dzekuchinja.
\v	23	Naamani ndokuti: Tenda, utore matarenda maviri. Zvino wakamugombedzera ndokusungira matarenda maviri esirivha muhomwe mbiri, pamwe nenguvo mbiri dzekuchinja, ndokuisa pavaviri vevaranda vake, vakazvitakura pamberi pake.
\v	24	Wakati asvika pachikomo, waka zvitora paruoko rwavo, ndokuisa mumba, ndokurega varume kuti vaende, ivo ndokuenda.
\v	25	Zvino iye wakapinda, ndokumira pamberi patenzi wake. Erisha ndokuti kwaari:  Unobva kupi Gehazi? Iye ndokuti: Muranda wenyu wanga asina kuenda uko kana uko.
\v	26	Zvino wakati kwaari: Moyo wangu hauna kuenda  newe here murume paadzoka kubva pangoro yake kukuchingamidza? Inguva yekugamuchira mari nekugamuchira nguvo neminda yemiorivhi neminda yemizambiringa nemakwai nenzombe nevaranda nevarandakadzi here?
\v	27	Naizvozvo maperembudzi aNaamani achanamatira kwauri nekumbeu yako nekusingaperi. Zvino wakabuda pamberi pake ava wemaperembudzi,  akachena semagada echando.

\v	1	Zvinovanakomana vevaporofita vakati kuna Erisha: Tarirai ikozvino, nzvimbo patigere mberi kwako yakamanikana zvikuru kwatiri.
\v	2	Regaihenyu tiende kuJoridhani tigonotora kubvako umwe neumwe danda, uye ngatizviitirei nzvimboko  yekugarako. Iye ndokuti: Endai.
\v	3	Zvinoumwe wakati: Tendai henyu mugoenda nevaranda venyu. Iye ndokuti: Ini ndichaenda.
\v	4	Zvinowakaenda navo. Pavakasvika kuJoridhani, vakatema matanda.
\v	5	Asiumwe wakati achitema danda, musoro wedemo ndokuwira mumvura. Zvino wakadanidzira ndokuti: Haiwa, tenzi wangu! Nokuti raiva rakweretwa.

\s	VaSiriya vanopofumadzwa paSamariya

\v	8	Zvinomambo weSiriya wairwisana naIsraeri; ndokurangana nevaranda vake achiti: Panzvimbo yekuti neyekuti uchava musasa wangu.

\v	16	Zvino iye wakati: Usatya, nokuti vanesu vakawanda kupfuura vaanavo.
\v	17	Zvino Erisha wakanyengetera ndokuti: Jehovha, zarurai henyu meso ake kuti aone. Jehovha ndokuzarura meso ejaya iro, iro ndokuona, zvino tarira, gomo rizere nemabhiza nengoro dzemoto zvakakomba Erisha.

\v	20	Zvino zvakaitika pakupinda kwavo muSamariya kuti Erisha wakati: Jehovha, zarurai meso evava kuti vaone. Jehovha ndokuzarura meso avo, zvino ndokuona, zvino tarira, vaiva pakati peSamariya.
\v	21	Mambo waIsraeri wakati achivaona ndokuti kuna Erisha: Baba vangu, ndirove here, ndirove here?

\v	13	Zvino umwe wevaranda vake wakapindura ndokuti: Regai vatore mashanu emabhiza asara, asara mukati me guta. Tarirai, vakaita sechaunga chose chaIsraeri chakasara mariri; tarirai vakaita sechaunga chose chaIsraeri chakapedzwa; ngatitume tione.

\v	16	Zvino vanhu vakabuda, ndokupamba musasa wevaSiriya. Naizvozvo chiyero cheupfu hwakatsetseka chakava cheshekeri, nezviyero zviviri zvebhari zveshekeri, zvichienderana neshoko raJehovha.

\v	18	Zvino zvakaitika munhu waMwari sezvaaiva ataura kuna mambo achiti: Zviyero zviviri zvebhari neshekeri, nechiyero cheupfu hwakatsetseka neshekeri panguva inenge ino mangwana zvichava pasuwo reSamariya.
\v	19	Zvino ishe wakapindura munhu waMwari ndokuti: Tarira, dai Jehovha akaita mahwindo kumatenga, zvichava sechinhu ichi here? Iye ndokuti: Tarira, uchazviona nemeso ako, asi haungazvidyi.
\v	20	Zvino zvakava saizvozvo kwaari; nokuti vanhu vakamutsikira pasi pasuwo, ndokufa.
